Your pet is family. We treat them that way.

Moving a pet internationally involves a maze of rules that most people have never come across before, and honestly, why would you have? Did you know Australia won't allow your dog to land unless a specific blood test was completed at least 180 days before travel? Or that New Zealand inspectors can turn an animal away at the border if a single field on the health certificate has been filled in incorrectly? Some countries ban entire breeds. Others require import permits that take weeks to obtain. Some airlines won't carry flat-faced dogs at all, regardless of how healthy they are.

That's where we come in. Our team works with pet owners relocating to and from every corner of the world. We know the regulations for every animal, every country, every airline. Whether your dog is heading from Dubai to London, or your cat needs to make the journey from Sydney to Vancouver, we've handled that route before and we know exactly what's required.

You decide how much help you want. Some clients just need us to book the right cargo space on the right airline. Others hand everything over from day one: doorstep collection, vet coordination, documentation, airport handling, and delivery at the other end. Most people land somewhere in the middle. Whatever feels right for you, our job is to make sure nothing gets missed.

A lot of people trust us with the animals they love most. That's not something we take lightly.

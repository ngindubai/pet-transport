## Indonesia to Australia: The Process, Honestly

Moving a pet from Indonesia to Australia is demanding. The 180-day titre test window, DAFF's strict permit conditions, the need for an overseas titre test lab, BARANTAN endorsement logistics and mandatory quarantine all require careful coordination. But hundreds of owners complete this move successfully every year, the process is known, the steps are clear, and if you start early enough, it is achievable.

**Start planning at least 24–40 weeks before your intended travel date.** Most owners who run into problems underestimated the titre test timeline or had to rebook because they missed their quarantine slot.

---

## Indonesia's Classification

Indonesia is a **Group 3 country** for Australian pet imports. Group 3 means:

- DAFF import permit (apply 6–12 months in advance)
- FAVN titre test (≥0.5 IU/ml, blood collected 180+ days before Australian arrival)
- BARANTAN-endorsed export health certificate
- Pre-export treatments as specified in the permit
- 10 nights mandatory quarantine at ACDP Mickleham

---

## BARANTAN Export Health Certificate

BARANTAN offices in Jakarta, Bali (DPS area), Surabaya and other cities issue export health certificates. The process:

1. Book an appointment with your local BARANTAN office (advance booking essential)
2. Bring the animal and all documents (vaccination records, DAFF import permit, microchip certificate)
3. BARANTAN vet inspects the animal and reviews documents
4. Certificate is issued and endorsed
5. Your DAFF permit specifies how many days before departure the certificate must be issued, typically 5 days

Bali owners note: the Bali BARANTAN office handles many international pet export certificates and is familiar with DAFF requirements. Still book well in advance.

---

## Getting the Titre Test Done

No DAFF-approved titre test lab exists in Indonesia. Steps:

1. Confirm blood draw date (must be 180+ days before Australian arrival)
2. Your vet draws blood in EDTA tubes per the lab's submission protocol
3. Ship via FedEx International Priority or DHL to CSIRO AAHL (Geelong), VSL (UK) or ANSES (France)
4. Results returned by email in 5–10 business days
5. File the result certificate with DAFF as part of your permit documentation

For Bali-based owners, international couriers including FedEx and DHL operate from DPS for biological sample shipping.

---

## Airlines from Indonesia to Australia

**Garuda Indonesia (GA)** operates CGK–MEL, CGK–SYD, CGK–BNE, CGK–PER and DPS–PER. Garuda accepts pet cargo: book through Garuda Cargo with 48-hour advance notice minimum. Live animal handling fees apply.

**Singapore Airlines via SIN** is the strongest alternative, particularly for connecting from regional Indonesian cities. Changi's live animal centre is one of Asia's best.

**Jetstar Asia via SIN** is an option for budget-conscious owners but check pet cargo policies carefully: LCC cargo infrastructure varies.

---

## ACDP Quarantine: Bali Owners' Notes

Mickleham is in Victoria. If your final destination is Perth, Brisbane or Sydney, ACDP will still be your quarantine facility for 10 nights, Australia has only one post-arrival quarantine facility for companion animals. After release, book an ACDP-approved transport company to arrange onward travel.

ACDP contact and booking: through DAFF when your import permit is issued. Do not book quarantine independently, DAFF coordinates the booking as part of the permit process.

Expect total costs of AUD 6,000–15,000 for the end-to-end Indonesia to Australia pet move, depending on animal size, agent use and airline.

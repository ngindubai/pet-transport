## Croatia Airlines: Flying to the Adriatic

Croatia Airlines is the national carrier of Croatia, operating from Zagreb Airport (ZAG) and Split Airport (SPU). A Star Alliance member, it connects Croatian cities to major European hubs. For pet owners relocating to Croatia or transiting through Zagreb, Croatia Airlines is the main option.

## Cabin Pet Policy

Croatia Airlines accepts small pets in the passenger cabin:

- **Species accepted**: dogs, cats
- **Combined weight limit**: up to 8 kg (pet plus carrier)
- **Carrier**: IATA-compliant soft or semi-rigid, fits under the seat in front
- **One pet per passenger** in the cabin
- **Advance booking required** -- do not leave this to check-in

## Hold Travel for Larger Pets

Dogs and cats that exceed the cabin weight limit travel in the hold:

- IATA-compliant rigid crate required
- Temperature-controlled, pressurised hold on all jet aircraft in the fleet
- Charged as excess baggage based on combined weight
- Some restrictions on brachycephalic breeds and very large animals -- confirm with Croatia Airlines cargo directly

## EU Pet Travel Rules for Croatia

Croatia joined the EU in 2013 and the Schengen Area in January 2023.

**From EU countries:**
- Valid EU Pet Passport
- Microchip (ISO standard)
- Current rabies vaccination (21-day waiting period for first-time vaccines)

**From the UK (post-Brexit):**
- Animal Health Certificate (AHC) -- issued by a UK Official Vet within 10 days of travel, countersigned by APHA
- Microchip
- Current rabies vaccination
- Tapeworm treatment for dogs (1 to 5 days before entering any EU member state)

The EU border inspection post at Zagreb Airport (ZAG) handles live animal checks on arrival. Allow extra time.

## Summer Travel in Croatia

Croatia is a top summer destination. July and August temperatures can exceed 35 degrees C in Dalmatia. Croatia Airlines may restrict hold pet acceptance during extreme heat periods. If you are moving in summer, confirm with the airline well in advance and consider whether driving might be a safer option for your pet.

## Booking Process

Contact Croatia Airlines directly to add a pet to your booking. The online booking system does not always handle pet additions -- call or email the airline to confirm. Get written confirmation with your pet details (species, breed, weight, crate dimensions).

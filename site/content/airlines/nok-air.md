Nok Air is one of Thailand's leading low-cost domestic carriers, operating from its hub at Bangkok Don Mueang International Airport (DMK) to destinations throughout Thailand. For pet owners living or relocating within Thailand, Nok Air offers a useful domestic option for inter-city travel with a small cat or dog.

## Does Nok Air Accept Pets?

Nok Air accepts **small cats and dogs in the passenger cabin** on its domestic Thai routes. This is an in-cabin service only -- Nok Air does not operate a standard live animal cargo service for pets on most domestic routes.

The cabin pet service is available on selected domestic routes and is subject to:

- Combined weight of pet plus carrier not exceeding 7 kg
- An approved ventilated carrier that fits under the seat in front
- Maximum 1 pet per passenger
- Limited pet spaces per flight (typically 1 to 2); book early
- A valid health certificate issued by a licensed Thai veterinarian within 7 days of travel
- The pet must remain in the carrier and under the seat at all times

## What Documents Do You Need?

For domestic flights within Thailand with Nok Air, you typically need:

- A **health certificate** from a licensed veterinarian confirming the animal is fit to fly and free from infectious disease
- **Vaccination records** showing the pet is current on rabies and core vaccines

You do not need an import permit or government endorsement for domestic travel within Thailand -- these requirements apply at international borders, not for internal flights.

## Booking a Pet on Nok Air

Nok Air does not allow pets to be added through its standard online booking system in all cases. To add a cabin pet:

1. Book your ticket online or through Nok Air's call centre
2. Contact Nok Air customer service to register your pet on the booking (at least 24 hours before departure, preferably earlier)
3. Obtain written confirmation of pet acceptance
4. Bring the health certificate to check-in

Arrive early at Don Mueang or your departure airport -- check-in for cabin pets may take longer.

## Routes Where Pets Are Accepted

Nok Air's domestic network covers routes including:
- Bangkok Don Mueang (DMK) to Chiang Mai (CNX)
- Bangkok Don Mueang (DMK) to Phuket (HKT)
- Bangkok Don Mueang (DMK) to Udon Thani (UTH)
- Bangkok Don Mueang (DMK) to Hat Yai (HDY)
- Various secondary Thai domestic routes

Pet acceptance on any specific route should be confirmed with Nok Air directly, as some routes may be operated by aircraft types with different hold or cabin configurations.

## Moving to or from Thailand Internationally

Nok Air operates primarily within Thailand and does not operate long-haul international routes. If you are relocating internationally to Thailand, you will need to use an international carrier for the first leg and possibly connect to a domestic Nok Air flight onward.

For international pet import requirements for Thailand, the Department of Livestock Development (DLD) governs the import process. See the [Thailand pet import guide](/blog/thailand-pet-import-guide/) for a full breakdown.

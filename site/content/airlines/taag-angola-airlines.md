TAAG Angola Airlines (Linhas Aereas de Angola) is Angola's national carrier, connecting Luanda (Quatro de Fevereiro International Airport, LAD) to Europe, the Middle East, and other African destinations. For pet owners relocating to Angola, TAAG and connecting carriers on European routes provide the main transport options.

## In-Cabin Policy

TAAG Angola Airlines does not generally accept pets in the passenger cabin on international routes. All pet transport is arranged as hold cargo.

## Hold Cargo for Pets

TAAG accepts dogs and cats as hold cargo on applicable routes. Requirements include:

- **IATA-compliant crate**: Solid base, ventilated sides, secure door, internal water dish
- **Health certificate**: Issued by an official government veterinarian, within ten days of travel
- **Angola import permit**: Obtained from the Angolan Ministry of Agriculture before travel
- **Rabies and core vaccinations**: Current and recorded on the health certificate
- **Microchip**: ISO standard

Contact TAAG Angola Airlines cargo well in advance to book the live animal space. Cargo capacity for live animals on TAAG is limited.

## Angola Pet Import Requirements

Angola's live animal import regulations are managed by the Ministry of Agriculture. Requirements typically include:

- Veterinary import permit (apply before travel -- allow two to four weeks)
- Health certificate from an official government veterinarian, within ten days of departure
- Rabies vaccination (current)
- Core vaccinations for dogs and cats
- Microchip (ISO 11784/11785)
- Internal and external parasite treatment records

Confirm the current requirements with the Angolan Ministry of Agriculture or a pet transport specialist with Luanda experience, as requirements can be updated.

## Routes and Network

TAAG Angola Airlines operates from Luanda (LAD) to:
- **Lisbon (LIS)** -- primary European connection
- **London Heathrow (LHR)** -- UK connection
- **Dubai (DXB)** -- Middle East connection
- **Johannesburg (JNB)**, **Cape Town (CPT)** -- southern Africa
- **Nairobi (NBO)**, other African cities

## Alternative Carriers

For European origins, TAP Air Portugal (Lisbon) and British Airways (London) also serve Luanda and may provide alternative routing options for pet cargo.

## Booking

Contact TAAG Angola Airlines cargo to confirm live animal availability on your specific route and dates. A pet transport specialist familiar with Luanda routings can help navigate the booking and documentation process.

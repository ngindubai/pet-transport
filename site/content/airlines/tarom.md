TAROM is Romania's national carrier and a SkyTeam member, operating from Henri Coanda International Airport (OTP) in Bucharest. TAROM offers European short-haul routes as well as some longer-range services to the Middle East and North America, and is the primary carrier for pet transport into and out of Romania.

## Pets in the Cabin

TAROM accepts small dogs, cats, and caged birds in the cabin on most routes. The combined weight of the animal and carrier must not exceed 8kg. The carrier must be soft-sided and fit under the seat in front of you. One pet per passenger is allowed.

## Pets as Cargo

Larger pets travel as checked baggage in the cargo hold in an IATA-compliant hard crate. The crate must allow the animal to stand, turn, and lie down. Water dispensers must be fitted inside and dry food secured to the door for longer journeys.

## EU Pet Requirements

For intra-EU travel, pets need an EU pet passport, ISO microchip, and current rabies vaccination. Pets entering Romania from outside the EU (including from the UK) need the standard EU pathway: AHC, titre test, three-month wait, and border inspection at OTP.

## Booking Process

Pets must be added to the booking through TAROM customer service. The process cannot be completed online. Call TAROM or visit a ticketing office. Fees apply per segment.

## Romania Pet Import

Pets entering Romania are inspected by ANSVSA officials at Bucharest Henri Coanda Airport. Keep documentation accessible -- not packed in checked luggage. For pets from non-EU countries, the official AHC must be presented along with the titre test result.

## Practical Note

TAROM is a solid choice for intra-European pet moves involving Romania. For intercontinental routes, TAROM may need to be combined with a longer-haul partner carrier. SkyTeam partners include Air France, KLM, and Delta, which all have more developed intercontinental pet cargo programmes.

## Royal Jordanian Pet Transport Policy

Royal Jordanian is Jordan's national carrier and a Oneworld member, operating from Queen Alia International Airport in Amman (AMM). It connects Jordan to the UK, Europe, the USA, and countries across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia.

For expats and international workers relocating to Jordan - particularly those working with NGOs, embassies, or international organisations based in Amman - Royal Jordanian is often the most direct routing option for bringing pets into the country.

Pets travel as cargo on Royal Jordanian. There is no cabin pet option. Live animal cargo acceptance should be confirmed with the airline when booking.

**Entering Jordan with a pet:** The Jordanian Ministry of Agriculture manages pet imports. Requirements include a veterinary health certificate (issued within a short window before travel), microchip documentation, and a valid rabies vaccination. An advance import permit from the Ministry is also typically required - do not assume you can bring a pet into Jordan without this in hand. Requirements can change, so confirm current rules directly with the Ministry or through an IPATA-accredited agent with Middle East experience.

**Temperature:** Amman summers are hot. Live animal embargoes may apply during peak heat months. If you're routing through Amman or flying to Jordan, check temperature restrictions at the time of booking.

**Booking:** Contact Royal Jordanian Cargo directly, or use an IPATA-accredited pet transport agent with experience in Middle East routes.

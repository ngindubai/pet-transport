Royal Air Maroc is Morocco's national carrier and a oneworld member, operating from Mohammed V International Airport in Casablanca (CMN). The airline connects Morocco and North Africa to Europe, North America, the Middle East, and a broad Africa network. For pet owners relocating to or from Morocco or routing through Casablanca to African destinations, Royal Air Maroc is a well-established option.

**Cabin pets**

Royal Air Maroc accepts small pets in the cabin on qualifying routes. Requirements:
- Combined weight (pet plus carrier): generally up to 8kg
- Soft carrier that fits under the seat in front
- Pet must remain in the carrier throughout the flight
- One pet per passenger
- A pet fee applies

Confirm cabin pet availability for your specific route and booking class when purchasing tickets.

**Cargo pets**

Larger pets travel as checked cargo. IATA-approved hard-sided crates are required. The crate must allow the animal to stand, turn, sit, and lie down naturally. Water and food dishes must be accessible from outside.

**Breed restrictions**

Brachycephalic breeds are restricted from cargo transport. The restricted list typically includes Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, Pugs, Boston Terriers, and flat-faced cats (Persians, Himalayans, and others). Confirm your breed with Royal Air Maroc before booking.

**Morocco import requirements**

Pets arriving in Morocco from most European and North American countries need a government-endorsed health certificate, rabies vaccination, and ISO-compliant microchip. Morocco does not generally require a titre test for pets from most EU and European countries. Confirm current requirements with the Moroccan agricultural authority (ONSSA - Office National de Securite Sanitaire des Produits Alimentaires) for your specific origin country.

**Africa network**

Royal Air Maroc's Casablanca hub is a well-positioned gateway between Europe and Africa. The airline operates to destinations including Dakar, Abidjan, Bamako, Conakry, Douala, Nairobi, and others - making it a practical routing option for pets moving between Europe and West or Central Africa.

**Booking**

Add cabin pets through Royal Air Maroc's website or customer service. For cargo pets, contact the airline's cargo division. Confirm route-specific acceptance and any seasonal restrictions before purchasing tickets.

Gulf Air is Bahrain's national carrier and one of the Gulf region's established airlines, operating from Bahrain International Airport (BAH) to destinations across the Middle East, Asia, Europe, and Africa. For pet owners relocating to Bahrain or routing through the Gulf, Gulf Air is one of the options alongside the larger Emirates, Etihad, and Qatar Airways networks.

**Cabin pets**

Gulf Air does not allow pets in the passenger cabin on international routes. Pets travel as checked cargo.

**Cargo transport**

Gulf Air accepts live animals as checked cargo. Advance booking through Gulf Air's cargo or passenger services team is required. Not all routes or aircraft types accept live animals - confirm route-specific acceptance before booking flights.

**Crate requirements**

IATA live animal standards apply:
- Hard-sided, ventilated crate
- Size appropriate for the animal to stand, turn, sit, and lie down
- Water and food dishes accessible from outside
- "LIVE ANIMAL" labels on all sides

**Temperature restrictions**

Bahrain and the wider Gulf region experiences extreme heat from May through September, with temperatures regularly exceeding 40C. Most airlines including Gulf Air apply temperature embargoes on live animal cargo during peak summer. If you must travel in summer, schedule flights for the coolest part of the day (early morning or late evening) and confirm that cargo hold conditions meet live animal requirements.

**Bahrain import requirements**

Pets arriving in Bahrain need: a government-endorsed health certificate from the origin country, rabies vaccination, microchip, and an import permit from the Ministry of Municipalities Affairs and Agriculture. Certain dog breeds (particularly large or powerful types) may face additional restrictions or require prior approval. Check current Bahrain import requirements well before your planned travel date.

**Booking**

Contact Gulf Air directly to arrange live animal cargo. Confirm route acceptance, fees, and any specific requirements for your origin country and destination.

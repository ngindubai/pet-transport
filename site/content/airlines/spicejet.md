## SpiceJet Pet Policy Overview

SpiceJet is one of India's most popular low-cost airlines, operating an extensive domestic network across India and international routes to the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and a few other destinations.

SpiceJet accepts small pets in the cabin on domestic Indian routes -- one of the more accessible options for pet owners moving within India or making domestic connections as part of a larger international journey.

## Cabin Pet Policy (Domestic India)

**Weight limit:** Combined weight of pet and carrier must not exceed 7 kg. If the pet plus carrier exceeds 7 kg, the booking will not be accepted.

**Carrier:** Must be a soft-sided, airline-approved pet carrier. The carrier must fit under the seat in front of you. Rigid plastic crates are not accepted in the SpiceJet cabin.

**Species:** Dogs and cats. Exotic animals, birds, and other species are not accepted.

**Number of pets:** One pet per passenger.

**In-flight:** The pet must remain in the carrier and under the seat throughout the flight. Pets cannot be taken out of the carrier on board.

## Breed Restrictions

SpiceJet restricts:
- Brachycephalic dog breeds (Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, Pugs, Shih Tzu, Boxer, Pekingese, and similar)
- Brachycephalic cat breeds (Persian, Exotic Shorthair, Himalayan)
- Any breed on India's dangerous dog list

Always confirm with SpiceJet directly for your specific breed before booking.

## How to Book

Online booking is not available for pets on SpiceJet. You must:
1. Call SpiceJet customer service: spicejet.com or 0124-4983410 (India)
2. Request a pet booking on your specific flight
3. Receive confirmation and fee details
4. At the airport: check in at the SpiceJet counter with your pet carrier and documentation

**Fee:** SpiceJet charges a per-flight fee for cabin pets. The fee is fixed per sector (not per kilogram for cabin travel). Confirm the current fee when booking.

## SpiceJet for International Connections

If you are making an international pet move through India with SpiceJet for a domestic connection leg, be aware:
- The domestic SpiceJet cabin pet policy only applies to SpiceJet-operated domestic sectors
- For the international leg, you will need a different carrier with an international pet policy
- Ensure your pet's international documentation is complete for the international carrier

## Alternatives for Indian Routes

| Route | Alternatives |
|-------|-------------|
| Delhi to Mumbai | Air India, IndiGo, Vistara/Air India |
| Delhi to Bangalore | Air India, IndiGo |
| Mumbai to Chennai | Air India, IndiGo |
| International from India | Air India (most international routes), Emirates, Qatar Airways, Singapore Airlines |

**Air India** (the full-service flag carrier) has broader pet policies including international routes. For international pet transport from India, Air India is generally the best Indian carrier option.

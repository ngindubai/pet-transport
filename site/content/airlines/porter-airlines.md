Porter Airlines is a Canadian regional carrier based at Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (YTZ) and Halifax Stanfield International Airport (YHZ). Known for its focus on business travellers and its boutique cabin experience, Porter serves Canadian domestic routes and US destinations. For pet owners with small dogs and cats, Porter is a practical option for in-cabin travel on applicable routes.

## In-Cabin Pet Policy

Porter Airlines accepts small dogs and cats in the passenger cabin subject to the following conditions:

- Combined weight of the pet and carrier: maximum 10 kg (22 lbs)
- The carrier must fit under the seat in front of you (check current Porter dimensions on their website)
- One pet per passenger; maximum two pets per cabin per flight
- The pet must remain in its carrier for the entire flight
- A pet fee applies per flight segment

Porter's 10 kg combined limit is slightly more generous than many airlines' 8 kg limit, which means slightly larger small breeds may qualify.

## Booking Your Pet on Porter

Add your pet to your booking when purchasing your ticket through Porter's website, or call Porter's reservations team to add a pet to an existing booking. Cabin pet spaces are limited -- book as early as possible to secure a space on your preferred flight.

## Accepted Species and Carrier Requirements

Porter accepts domestic dogs and cats as in-cabin pets. The carrier must:
- Be soft-sided
- Fit fully under the seat in front of you (without protruding into the aisle or adjacent seat)
- Have mesh ventilation on at least one side
- Be leak-proof and escape-proof

Your pet must be comfortable in the carrier throughout the flight without showing signs of distress. Disruptive pets may be refused boarding.

## Hold Cargo

Porter Airlines does not operate a dedicated live animal hold cargo programme. Pets travelling as hold cargo are not accepted by Porter. For larger dogs or cargo shipments, you will need a full-service carrier (Air Canada, WestJet, or a specialist charter).

## Routes Served

Porter's network covers:
- Toronto (YTZ) to Ottawa (YOW), Montreal (YUL), Halifax (YHZ), and other Canadian cities
- Toronto to New York Newark (EWR), Chicago Midway (MDW), Boston (BOS), and other US destinations
- Halifax to Boston (BOS) and New York (EWR)
- Porter has been expanding its network with new Airbus A220 aircraft on longer routes

For Toronto-based pet owners with small dogs or cats travelling within Canada or to eastern US cities, Porter is a convenient in-cabin option.

## Documentation

For Canadian domestic travel: no specific veterinary documentation required (though current vaccinations are good practice).

For US-Canada cross-border travel: CDC requirements for dogs include a current rabies vaccination for dogs from Canada, though Canada is a low-risk origin. Confirm current US entry requirements with the CDC before travel.

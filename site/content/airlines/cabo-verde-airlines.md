Cabo Verde Airlines (TACV -- Transportes Aereos de Cabo Verde) is the national carrier of Cape Verde, connecting the Atlantic archipelago to Portugal, the Netherlands, the United States, and other destinations. For pet owners moving to or from Cape Verde, the airline provides limited live animal cargo capacity.

## Live Animal Policy

Cabo Verde Airlines accepts pets as cargo on applicable international routes, but the service has limited capacity compared to major European carriers. In-cabin pet acceptance is limited or not available on most international routes -- confirm directly with TACV for current cabin pet policy.

For hold cargo, pets travel in IATA-compliant crates in the temperature-controlled hold. Standard requirements apply:
- IATA-compliant crate with secure door, solid base, ventilation on at least three sides
- Valid health certificate from an accredited vet
- Advance booking required -- cargo space for live animals is limited on TACV aircraft

## Routes and Network

Cabo Verde Airlines serves:
- **Amilcar Cabral International (SID)** -- Sal island, resort destination
- **Nelson Mandela International (RAI)** -- Praia, Santiago island, national capital
- **Lisbon (LIS)** -- primary European connection
- **Amsterdam (AMS)** -- European routes
- **Boston (BOS)** -- transatlantic North American connection

## Cape Verde Pet Import Requirements

Before flying a pet to Cape Verde, confirm the current import requirements with the Cape Verde Ministry of Agriculture and Environment. Key requirements typically include:
- A health certificate from an official government veterinarian, within ten days of travel
- Rabies vaccination (current and valid on arrival)
- Core vaccinations
- Microchip

Cape Verde is an island nation with biosecurity interests, so confirming the most current requirements before booking is important.

## Alternative Carriers

If TACV cargo capacity is limited for your travel dates, TAP Air Portugal and other European carriers serving Cape Verde may provide alternatives. A pet transport specialist familiar with Atlantic island routes can identify the most reliable carrier option for your specific dates and pet size.

## Booking

Contact Cabo Verde Airlines cargo or reservations directly to confirm live animal availability and book your pet's space. Advance notice of at least two weeks is recommended.

## LATAM Airlines Pet Transport Policy

LATAM Airlines Group is South America's largest airline group, operating from hubs in Sao Paulo, Santiago, Lima, and Bogota. It is the dominant carrier for pet transport within South America and between South America and North America or Europe.

One important note: LATAM operates as several country-specific entities (LATAM Brasil, LATAM Chile, LATAM Colombia, LATAM Peru, LATAM Ecuador). Pet policies, weight limits, and accepted breeds can vary slightly by entity. Always confirm the rules for your specific origin country's LATAM operation.

**Within South America:** LATAM connects Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Peru, Ecuador, Argentina, Bolivia, Uruguay, Paraguay, and other destinations. Small pets meeting the in-cabin weight limit (typically carrier plus pet under 10kg) can travel in the cabin on most routes. Larger pets travel as cargo.

**Europe routes:** LATAM Brasil operates Sao Paulo to London Heathrow, Madrid, Frankfurt, Paris, and other European cities. LATAM Chile flies Santiago to Madrid. On these transatlantic routes, pets travel as cargo. The UK, EU, and individual South American countries each have their own documentation requirements.

**USA routes:** LATAM connects multiple South American cities to Miami, New York, Los Angeles, and other US destinations.

**Booking:** Book in-cabin pets via the LATAM website for your country. For cargo, contact LATAM Cargo in your origin country directly.

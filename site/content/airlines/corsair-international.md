Corsair International is a French leisure carrier operating primarily from Paris Orly Airport (ORY) to French Overseas Territories and long-haul leisure destinations. It is a specialist carrier for travellers between metropolitan France and the French Antilles, Reunion, Mayotte, and similar destinations -- routes that are significant for French nationals and expats moving pets to and from French Overseas Territories.

## Route Network

Corsair's main destinations from Paris Orly include:
- Martinique (FDF) and Guadeloupe (PTP) in the Caribbean
- Reunion (RUN) and Mayotte in the Indian Ocean
- Mauritius (MRU)
- Canada (Montreal YUL, Toronto YYZ)
- Senegal (Dakar DSS)

For pet owners, the most significant routes are to the French Overseas Territories (DOM-TOM) and to Mauritius, both of which have specific biosecurity requirements.

## In-Cabin Pet Travel

Corsair generally accepts small pets in the passenger cabin on applicable routes. Standard conditions:
- Combined weight of pet and carrier typically up to 8 kg
- Soft-sided carrier that fits under the seat
- One pet per passenger
- Pet supplement fee applies
- Book in advance -- cabin pet spaces are limited

## Hold Cargo

Corsair accepts larger animals in the hold on applicable routes. Contact Corsair's cargo team directly for hold cargo bookings. Have your pet's species, breed, dimensions, and weight ready when enquiring.

## French Overseas Territories: Key Biosecurity Notes

The French Overseas Territories (Martinique, Guadeloupe, Reunion, Mayotte) are EU territories, so the standard EU pet travel framework applies for pets from EU member states. However, some territories have additional local requirements:

- **Reunion**: Reunion has strict controls on pet import due to its island biosecurity. Contact the local DAAF Reunion for the current import requirements, which include specific parasite treatments and potentially a titre test for pets from some origins.
- **Martinique and Guadeloupe**: Standard EU rules apply for EU-origin pets. Non-EU origin requires third-country import process.
- **Mayotte**: Mayotte became an EU member territory in 2014. Import rules are stricter than mainland France for some species -- confirm with the local veterinary authority.

## Mauritius via Corsair

Corsair serves Mauritius (MRU). Mauritius has its own strict pet import requirements, including quarantine for all incoming pets. Do not confuse Mauritius's national import rules with French territory rules -- Mauritius is an independent nation with its own process. See our dedicated Mauritius pet import guide for full details.

## Documentation

- For French territories (EU): EU pet passport or EU-format health certificate
- For Mauritius: Mauritius Veterinary Services import permit, titre test, quarantine booking
- For Canada: CFIA requirements (rabies vaccination for dogs)
- For Senegal: Ministry of Livestock import permit

Always confirm the current documentation requirements with Corsair and with the destination country's veterinary authority before booking.

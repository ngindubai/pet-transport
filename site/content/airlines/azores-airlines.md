Azores Airlines (operating as SATA International) is the flag carrier of the Azores archipelago and a specialist on Atlantic routes connecting the Azores and Portugal to North America. For pet owners in the Azores or mainland Portugal, the airline provides a practical option for transatlantic pet transport.

## In-Cabin Pet Policy

Azores Airlines accepts small dogs and cats in the passenger cabin on applicable routes. The requirements are:

- **Combined weight**: Pet plus carrier must not exceed 8 kg
- **Carrier type**: Soft-sided carrier that fits under the seat in front of you
- **Carrier dimensions**: Must be secure, fully enclosed, and meet under-seat dimensions (confirm current limits when booking)
- **One pet per passenger**
- Limited pet spaces per flight -- book in advance

The pet must remain inside the carrier throughout the flight and cannot be placed on the seat.

## Hold Cargo for Larger Animals

For dogs and cats exceeding the in-cabin weight limit, Azores Airlines accepts pets as checked baggage (accompanying the owner) or as live animal cargo on applicable routes. Animals travel in IATA-compliant crates in the temperature-controlled hold.

Contact Azores Airlines cargo or reservations to arrange hold transport. IATA crate specifications and a valid health certificate are required for hold travel.

## Routes and Network

Azores Airlines' core network connects:
- **Ponta Delgada (PDL)** -- Azores hub, serving inter-island routes and transatlantic connections
- **Lisbon (LIS)** -- mainland Portugal connections and transatlantic services
- **North America**: Boston (BOS), Toronto (YYZ), Oakland (OAK), Montreal (YUL), and other US and Canadian cities

These Atlantic routes are particularly relevant for expat families in the Azores or Portugal relocating to North America, or vice versa.

## Brachycephalic Breed Restrictions

Azores Airlines restricts certain brachycephalic breeds for welfare reasons. Confirm acceptance of your specific breed -- particularly flat-faced dogs (Pugs, French Bulldogs, English Bulldogs) and Persian or Himalayan cats -- when booking.

## Booking

Contact Azores Airlines reservations to add a pet to your booking. Pet fees apply and pet spaces are limited per flight. Have your pet's microchip number, vaccination records, and destination country import documentation ready when booking.

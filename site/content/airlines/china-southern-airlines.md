China Southern Airlines is China's largest carrier by fleet size, operating from its Guangzhou Baiyun (CAN) hub to destinations across Asia, Europe, North America, Australia, and Africa. It is a member of the SkyTeam alliance and a key option for pets travelling to and from southern China and Guangzhou-accessible destinations.

**Cabin travel**

China Southern does not allow pets in the passenger cabin on international flights. All pets travel as hold cargo.

**Booking live animal cargo**

Live animal cargo must be arranged before the day of travel. Contact China Southern's cargo or passenger services team when booking to confirm your specific route accepts live animals. The airline's Guangzhou hub is well set up for cargo handling, including live animals.

**Crate standards**

China Southern follows IATA live animal transport standards:
- Hard-sided, ventilated crate with secure door mechanism
- Size: animal must be able to stand at full height, turn around, sit, and lie down comfortably
- Food and water dishes must be accessible from outside without opening the crate
- Labels: owner name, address, contact number, and "LIVE ANIMAL" in English and Chinese

**Breed restrictions**

Brachycephalic breeds are restricted. The list includes English Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, American Bulldogs, Pugs, Boston Terriers, Shih Tzus, Lhasa Apsos, Pekingese, Boxers, and flat-faced cats including Persians and Himalayans. If you are unsure whether your breed is on the restricted list, contact China Southern before booking.

**Temperature policies**

China Southern, like most carriers, has temperature restrictions that affect live animal acceptance. Summer months can result in embargoes for cargo pets on routes through hot airports. If travelling in summer, confirm live animal acceptance with China Southern for your specific route before booking flights.

**Arriving in China**

China's pet import process is complex and involves GACC documentation, quarantine booking, and in many cases embassy endorsement of health certificates. The China Southern airline process is separate from China's import requirements - you need to satisfy both. See the China pet import guide for full preparation details.

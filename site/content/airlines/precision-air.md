Precision Air is Tanzania's largest private carrier, operating domestic routes across Tanzania and some regional East African services. For pet owners living in or relocating to Tanzania, Precision Air provides a useful domestic travel option for small pets.

## In-Cabin Pet Policy

Precision Air accepts small dogs and cats in the passenger cabin on applicable domestic Tanzanian routes. The pet must:

- Travel in a soft-sided carrier that fits under the seat in front of you
- Not exceed the airline's combined weight limit (confirm current limits directly with Precision Air)
- Remain in the carrier throughout the flight

Pet spaces are limited per flight. Book in advance and confirm pet acceptance at the time of reservation.

## Cargo for Larger Animals

Precision Air operates smaller turboprop and regional jet aircraft on most domestic routes. Hold cargo capacity for live animals is limited. For larger dogs or cats requiring hold transport, contact Precision Air cargo to confirm whether your specific route and aircraft type can accommodate the animal.

## Tanzania Pet Import Requirements

For international arrivals into Tanzania with a pet, the Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries Development manages live animal imports. Requirements typically include:

- Veterinary import permit
- Health certificate from an official government veterinarian, within ten days of travel
- Rabies vaccination (current)
- Core vaccinations
- Microchip (ISO standard)

International pets arriving at Julius Nyerere International Airport (DAR) are inspected by Ministry of Livestock officials on arrival. Confirm the current requirements with the Ministry or a pet transport specialist before booking.

## Routes and Network

Precision Air's network includes:
- **Dar es Salaam (DAR)** -- main hub
- **Kilimanjaro (JRO)** -- northern Tanzania gateway, Serengeti access
- **Zanzibar (ZNZ)** -- island routes
- **Mwanza (MWZ)** -- lake Victoria
- Regional East African routes (confirm current schedules)

## Booking

Contact Precision Air reservations to add a pet to your booking. Confirm the pet policy for your specific route, as aircraft type and capacity vary across the network.

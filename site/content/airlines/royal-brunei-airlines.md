Royal Brunei Airlines is the flag carrier of the Sultanate of Brunei, operating international routes from Brunei International Airport (BWN) to destinations in Asia, the Middle East, and Australia. The airline is small by global standards but well regarded for its service.

## Royal Brunei Airlines and pet transport

Royal Brunei Airlines does not publish a standard live animal transport policy for privately owned pets on its website. The airline's limited route network and small fleet mean that pet cargo services, where available, may be arranged on a case-by-case basis through the cargo division.

Pet owners planning to move to or from Brunei are advised to contact Royal Brunei's cargo team directly at BWN, or to work with a specialist pet relocation agent who has experience with Brunei routing.

## Routing pets to Brunei

The most practical international routing for pets to Brunei is via Singapore Changi (SIN). Singapore Airlines Cargo and its subsidiaries operate cargo services to Brunei, and Changi has excellent live animal handling facilities. A specialist agent can manage the full chain from your origin country to BWN.

Some pet owners route via Kuala Lumpur (KUL) on Malaysia Airlines Cargo, which also has connections to Brunei.

## Brunei import requirements

The Department of Agriculture and Agrifood (DAA) under Brunei's Ministry of Primary Resources and Tourism manages animal imports. Requirements for dogs and cats include:

- An import permit from DAA (apply in advance)
- Microchip (ISO standard)
- Current rabies vaccination
- Veterinary health certificate endorsed by the national authority in the origin country

Brunei's animal import rules have historically been strict. Contact DAA or the Brunei High Commission in your country for the most current requirements before making any bookings.

## Practical notes

Brunei has a significant expat community, particularly in the oil and gas sector. Several specialist pet relocation agencies with South East Asian experience can advise on current routing options and documentation requirements for Brunei.

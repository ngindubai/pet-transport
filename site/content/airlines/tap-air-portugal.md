## TAP Air Portugal Pet Transport Policy

TAP Air Portugal is Portugal's flag carrier and a Star Alliance member, operating from Lisbon (LIS). It connects Portugal to Europe, Brazil, the USA, Canada, Africa, and some Asian destinations.

TAP is often the airline of choice for pets moving between the UK or Europe and Portugal, as well as between Europe and Brazil. Small pets can travel in the cabin on TAP's intra-European routes - dogs and cats meeting the 8kg combined weight limit can sit with you in an approved carrier. Larger pets, and all pets on transatlantic routes, travel as cargo.

**Madeira and the Azores:** These Portuguese island territories are rabies-free and apply stricter import rules. Pets arriving at Funchal (Madeira) or Ponta Delgada (Azores) from non-EU countries need a valid rabies antibody titre test (FAVN test) showing a result of at least 0.5 IU/ml, taken at least 90 days before travel. This is a critical detail that catches many pet owners off guard. Plan for it well ahead of time.

**Brazil routes:** TAP's transatlantic network to Brazil is one of Europe's strongest. Pets travel as cargo, and Brazil requires a health certificate in the format approved by MAPA (Ministry of Agriculture, Livestock and Food Supply), endorsed by the appropriate government authority.

**Booking:** Add an in-cabin pet when booking your ticket online. For cargo, contact TAP Cargo directly.

## Lion Air Group Overview

Lion Air Group is Southeast Asia's largest privately-owned airline group, operating multiple brands:
- **Lion Air** (low-cost domestic and regional)
- **Batik Air** (full-service domestic and international, also known as Batik Air Indonesia)
- **Wings Air** (turboprop regional)
- **Malindo Air** (Malaysia -- now Batik Air Malaysia)

For pet transport purposes, Lion Air and Batik Air are the most relevant for international considerations.

## Lion Air Pet Policy

Lion Air accepts pets as **checked baggage/cargo** on many domestic Indonesian routes. The pet travels in the aircraft hold, not the cabin.

**What is accepted:** Dogs, cats in appropriate carriers

**Crate requirements:** IATA-compliant crates required. The animal must be able to stand, turn around, and lie down. Crate must be well-ventilated and secure.

**Weight:** Charged per kilogram of combined weight (pet plus crate). Fees apply in addition to standard baggage allowance.

**Restrictions:** Brachycephalic breeds (flat-faced dogs such as Bulldogs, Pugs, French Bulldogs) may be restricted. Confirm before booking.

## Booking Process

1. Contact Lion Air cargo or customer service: lionair.co.id
2. Provide details of the animal, breed, weight, crate dimensions, and route
3. Receive confirmation and fee quote
4. Present at check-in with all veterinary documentation required by the destination country

**Allow extra time at check-in** -- live animal check-in takes longer than standard baggage.

## International Routes

For international routes involving Lion Air Group, Batik Air is the more established carrier for live animal transport. Batik Air Indonesia operates routes to Malaysia, Singapore, India, Australia, Japan, and other Asian destinations. Confirm live animal acceptance for your specific route and date with Batik Air directly: batikair.com.

## Indonesia Pet Import Requirements

If travelling to Indonesia with a pet (inbound), the relevant authority is the Ministry of Agriculture's Directorate General of Livestock and Animal Health Services (Ditjen PKH). Requirements include import permit, health certificate from country of origin endorsed by national authority, rabies vaccination, and relevant parasite treatment.

Air Caraibes is the leading carrier serving the French Caribbean islands, connecting Guadeloupe (Pointe-a-Pitre, PTP), Martinique (Fort-de-France, FDF), French Guiana (Cayenne, CAY) and Saint Martin (SXM) with Paris and other European destinations. For pet owners in the French Antilles or those relocating to the Caribbean, Air Caraibes is a key carrier to understand.

## Does Air Caraibes Accept Pets?

Air Caraibes accepts **small pets in the passenger cabin** and **larger animals in the cargo hold** on its routes.

**Pets in the cabin:**
- Cats and small dogs with a combined weight (pet plus carrier) not exceeding 6 kg
- Soft-sided, ventilated carrier required, fitting under the seat
- Maximum 1 pet per passenger; limited spaces per flight
- Must remain in the carrier under the seat throughout the flight
- Health certificate from a veterinarian required

**Pets in the cargo hold:**
- Larger dogs and cats that exceed the cabin weight limit travel in the pressurised hold
- IATA-compliant hard-shell crate required (appropriate size, adequate ventilation)
- Advance booking required through Air Caraibes cargo services

## French Overseas Territories and EU Pet Rules

This is an important nuance for pet owners travelling to or from Guadeloupe, Martinique and French Guiana: these are **French overseas regions and therefore full EU member territory**. EU pet travel rules apply.

**From France (metropolitan) to Guadeloupe/Martinique:**
- EU Pet Passport (or equivalent for non-EU animals) required
- Current rabies vaccination
- ISO microchip
- No separate import permit required for EU-to-overseas-region travel

**From non-EU countries (including the UK and USA) to Guadeloupe/Martinique:**
- ISO microchip
- Current rabies vaccination
- Rabies antibody titre test (FAVN) conducted at an EU-approved laboratory; result at least 0.5 IU/ml; 3-month wait after successful titre test before EU/DOM-TOM entry
- Official health certificate in the EU format (endorsed by the competent authority of the country of origin)
- TRACES NT pre-notification required

This is the same process as entering metropolitan France or any other EU country -- the fact that the destination is a Caribbean island does not simplify the requirements.

## Key Routes for Pet Owners

Air Caraibes operates scheduled services on:
- **Paris Orly (ORY) to Pointe-a-Pitre (PTP)** -- flagship route, multiple weekly services
- **Paris Orly (ORY) to Fort-de-France (FDF)** -- Martinique services
- **Paris Orly (ORY) to Cayenne (CAY)** -- French Guiana
- **Paris Orly (ORY) to Saint Martin (SXM)**
- Interisland Caribbean routes connecting the French islands

## Booking a Pet on Air Caraibes

Add your pet to the booking by contacting Air Caraibes customer service when booking your ticket. Live animal spaces are limited, particularly in the cabin -- register your pet early. Bring the health certificate and all documentation to check-in.

## Connecting from London or Other European Hubs

If you are travelling from the UK or elsewhere in Europe, you will typically connect through Paris Orly for the Air Caraibes transatlantic leg. Ensure your routing allows adequate connection time for live animal transfer and that both the connecting carrier and Air Caraibes accept your pet on the combined journey.

Zambia Airways is Zambia's national carrier, which resumed operations in November 2021 after a suspension since 1994. The airline operates a limited regional network from Kenneth Kaunda International Airport (LUN) in Lusaka. For pet owners relocating to Zambia, the airline landscape for live animal cargo is currently developing.

## Current Operational Status

Zambia Airways relaunched in 2021 with a limited fleet and regional route network. The live animal cargo programme is in development. Confirm current pet transport availability directly with Zambia Airways cargo before planning to use this carrier for your pet.

## Live Animal Policy

Due to the airline's limited operational status and fleet size, live animal cargo capacity on Zambia Airways is not guaranteed at the same level as established carriers. Contact Zambia Airways cargo to confirm:
- Whether live animal cargo is accepted on your specific route
- Current crate size restrictions
- Health certificate and documentation requirements
- Available flight dates with live animal capacity

## Zambia Pet Import Requirements

The Ministry of Fisheries and Livestock manages live animal imports into Zambia. Requirements typically include:

- Import permit from the Ministry of Fisheries and Livestock (apply before travel)
- Health certificate from an official government veterinarian, within ten days of travel
- Rabies vaccination (current)
- Core vaccinations for dogs and cats
- Microchip (ISO standard)
- Internal and external parasite treatment records

Confirm the current requirements with the Zambian Ministry or a pet transport specialist with Lusaka experience.

## Alternative Carriers for Lusaka

For more established live animal cargo services to Lusaka (LUN), consider:
- **Ethiopian Airlines** (via Addis Ababa) -- extensive Africa network, established live animal cargo
- **Kenya Airways** (via Nairobi) -- East and southern Africa routes with live animal capability
- **South African Airways** (via Johannesburg, subject to current schedule)

## Routes and Network

Zambia Airways' current network (subject to change) focuses on regional southern and East African connections from Lusaka (LUN). International connectivity beyond the region is limited at this stage.

## Booking

Check current Zambia Airways schedules and contact their cargo team directly. Given the limited network, many pet transport specialists route Zambia-bound pet shipments through Ethiopian Airlines or Kenya Airways for reliability.

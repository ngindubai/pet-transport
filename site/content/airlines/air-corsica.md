Air Corsica, operating under IATA code XK, is a French regional airline based at Ajaccio Napoleon Bonaparte Airport (AJA) on the island of Corsica. Air Corsica operates routes between Corsica and mainland France (Paris Orly, Paris CDG, Lyon, Marseille, Nice, Toulouse, Bordeaux, Nantes) as well as intra-Corsican services and some routes to other French cities. It is the primary airline for residents of Corsica travelling to and from the French mainland.

## Cabin Pet Policy

Air Corsica accepts small pets in the passenger cabin:

- **Eligible pets**: cats and small dogs
- **Weight limit**: combined weight of pet plus carrier up to 10 kg; Air Corsica's cabin pet weight limit is relatively generous compared with some European carriers; confirm current limit
- **Carrier type**: soft-sided carrier that fits under the seat in front; the carrier must have a waterproof base and be ventilated
- **Advance booking required**: add the pet at time of booking through Air Corsica or a travel agent; limited cabin pet spaces per flight; walk-up pets are not guaranteed
- **EU pet passport required**: for pets travelling between Corsica (France) and other French/EU destinations; the EU pet passport with current rabies vaccination and ISO microchip is the standard document
- **Fee**: a pet fee is charged; confirm the current rate with Air Corsica at time of booking

## Intra-EU Travel on Air Corsica Routes

Corsica is a French territory and therefore an EU territory. All Air Corsica routes between Corsica and mainland France are domestic French (intra-EU) routes:

- Standard EU pet passport rules apply
- No border inspection, no import permit, no titre test required for EU-origin pets
- The EU pet passport covers travel across all mainland France and Corsica routes

## Cargo Hold for Larger Pets

For dogs and cats exceeding the cabin weight limit:

- Air Corsica accepts larger pets in the cargo hold as excess baggage on eligible routes
- **IATA-compliant hard-shell crate** required
- Contact Air Corsica cargo in advance; live animal cargo must be pre-booked and is not accepted as walk-up
- Confirm combined weight (pet plus crate) limits for the specific aircraft type; Air Corsica operates ATR 72-600 turboprops on some routes and Airbus A320-family aircraft on higher-frequency routes; cargo hold dimensions differ

## Travel with Pets to Corsica: Practical Notes

Corsica has a well-developed network of outdoor spaces, coastal paths and mountains suitable for dogs. The island's relatively mild Mediterranean climate makes it popular with pet owners for holidays as well as relocation.

For non-EU pets travelling from outside the EU to Corsica, all EU third-country import rules apply (ISO microchip, rabies vaccination, titre test, 3-month wait, AHC/equivalent health certificate, TRACES NT notification). Entry through mainland France (Paris CDG or Marseille) and then a domestic Air Corsica connection is the standard routing.

## Microchip Registration

As part of France, Corsica uses the ICAD national database for dog registration. All dogs in France, including Corsica, must be identified by microchip or tattoo and registered with ICAD.

## Key Routes

- **Ajaccio (AJA) to Paris Orly (ORY)**: most frequented route for pet transport
- **Ajaccio to Lyon (LYS), Marseille (MRS), Nice (NCE)**
- **Bastia (BIA) to Paris (ORY, CDG)**: Bastia is Corsica's northern capital; routes to mainland France also served by Air Corsica

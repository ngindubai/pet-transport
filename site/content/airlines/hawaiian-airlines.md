Hawaiian Airlines is Hawaii's dominant carrier, operating inter-island routes and mainland US-Hawaii routes from Honolulu (HNL). For pet owners relocating to Hawaii, Hawaiian Airlines is the most commonly used carrier for the mainland-Hawaii leg. However, the airline's pet transport process is inseparable from Hawaii's unique quarantine requirements.

**Why Hawaii is different**

Hawaii is the only US state that is rabies-free, and it intends to stay that way. All animals arriving in Hawaii from outside the state are subject to the Hawaii Department of Agriculture's Animal Quarantine programme. This is a state law, not an airline policy - it applies regardless of which carrier you use or how the pet travels.

**The 5-Day or Less programme**

The standard quarantine is 120 days. The 5-Day or Less programme reduces this to 5 days or fewer, but it requires extensive advance preparation:
- Microchip (AVID, HomeAgain, or 9-digit or 10-digit - check current approved types)
- Two rabies vaccinations (primary and booster)
- Two OIE-approved laboratory titre tests - the second test must be conducted at least 90 days before the arrival date in Hawaii
- The second titre must show 0.5 IU/ml or higher
- USDA-APHIS veterinarian-endorsed health certificate issued within 14 days of travel
- Advance arrival notification to the Hawaii Department of Agriculture at least 10 days before travel

Miss any of these requirements or get the timing wrong, and the result is 120 days of quarantine.

**Hawaiian Airlines cabin policy**

Hawaiian Airlines accepts small pets in the cabin on inter-island flights and select mainland routes. Cabin pets must be in a soft carrier that fits under the seat in front, with combined pet and carrier weight limits applying. Pets in cabin still go through the Hawaii quarantine process on arrival.

**Hawaiian Airlines cargo policy**

Larger dogs travel as checked baggage in IATA-approved hard-sided crates. Hawaiian Airlines has temperature restrictions - live animals are not accepted if ambient temperatures at origin, destination, or stopovers exceed 29.4C (85F) or fall below -12.2C (10F).

**Booking**

Add pets to Hawaiian Airlines bookings through their website or customer service. For cargo pets, arrange the kennel check-in process when arriving at the airport. Ensure quarantine documents (especially the health certificate with USDA endorsement) are complete and in the correct format before check-in.

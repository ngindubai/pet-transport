## Iberia Pet Transport Policy

Iberia is Spain's flag carrier and a Oneworld member, operating from Madrid Barajas Airport (MAD). It connects Spain and Europe to Latin America, the USA, Africa, and the Middle East, making it one of the primary carriers for pet transport between Europe and Latin America.

Small pets can travel in the cabin on Iberia flights within weight and size limits. Cats and small dogs travelling within Europe will often qualify for cabin travel, which is less stressful than cargo. Larger pets travel in the hold as cargo on all routes.

Iberia's Latin American network is one of the most extensive in the industry, with regular services to Argentina, Colombia, Mexico, Brazil, Peru, Chile, Venezuela, Cuba, and others. This makes it a natural choice for pets relocating between Europe and Latin America. Bear in mind that each Latin American country has its own pet import requirements - some are straightforward, others require advance import permits and government-endorsed certificates.

**EU travel:** Spanish-registered pets have EU pet passports. For pets from non-EU countries arriving into Spain, an Animal Health Certificate in the format required by EU Regulation 576/2013 is needed.

**Booking:** Add in-cabin pet when reserving your ticket via the Iberia website. For cargo, contact Iberia Cargo directly.

Air Astana is Kazakhstan's national airline and one of Central Asia's most established carriers. It operates an expanding network from Almaty and Nursultan to destinations across Europe, Asia, Russia, and the Middle East. For expats in Kazakhstan -- a significant international community due to the oil and gas sector -- Air Astana is often the most practical option for pet transport.

## Cabin Pet Policy

Air Astana accepts small pets in the cabin:

- Combined weight limit: **8 kg** (pet plus carrier)
- Carrier must fit under the seat in front
- One pet per passenger
- Limited spaces per flight -- pre-book in advance
- Fees apply per sector

Approved pets: domestic dogs and cats. Exotic animals are not accepted as cabin pets.

## Hold / Checked Baggage Policy

For larger pets that do not qualify for cabin:

- Accepted as checked excess baggage in the hold
- IATA-compliant rigid crate required
- Combined crate plus animal weight limits apply (confirm current limits with Air Astana)
- Brachycephalic breed restrictions apply in summer (May to September) and on routes with extreme temperatures

## Kazakhstan Entry Requirements

For pets being imported into Kazakhstan:

1. **Veterinary health certificate** from the country of origin, endorsed by the national authority (APHA for UK, USDA for USA)
2. **Rabies vaccination** current
3. **Additional vaccinations** as required by Kazakhstan (distemper, parvovirus for dogs; FVRCP for cats)
4. **Import permit** -- Kazakhstan's Committee of Veterinary Control and Supervision (KVCS) may require advance notification or import clearance. Verify current rules at gov.kz or through Air Astana's cargo team
5. **Microchip** strongly recommended and required by most origin countries

## Kazakhstan Export Requirements

For pets leaving Kazakhstan:

- Health certificate from a Kazakhstan government-licensed vet
- Export permit from KVCS
- Destination country-specific requirements (UK AHC, USA USDA endorsement, EU AHC etc.)

The export process typically takes 2 to 3 weeks to complete.

## Key Routes

Air Astana connects Kazakhstan to major pet transport hubs:

- **Almaty / Nursultan to London Heathrow** (UK)
- **Almaty to Frankfurt** (Germany -- EU entry point)
- **Almaty / Nursultan to Dubai** (UAE)
- **Routes to Moscow, Istanbul, Beijing, and major Asian cities**

## Practical Advice for Kazakhstan-Based Expats

The international expat community in Almaty and Nursultan is substantial and well-organised. Expat Facebook groups and corporate HR teams regularly share updated information on pet transport. Given that Kazakhstan's regulations can change with relatively short notice, a specialist agent with Kazakhstan/Central Asia experience is worth considering for complex moves.

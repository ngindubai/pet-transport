## Sunwing Does Not Carry Pets

Sunwing Airlines is Canada's largest leisure airline, operating package holiday flights from Canadian cities to the Caribbean, Mexico, Central America, and Florida. Like similar leisure carriers, Sunwing does not accept pets.

No pets can travel on Sunwing:
- Not in the cabin
- Not in the hold
- Not as cargo

The only exception is **registered service animals** that comply with Sunwing's special needs passenger policy.

## Why Sunwing Does Not Accept Pets

Sunwing operates a leisure charter model similar to Jet2 and TUI in the UK. The focus is on package holiday operations with efficient turnarounds between sun destinations. Live animal transport adds operational complexity -- veterinary documentation, specialised handling, hold monitoring -- that is not compatible with the leisure charter model.

## Service Animals on Sunwing

Registered service animals (guide dogs, mobility assistance dogs) can travel with their handlers on Sunwing flights. Requirements:
- Advance notification (contact Sunwing before booking)
- Official assistance dog documentation
- Dog must remain with the handler throughout the flight

Contact Sunwing special needs travel: sunwing.ca/en/pages/special-needs-travel or 1-877-786-9464 (Canada)

## Moving From Canada to a Sunwing Destination With a Pet

If you are relocating to Mexico, Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Jamaica, Barbados, or other Sunwing destinations and need to bring a pet:

**For the pet's transport:** Use Air Canada or WestJet on the same route. Both serve most Sunwing destinations from major Canadian cities and accept pets as cabin or cargo animals.

**Alternative:** Use a cargo-only carrier and a specialist IPATA-member pet transport agent.

You can travel to your destination on Sunwing while your pet travels separately on a cargo flight -- but this requires careful coordination of arrival timing.

| Destination | Canadian alternatives with pet policies |
|-------------|----------------------------------------|
| Mexico (Cancun, Puerto Vallarta, Cabo) | Air Canada, WestJet, Aeromexico |
| Dominican Republic | Air Canada, WestJet, Sunwing (not pets) |
| Cuba | Air Canada, WestJet |
| Jamaica | Air Canada, WestJet, Air Jamaica connections |
| Barbados | Air Canada |

## Summary

Sunwing is not an option for pet transport. Plan your pet's travel separately through Air Canada, WestJet, or a specialist agent.

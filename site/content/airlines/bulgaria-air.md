## Bulgaria Air: Sofia Gateway

Bulgaria Air is the national airline of Bulgaria, operating from Sofia Airport (SOF) to European destinations. For pet owners moving to or from Bulgaria, it is the most direct carrier for the Sofia leg.

## Cabin Pet Policy

Bulgaria Air accepts small pets in the cabin on European routes:

- **Species**: dogs and cats
- **Combined weight**: up to 8 kg (pet plus carrier)
- **Carrier**: IATA-compliant soft carrier, dimensions to fit under the seat
- **One per passenger** in cabin
- **Advance notification required** -- book the pet alongside your ticket or call to add it

The cabin pet fee is charged per sector. Confirm the current fee at the time of booking at air.bg.

## Hold Travel

Larger pets travel in the hold as accompanied excess baggage:

- IATA-compliant hard-shell crate required
- Charged by combined weight (animal plus crate)
- Not all flights in the Bulgaria Air schedule have hold capacity for live animals -- confirm your specific route

## EU Pet Entry Rules for Bulgaria

Bulgaria joined the EU in 2007. Standard EU pet travel rules apply.

**From EU countries:**
- EU Pet Passport + microchip + current rabies vaccination

**From the UK (post-Brexit):**
- AHC issued by a UK OV within 10 days of travel
- APHA endorsement
- Microchip
- Rabies vaccination (current)
- Tapeworm treatment for dogs (1 to 5 days before entry)

The Bulgarian Food Safety Agency (BFSA) oversees live animal border checks at Sofia Airport. Keep original certificates accessible.

## Sofia Airport (SOF)

Sofia Airport handles live animal arrivals through its veterinary inspection post. Allow extra time on arrival for document checks. The process is typically straightforward for pets with complete paperwork.

## Not Schengen (Yet)

As of May 2026, Bulgaria is in the process of joining the Schengen Area. Check the current status before travel, as Schengen accession affects land border crossing procedures (though not airport pet import rules significantly).

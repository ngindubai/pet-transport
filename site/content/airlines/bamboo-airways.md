Bamboo Airways is a Vietnamese carrier with its operational base in Hanoi (Noi Bai International Airport, HAN) and services across Vietnam's domestic network as well as select international routes. For pet owners living in or relocating to Vietnam, Bamboo Airways offers pet transport services on domestic and some international flights.

## Does Bamboo Airways Accept Pets?

Bamboo Airways accepts **cats and small dogs in the passenger cabin** on eligible routes, and offers **cargo hold** transport for larger animals.

**Cabin pets:**
- Available on domestic Vietnamese routes and selected international services
- Combined weight of pet plus carrier: generally not exceeding 7 to 8 kg (confirm the current limit with Bamboo Airways)
- Approved soft-sided ventilated carrier required; must fit under the seat
- Maximum 2 pets per flight in the cabin (1 per passenger)
- Not all routes or aircraft types accommodate cabin pets

**Cargo hold pets:**
- Larger dogs that exceed cabin weight limits may be booked as live animal cargo
- An IATA-compliant hard-shell crate is required
- Advance booking through Bamboo Airways cargo is required

## What Documents Does Vietnam Require?

For pets moving within Vietnam, the Department of Animal Health (DAH) under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development (MARD) governs the movement of animals. Domestic animal movement documents are required including a health certificate from a registered Vietnamese veterinary clinic.

For international travel, either importing a pet to Vietnam or exporting one, the requirements include:

**Importing to Vietnam:**
- ISO microchip
- Rabies vaccination (current)
- Health certificate endorsed by the government veterinary authority of the country of origin
- Import permit from the DAH/MARD
- Quarantine inspection on arrival

**Exporting from Vietnam:**
- Health certificate issued by a DAH-registered vet
- Endorsed export health certificate from DAH/MARD
- Vaccinations and treatments as required by the destination country

See the [Vietnam pet import guide](/blog/vietnam-pet-import-guide/) for detailed requirements.

## Booking Your Pet on Bamboo Airways

To add a pet to a Bamboo Airways booking:
1. Book your passenger ticket first
2. Contact Bamboo Airways customer service or use their website's special assistance section to add a pet
3. Provide the animal's weight (including carrier) and species
4. Obtain written confirmation before travel

At check-in, present the health certificate and all documentation. Arrive early -- airport check-in for pets typically takes additional time.

## Key Domestic Routes for Pet Owners

Bamboo Airways' domestic network includes routes between:
- Hanoi (HAN) and Ho Chi Minh City (SGN)
- Hanoi (HAN) and Da Nang (DAD)
- Ho Chi Minh City (SGN) and Phu Quoc (PQC)
- Various other Vietnamese domestic city pairs

International routes include Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City to selected Asian destinations. Confirm pet acceptance on any specific international route.

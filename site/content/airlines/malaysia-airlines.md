Malaysia Airlines is the national carrier of Malaysia and a oneworld member, operating from Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KUL) to destinations across Asia, Europe, Australia, and the Middle East. For pet owners relocating to or from Malaysia or routing through Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia Airlines is one of the primary options in the region.

**Cabin travel**

Malaysia Airlines does not permit pets in the passenger cabin. All pets travel as cargo.

**Cargo transport**

Malaysia Airlines accepts dogs and cats as air cargo. Live animal cargo must be booked in advance through Malaysia Airlines' cargo division. Confirm that your specific route accepts live animals - not all routes or aircraft have the same capabilities.

**Crate requirements**

IATA live animal standards apply:
- Hard-sided, well-ventilated, escape-proof crate
- Size: animal must stand at full height, turn completely, sit, and lie down without touching the sides
- Water and food dishes accessible from outside
- LIVE ANIMAL labelling, owner details visible on the crate

**Breed restrictions**

Malaysia Airlines restricts brachycephalic breeds from cargo. The restricted list commonly includes English Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, American Bulldogs, Pugs, Boston Terriers, Shih Tzus, and flat-faced cats. Verify your breed's status with Malaysia Airlines before purchasing tickets.

**Malaysia import requirements**

Pets arriving in Malaysia need an import permit from the Department of Veterinary Services (DVS) before travel. Rabies vaccination, microchip, and a government-endorsed health certificate from the origin country are required. Some origin countries trigger a quarantine requirement - check current DVS requirements for your specific origin country.

**Kuala Lumpur hub**

KUL is Southeast Asia's second-busiest international hub and has well-established cargo facilities including live animal handling. If your pet is transiting KUL, confirm transit live animal procedures with Malaysia Airlines.

**Booking**

Contact Malaysia Airlines' cargo team or passenger services to arrange live animal transport. Book well in advance to secure cargo space and confirm seasonal temperature restrictions for your travel period.

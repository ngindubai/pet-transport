Air Zimbabwe is the national flag carrier of Zimbabwe, operating from Robert Gabriel Mugabe International Airport (HRE) in Harare. The airline's operational capacity has been limited in recent years, which affects its live animal cargo service.

## Pet transport via Air Zimbabwe

Air Zimbabwe's live animal cargo service is not consistent and we recommend confirming current availability directly with the airline before planning any pet shipment. The airline's network and cargo capacity have been subject to significant changes.

For most pet owners moving to or from Zimbabwe, a more reliable approach is to use an established carrier with a proven live animal programme.

## Recommended alternatives for Zimbabwe pet transport

**Ethiopian Airlines** operates regular flights between Harare (HRE) and Addis Ababa (ADD) and has an active live animal cargo network across Africa. Ethiopian Airlines Cargo can handle pet shipments with appropriate documentation.

**Kenya Airways** connects Nairobi (NBO) to Harare and has experience with live animal cargo on this corridor.

**Emirates SkyCargo** offers a routing via Dubai (DXB) with connections to Harare. This is a longer routing but uses a carrier with an excellent live animal programme.

**British Airways** routes via London Heathrow (LHR) with connections to Harare or Johannesburg. BA World Cargo accepts live animals on applicable routes.

## Zimbabwe import requirements

Pets entering Zimbabwe need:
- A health certificate from an accredited veterinarian, issued within 10 days of travel
- Current rabies vaccination (and other core vaccines)
- An import permit from the Zimbabwe Veterinary Services Directorate
- Compliance with IATA crate standards for hold or cargo travel

The Zimbabwe Veterinary Services Directorate can be contacted through the Ministry of Lands, Agriculture, Fisheries, Water and Rural Development.

## Booking and contacts

For live animal cargo, contact Air Zimbabwe at airzimbabwe.aero. For alternative carriers, contact Ethiopian Airlines Cargo, Kenya Airways Cargo, or a specialist pet freight forwarder with Africa experience.

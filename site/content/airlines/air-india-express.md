Air India Express is the low-cost subsidiary of Air India, operating a large network of routes between Indian cities and destinations in the Middle East, South East Asia, and other regional markets. It operates primarily on Boeing 737-800 aircraft and is positioned as a budget alternative on high-traffic India routes.

## Air India Express and pet transport

Air India Express does not offer a standard pet transport service for privately owned dogs or cats. As a low-cost carrier, its service model does not include the live animal handling infrastructure required for pet cargo.

Pet owners planning to travel with a pet to or from India on Air India Express routes should consider the following alternatives:

## Air India (full-service parent)

Air India, the full-service flag carrier, has a more developed pet policy. Air India accepts pets as checked baggage (in the hold) on many international routes, subject to crate size limits and route availability. On some routes, Air India also accepts pets as manifest cargo through its cargo division.

If your routing requires Air India Express for a domestic sector, the pet would need to be transferred between carriers, which adds complexity. Confirm all arrangements with both airlines before booking.

## International carriers from India

For international pet transport from India, the following carriers are commonly used:

- **Lufthansa** from Delhi (DEL) and Mumbai (BOM) to European destinations
- **British Airways** from Delhi and Mumbai to London Heathrow
- **Singapore Airlines** from multiple Indian cities to Singapore and onward
- **Emirates** from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and other cities via Dubai
- **Qatar Airways** from Delhi and Mumbai via Doha

All of these carriers have established cargo live animal programmes and accept pets on their India routes.

## Indian export requirements for pets

Pets leaving India need a health certificate from a registered veterinarian and endorsement by a government authority. Microchipping is required for most destinations. Check your destination country guide for import requirements.

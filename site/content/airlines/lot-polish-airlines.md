LOT Polish Airlines is Poland's flag carrier and a Star Alliance member, operating from Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) to destinations across Europe, North America, Asia, and the Middle East. For pet owners relocating to Poland or routing through Warsaw, LOT is a practical option with a clear pet transport policy.

**Cabin pets**

LOT accepts small cats and dogs in the cabin. Requirements:
- Maximum combined weight (pet plus carrier): 8kg
- Soft carrier must fit under the seat in front
- Pet must stay in the carrier throughout the flight
- One pet per passenger
- A pet transport fee applies (check current rates on LOT's website)

**Cargo pets**

Dogs and cats too large for cabin travel go as checked baggage or cargo. An IATA-approved hard-sided crate is required. The crate must allow the animal to stand, turn, sit, and lie down comfortably. Water and food dishes must be accessible from outside.

Health certificate from a vet is required. For international travel, government endorsement is needed (e.g., APHA in the UK, USDA for USA pets).

**Breed restrictions**

LOT restricts brachycephalic breeds from cargo. These include Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, Pugs, Boston Terriers, Boxers, Pekingese, Shih Tzus, and flat-faced cats (Persian, Himalayan, Exotic Shorthair). These breeds may also face restrictions in cabin depending on specific conditions.

**Travelling to Poland (EU entry rules)**

Poland is an EU member state. Pets arriving from EU countries with a valid EU pet passport travel freely. Pets from non-EU countries (including UK post-Brexit) need an Animal Health Certificate (AHC) in EU format, microchip, and current rabies vaccination. Dogs need tapeworm treatment 1-5 days before arrival.

**Warsaw Chopin Airport**

WAW is LOT's main hub and handles live animal cargo regularly. The airport has EU border inspection post (BIP) facilities for non-EU pet arrivals.

**Booking**

Add cabin pets when booking online or by contacting LOT's customer service. For cargo pets, contact LOT Cargo or the passenger services line to arrange transport and confirm route availability.

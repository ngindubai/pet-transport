## Jet2 Does Not Carry Pets

Jet2 is one of the UK's most popular holiday airlines, serving Mediterranean, Canary Islands, and European destinations from UK regional airports. Despite its popularity, Jet2 does not carry pets on any of its flights.

No pets can travel:
- In the aircraft cabin
- In the aircraft hold
- As cargo

Only **registered assistance dogs and guide dogs** meeting Jet2's special assistance requirements can travel on Jet2 flights.

## Why Jet2 Does Not Accept Pets

Like other leisure charter airlines (TUI, Monarch before it ceased operations), Jet2 focuses on package holiday operations with standardised, high-frequency route operations. Live animal transport requires additional veterinary documentation, specialised ground handling, and hold management procedures that are not compatible with the high-efficiency charter model.

## Assistance Dogs on Jet2

Jet2 does accept registered assistance dogs. Requirements:
- Advance notification: contact Jet2 special assistance before booking
- Assistance dog organisation registration documentation required
- Dog must comply with in-flight behaviour requirements

Contact Jet2 special assistance: jet2.com/special-assistance or call 0800 408 5591 (UK)

## Moving to a Jet2 Destination With a Pet

If you are making a permanent or extended move to a Jet2 destination (Spain, Turkey, Greece, Portugal, Croatia, etc.) and need to transport your pet, the options are:

**For your pet's transport:** Book on a different airline. For most Jet2 Mediterranean destinations, flag carriers and full-service airlines serve the same routes and do accept pets:

| Destination | Carriers accepting pets |
|------------|------------------------|
| Spain | Iberia, Vueling, British Airways |
| Portugal | TAP Air Portugal, British Airways |
| Greece | Aegean Airlines, British Airways |
| Turkey | Turkish Airlines |
| Croatia | Croatia Airlines, British Airways |
| Cyprus | British Airways, Aegean Airlines |
| Malta | British Airways, Air Malta/KM Malta Airlines |

**You can travel Jet2 while your pet travels separately** on a cargo flight on the same day. A specialist pet transport agent can arrange this.

## Summary

Jet2 is not available for pet transport. If you are relocating to a Jet2-served destination, plan your pet's transport separately using a flag carrier or specialist cargo service.

*Policy information accurate as of May 2026. Always verify with Jet2 and alternative carriers before travel.*

Sun Country Airlines is a US low-cost carrier based at Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport (MSP). It operates domestic US routes and some leisure routes to Mexico and the Caribbean.

## In-cabin pets only

Sun Country accepts small dogs and cats in the cabin on applicable routes. The combined weight of the pet and carrier must not exceed 35 lbs (approximately 16 kg). The pet carrier must fit under the seat in front of you and the pet must remain inside the carrier throughout the flight.

A pet fee applies per carrier per one-way segment. Contact Sun Country at the time of booking to add your pet, as space is limited.

## No hold or cargo pet service

Sun Country does not accept pets in the aircraft hold or as checked baggage. This is common among US low-cost carriers. If you have a large dog that cannot travel in the cabin, you will need to use a full-service airline or a dedicated pet freight service on a different carrier.

## Travelling with a pet to Minnesota or other Sun Country destinations

If you are relocating with a pet to the Minneapolis area or another Sun Country destination, and your pet is too large for in-cabin travel, consider using a carrier such as Delta, United, or American Airlines for the pet while you travel on Sun Country.

## Booking your pet on Sun Country

Add your pet to your booking at the time of purchase or by contacting Sun Country's customer service team. The airline limits the number of pet carriers per flight, so add this as early as possible.

Full details and current fees are on Sun Country's website at suncountry.com.

Copa Airlines is Panama's national carrier and a Star Alliance member, operating one of the most comprehensive Latin American networks from its Panama City Tocumen hub (PTY). Copa connects North America, Central America, South America, and the Caribbean from a single hub, making it an important option for pet owners relocating anywhere within the Americas.

**Cabin pets**

Copa allows small pets in the cabin on eligible routes:
- Combined weight (pet plus soft carrier): 10kg maximum
- Carrier fits under the seat in front
- One pet per passenger
- Cats and small dogs only
- Pet travel fee applies (check current rates with Copa)

Cabin pet availability is route-dependent. Confirm when booking.

**Cargo pets**

Larger pets travel as checked cargo in the hold. IATA-compliant hard-sided crates are required. The crate must allow the animal to stand at full height, turn around, sit, and lie down without touching the walls. Water and food dishes must be accessible from outside.

Book cargo pets in advance - live animal cargo space is limited on each flight.

**Breed restrictions**

Copa restricts brachycephalic breeds from cargo travel. Common restricted breeds: English Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, American Bulldogs, Pugs, Boston Terriers, Pekingese, Shih Tzus, Boxers, and flat-faced cats. Confirm your breed's status directly with Copa.

**The Panama hub advantage**

PTY is one of the best-connected airports in the Americas for Latin American routing. If you're moving a pet between, say, Colombia and Argentina, or Brazil and Canada, routing through Panama is often the most direct option. Copa's live animal handling at PTY is experienced due to the volume of inter-Latin America pet moves they handle.

**Latin America import requirements**

Import requirements vary significantly across Latin American countries. Most require a health certificate endorsed by the origin country's government authority, rabies vaccination, and microchip. Some countries (notably Brazil and Argentina) have specific formats for health certificates. See country-specific guides for details.

**Booking**

Add cabin pets during online booking or through Copa's customer service. For cargo pets, arrange through Copa's cargo team or passenger services. Confirm route acceptance and fees before purchasing tickets.

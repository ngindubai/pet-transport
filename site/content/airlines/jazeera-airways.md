Jazeera Airways is Kuwait's first and largest private low-cost carrier, operating from Kuwait International Airport to destinations across the Middle East, Indian Subcontinent, and short-haul international routes. For pet owners, Jazeera's pet policy is restrictive.

## Pet Policy: Limited Acceptance

As of 2026, Jazeera Airways does not offer a general pet-in-cabin or pet-as-cargo service on its scheduled operations. This is common among low-cost carriers in the Gulf region.

If you need to transport a pet to or from Kuwait, your main options are:

**Kuwait Airways (KU):** Kuwait's national flag carrier. Accepts pets in the hold on some international routes as excess baggage. Contact Kuwait Airways cargo or reservations directly for current policy and availability.

**Emirates (EK):** Operates from Kuwait (KWI) to Dubai (DXB) and beyond. Emirates accepts pets as cargo through Emirates SkyCargo on most routes. They are one of the more established carriers for Kuwait-based pet moves.

**Qatar Airways (QR):** Operates Kuwait to Doha with connections worldwide. Qatar Airways accepts pets in the hold on most routes.

**Etihad Airways (EY):** Operates Kuwait to Abu Dhabi with connections. Etihad accepts pets in hold.

## Kuwait Pet Import Requirements

For pets entering Kuwait:

1. **Import permit** from the Kuwait Ministry of Agriculture (apply through the Border Inspection Post or via a local agent)
2. **Official health certificate** from origin country, endorsed by national authority
3. **Rabies vaccination** -- current
4. **Additional vaccinations** as required (distemper, parvovirus for dogs; FVRCP for cats)
5. **Microchip** strongly recommended

Kuwait has restrictions on certain dog breeds -- Pit Bulls, Rottweilers, and other breeds classified as dangerous are restricted or prohibited from import. Verify your breed's status before proceeding.

## Kuwait Pet Export Requirements

For pets leaving Kuwait:

- Health certificate from a Kuwait government-licensed veterinary clinic
- Export permit from the Kuwait Ministry of Agriculture
- Destination country-specific requirements

The export permit process typically takes 1 to 2 weeks. Use a vet with experience in international export certification.

## Practical Notes for Kuwait-Based Expats

The expat community in Kuwait (corporate, diplomatic, and oil sector) is substantial. Many use specialist Gulf-based pet relocation agents for international moves, given the complexity of Gulf-to-Europe or Gulf-to-Australia routes. An IPATA member agent with Gulf experience is recommended for moves to Australia, New Zealand, Japan, or the UK.

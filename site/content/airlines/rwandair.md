RwandAir is Rwanda's national carrier, operating a growing network of routes from Kigali International Airport (KGL) to destinations across Africa, Europe, and Asia. The airline has expanded rapidly since its relaunch and is increasingly well regarded for its service quality.

## RwandAir and pet transport

RwandAir does not currently publish a standard live animal or pet transport policy for privately owned dogs and cats. This means it is not straightforward to book a pet on a RwandAir flight in the way you would with, say, Lufthansa or British Airways.

For owners moving to or from Rwanda with a pet, the practical approach is to route via a hub carrier that has established pet cargo services. The most commonly used options are:

- **Ethiopian Airlines** via Addis Ababa (ADD) -- Ethiopian Airlines has cargo services that handle live animals on a number of African routes
- **Kenya Airways** via Nairobi (NBO) -- Kenya Airways accepts pets as cargo on some international routes
- **Qatar Airways Cargo** via Doha (DOH) -- Qatar Airways Cargo is one of the most reliable options for complex African routing

## Rwanda import requirements for pets

The Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) handles animal import permits and veterinary certificates for pets entering Rwanda.

Key requirements include:
- A veterinary health certificate from an accredited vet in the country of origin, endorsed by the national authority
- Rabies vaccination record (current)
- Microchip (ISO standard recommended)
- Import permit or declaration to RAB on arrival

Requirements can change. Contact RAB or the Rwandan High Commission in your country for the most current information before planning your pet's journey.

## Practical advice

Rwanda is a popular destination for NGO workers and expats. Several specialist pet relocation companies have experience with the Kigali routing and can advise on the most current carrier options and documentation requirements. If you are relocating to Kigali with a pet, working with a specialist is strongly recommended given the limited direct airline options.

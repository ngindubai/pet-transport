Oman Air is the national carrier of Oman, operating from Muscat International Airport (MCT) to destinations across the Middle East, Asia, Africa, and Europe. For pet owners relocating to Oman or routing through Muscat, Oman Air is one of the options alongside other Gulf carriers.

**Cabin pets**

Oman Air does not permit pets in the passenger cabin on its international flights. All pets travel as checked cargo.

**Cargo transport**

Oman Air accepts dogs and cats as checked cargo. The booking must be arranged in advance through the airline's cargo or passenger services team. Confirm that your specific route accepts live animal cargo before booking - not all routes or aircraft types are approved for live animals.

**Crate requirements**

IATA live animal regulations apply:
- Hard-sided, escape-proof, well-ventilated crate
- Size must allow the animal to stand, sit, turn, and lie down comfortably
- Food and water dishes accessible from outside
- Labels: "LIVE ANIMAL" and owner contact information

**Summer temperature restrictions**

Oman's climate is extremely hot from April through October, with Muscat temperatures commonly above 40C in summer. This creates temperature embargoes for live animal cargo at many Gulf airports. If you are relocating to or from Oman in summer, plan travel for early morning or evening departures and confirm current live animal acceptance with Oman Air for your travel months.

**Oman import requirements**

Pets arriving in Oman need an import permit from the Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Water Resources before travel. The process requires a health certificate from the origin country (government-endorsed), rabies vaccination, microchip, and advance permit application. Some breeds may face restrictions - confirm with the ministry before finalising plans.

**Booking**

Arrange live animal cargo with Oman Air well in advance. Confirm fees, route acceptance, and any seasonal restrictions for your travel dates.

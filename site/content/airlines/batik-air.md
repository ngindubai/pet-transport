Batik Air is the full-service arm of the Lion Air Group, operating domestic Indonesian routes and a growing number of international services from Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK) in Jakarta.

## Pets on domestic Batik Air routes

Batik Air's pet policies for domestic Indonesian flights are more accommodating than those of Lion Air (the group's low-cost carrier). Small pets in approved carriers may be permitted in the cabin on some routes. Larger animals and pets that do not meet cabin requirements travel in the hold.

Policies can vary by aircraft type: Batik Air operates Boeing 737 and ATR aircraft, and live animal acceptance may differ between them. Contact Batik Air's passenger services or cargo team before booking to confirm availability for your route and date.

## International routes

Batik Air operates international routes to destinations including Malaysia, Singapore, and some Middle Eastern cities. International pet policies are more restrictive. For international pet transport involving Indonesia, a specialist freight forwarder with knowledge of Indonesian BARANTAN (the quarantine authority) requirements is strongly recommended.

## Indonesian import and export requirements

Pets entering or leaving Indonesia face quarantine inspection by BARANTAN (the Agricultural Quarantine Agency). Import requirements include a health certificate from an approved vet, current vaccination records, and a microchip. Quarantine periods can apply depending on origin country.

For pets arriving in Bali specifically, additional quarantine rules apply as Bali has a special rabies status. Contact BARANTAN or a local vet specialist before planning any Bali-involved pet move.

## Booking pets on Batik Air

Contact Batik Air through batikair.com or the Lion Air Group reservations team. Live animal bookings for cargo travel are handled through the Batik Air Cargo or Lion Air Cargo divisions.

MIAT Mongolian Airlines (IATA: OM) is the flag carrier of Mongolia, connecting Ulaanbaatar's Chinggis Khaan International Airport (ULN) to destinations across Asia, Russia and Europe. For those relocating to or from Mongolia with a companion animal, MIAT is often the most direct routing option.

## Pet Policy Overview

MIAT accepts small pets in the cabin on selected routes (small dogs and cats meeting weight and carrier requirements) and live animals in the cargo hold. **Confirm current policies, weight limits, carrier specifications and route-specific acceptance with MIAT cargo directly before booking** -- policies on smaller national carriers change periodically and are not always comprehensively published in English.

MIAT's cabin pet acceptance typically covers animals with a combined carrier and pet weight under 8 kg, carried in an approved soft-sided carrier under the seat. Cargo hold pets travel as live animal cargo (IATA LAR compliant).

## Mongolia's Pet Import Requirements

The General Agency for Veterinary and Breeding (GAVB) regulates live animal imports into Mongolia:

1. **Import permit** from GAVB -- apply at least 3 to 4 weeks before travel
2. **ISO 15-digit microchip**
3. **Rabies vaccination** -- current; primary vaccination at least 21 to 30 days before travel
4. **Core vaccinations** -- dogs: distemper, parvovirus, hepatitis; cats: panleukopenia, herpesvirus, calicivirus
5. **FAVN titre test** may be required for pets from countries outside a GAVB recognised low-risk list; result must be 0.5 IU/ml or above
6. **Government-endorsed health certificate** -- issued by the government veterinary authority of the country of origin; English-language certificates are generally accepted; Mongolian translation available on request
7. **Veterinary inspection on arrival** at Chinggis Khaan International Airport (ULN)

No mandatory quarantine for pets from most recognised countries with complete documentation.

## Exporting Pets from Mongolia

A GAVB export certificate is required. Apply well in advance of departure -- GAVB inspection and certificate issuance can take several business days. The destination country's health certificate format must also be completed.

## MIAT's International Network

MIAT serves (subject to schedule changes):
- Moscow Sheremetyevo (SVO)
- Beijing Capital (PEK)
- Seoul Incheon (ICN)
- Tokyo Narita (NRT)
- Berlin Brandenburg (BER)
- Frankfurt (FRA)
- Istanbul (IST)
- Hong Kong (HKG)
- Singapore (SIN) -- seasonal

The German and Russian routes are the primary connections for European expats relocating to or from Mongolia.

## Crate Requirements

IATA-compliant hard-shell crates are required for hold transport. Ventilation on at least three sides, water container attached to the door, padlock-free but secure closures and clear live animal labels are all required. Crate dimensions must allow the animal to stand, turn and lie in a natural position.

## Climate Considerations

Ulaanbaatar experiences extreme cold in winter (November to March temperatures regularly below -20 to -30 degrees Celsius). If your pet is arriving in or departing from Mongolia in winter, temperature restrictions on the tarmac may affect scheduling. Cold-sensitive breeds need careful management during ground handling in Mongolian winters.

## TUI Airways: No Pets Policy

TUI Airways is the UK's largest holiday airline, operating charter and package holiday flights from Gatwick, Manchester, Birmingham, and other UK airports to leisure destinations across Europe, the Mediterranean, and beyond. For pet owners, TUI's position is straightforward: pets are not accepted.

## The Policy

**Cabin pets**: Not accepted on any TUI Airways route.

**Hold/cargo**: TUI does not offer live animal cargo services.

**Exception -- Assistance Dogs**: Registered assistance dogs (guide dogs, hearing dogs, trained service animals) are accepted with advance notice and full certification. Contact TUI's customer support before booking.

## Why Charter Airlines Typically Don't Accept Pets

Charter airlines like TUI operate on tight turnarounds. Live animal handling requires additional ground procedures, veterinary checks, and specific loading protocols that add time and cost to operations designed around maximum efficiency. Most leisure charter carriers (TUI, Jet2, Thomson historically) have taken the same position.

## Your Alternatives

If you need to transport a pet to a destination TUI serves, you need a scheduled carrier with a live animal acceptance policy. Options depend on the destination:

**Spain (Canary Islands and mainland):**
- Iberia (cargo and cabin)
- Vueling (cabin)
- British Airways (cargo)
- Ryanair (check current pet policy -- varies)

**Greece and Cyprus:**
- British Airways (cargo for larger animals)
- easyJet (check current cabin pet policy for specific routes)
- Cyprus Airways (cabin and cargo -- verify current policy)

**Malta:**
- KM Malta Airlines (cabin and hold -- see our guide)
- Air Malta routes via other EU carriers

**Turkey:**
- Turkish Airlines (cabin and cargo)
- Pegasus Airlines (cabin only -- no cargo)

**Caribbean:**
- British Airways, Virgin Atlantic (hold cargo for larger dogs)

## General Principle

For holiday destinations served by charter airlines, scheduled flag carriers and low-cost scheduled services are almost always the right solution for pet transport. Always verify the current policy of any alternative airline, as pet policies change seasonally.

## Pegasus Airlines: Budget Carrier from Istanbul

Pegasus Airlines is a major Turkish low-cost carrier based at Istanbul Sabiha Gokcen Airport (SAW) on the Asian side of Istanbul. It operates a large European and Middle Eastern network, and is a popular choice for travel to Turkey and connecting points.

## Cabin-Only Pet Policy

Pegasus accepts small pets in the passenger cabin on most routes. Importantly, it does not offer hold/cargo transport for animals -- this is cabin-only.

**What this means for pet owners:**

- Only pets that fit in an approved carrier under the seat can travel on Pegasus
- Larger dogs, cats over the weight limit, and animals that require hold travel cannot use Pegasus for international moves
- Guide dogs and assistance animals are accepted subject to separate rules -- contact Pegasus directly

**Cabin pet requirements:**
- Species accepted: cats, small dogs, small cage birds
- Combined weight: up to 8 kg (pet plus carrier)
- Carrier: IATA-compliant soft carrier, fitting under the seat
- One per passenger in cabin
- Advance booking required

## Booking a Pet on Pegasus

Visit flypgs.com or call the Pegasus contact centre. Cabin pets are added as a paid extra at the time of booking or up to a specified deadline before departure. Check the current fee on the Pegasus website.

Do not assume the pet booking is automatic when you select "travel with pet" -- confirm receipt of the booking and get written confirmation.

## Turkey's Pet Import Requirements

Turkey is not an EU member. Turkish customs and the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry (TARIMORMAN) oversee pet imports.

Requirements typically include:
1. **Microchip** (ISO 11784/11785)
2. **Rabies vaccination** -- current, administered at least 30 days before entry (for primary vaccines)
3. **Official health certificate** -- issued by an official vet and countersigned by your country's national authority (APHA for UK, USDA for USA)
4. **No quarantine** -- Turkey does not impose mandatory quarantine for pets from most countries if documentation is complete

EU Pet Passports are not accepted as standalone documentation for entry into Turkey. However, the passport's vaccination record information will be referenced in the health certificate that is prepared.

## Istanbul Sabiha Gokcen Airport (SAW)

SAW has a live animal inspection facility for arriving pets. Keep all documentation accessible. Turkish customs may check microchip and health certificate even for cabin pets.

## Istanbul Ataturk vs SAW

Note that Pegasus operates from SAW, not Istanbul Airport (IST), which is on the European side. If you have onward connections or hotel accommodation near IST, factor in the 45 to 60-minute drive between the two airports.

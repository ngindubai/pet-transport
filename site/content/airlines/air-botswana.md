Air Botswana (IATA: BP) is the national airline of Botswana, operating from Sir Seretse Khama International Airport in Gaborone (GBE). The airline connects Botswana's main population centres and tourist destinations -- including Maun (the gateway to the Okavango Delta) and Kasane (near Chobe National Park) -- and provides regional connections to Johannesburg and Harare.

## Pet Policy Overview

Air Botswana is a small regional carrier. **Contact Air Botswana's cargo division directly before making any arrangements for pet transport** -- published policies in English are limited and cargo capacity on regional turboprop routes is constrained.

For live animal shipments on the Gaborone to Johannesburg route, it may be more practical to book through a South African-based IPATA agent who can arrange the shipment on this segment as part of a wider international move.

## Botswana's Pet Import Requirements

The Department of Animal Health and Production (DAHP) under Botswana's Ministry of Agriculture regulates live animal imports:

1. **Import permit** from DAHP -- apply at least 3 to 4 weeks before travel
2. **ISO microchip** (15-digit)
3. **Rabies vaccination** -- current and within valid period; primary vaccination at least 30 days before travel
4. **Core vaccinations** -- dogs: distemper, parvovirus, hepatitis; cats: panleukopenia, herpesvirus, calicivirus; all current
5. **Government-endorsed health certificate** from the country of origin, issued within 10 days of arrival
6. **External parasite treatment** (tick and flea) within a specified period before travel
7. **Veterinary inspection on arrival** at Gaborone (GBE)

No mandatory quarantine applies for pets from recognised countries with complete documentation.

## Exporting Pets from Botswana

An export certificate from DAHP is required when taking a pet out of Botswana. The destination country's health certificate requirements also apply. Allow several days for the DAHP export certificate process.

## Air Botswana's Domestic and Regional Network

Key routes:
- **Gaborone (GBE) - Maun (MUB)**: main tourist gateway route; connects Gaborone to the Okavango Delta region
- **Gaborone (GBE) - Kasane (BBK)**: Chobe/Victoria Falls area
- **Gaborone (GBE) - Francistown (FRW)**: northern Botswana
- **Gaborone (GBE) - Johannesburg (JNB)**: main international connection
- **Gaborone (GBE) - Harare (HRE)**: Zimbabwe connection

For expats based in the Okavango Delta tourism industry or in Kasane, routing a pet through Maun or Kasane and then to Gaborone for international departure is the standard approach.

## International Connections via Johannesburg

For most international pet moves to and from Botswana, Johannesburg O.R. Tambo International Airport (JNB) serves as the international hub. South African Airways, British Airways (Comair), Ethiopian Airlines, Qatar Airways and other carriers connect JNB to Europe, the Americas and Asia. An IPATA agent with South Africa experience can coordinate the full routing.

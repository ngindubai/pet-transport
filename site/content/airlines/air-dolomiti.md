Air Dolomiti, operating under IATA code EN, is an Italian regional carrier owned by the Lufthansa Group. Based at Verona Villafranca Airport (VRN), it operates a network of routes connecting Italian cities -- including Venice, Bologna, Naples, Palermo and others -- to Munich (MUC) and Frankfurt (FRA), feeding passengers into the wider Lufthansa network. Air Dolomiti flights are often booked as part of Lufthansa connections.

## Cabin Pet Policy

Air Dolomiti accepts small pets in the cabin on its European routes, following Lufthansa Group guidelines:

- **Eligible pets**: cats, small dogs, and domestic ferrets
- **Weight limit**: combined weight of pet plus soft carrier must not exceed 8 kg
- **Carrier dimensions**: the soft-sided carrier must fit under the seat in front of you; typically around 55 cm x 40 cm x 20 cm, but confirm with the carrier
- **Advance booking required**: notify Air Dolomiti or your booking agent at the time of reservation; limited cabin pet spaces per flight
- **Veterinary documentation**: a valid EU pet passport with current rabies vaccination is required for pets travelling on European routes; for non-EU pets, the appropriate health certificate and documentation applies
- **One pet per passenger**

The cabin pet fee is charged separately from the ticket price; confirm the current fee with Air Dolomiti or through the Lufthansa booking system.

## Hold Cargo for Larger Pets

Air Dolomiti is primarily a regional short-haul carrier. Its fleet consists mainly of Embraer E-Jet regional aircraft (E195, E175 and similar). These aircraft have relatively small cargo holds, which limits the size of pets that can be transported as hold cargo. For larger pets:

- Contact Air Dolomiti or Lufthansa Cargo directly to confirm whether hold pet transport is available on your specific route
- If connecting to a Lufthansa long-haul flight via Munich or Frankfurt, the hold pet transport on the long-haul segment would be handled under Lufthansa Cargo's live animal programme
- On regional Embraer aircraft, hard-shell crate dimensions are limited; oversized crates may not be accepted

## EU Pet Travel on Air Dolomiti Routes

For travel within the EU and EEA on Air Dolomiti:

- **EU pet passport** is the standard travel document for cabin pets on intra-EU routes
- The passport must show: ISO microchip number, current rabies vaccination (and core vaccinations), and owner details
- Microchip must be implanted before or on the same date as the vaccination

For travel involving Italy as origin or destination:
- Italy aligns fully with EU Regulation 576/2013 for intra-EU travel
- Italy's UVAC offices handle import endorsements for non-EU pets arriving in Italy

## Booking a Pet on Air Dolomiti

Air Dolomiti flights are typically booked as part of Lufthansa itineraries. When booking:
1. Book via the Lufthansa website or call centre and specify the pet requirement
2. Confirm that Air Dolomiti (operating carrier) accepts pets on the specific route; some code-share arrangements may apply different rules
3. Obtain written confirmation of the pet booking before check-in

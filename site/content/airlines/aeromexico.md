## Aeromexico Pet Transport Policy

Aeromexico is Mexico's flag carrier and a SkyTeam member, operating from Mexico City's Benito Juarez International Airport (MEX). It connects Mexico to the USA, Canada, Europe, Latin America, and selected Asian destinations, making it the primary carrier for international pet moves into and out of Mexico.

Small pets can travel in the Aeromexico cabin within the weight limits (typically pet and carrier combined under 10kg, confirming by route). Larger pets travel as cargo. On routes to and from the USA, Aeromexico cargo is frequently used by families relocating from Mexico City or Guadalajara.

**Entering Mexico:** SENASICA (Mexico's National Service for Agri-Food Safety) manages pet imports. Requirements are relatively straightforward: a veterinary health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, proof of microchip, and up-to-date vaccination records including rabies. No advance import permit or titre test is required for dogs and cats from most countries. This makes Mexico one of the easier destinations in the Americas.

**US routes:** Aeromexico's US network includes Dallas, New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Chicago, Houston, and others. For pets entering the USA, the updated CDC requirements apply - microchip date relative to rabies vaccination matters, and a USDA-endorsed certificate is needed.

**Booking:** Add in-cabin pets when booking your ticket via Aeromexico's website. For cargo, contact Aeromexico Cargo directly.

## Air Serbia: Connecting the Balkans

Air Serbia is the flag carrier of Serbia, operating from Belgrade Nikola Tesla Airport (BEG). It connects Belgrade to major European cities and some destinations in the Middle East and the USA. For pet owners moving to or from Serbia, Air Serbia is the natural choice for the Belgrade leg.

## Cabin Pet Policy

Air Serbia accepts small pets in the cabin on most of its routes:

- **Accepted species**: dogs, cats
- **Weight limit**: combined weight (pet plus carrier) up to 8 kg
- **Carrier dimensions**: must fit under the seat in front (check current specifications before booking)
- **One pet per passenger** in cabin
- **Advance booking required**: add your pet to the booking as soon as flights are confirmed

## Hold Cargo for Larger Pets

Larger animals travel in the hold as excess baggage:

- Pressurised and temperature-controlled hold on most aircraft types
- IATA-compliant rigid crate required (no soft carriers for hold travel)
- Charged by combined weight (animal plus crate)
- Some breed restrictions may apply -- confirm with Air Serbia for large or brachycephalic breeds

## Serbia's Pet Import Requirements

Serbia is not an EU member state, so EU Pet Passports do not apply. Pets entering Serbia need:

1. **Microchip** (ISO 11784/11785)
2. **Current rabies vaccination** -- within validity period
3. **Official health certificate** -- issued by an official vet in the country of origin
4. **National endorsement** -- countersigned by your home country's national authority (APHA for UK, USDA for USA)
5. **No quarantine** -- Serbia does not impose mandatory quarantine for pets from most countries if documentation is complete

The Serbian Ministry of Agriculture (Veterinary Directorate) oversees pet imports. For the current health certificate template accepted by Serbia, contact the ministry or a specialist pet relocation agent with Balkans experience.

## Belgrade Nikola Tesla Airport (BEG)

BEG has a veterinary inspection facility for arriving live animals. Border vets check documentation and may physically inspect the animal. Keep all original certificates accessible in your hand luggage.

## Regional Connectivity

Air Serbia's hub at BEG connects to destinations across the Western Balkans (Croatia, North Macedonia, Montenegro, Bosnia). If you are relocating to any of these countries and connecting through Belgrade, check that the transit procedures for live animals are confirmed with Air Serbia cargo in advance.

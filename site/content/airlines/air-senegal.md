Air Senegal is the national carrier of Senegal, operating from its main hub at Blaise Diagne International Airport (DSS) near Dakar. For pet owners relocating to Senegal or other West African destinations, Air Senegal provides connectivity across the region, though international live animal cargo is limited.

## Live Animal Policy

Air Senegal accepts pets on applicable routes, but the live animal cargo programme is less developed than those of major European or Gulf carriers. For pet shipments to Dakar from Europe or North America, many pet transport specialists use Air France or other European carriers with more established live animal procedures.

Contact Air Senegal cargo to confirm availability for your specific route, travel dates, and pet size before planning your routing through Air Senegal.

## Senegal Pet Import Requirements

The Direction des Services Veterinaires (DSV) under the Ministry of Livestock regulates pet imports into Senegal. Requirements typically include:

- Health certificate from an official government veterinarian, within ten days of travel
- Rabies vaccination (current)
- Core vaccinations for dogs and cats
- Microchip (ISO standard)
- Import permit or advance clearance from DSV (confirm before travel)

Confirm the most current requirements with the DSV or a pet transport specialist with Dakar experience, as West African import requirements can vary.

## Routes and Network

Air Senegal operates from Dakar (DSS) to:
- Paris (CDG) -- main European connection
- Brussels (BRU) -- European routes
- Madrid (MAD) -- European routes
- Other West and Central African destinations including Abidjan, Conakry, Banjul, Bamako, and others

## Alternative Carriers for Dakar

For more reliable live animal cargo to Dakar, consider:
- **Air France** (Paris CDG -- Dakar) -- Air France has established live animal cargo procedures
- **Brussels Airlines** (Brussels -- Dakar) -- West Africa specialist with live animal experience
- **Royal Air Maroc** (via Casablanca) -- Atlantic and African connections

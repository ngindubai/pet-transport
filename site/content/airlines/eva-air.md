EVA Air is Taiwan's second-largest international carrier, operating from its Taipei Taoyuan (TPE) hub across Asia, Europe, North America, and Australia. As a Star Alliance member, EVA Air is a popular choice for long-haul routes through the Taiwan hub, and is one of the key options for pets travelling to or from Taiwan.

**Cabin pets**

EVA Air permits small pets in the cabin on qualifying routes. Typical requirements:
- Combined weight of pet and carrier: 7kg maximum (confirm current limit with EVA Air)
- Carrier must fit under the seat in front (usually 45cm x 35cm x 20cm or similar)
- Pet must remain in the carrier throughout the flight
- Only one pet per carrier is permitted

Cabin pet availability is route-dependent. Confirm when booking - not all EVA Air routes accept in-cabin pets.

**Cargo pets**

Larger dogs and pets not accepted in cabin travel as checked cargo in the hold. IATA-compliant hard-sided crates are required. The crate must allow the animal to stand, turn, sit, and lie down without touching the sides. Food and water dishes must be accessible from outside.

**Breed restrictions**

EVA Air follows standard airline brachycephalic breed restrictions. Flat-faced dogs and cats including Bulldogs, French Bulldogs, Pugs, Persian cats, and similar breeds are restricted from cargo travel. Confirm your specific breed with EVA Air before booking.

**Taiwan-specific requirements**

Pets arriving in Taiwan face a quarantine requirement managed by the Bureau of Animal and Plant Health Inspection and Quarantine (BAPHIQ). Most countries require a quarantine period of 7-21 days depending on origin. A Taiwan import permit must be obtained before the pet travels.

**Transit through Taoyuan**

EVA Air's Taoyuan hub is an efficient transit point for long-haul Asia routes. If your pet is transiting TPE, confirm the live animal transit handling with EVA Air - transit live animals are a separate consideration from origin or destination handling.

**Booking**

Contact EVA Air's passenger services or cargo team to confirm live animal acceptance on your specific route and booking class. Do this well before purchasing tickets.

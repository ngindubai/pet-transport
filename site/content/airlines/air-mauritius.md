## Air Mauritius and Pet Transport

Air Mauritius, the national flag carrier of Mauritius, operates flights from Mauritius to Europe (London, Paris, Zurich), the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Australia. The airline accepts pets as cargo on selected routes.

**Pets in the cabin:** Not accepted. The only exception is registered assistance animals.

**Pets in the hold (cargo):** Accepted on selected routes, subject to prior booking and confirmation.

## Booking a Pet on Air Mauritius

1. Contact Air Mauritius cargo or special services before booking: airmauritius.com
2. Confirm pet acceptance on your specific route, date, and aircraft type
3. Provide breed, age, combined weight (pet plus crate), and crate dimensions
4. Receive confirmation and fee quote
5. Prepare documentation required for both the airline and Mauritius (or origin country) authorities

Advance booking is essential -- do not assume acceptance. Contact Air Mauritius at least 4 to 6 weeks before travel.

## Mauritius Pet Import: A Strict Process

Mauritius is an island nation with well-developed biosecurity controls. Pet import involves:

**The Mauritius Ministry of Agro-Industry and Food Security (MAIFS)** manages animal imports at maifs.govmu.org.

**Quarantine:** Most imported dogs and cats undergo a quarantine period at the Animal Quarantine Station at SSR International Airport. Duration varies by country of origin and documentation:
- Animals from approved low-risk countries (generally including UK, France, Australia with full documentation) may have shorter quarantine periods
- Animals from higher-risk countries face longer mandatory quarantine

**Required documentation:**
- Import permit from MAIFS (apply before travel)
- Current rabies vaccination
- Core vaccinations
- FAVN titre test (for some origin countries -- verify with MAIFS)
- Official health certificate endorsed by national authority
- Microchip (ISO 11784/11785)
- Parasite treatment records

Contact MAIFS well before travel to confirm exact current requirements for your origin country.

## Key Routes Where Air Mauritius May Accept Pets

**Last updated: 22 April 2026**

This privacy policy explains how PetTransportGlobal collects and uses personal data when you visit our website or submit an enquiry.

## Who We Are

PetTransportGlobal is a pet relocation service operating at pettransportglobal.com. When we refer to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy, we mean PetTransportGlobal.

## What Data We Collect

**When you submit a quote request or contact form**, we collect:
- Your name and email address
- The details you provide about your pet and relocation (origin, destination, breed, travel date)
- The date and time of your submission

**When you browse the site**, standard web server logs may record:
- Your IP address
- Browser type and version
- Pages you visit and time spent on them
- Referring URL

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking beyond basic analytics.

## How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to:
- Respond to your enquiry and prepare a quote
- Follow up on your enquiry if you have expressed interest in our services
- Improve our guides and content based on the questions we receive

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your contact details for unsolicited marketing.

## How Long We Keep Your Data

Enquiry data is retained for up to 24 months so we can follow up on multi-stage relocations (some international moves take 6-12 months to arrange). After that period, your data is deleted unless you are an active customer.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- Ask us to correct inaccurate data
- Ask us to delete your data
- Withdraw consent for us to contact you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address in the site footer.

## Third-Party Services

Our site uses:
- **Hugo** (static site generator): no personal data processed
- **Cloudflare** (CDN and form handling): subject to [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)
- **Google Analytics** (traffic analysis, if active): subject to [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy when our practices change. The date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision.

## Contact

For privacy enquiries, email us using the contact address in the site footer.

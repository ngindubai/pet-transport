  - heading: "Export requirements from Brazil"
    body: |
      **Export Permit:** Export health certificate endorsed by MAPA (Ministry of Agriculture) required.

      **Health Certificate:** Required. Official export health certificate from an accredited veterinarian, endorsed by MAPA.

      **Government Endorsement:** MAPA endorsement required. Allow 5-10 business days processing time.

      **Issuing Authority:** Ministry of Agriculture, Livestock and Food Supply (MAPA)

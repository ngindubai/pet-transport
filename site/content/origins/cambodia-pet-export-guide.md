# Pet Transport from Cambodia: Export Guide

Moving abroad from Cambodia with a dog or cat means preparing the correct export documentation before you leave. The rules depend on where you are heading -- but the starting point is always the same: get your pet microchipped (if not already), vaccinate against rabies, and obtain an official health certificate endorsed by GDAHP.

This guide covers what Cambodia requires before your pet leaves, the most common destination requirements for Cambodia pets, and which airlines serve the main corridors.

## Export requirements for Cambodia

Before your pet can leave Cambodia, the following must be in place:

- **Microchip:** ISO 11784/11785 standard. Must be implanted before or on the same day as the first rabies vaccination.
- **Rabies vaccination:** Required and must be valid at the time of travel. Allow the full post-vaccination wait before international travel.
- **Titre test (if required):** Required for: Check with GDAHP; requirements change. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml.
- **Health certificate:** Official health certificate endorsed by GDAHP. GDAHP export permit and endorsement required.
- **Quarantine at destination:** No routine quarantine for compliant pets. Veterinary inspection on arrival.
- **Import permit (destination):** Required (apply 4 weeks in advance from GDAHP)
- **Banned breeds at destination:** None (varies by destination country -- always verify).

- **Cathay Pacific** - cargo only
- **Thai Airways** - cargo only
- **Korean Air** - cabin and cargo


## Quick checklist: leaving Cambodia with your pet

1. Microchip (ISO standard) -- must be in before any rabies vaccination
2. Rabies vaccination -- must be current at travel date
3. Titre test (if destination requires it) -- blood drawn 30+ days after vaccination
4. Wait period after titre test (90 or 180 days depending on destination)
5. Apply for destination country import permit
6. Book IATA-compliant crate and cargo/cabin space with airline
7. Obtain official health certificate from GDAHP-endorsed veterinarian
8. Travel day -- arrive at airport 3-4 hours early for cargo pets

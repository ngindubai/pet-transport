faqs:
  - question: "What do I need to export my pet from Germany?"
    answer: "You'll need a health certificate from an official veterinarian, proof of microchip and vaccinations, and any government endorsement required by your destination country. Free movement within EU with valid pet passport. No additional export documentation needed."
  - question: "Where can I get a pet health certificate in Germany?"
    answer: "Any government-approved veterinarian in Germany can issue a health certificate for pet export. For some destinations, the certificate then needs endorsement from the national veterinary authority."

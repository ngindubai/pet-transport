  - heading: "Export requirements from Canada"
    body: |
      **Export Permit:** No CFIA export permit required for personal pet exports. Some destination countries may require CFIA endorsement of health certificate.
      
      **Health Certificate Required:** If required by destination country
      
      **Health Certificate Issued By:** CFIA District Veterinarian or accredited veterinarian (endorsed by CFIA if needed)
      
      **Health Certificate Process:** Visit accredited vet for examination, vet prepares health certificate, submit to CFIA for endorsement if destination requires government endorsement
      
      **Health Certificate Timing:** Typically within 10 days of travel (or as specified by destination country)
      
      **Cfia Endorsement Required For:** Countries that require official government endorsement of health certificate
      
      **Cfia Endorsement Cost Cad:** Varies (check CFIA fee schedule)
      
      **Cfia Endorsement Processing Time:** Allow several business days

faqs:
  - question: "What do I need to export my pet from Canada?"
    answer: "You'll need a health certificate from an official veterinarian, proof of microchip and vaccinations, and any government endorsement required by your destination country. No CFIA export permit required for personal pet exports. Some destination countries may require CFIA endorsement of health certificate."
  - question: "Where can I get a pet health certificate in Canada?"
    answer: "Any government-approved veterinarian in Canada can issue a health certificate for pet export. For some destinations, the certificate then needs endorsement from the national veterinary authority."

sections:
  - heading: "Export requirements from Australia"
    body: |
      **Export Permit:** Required for live animal exports from Australia
      
      **Veterinary Inspection Timing:** Within 5 days of export (or as specified by destination country)
      
      **Veterinary Inspection Issued By:** DAFF-approved veterinarian
      
      **Government Endorsement:** Health certificate must be endorsed by DAFF for export
      
      **Notes:** Australia being rabies-free means Australian pets have simplified entry into many countries. The export process is the main requirement.
      
      **Last Verified:** 2026-04-16

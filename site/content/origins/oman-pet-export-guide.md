# Pet Transport from Oman: Export Guide

Moving abroad from Oman with a dog or cat means preparing the correct export documentation before you leave. The rules depend on where you are heading -- but the starting point is always the same: get your pet microchipped (if not already), vaccinate against rabies, and obtain an official health certificate endorsed by MAFWR.

This guide covers what Oman requires before your pet leaves, the most common destination requirements for Oman pets, and which airlines serve the main corridors.

## Export requirements for Oman

Before your pet can leave Oman, the following must be in place:

- **Microchip:** ISO 11784/11785 standard. Must be implanted before or on the same day as the first rabies vaccination.
- **Rabies vaccination:** Required and must be valid at the time of travel. Allow the full post-vaccination wait before international travel.
- **Titre test (if required):** Required for: Pets from countries deemed high risk by MAFWR. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml.
- **Health certificate:** Official health certificate endorsed by MAFWR. MAFWR endorsement required.
- **Quarantine at destination:** No routine quarantine for compliant pets. Veterinary inspection on arrival.
- **Import permit (destination):** Required (apply 4 weeks in advance from MAFWR General Directorate of Veterinary Services)
- **Banned breeds at destination:** American Pit Bull Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Rottweiler, Dogo Argentino and others (varies by destination country -- always verify).

## Quick checklist: leaving Oman with your pet

1. Microchip (ISO standard) -- must be in before any rabies vaccination
2. Rabies vaccination -- must be current at travel date
3. Titre test (if destination requires it) -- blood drawn 30+ days after vaccination
4. Wait period after titre test (90 or 180 days depending on destination)
5. Apply for destination country import permit
6. Book IATA-compliant crate and cargo/cabin space with airline
7. Obtain official health certificate from MAFWR-endorsed veterinarian
8. Travel day -- arrive at airport 3-4 hours early for cargo pets

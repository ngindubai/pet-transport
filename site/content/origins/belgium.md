    body: |
      **Export Permit:** Belgium uses the EU Pet Travel Scheme. EU Pet Passport for EU/EFTA travel. For non-EU destinations, an EU AHC endorsed by FASFC is required.

      **Health Certificate:** EU AHC for non-EU travel. EU Pet Passport for EU/EFTA travel.

      **Government Endorsement:** FASFC endorsement required for non-EU/EFTA destinations

      **Issuing Authority:** Federal Agency for the Safety of the Food Chain (FASFC)

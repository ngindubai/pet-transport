    answer: "EU Pet Travel Scheme applies. EU Pet Passport for EU/EFTA travel. For non-EU destinations, an EU Animal Health Certificate (AHC) endorsed by NVWA is required. EU AHC for non-EU travel. EU Pet Passport for EU/EFTA travel."

    answer: "Any government-approved veterinarian in Netherlands can issue a health certificate for pet export. The certificate then needs endorsement from Netherlands Food and Consumer Product Safety Authority (NVWA) before departure."

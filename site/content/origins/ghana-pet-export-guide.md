# Pet Transport from Ghana: Export Guide

Moving abroad from Ghana with a dog or cat means preparing the correct export documentation before you leave. The rules depend on where you are heading -- but the starting point is always the same: get your pet microchipped (if not already), vaccinate against rabies, and obtain an official health certificate endorsed by VSD.

This guide covers what Ghana requires before your pet leaves, the most common destination requirements for Ghana pets, and which airlines serve the main corridors.

## Export requirements for Ghana

Before your pet can leave Ghana, the following must be in place:

- **Microchip:** ISO 11784/11785 standard. Must be implanted before or on the same day as the first rabies vaccination.
- **Rabies vaccination:** Required and must be valid at the time of travel. Allow the full post-vaccination wait before international travel.
- **Titre test (if required):** Required for: Most countries. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml.
- **Health certificate:** Official health certificate endorsed by VSD. VSD endorsement required.
- **Quarantine at destination:** Mandatory quarantine at government facility (7 days)
- **Import permit (destination):** Required (apply 6 weeks in advance from Veterinary Services Directorate)
- **Banned breeds at destination:** None (varies by destination country -- always verify).


## Airlines commonly used from Ghana

- **African Airlines** - cargo only
- **British Airways** - cargo only
- **Virgin Atlantic** - cargo only
- **Emirates** - cargo only
- **KLM** - cabin and cargo
- **Turkish Airlines** - cabin and cargo


## Quick checklist: leaving Ghana with your pet

1. Microchip (ISO standard) -- must be in before any rabies vaccination
2. Rabies vaccination -- must be current at travel date
3. Titre test (if destination requires it) -- blood drawn 30+ days after vaccination
4. Wait period after titre test (90 or 180 days depending on destination)
5. Apply for destination country import permit
6. Book IATA-compliant crate and cargo/cabin space with airline
7. Obtain official health certificate from VSD-endorsed veterinarian
8. Travel day -- arrive at airport 3-4 hours early for cargo pets

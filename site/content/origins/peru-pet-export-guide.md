# Pet Transport from Peru: Export Guide

Moving abroad from Peru with a dog or cat means preparing the correct export documentation before you leave. The rules depend on where you are heading -- but the starting point is always the same: get your pet microchipped (if not already), vaccinate against rabies, and obtain an official health certificate endorsed by SENASA-PE.

This guide covers what Peru requires before your pet leaves, the most common destination requirements for Peru pets, and which airlines serve the main corridors.

## Export requirements for Peru

Before your pet can leave Peru, the following must be in place:

- **Microchip:** ISO 11784/11785 standard. Must be implanted before or on the same day as the first rabies vaccination.
- **Rabies vaccination:** Required and must be valid at the time of travel. Allow the full post-vaccination wait before international travel.
- **Titre test (if required):** Required for: Pets from some countries (check SENASA list). Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml.
- **Health certificate:** Official health certificate endorsed by SENASA-PE. SENASA Peru endorsement required.
- **Quarantine at destination:** No routine quarantine for compliant pets. Veterinary inspection on arrival.
- **Import permit (destination):** Required (apply 4 weeks in advance from SENASA Peru)
- **Banned breeds at destination:** American Pit Bull Terrier (varies by destination country -- always verify).


## Airlines commonly used from Peru

- **American Airlines** - cabin and cargo
- **Delta Air Lines** - cabin and cargo
- **United Airlines** - cabin only


## Quick checklist: leaving Peru with your pet

1. Microchip (ISO standard) -- must be in before any rabies vaccination
2. Rabies vaccination -- must be current at travel date
3. Titre test (if destination requires it) -- blood drawn 30+ days after vaccination
4. Wait period after titre test (90 or 180 days depending on destination)
5. Apply for destination country import permit
6. Book IATA-compliant crate and cargo/cabin space with airline
7. Obtain official health certificate from SENASA-PE-endorsed veterinarian
8. Travel day -- arrive at airport 3-4 hours early for cargo pets

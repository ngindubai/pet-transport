  - heading: "Export requirements from Argentina"
    body: |
      **Export Permit:** No formal export permit required for household pets from Argentina. A health certificate from an accredited veterinarian, endorsed by SENASA, is required for international travel.

      **Health Certificate:** Official health certificate endorsed by SENASA (Servicio Nacional de Sanidad y Calidad Agroalimentaria). Some destinations require consulate endorsement in addition.

      **Government Endorsement:** SENASA endorsement required. Allow 3-5 business days for endorsement processing.

      **Issuing Authority:** Servicio Nacional de Sanidad y Calidad Agroalimentaria (SENASA)

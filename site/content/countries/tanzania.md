# Importing Pets to Tanzania: Complete Guide

Tanzania's Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries administers pet imports. A permit and quarantine are required. Tanzania is treated as rabies-endemic. The main entry/exit airports are Dar es Salaam's Julius Nyerere International and Kilimanjaro International.

## Key requirements for pet import to Tanzania

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Microchip | ISO 11784/11785 required |
| Rabies vaccination | Required |
| Titre test | Required for: Most countries. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml. |
| Quarantine | Mandatory quarantine at government facility (10 days minimum) |
| Import permit | Required -- apply in advance |
| Banned breeds | None confirmed |
| Authority | Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries (MLF) |

## Tanzania pet import requirements

Import permit from MLF is required. Quarantine on arrival (minimum 10 days). Health certificate endorsed by national authority required, along with microchip and vaccination records.

Tanzania does not have an online import permit system; applications go through the MLF office in Dar es Salaam or via a registered pet transport agent.

## Exporting from Tanzania

Pets from Tanzania leaving for strict destinations (UK, AU, NZ) require a titre test. Export permit from MLF and endorsed health certificate are required.

For South Africa: a simpler health certificate-based process applies. For Kenya: health certificate and proof of vaccinations typically sufficient.

## Sources and regulatory authority

Requirements are sourced from Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries (MLF) official guidance. Regulations can change. Always verify current requirements directly with MLF or your pet transport agent before finalising travel arrangements.

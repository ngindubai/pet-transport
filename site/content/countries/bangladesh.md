# Importing Pets to Bangladesh: Complete Guide

Bangladesh's pet export process is managed by the Department of Livestock Services (DLS). The large Bangladesh diaspora in the UK makes UK-Bangladesh the most active route. Export permit from DLS required. Pets from Bangladesh are treated as coming from a rabies-endemic country by most strict destinations.

## Key requirements for pet import to Bangladesh

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Microchip | ISO 11784/11785 required |
| Rabies vaccination | Required |
| Titre test | Required for: Most countries. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml. |
| Quarantine | Mandatory quarantine at government facility (7 days minimum) |
| Import permit | Required -- apply in advance |
| Banned breeds | None confirmed |
| Authority | Department of Livestock Services, Ministry of Fisheries and Livestock (DLS) |

## Leaving Bangladesh with your pet

Export permit from DLS is required. Apply well in advance. You will need a DLS-endorsed health certificate, microchip certificate, and rabies vaccination record. For the UK, Australia, and New Zealand, a titre test is required with the associated waiting period.

Direct flights from Dhaka (Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport) serve the UK, UAE, Singapore, and Malaysia. Some routes require stopovers which add complexity.

## Pet-friendly considerations in Bangladesh

Pet culture in Bangladesh is growing but is not as established as in Western countries. International-grade veterinary clinics are available in Dhaka. When preparing for export, use a vet experienced with international export documentation -- the certificate format requirements are strict and errors cause rejection.

## Sources and regulatory authority

Requirements are sourced from Department of Livestock Services, Ministry of Fisheries and Livestock (DLS) official guidance. Regulations can change. Always verify current requirements directly with DLS or your pet transport agent before finalising travel arrangements.

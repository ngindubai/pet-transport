sections:
  - heading: "Import requirements for Spain"
    body: |
      **Microchip:** Required (ISO 11784/11785). Must be implanted before rabies vaccination
      
      **Rabies Vaccination:** Required. Minimum age 12 weeks. Must be administered after microchip. Valid 21 days after primary vaccination
      
      **Rabies Titre Test:** Required for unlisted third countries only. Not required from EU, UK, or EU-listed countries
      
      **Quarantine:** No quarantine for pets meeting all EU entry requirements
      
      **Import Permit:** Not required for cats and dogs from EU-listed countries
      
      **Health Certificate:** EU health certificate (Annex IV Part 1) required for non-EU pets
  - heading: "Breed restrictions in Spain"
    body: |
      Spain's Royal Decree 287/2002 regulates potentially dangerous dogs. Breeds classified as potentially dangerous include: Pit Bull Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Rottweiler, Dogo Argentino, Brazilian Fila, Tosa Inu, and Akita Inu. Owners must obtain a licence from the local municipality.
  - heading: "Entry points for Spain"
    body: |
      Pets entering Spain from non-EU countries must enter through a designated Travellers' Point of Entry (TPE). Major airports with TPE facilities include Madrid-Barajas, Barcelona-El Prat, Malaga, and Palma de Mallorca.
faqs:
  - question: "What vaccinations does my pet need to enter Spain?"
    answer: "A rabies vaccination is required. It must be given after the microchip is implanted and at least 21 days before travel for a primary vaccination."
  - question: "Is there quarantine in Spain?"
    answer: "No. Spain does not quarantine pets that arrive with valid EU health documentation, an ISO microchip, and a current rabies vaccination."
  - question: "Can I bring my dog to Spain from the UK after Brexit?"
    answer: "Yes, but the UK is now treated as a Part 2 listed third country. Your dog needs a microchip, rabies vaccination at least 21 days before travel, and an Animal Health Certificate issued by an OV within 10 days of travel. No titre test is required."

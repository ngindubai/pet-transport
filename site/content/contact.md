# Contact Us

Tell us where your pet is going and we will put together a clear quote covering documentation, travel, and any quarantine requirements.

## What to Include in Your Message

To give you a useful quote, we need:

- **Origin country** and city if known
- **Destination country** and city if known
- **Pet type** (dog or cat), **breed**, and **approximate weight**
- **Planned travel date** or timeframe (even a rough one helps)
- **Current vaccination status** if you know it

The more detail you include, the more specific we can be. For routes involving mandatory quarantine (Australia, New Zealand, Singapore), lead time is critical, so reaching out early is always better.

## Response Time

We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours on business days (Monday to Friday, UK time).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can you help with any country?**
We cover the major international pet relocation routes, with specialist knowledge in high-complexity destinations: Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, the United States, and the United Arab Emirates. For other destinations, contact us and we will let you know if we can help.

**Do you handle the paperwork or just advise?**
We coordinate the full process, including arranging government-endorsed health certificates, titre test requirements, import permits, and quarantine bookings. We work directly with official veterinarians and government agencies on your behalf.

**What if my breed is restricted?**
Some breeds are banned from entry to certain countries (Pit Bull types in the UK, certain fighting breeds elsewhere) and some airlines will not carry brachycephalic breeds (flat-faced dogs and cats) in cargo. Tell us your breed when you enquire and we will check the relevant restrictions before quoting.

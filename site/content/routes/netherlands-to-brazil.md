    - heading: "NVWA endorsement: what it involves"
      body: |
        The NVWA (Netherlands Food and Consumer Product Safety Authority) is the Dutch competent authority for animal health export certification. Your official vet issues the CVI in the MAPA-required format, and the NVWA endorses it with an official stamp and signature.

        This endorsement is not just a rubber stamp: the NVWA reviews the document for accuracy before endorsing. Allow 2 to 5 working days for the endorsement, and remember the 10-day validity window starts from the endorsement date. Co-ordinate this with your KLM cargo booking.
    - heading: "KLM direct Amsterdam to São Paulo: the practical benefits"

        KLM's direct Amsterdam to Guarulhos service means your pet travels without a connecting flight. Fewer connections mean less handling, shorter total journey time, and a simpler live animal logistics chain.

faqs:
  - question: "Is there quarantine for pets from the Netherlands entering Brazil?"
    answer: "No quarantine is required for dogs and cats from the Netherlands entering Brazil, provided documentation is complete. The MAPA inspection at Guarulhos is a documentary check, not a quarantine hold. MAPA verifies the microchip, vaccination, antiparasitic treatment, and the endorsed CVI. If all are in order, the pet is released immediately."
  - question: "Can KLM carry a large dog in the hold from Amsterdam to São Paulo?"
    answer: "KLM Cargo accepts pets of various sizes, including large dogs in appropriate IATA-compliant crates. However, space for large crates in the hold is limited. Book cargo space as early as possible, provide the crate dimensions and estimated weight when enquiring, and confirm acceptance before purchasing the booking. KLM has specific crate dimension limits per aircraft type."
  - question: "Does my dog need a rabies booster before Brazil?"
    answer: "Brazil requires a current rabies vaccination with at least 21 days having passed after the primary vaccination before entry. If your dog's last rabies vaccination is more than 12 months old, a booster is needed and the 21-day wait applies from the booster date. Confirm the current Dutch vaccination record with your vet before booking."
  - question: "What does MAPA check at Guarulhos Airport?"
    answer: "The MAPA inspector at GRU checks the NVWA-endorsed CVI, verifies the microchip number by reader, confirms the rabies vaccination date and product, and checks the antiparasitic treatment record for dogs. They also inspect the pet visually for health and absence of ectoparasites. The inspection takes 10 to 30 minutes for most arrivals. If documentation is complete, the pet is cleared for entry."

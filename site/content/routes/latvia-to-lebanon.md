  overview: |
    Latvia to Lebanon is a straightforward cargo route for compliant pets. There is no quarantine on arrival and no titre test required for EU-origin pets. The requirements are an ISO microchip implanted before vaccination, a current rabies vaccination, core vaccinations, parasite treatment records, and a PVD-endorsed official health certificate from a Partika un veterinara dienests-authorised vet.

  sections:
    - heading: "PVD health certificate and Lebanon entry requirements"
      body: |
        Latvia's official veterinary authority for non-EU export is the Partika un veterinara dienests (PVD). An official health certificate must be issued by a PVD-authorised vet and cover the animal's microchip number, rabies vaccination date, core vaccinations, and parasite treatment history. The EU pet passport is not valid for Lebanon.

        Lebanon's Ministry of Agriculture requires: an ISO-compliant microchip (implanted before any vaccination), a current rabies vaccination (30 days to 12 months before entry), distemper, hepatitis, parvovirus, and leptospirosis for dogs, and panleukopenia, rhinotracheitis, and calicivirus for cats. Internal and external parasite treatment records are also required. Veterinary inspection takes place at Beirut-Rafic Hariri International Airport on arrival. No quarantine for compliant pets. Confirm all requirements directly with the Lebanese Ministry of Agriculture before travel.

    - heading: "Cargo from Riga to Beirut"
      body: |
        Riga International Airport (RIX) is airBaltic's main hub and offers connections across Europe including Frankfurt, Amsterdam, and Copenhagen. From these hubs, Turkish Airlines cargo via Istanbul (IST) is the most established option to Beirut-Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY). Middle East Airlines (MEA) serves Beirut from Frankfurt and other European cities and is a second cargo option.

        Book through a cargo agent and confirm live animal acceptance on every segment of the journey. Journey times from Riga to Beirut including connections run 8-14 hours depending on routing. Ensure the IATA-compliant crate is appropriately sized for the total transit duration.

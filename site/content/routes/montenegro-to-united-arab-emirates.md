  sections:
    - heading: "Why does MOCCAE treat Montenegro so differently from the UK or Australia?"
      body: |
        Each destination runs its own classification system, and they don't agree with each other. The UK sorts countries into listed and unlisted; Montenegro falls on the unlisted side, which is why its UK route on this site needs a 90-day wait after a titre test. DAFF sorts countries into Group 1, 2, 3 or non-approved; Montenegro is Group 3, which still means a titre test plus its own 180-day wait for the Australia route.

        MOCCAE runs a shorter low-risk list of around 50 countries, and Montenegro is on it. No titre test, no waiting period, just the standard microchip, vaccination and import permit any pet moving to the UAE needs regardless of origin. Three regulators, three separate judgements about the same country, and this route gets the easiest of the three.

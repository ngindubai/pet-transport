    - heading: "The DALRRD import permit and titre test"
      body: |
        South Africa's Department of Agriculture, Land Reform and Rural Development (DALRRD) issues the veterinary import permit that governs entry. The permit sets out the exact conditions your pet must meet, including vaccination timing and, for dogs, the rabies titre test.

        Apply for the permit 4-6 weeks before travel. The rabies titre test for dogs has specific timing relative to vaccination and travel, so plan it early. Your health certificate, issued by an APHA Official Veterinarian, must match the permit conditions precisely; any mismatch can cause problems on arrival.
    - heading: "Direct cargo to Johannesburg"
      body: |
        British Airways operates a direct Heathrow to Johannesburg service with cargo pet carriage via IAG Cargo, the most common route. Virgin Atlantic and Emirates (via Dubai) are alternatives. A direct flight is around 11-12 hours. Book cargo space early, as direct pet capacity is limited.

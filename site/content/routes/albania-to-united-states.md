  sections:
    - heading: "The first direct flight, and its limits"
      body: |
        Delta's Tirana-JFK route ends decades of every Albania-US pet move needing at least one European connection. It's worth being precise about what it actually offers a pet owner, though: Delta carries pets in the cabin only, under its standard size and weight rules, so this new route helps a small dog or cat and does nothing for a larger one.

        A bigger dog still needs a cargo booking, and that still means routing through a hub, Istanbul with Turkish Airlines or Frankfurt and Munich with Lufthansa, both long-established options with genuine live-animal cargo capability on the US legs that follow.

    - heading: "Why Albania's US paperwork stays light"
      body: |
        This isn't the same exemption that applies on the UK route. There, Albania's titre-test exemption rests on the UK sitting on the EU's own approved third-country list, an entirely European mechanism. Here, the reason is American: Albania simply isn't on the CDC's high-risk list for dog rabies, a separate classification with its own criteria and its own review cycle.

        The practical result matches the UK route's outcome even though the underlying logic differs. No titre test, no quarantine, no 3-month or 28-day wait. A dog needs a free CDC Dog Import Form; a cat needs nothing from the federal government at all.

    - heading: "Getting a pet on the plane"
      body: |
        For anyone whose pet fits the size and weight limits, Delta's new nonstop service is the simplest option on this route by a wide margin, one flight, one airline, no interline handover. For anything larger, Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is the established alternative, with a dedicated Pet Lounge that eases a longer layover, and Lufthansa via Frankfurt or Munich offers a second cargo-capable path with its own long track record.

        Whichever option is chosen, check the receiving US state's own entry rules before assuming the CDC's light federal requirements are the whole story.

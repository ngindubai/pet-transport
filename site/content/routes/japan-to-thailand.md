    Relocating a pet from Japan to Thailand is a moderate-complexity move with direct flights and no titre test requirement. Thai Airways operates direct services from Tokyo Narita (NRT) and Osaka Kansai (KIX) to Bangkok Suvarnabhumi (BKK), making routing straightforward.

    On the Thai side, the key step is applying for the Department of Livestock Development (DLD) import permit before travel -- allow 3-4 weeks. On the Japan side, your accredited vet prepares the health certificate, which is then endorsed by the MAFF regional office. That certificate has a 10-day validity window.

    Your pet needs a microchip (implanted before vaccinations) and a current rabies vaccination. No quarantine applies if the documentation is complete. DLD officers inspect pets at Suvarnabhumi Airport on arrival. Allow 6-10 weeks from starting the process to travel day.

  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Stockholm Arlanda to Entebbe"
      body: |
        Stockholm Arlanda Airport (ARN) has no direct service to Entebbe. Two main routings are practical for Sweden-to-Uganda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: SAS or another carrier connects ARN to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its established BRU-EBB East Africa service. This is a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-EBB leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from ARN to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-EBB. Ethiopian is the dominant carrier for Africa-bound live animal freight and ADD-EBB is a regular short East African hop. Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO-EBB) provides a third option accessible via Gulf or European connections from Stockholm. Gothenburg Landvetter (GOT) is an alternative departure point for western Sweden.

    - heading: "Jordbruksverket export health certificate for Uganda"
      body: |
        The Swedish EU pet passport is valid for intra-EU travel only. For Uganda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by Jordbruksverket (Swedish Board of Agriculture) at jordbruksverket.se.

        Contact Jordbruksverket or your regional veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow time for Jordbruksverket endorsement to fall within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Uganda's specific import requirements. A pet transport agent with experience in Swedish exports to Africa can help ensure correct formatting for the MAAIF inspection at Entebbe.

    - heading: "MAAIF: Uganda's import permit requirement"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug processes companion animal imports. The MAAIF permit is the prerequisite document; without it, MAAIF officers at Entebbe cannot clear your animal.

        Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date. Follow up after submitting to confirm progress. On arrival at Entebbe International Airport, MAAIF inspects the permit alongside the Jordbruksverket health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

  sections:
    - heading: "Hungary's breed restrictions: what is banned and what is restricted"
      body: |
        Government Decree 35/1997 prohibits the American Pit Bull Terrier and American Staffordshire Terrier from entering Hungary. These are not restricted with conditions; they are banned outright. No documentation, no exemption, no workaround exists for these breeds.

        Several other breeds are restricted rather than banned. Rottweilers, Bull Terriers, and Staffordshire Bull Terriers can be kept in Hungary but are subject to specific requirements: registration with local authorities, compulsory third-party liability insurance, muzzle and lead use in public, and in some cases a temperament assessment. If you own one of these breeds, contact Hungary's National Food Chain Safety Office (NEBIH) before booking.

        For all other breeds, and for all cats, Hungary applies standard EU rules and the journey is uncomplicated.

    - heading: "Flights from Vilnius to Budapest"
      body: |
        LOT Polish Airlines is the most practical carrier for this route. The Vilnius to Warsaw leg takes under an hour, and LOT connects onward to Budapest. Small pets (up to 8kg including carrier) travel in the cabin throughout. Larger dogs go in the pressurised hold as checked baggage or cargo.

        Lufthansa via Frankfurt is an alternative, particularly for larger dogs that need cargo transport. Lufthansa Cargo is well established on the Frankfurt to Budapest corridor.

        Wizz Air operates heavily between Budapest and various European airports but does not accept pets on any route. Do not book it for this journey.

    - heading: "Documentation from Lithuania"
      body: |
        Lithuania's VMVT-authorised vets can issue EU pet passports. The passport records microchip number, vaccination history, and owner details. It is valid throughout the EU and in several non-EU countries with bilateral agreements, including Switzerland and Andorra.

        For a one-way relocation to Hungary, an AHC is an acceptable alternative. It must be endorsed within 10 days of crossing the Hungarian border. If the documentation is incomplete or out of date at the border, NEBIH may impose conditional quarantine at the owner's cost. Having correct paperwork prevents this.

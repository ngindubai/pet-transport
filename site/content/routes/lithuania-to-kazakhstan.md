  sections:
    - heading: "Kazakhstan's border inspection for pets"
      body: |
        The Committee of Veterinary Control and Supervision under Kazakhstan's Ministry of Agriculture manages companion pet imports. At Almaty International Airport (ALA) or Astana International Airport (NQZ), a border veterinary officer inspects your pet's documents on arrival. For personal pets from Lithuania, they are checking for a current rabies vaccination and an official health certificate from the VMVT.

        Kazakhstan does not require a titre test and does not impose quarantine on compliant arrivals. The border process is documented, but not unusually strict for personal companion pets. Documentation just needs to be accurate and complete.

        Choose your entry city carefully. Almaty is the largest commercial hub and handles the most live cargo. Astana is the capital and also handles cargo, though its infrastructure differs. Which city you choose depends on your final destination within Kazakhstan.

    - heading: "The VMVT export certificate for non-EU travel"
      body: |
        The EU pet passport is effective within EU and EEA countries but has no standing at Kazakhstan's borders. For this move, the Valstybine maisto ir veterinarijos tarnyba (VMVT) at vmvt.lt issues the official export health certificate through an authorised official veterinarian.

        Contact the VMVT before the vet appointment to identify an officially authorised vet in Lithuania (not all vets hold this status) and to confirm the certificate format Kazakhstan accepts. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel.

    - heading: "Routing from Vilnius to Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        Vilnius Airport (VNO) is a regional airport with limited direct international cargo options. The journey to Kazakhstan requires at least two legs.

        One option routes through Istanbul Ataturk (IST) via LOT from VNO to Warsaw (WAW), then Turkish Airlines Cargo from WAW or a European hub to IST, and from IST to either Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ). Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animals at IST regularly.

        Another option routes through Frankfurt (FRA): airBaltic or LOT from VNO to RIX or WAW, then to FRA, and from FRA on Air Astana directly to Kazakhstan. Air Astana cargo from Frankfurt is a direct Central Asia route that avoids the IST transit.

        Book cargo early and obtain written confirmation of live animal acceptance on every segment. A pet transport agent with Baltic and Central Asian experience is genuinely useful here.

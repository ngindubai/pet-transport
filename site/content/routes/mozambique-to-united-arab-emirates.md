content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Mozambique to the United Arab Emirates"
  overview: |
    Maputo has no laboratory accredited to run the rabies titre test MOCCAE requires. That single gap adds real weeks to this route before a flight is even booked, since blood has to travel internationally, usually to South Africa or Europe, before a result comes back. It's the same infrastructure gap this site's Mozambique-to-UK page already flags, and it applies here too.

    The flight itself has a workable answer even without a direct service: Ethiopian Airlines' daily Maputo-Addis Ababa connection feeds onward to Dubai, since Ethiopian and Emirates both fly the Addis-Dubai sector. Qatar Airways via Doha covers the ground faster, though Doha isn't itself in the UAE, so a short additional connection is still needed either way.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why does the titre test take longer here than for most MOCCAE routes?"
      body: |
        Mozambique isn't on MOCCAE's roughly 50-country low-risk list, so a rabies antibody titre test applies in the usual way: blood drawn at least 30 days after vaccination, result of 0.5 IU/ml or above, no fixed wait once it clears. What's different is the logistics behind getting that result.

        There's no MOCCAE-accredited laboratory in Maputo itself. The blood sample has to be couriered out, usually to South Africa or Europe, which typically stretches the standard 2-3 week turnaround to closer to 4-5 weeks. That's not a regulatory delay; it's an infrastructure gap specific to Mozambique, and it's worth planning around from the first vet visit rather than discovering partway through.

    - heading: "Getting out of Maputo: Ethiopian Airlines versus Qatar Airways"
      body: |
        Ethiopian Airlines flies Maputo to Addis Ababa daily, and Addis Ababa connects onward to Dubai on both Ethiopian's own network and Emirates. That makes it the practical single-region routing for this journey, even without a direct Maputo-Dubai flight existing on any carrier.

        Qatar Airways flies Maputo to Doha directly, the shortest elapsed journey time of the realistic options, but Doha is in Qatar, not the UAE, so a further short connection to Dubai or Abu Dhabi is still required. Either route works; the choice mostly comes down to whichever connection lines up better with the titre test result and import permit timing.

    - heading: "What LAM Mozambique Airlines can and can't do"
      body: |
        LAM, the national carrier, is worth ruling out early rather than discovering its limits at booking time. It serves Mozambique's domestic routes and regional African points, nothing further. For a UAE-bound pet, LAM can only ever be the feeder flight into Maputo from elsewhere in the country, never the international sector itself.

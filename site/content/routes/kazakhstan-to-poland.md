      body: |
        Almaty has veterinary clinics experienced in international pet documentation. The FAVN titre test process starts with a vet in Almaty drawing blood from a jugular or cephalic vein, adding the correct preservative (typically sodium azide at 0.5% or an alternative approved by the laboratory), and packaging the sample for international shipment.

        International courier services in Almaty (DHL, FedEx, and local equivalents) handle biological sample exports. The sample must be declared correctly at customs. Shipping from Almaty to a Western European lab takes 3-6 business days. Lab processing takes 1-2 weeks after receipt.

        DHL Express has offices in Almaty and has experience with diagnostic sample shipments. Your vet or agent can coordinate the sample shipping. Total time from blood draw to results: 2-4 weeks.

content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Sweden to Dominican Republic"
  overview: |
    Sweden to the Dominican Republic is one of the more accessible Caribbean routes for pet owners. The Dominican Republic does not require a rabies titre test or quarantine for compliant arrivals. The main requirements are a valid microchip, a rabies vaccination given at least 30 days before entry, current DHPP vaccination for dogs, and an official health certificate from a Jordbruksverket-authorised official vet.

    Four to six weeks is enough preparation time for most pets with current vaccinations. Cargo goes via Amsterdam with KLM or via Madrid with Iberia.

  sections:
    - heading: "Dominican Republic entry requirements"
      body: |
        Veterinary inspection at Las Americas International Airport (SDQ) in Santo Domingo is handled by DGAG (Direccion General de Ganaderia), part of the Ministerio de Agricultura. The inspectors check documentation before releasing your pet.

        The requirements for personal companion pets from Sweden are: an ISO-compliant microchip, a valid rabies vaccination (30 days to 12 months before entry), current DHPP vaccination for dogs or panleukopenia/rhinotracheitis/calicivirus for cats, and an official health certificate issued within 15 days of travel. No titre test is required from Sweden, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals. Up to five personal pets can be imported without an import permit.

    - heading: "Health certificate from Sweden"
      body: |
        The EU pet passport is not accepted at Dominican Republic entry. You need an official veterinary health certificate issued by a Jordbruksverket-authorised official veterinarian in Sweden. Contact Jordbruksverket (jordbruksverket.se) to find authorised official vets in your region.

        The health certificate must be issued within 15 days of travel and include full owner details, microchip number, vaccination history, and the vet's authorisation credentials. If the issuing vet holds an accreditation but not a government official status, the certificate requires a countersignature from an official vet before departure.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Stockholm to Santo Domingo"
      body: |
        There are no direct cargo flights from Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) to Las Americas Airport (SDQ). The main routing is via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) with KLM. SAS connects ARN to AMS, and KLM operates cargo services to the Dominican Republic from Amsterdam.

        The alternative is via Madrid (MAD) with Iberia. SAS and Norwegian Air fly Stockholm to Madrid, and Iberia has established cargo routes to Santo Domingo. Book through a cargo agent on either path and confirm live animal acceptance before finalising your travel date.

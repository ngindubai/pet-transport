  sections:
    - heading: "The Ethiopian Veterinary Authority and the export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Ethiopian Veterinary Authority, operating under the Ministry of Agriculture, is responsible for issuing and endorsing animal health documentation for exports. The health certificate for UK import must be prepared by an Ethiopian Veterinary Authority-authorised official vet and endorsed by the authority before travel.

        The certificate must include: your pet's ISO microchip number, species and breed, vaccination history with titre test result (laboratory name, blood draw date, result in IU/ml, and the date 90 days from the draw), and for dogs the tapeworm treatment details. Contact the Ministry of Agriculture in Addis Ababa to identify authorised vets and obtain the current certificate format for UK export.

        Allow time for the endorsement process after the vet completes the certificate. The fully endorsed certificate must be in your possession within 10 days of travel, so plan the endorsement appointment accordingly.

    - heading: "Addis Ababa altitude and the Ethiopian Airlines direct option"
      body: |
        Addis Ababa Bole International Airport (ADD) sits at approximately 2,400 metres above sea level, one of the highest major international airports in the world. This affects aircraft performance and maximum cargo payloads, particularly on hot days. For most dogs and cats in standard-sized crates this is manageable, but very large dogs in heavy crates may encounter weight restrictions on certain aircraft and routes.

        Ethiopian Airlines operates direct cargo flights from Addis Ababa to London Heathrow. Ethiopian Cargo has considerable experience handling live animals and the direct routing avoids additional hub transit complications. Contact Ethiopian Cargo (ethiopianairlines.com) to confirm live animal acceptance, crate dimensions, and weight limits for your specific route and travel dates.

        Kenya Airways (via Nairobi) and Qatar Airways (via Doha) are alternatives if the direct routing is unavailable or unsuitable. Both carriers accept live animals on suitable aircraft.

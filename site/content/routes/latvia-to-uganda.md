  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Riga to Entebbe"
      body: |
        Riga International Airport (RIX) is airBaltic's main hub. AirBaltic connects RIX to Amsterdam (AMS), Frankfurt (FRA), and other European cargo points, giving Latvia better Baltic-to-Africa connection options than smaller regional airports.

        From AMS, a connection to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) gives access to Brussels Airlines' established BRU-EBB East Africa service. From FRA, Ethiopian Cargo or Lufthansa Cargo connects to Addis Ababa (ADD), then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-EBB. Both routings require coordination across multiple carriers, which is where a pet transport agent adds value.

        Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO-EBB) is a third option for the final East African leg, accessible via Gulf or European connections from Riga. A transport agent can advise which routing provides the most reliable live animal cargo acceptance for your travel dates.

    - heading: "PVD export health certificate for Uganda"
      body: |
        The Latvian EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Uganda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by the Pārtikas un veterinārais dienests (PVD - Food and Veterinary Service) at pvd.gov.lv.

        Contact PVD to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for PVD endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Uganda's specific requirements, which differ from EU-format documents. Your pet transport agent can verify this for the MAAIF inspection at Entebbe.

    - heading: "MAAIF: Uganda's import permit requirement"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug manages companion animal imports. The MAAIF permit is the prerequisite for Uganda entry. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Follow up after submitting to confirm progress. On arrival at Entebbe International Airport, MAAIF inspects the permit alongside the PVD health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

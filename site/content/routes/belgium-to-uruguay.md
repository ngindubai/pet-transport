  sections:
    - heading: "Uruguay's requirements for pets from Belgium"
      body: |
        Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca, gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) governs pet imports. The requirements are: MGAP import permit (apply well in advance, allow 6-10 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; rabies vaccination administered between 30 days and 12 months before arrival; and a FASFC/AFSCA-endorsed health certificate issued within 10 days of travel.

        For dogs, one additional requirement applies: Praziquantel treatment against Echinococcus administered between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival. A veterinarian records the treatment. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. MGAP inspects pets on arrival at Carrasco International Airport (MVD).

    - heading: "The MGAP import permit"
      body: |
        The MGAP import permit is the item with the longest lead time. Apply to MGAP (gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) with your pet's details and proposed travel date. Processing takes 6-10 weeks. Do not book cargo or finalise travel dates until the permit is confirmed.

        The permit must accompany the FASFC/AFSCA-endorsed health certificate on arrival at MVD. MGAP inspectors check the permit reference before releasing the animal.

    - heading: "Praziquantel treatment for dogs"
      body: |
        Dogs entering Uruguay must be treated with Praziquantel against Echinococcus between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival at MVD. Calculate the window from your scheduled arrival time in Montevideo, not departure from Brussels. A treatment given 71 hours before arrival is outside the window.

        Your veterinarian in Belgium administers and records the treatment: product name, dosage, date and time, and the dog's microchip number. This record accompanies the health certificate. Cats do not require the treatment.

    - heading: "Routing from Brussels to Montevideo"
      body: |
        Brussels Airport (BRU) connects to South America through several European hubs. The Iberia routing via Madrid (MAD) with Iberia's direct MAD-MVD service is one of the more efficient options for cargo, as it minimises transfers. BRU to MAD via Iberia or another carrier, then MAD to MVD direct with Iberia Cargo.

        Lufthansa via Frankfurt (FRA) to Sao Paulo (GRU) or Buenos Aires (EZE), then LATAM Airlines to MVD, is a well-established alternative. Air France via Paris CDG offers a third transatlantic option. Confirm live animal cargo availability on the specific flights before booking.

    - heading: "FASFC/AFSCA export endorsement from Belgium"
      body: |
        The Federal Agency for the Safety of the Food Chain (FASFC/AFSCA, favv-afsca.be) endorses official export health certificates for pets leaving Belgium for non-EU destinations. An official veterinarian prepares the certificate referencing the MGAP permit, microchip, vaccination details, and Praziquantel treatment record for dogs.

        Allow three to five business days for the FASFC/AFSCA endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel.

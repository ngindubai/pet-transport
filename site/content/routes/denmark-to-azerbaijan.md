  overview: |
    Relocating a dog or cat from Denmark to Azerbaijan is manageable with the right preparation. Azerbaijan does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets, and no quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. The entry point is fixed: all companion animals must arrive through Heydar Aliyev International Airport (GYD) in Baku. There is no alternative border crossing that handles pet imports.

    Dog owners face one additional requirement that cat owners do not: an Echinococcus (tapeworm) treatment administered within a set period before travel, recorded in the health documentation. This is a straightforward veterinary procedure, but it needs to be timed correctly and recorded in writing on the health certificate or a supporting document.

    The cargo routing from Copenhagen goes through Istanbul (IST) on Turkish Airlines. No direct cargo service operates between CPH and GYD. Budget three to five weeks for preparation, a little more if summer temperature embargoes are likely to affect your travel window.

  sections:
    - heading: "Entry rules for pets arriving in Azerbaijan"
      body: |
        Azerbaijan's pet import rules are administered by the Ministry of Agriculture, agro.gov.az. Veterinary inspection takes place at Heydar Aliyev International Airport (GYD). Officers examine your pet and check documents before releasing it. Baku (GYD) is the only entry airport for companion animals; do not plan a routing that arrives at any other Azerbaijani airport.

        The core requirements are an ISO microchip, a valid rabies vaccination (21 days to 12 months before entry), and an EU-format official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel. No titre test is required from EU member states. For dogs, an Echinococcus treatment is also required and must appear in writing on the health documentation. Your vet will administer the treatment and record it; confirm the exact timing window required by Azerbaijan with your vet and, if in any doubt, with the Ministry of Agriculture directly.

        No import permit is required for personal companion animals. If you are relocating with three or more pets, check whether Azerbaijan applies a commercial movement threshold.

    - heading: "The Echinococcus requirement for dogs"
      body: |
        If you are travelling with a dog, the Echinococcus treatment is a specific requirement that has no equivalent in many EU-to-EU moves. Echinococcus is a tapeworm parasite. Azerbaijan, like several Caucasian and Central Asian countries, requires that dogs be treated before entry.

        Your vet will administer the treatment, typically praziquantel-based, and record it in your dog's health documentation with the date and product used. This record needs to appear on the official health certificate or in an accompanying veterinary statement that travels with the certificate. Do not treat this as a minor note: Azerbaijani border vets look for it specifically.

        Cats do not require this treatment for entry into Azerbaijan. If you are travelling with both a dog and a cat, the cat's paperwork will be simpler on this point.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Copenhagen to Baku"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo flight between Copenhagen (CPH) and Baku (GYD). Turkish Airlines is the established option, routing via Istanbul (IST). The carrier operates cargo services from CPH to IST and from IST to GYD, making it the natural choice for this route.

        Cargo bookings for live animals go through a cargo agent, not the standard passenger booking system. The agent will confirm pet acceptance, obtain the required space, advise on crate sizing to IATA standards, and coordinate documentation submission. Allow 4-6 weeks ahead of travel to secure a booking, as live animal cargo space is limited.

        Turkish Airlines applies temperature embargoes on some routes during June to September. If you are moving in summer, ask the cargo agent explicitly whether the CPH-IST-GYD routing is subject to any embargo for your travel dates. If it is, and if an alternative routing via Frankfurt or Amsterdam is needed, your agent can arrange a SAS feeder flight to connect with another cargo carrier. Always confirm the full chain before committing to a date.

    - heading: "ASV and the Luxembourg health certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        The Administration des Services Vétérinaires (ASV) is Luxembourg's competent authority for animal health documentation. The health certificate for US import must be issued by an ASV-authorised official vet and endorsed by ASV.

        Luxembourg has a high proportion of international residents working in EU institutions and the financial sector, which means local veterinary practices are generally experienced with the requirements for international pet export. The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel.

        Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication is required at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Luxembourg.

    - heading: "Frankfurt as the practical US cargo hub"

        Luxembourg Airport (LUX) does not have direct transatlantic service. Lufthansa offers the widest range of direct US connections from Frankfurt: JFK, LAX, ORD, MIA, IAD, and others.

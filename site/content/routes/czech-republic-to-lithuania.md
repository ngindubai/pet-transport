    - heading: "Route options from Prague to Vilnius"
      body: |
        Czech Airlines operates Prague to Vilnius on some schedules, which is the most direct connection. For owners with small cabin pets, a direct Czech Airlines service is the simplest option. For large dogs in cargo, confirm with Czech Airlines Cargo that live animal space is available on your specific flight before booking, as capacity on this route is limited.

        LOT Polish Airlines via Warsaw is the more predictable cargo option. Prague to Warsaw takes under an hour, and Warsaw to Vilnius is approximately 45 minutes. LOT Cargo handles live animals on both sectors, and Warsaw Chopin has the infrastructure to manage a transfer cargo pet smoothly.

        airBaltic via Riga is the third option. Prague to Riga, then Riga to Vilnius. Riga is airBaltic's home hub with established cargo handling. The RIX to VNO leg is under an hour.

        For small cabin pets, any of these carriers works within standard policies. For large dogs, LOT via Warsaw or airBaltic via Riga gives more cargo certainty.

    - heading: "Lithuania's breed legislation: what to check before you arrive"
      body: |
        Lithuania maintains breed-specific restrictions for certain dog types. The legislation targets dogs classified as potentially dangerous, including Pitbull Terriers, American Staffordshire Terriers, Staffordshire Bull Terriers, and similar bull-type or large guarding breeds.

        For EU arrivals, the EU pet passport covers the standard travel requirements. However, breed restrictions are a separate matter from EU pet travel rules. If your dog falls into any of the covered categories, check VMVT (Valstybinė maisto ir veterinarijos tarnyba) requirements at vmvt.lt before travel. Requirements may include muzzling in public, registration with local authorities, or other restrictions on the dog's activities in Lithuania.

        If your dog is a standard companion breed, this section does not apply. The standard EU pet passport process at Vilnius Airport is routine for a compliant arrival.

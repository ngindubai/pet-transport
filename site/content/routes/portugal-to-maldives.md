  overview: |
    A cat leaving Lisbon starts its journey through Terminal 6, the airport's dedicated cargo building, well away from the passenger arrivals hall most travellers know. It's a straightforward export process on the Portuguese side, which makes the destination the real question mark here.

Cats have a manageable path instead: microchip, rabies vaccination given 30 days to 12 months before arrival, the core feline vaccines, and a health certificate signed within 10 days of travel.

A health certificate signed within 10 days of departure closes the file. None of it demands the multi-month wait a titre test route would.

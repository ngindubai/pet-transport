  overview: |
    Emirates SkyCargo added a fourth daily Johannesburg-Dubai flight on 1 July 2026, which means the onward leg of this route now has more belly-hold capacity than it did a month earlier. The first leg, Gaborone to Johannesburg on Airlink or Air Botswana, runs around 60 combined departures a week, so getting a pet to Johannesburg is rarely the bottleneck here.

    The regulatory side asks more of Botswana than the UAE does in return. Botswana sits outside MOCCAE's roughly 50-country low-risk list, so a rabies titre test applies, though the UAE doesn't add a fixed wait once that result passes. What does slow this route down slightly is Botswana's own export process, which runs a standalone DVS permit alongside the health certificate rather than folding both into one document. Realistically, budget 12 to 16 weeks.

  sections:
    - heading: "A titre test with no clock attached"
      body: |
        MOCCAE sorts every origin country into two groups: named on its low-risk list, or unlisted by default. Botswana falls into the second group, so a titre test at an accredited laboratory is required, blood drawn after vaccination with a result of at least 0.5 IU/ml. Unlike Great Britain's 3-month rule, MOCCAE sets no mandatory waiting period once that result comes back. The pet can travel as soon as the test passes and the import permit is confirmed.

        That single difference is most of why this route moves faster than the equivalent trip to the UK, provided the Pretoria lab turnaround itself doesn't stretch the schedule.

    - heading: "The export permit Namibia's pet owners don't deal with"
      body: |
        Botswana's Department of Veterinary Services asks for a document that catches some owners off guard if they've researched a Namibia route first: a standalone export permit, separate from the health certificate, usually processed in 3 to 5 working days. Namibia's Directorate of Veterinary Services, just across the border, controls exports through the health certificate alone, with no equivalent permit step.

        It isn't a major delay on its own, but it's an extra document to track and an extra few days to fold into the schedule, on top of the titre test's lab turnaround and the MOCCAE import permit application.

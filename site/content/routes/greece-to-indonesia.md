  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Greece to Indonesia requires a BARANTAN (Agriculture Quarantine Agency) import permit, a Hellenic Ministry-issued official health certificate, and a minimum 14-day quarantine on arrival. Bali has additional restrictions as a declared rabies-free island. Emirates via Dubai and Singapore Airlines via Singapore are the main cargo routing options from Athens. This is a complex corridor: use a specialist agent familiar with both Greek and Indonesian regulations.

    - heading: "BARANTAN import permit and quarantine process"
      body: |
        BARANTAN (the Agriculture Quarantine Agency) is Indonesia's authority for animal imports. An import permit must be obtained from BARANTAN before the pet departs Greece. The permit specifies the entry airport and conditions.

        On arrival at Jakarta Soekarno-Hatta (CGK) or Bali Ngurah Rai (DPS), pets undergo a minimum 14-day quarantine at a BARANTAN-approved facility. Pets entering Bali may face additional requirements given Bali's declared rabies-free island status.

        Contact BARANTAN (pertanian.go.id) for the current permit application and the health certificate format required from Greece.

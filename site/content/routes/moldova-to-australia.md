content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Moldova to Australia"
  overview: |
    Moldova's UK route on this site already flags Austrian Airlines' direct Chisinau-Vienna service as one of three workable options. For an Australia-bound pet, that same flight does double duty: Austria's rabies-control profile matches the Group 3 EU countries DAFF names directly, such as France and Spain, while Moldova itself sits outside DAFF's approved system entirely. So the 180-day residency Australia demands from any non-approved origin can start on a flight most Moldovan pet owners are already considering.

    That doesn't remove Australia's own requirements once the clock starts. A titre test still has to clear during the Austrian residency, and Mickleham's 10-day quarantine still sits at the very end. Budget 30 to 36 weeks in total.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why not Turkey or Poland instead"
      body: |
        Moldova's UK and US routes on this site name Turkish Airlines and LOT Polish Airlines alongside Austrian as workable carriers. For an Australia-bound pet specifically, Austria has the edge: its EU rabies-control status lines up with the same Group 3 category DAFF applies to other Western European countries. Turkey's own DAFF status is less clear-cut given its own titre-test requirements for entry into the EU, so Vienna is the safer default here even though all three cities work equally well for a UK or US booking.

    - heading: "The 180-day residency, and the titre test running alongside it"
      body: |
        DAFF's RNATT guidance counts its 180-day wait from the date an approved laboratory receives the blood sample, not the vaccination date or the day the result arrives. Get the rabies vaccination and blood draw done early in the Austrian residency, once the standard post-vaccination gap has passed, and that wait runs alongside the 180-day residency clock rather than adding separate months on top of it.

        Leave the blood draw until near the end of the residency period and the schedule stretches considerably. This single decision is most of the difference between an eight-month process and something closer to fourteen.

    - heading: "Finishing the journey through Doha"
      body: |
        Qatar Airways flies Vienna to Doha five times a week on a Boeing 787, connecting to Melbourne on the same roughly 14-hour Doha-Melbourne sector this site's Serbia-to-Australia route already documents. Once the residency and titre requirements clear, book that connection with enough lead time to also lock in a Mickleham quarantine slot, since the facility only accepts weekday arrivals and spaces are limited.

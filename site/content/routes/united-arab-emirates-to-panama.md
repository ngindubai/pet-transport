  sections:

      body: |
        Every pet leaving the UAE goes through the same MOCCAE sequence: online application, in-person clinic exam, and a final airport check before departure. For Panama specifically, that MOCCAE certificate then needs legalisation from Panama's Embassy in Abu Dhabi or its Consulate General in Dubai, typically adding 2-3 working days to the process.

        Since the MOCCAE certificate carries its own 30-day validity window, sequence the embassy visit immediately after the clinic exam rather than waiting, so the whole document chain stays valid through to your travel date.
    - heading: "What Panama actually checks on arrival"
      body: |
        No titre test applies from the UAE, since Panama does not require one from any origin country. MIDA veterinarians at Tocumen confirm the microchip, a rabies vaccination at least 30 days old, core vaccines, and antiparasitic treatment within the last 30 days. Pets must be over 4 months old. The MIDA registration fee, roughly USD 20 per pet, is paid in cash on arrival.

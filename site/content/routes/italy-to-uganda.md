  sections:
    - heading: "MAAIF permit: Uganda's entry requirement from Italy"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) processes companion animal imports. Apply at maaif.go.ug at least eight weeks before travel. Provide your pet's microchip number, vaccination history, breed, and intended travel date.

        MAAIF is a functioning authority with a documented permit process. Turnaround times vary; applying with sufficient lead time ensures you can respond to any queries without rushing. The permit is checked at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) alongside the Italian health certificate. Compliant arrivals from Italy proceed without quarantine.

    - heading: "Ethiopian Airlines from Rome: the most direct routing"
      body: |
        Ethiopian Airlines operates from Rome Fiumicino (FCO) to Addis Ababa (ADD) as part of its European network. From ADD, Ethiopian's extensive African route network connects to Entebbe (EBB) via a short regional hop. Ethiopian Cargo handles live animal freight across this network and is one of the most experienced African carriers for pet cargo.

        The FCO-ADD-EBB routing takes approximately 10-14 hours including the Addis Ababa connection. This is a manageable transit for healthy adult dogs and cats in IATA-compliant crates. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance and crate size limits directly with Ethiopian Cargo before booking.

        Brussels Airlines via Brussels (BRU-EBB) is the alternative if the Addis Ababa routing does not suit your schedule. Italy to Brussels requires a connection from FCO or Milan Malpensa (MXP), then Brussels Airlines' direct BRU-EBB service. Brussels Airlines is part of the Lufthansa Group with strong East Africa cargo links.

    - heading: "ASL export certificate for Uganda"
      body: |
        Uganda is outside the EU, so the Italian EU pet passport is not valid for this journey. The export document is the certificato sanitario per l'esportazione issued by a veterinario ufficiale at your local ASL (Azienda Sanitaria Locale).

        Book your ASL appointment 3-4 weeks before the intended travel date. The official vet examines your pet, checks microchip and vaccination records, and issues the certificate. It is then endorsed by the relevant authority. Allow 2-3 working days for endorsement.

        The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel to Uganda. Time the appointment and endorsement to fall within that window.

  overview: |
    Japan is on the EU's approved third countries list, which simplifies the process for pets relocating to Greece considerably. There is no titre test requirement and no quarantine -- your pet needs a microchip (implanted before the rabies vaccination), a current rabies vaccination, and the correct EU-model health certificate.

    The key detail on the Japan side is the health certificate format. Pets entering EU member states from Japan need the EU Annex IV listed-country certificate template, completed by a MAFF-accredited vet and endorsed by the MAFF regional office. This is a specific document -- not a standard Japanese health certificate -- and it must be issued within 10 days of your pet entering Greece.

    There are no direct flights from Japan to Greece. Turkish Airlines via Istanbul (IST) is the most convenient routing, with onward connections to Athens (ATH). Lufthansa via Frankfurt is a solid alternative. Allow 4-8 weeks from starting preparation to travel day. Your pet will be inspected at the Athens Border Inspection Post on arrival.

  sections:
    - heading: "No direct UK flights to Panama: why Madrid is the best routing"
      body: |
        There are currently no direct scheduled flights from the UK to Panama City. All routes connect through European or US hubs.

        Iberia via Madrid (MAD) is the recommended option for pet cargo. Iberia runs multiple daily London-Madrid services and connects to Panama City (PTY) with high frequency. The Spanish-language documentation environment at Madrid aligns with MIDA's requirements, and Iberia's long-haul cargo to Latin America is well-established.

        KLM via Amsterdam is a practical alternative - Amsterdam to PTY is available and KLM Cargo has experience on Latin American routes. Air France via Paris CDG and Lufthansa via Frankfurt also connect to Panama and are reliable cargo carriers.

        Avoid routings through US airports unless necessary. A US connection means your pet may need to comply with US import requirements during transit, which adds complexity.
    - heading: "Panama MIDA import requirements: what you need before you fly"
      body: |
        Panama's Ministry of Agricultural Development (MIDA) governs pet imports. You will need a MIDA import permit before travel, a current ISO microchip, valid rabies vaccination, and an official health certificate.

        MIDA may require the health certificate to be in Spanish, or require a certified translation. This is worth confirming early - your UK Official Vet will need to know whether a specific format is required before issuing the AHC.

        On arrival at Tocumen International Airport, a MIDA vet will inspect your pet. With correct documentation, there is no extended quarantine. Arrive with original documents and allow extra time for the agricultural inspection process.

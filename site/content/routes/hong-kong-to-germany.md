    - heading: "AFCD Permission to Export Animals: what it involves"
      body: |
        All live animal exports from Hong Kong require Permission to Export Animals from AFCD. Applications go through the AFCD website (afcd.gov.hk). Allow at least 2 weeks. The permit specifies the animal, species, the destination, and the approval conditions.

        The health certificate must be endorsed by AFCD. It's issued by a government veterinary officer or AFCD-authorised vet within 14 days of Hong Kong departure, and must also be within 10 days of Germany arrival. With a typical HKG-FRA flight of 12-14 hours, these windows overlap cleanly — plan the vet appointment timing accordingly.

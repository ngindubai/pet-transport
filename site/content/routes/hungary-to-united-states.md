    - heading: "NÉBIH and the Hungarian health certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        NÉBIH (Nemzeti Élelmiszerlánc-biztonsági Hivatal, the National Food Chain Safety Office) is Hungary's competent authority for animal health documentation. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a NÉBIH-authorised official vet and endorsed by NÉBIH.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication is required at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Hungary.

    - heading: "Routing from Budapest via Vienna, Frankfurt, or Amsterdam"
      body: |
        Budapest Ferenc Liszt International Airport (BUD) has no direct transatlantic US routes. The most practical cargo option is via Vienna, which is approximately 250km from Budapest. Austrian Airlines flies direct from VIE to JFK, ORD, LAX, and MIA, making Vienna the most convenient hub for Hungarian-origin pets going to the USA.

        Frankfurt (Lufthansa) and Amsterdam (KLM) are alternatives with equally strong US connections. Coordinate cargo with your chosen carrier to ensure the live animal acceptance is confirmed on each leg of the journey.

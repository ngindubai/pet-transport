  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Tallinn to Kigali"
      body: |
        Tallinn Airport (TLL) is a regional Baltic airport with limited direct connections to major cargo hubs. Pet cargo to Rwanda from Estonia will require at least two legs from Tallinn.

        The most practical approach is via a major European hub. AirBaltic connects TLL to Riga (RIX) and from there to Amsterdam (AMS) or Frankfurt (FRA). From AMS, a connection to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) gives access to Brussels Airlines' direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. From FRA, Lufthansa Cargo or Ethiopian Cargo connects to Addis Ababa (ADD), then ADD-KGL on Ethiopian Cargo.

        A pet transport agent experienced in Baltic exports is essential for this route. They will know the live animal cargo acceptance on each leg, which carriers handle multi-leg transfers correctly, and the most reliable routing through European cargo hubs. Confirm all legs with a single agent to avoid gaps in documentation or handling.

    - heading: "Agricultural Board export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Estonian EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by the Põllumajandusamet (Agricultural Board of Estonia) at agri.ee.

        Contact the Agricultural Board to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Given the longer total journey from Estonia, time the vet appointment and endorsement carefully within the 10-day window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific requirements. Your pet transport agent can help verify this for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. On arrival at Kigali International Airport, RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the Agricultural Board health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

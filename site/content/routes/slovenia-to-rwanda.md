  h1: "Pet Transport from Slovenia to Rwanda"
  overview: |
    Rwanda's pet import process requires an import permit from the Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) before travel. Rwanda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Kigali International Airport.

    For Slovenia-based owners, the routing involves multiple connections. Ljubljana Airport is small, and international long-haul cargo typically originate at Vienna Airport, about two hours away. Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa and Qatar Airways via Doha are both practical options for the journey to Kigali. Allow at least six to eight weeks for the full process.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Rwanda requires at the border"
      body: |
        Rwanda's pet import requirements are administered by RAB (Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board), rab.gov.rw. Officers inspect documentation at Kigali International Airport (KGL) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid RAB import permit (obtained before travel), a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Rwanda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for arrivals with compliant documentation and a valid permit.

        Apply for the import permit early: processing can take several weeks and it must be in hand before travel.

    - heading: "UVHVVR health certificate process from Slovenia"
      body: |
        Slovenia's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate signed by a UVHVVR-authorised official vet and endorsed by UVHVVR. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. Allow 1-2 working days for UVHVVR endorsement. Check uvhvvr.gov.si for authorised official vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Slovenia to Kigali"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Ljubljana (LJU) to Kigali (KGL). Most owners originate at Vienna Airport (VIE), which is about two hours from Ljubljana and has established connections to Ethiopian Airlines and Qatar Airways hubs.

        Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the primary option: ADD is Ethiopian Airlines' main East Africa hub with direct connections to KGL. Qatar Airways via Doha (DOH) is a practical alternative with good live animal facilities at Hamad International Airport. Book through a cargo agent and confirm acceptance on all legs before committing.

  sections:
    - heading: "Rwanda's RAB: professional and organised"
      body: |
        The Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board is the competent authority for all animal imports into Rwanda. Its import permit process is one of the more professional in sub-Saharan Africa. Apply at rab.gov.rw at least eight weeks before travel.

        Your application should include your pet's microchip number, full vaccination history, breed, expected travel date, and the routing to Kigali. Once issued, the permit is the core document alongside the CFIA health certificate. Present both at Kigali International Airport (KGL) on arrival. The RAB inspection at KGL is a document and health check; for compliant arrivals from Canada, it proceeds without quarantine.

    - heading: "Routing from Canada to Kigali"
      body: |
        No direct cargo service connects Canada to Kigali. The two most practical routings go via Brussels on Brussels Airlines, or via Addis Ababa on Ethiopian Airlines.

        Brussels Airlines operates BRU-KGL directly and is part of the Lufthansa Group, with well-established East Africa cargo procedures. From Toronto or Montreal, Air Canada or other transatlantic carriers connect to Brussels. The Brussels routing is popular for pet transport on this corridor because Brussels Airlines has strong Kigali links and the connection at BRU is straightforward.

        Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa is an alternative. Ethiopian Cargo has the most extensive African network of any carrier and operates ADD-KGL with regularity. Getting from Canada to Addis typically involves connections through European or Gulf hubs. Total transit from Canada to Kigali runs 18-26 hours; the Ethiopian routing can be slightly longer depending on the connecting point.

    - heading: "CFIA endorsement for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Canada's CFIA endorses health certificates for international pet exports. Your accredited vet issues the certificate; CFIA's regional office endorses it. For Rwanda, confirm with your vet that the certificate covers Rwanda's specific requirements, as the format differs from EU-destination certificates.

        CFIA endorsement takes 1-5 working days through the regional office network. Allow time for this within the 10-day certificate validity window. A vet appointment at the start of the 10-day window with endorsement complete 2-3 days later gives sufficient margin for travel.

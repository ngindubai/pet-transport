        Every pet leaving the UAE, regardless of destination, goes through the same MOCCAE sequence: an online application, an in-person exam at an approved clinic in Dubai or Abu Dhabi, and a final check at the airport before departure. The certificate is valid for 30 days from issue, so it's worth confirming your Madrid or Miami connection before starting the paperwork.
    - heading: "Uruguay's side: import permit and echinococcus treatment, no titre test"
      body: |
        Uruguay's entry requirements for a UAE-origin pet are lighter than they look at first glance. No titre test applies, since the UAE is not on Uruguay's high-risk list. What is required: an MGAP import permit obtained before travel, a current rabies vaccination, and, for dogs only, a praziquantel treatment against Echinococcus given between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival at Carrasco International.

        Apply for the MGAP permit in parallel with the MOCCAE export process rather than after it, since running them one after another adds weeks to the total timeline for no regulatory benefit.

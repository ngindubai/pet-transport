  sections:
    - heading: "Uruguay's requirements for pets from Germany"
      body: |
        Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca, gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) governs pet imports. The requirements for pets arriving from Germany are: a valid MGAP import permit (apply well in advance); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; rabies vaccination administered between 30 days and 12 months before arrival; and a Veterinäramt-endorsed health certificate issued within 10 days of travel.

        For dogs, there is one additional requirement: Praziquantel treatment against Echinococcus must be administered between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival in Uruguay. A veterinarian must record the treatment. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. MGAP inspects pets on arrival at Carrasco International Airport (MVD).

    - heading: "The MGAP import permit"
      body: |
        The MGAP import permit is the critical path item for this route. Apply to MGAP (gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) with your pet's details (species, breed, sex, age, microchip number) and your proposed travel date. Processing typically takes 6-10 weeks. Do not book cargo or finalise dates until the permit is confirmed.

        The permit specifies the conditions of entry and must accompany the health certificate on arrival. MGAP inspectors at MVD check the permit against the pet's documentation before releasing the animal.

    - heading: "Praziquantel treatment for dogs: timing is precise"
      body: |
        Dogs entering Uruguay must be treated with Praziquantel against Echinococcus between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival at MVD. Calculate the window from your scheduled arrival time in Montevideo, not from your departure time in Germany. A treatment given 71 hours before arrival is outside the window and non-compliant.

        A veterinarian in Germany administers the treatment and records it: product name, dosage, date and time, and the dog's details. This record accompanies the health certificate. Cats do not require the treatment.

    - heading: "Routing from Germany to Montevideo"
      body: |
        Frankfurt (FRA) is the main departure point for South American cargo from Germany. The most direct routing is FRA to Madrid (MAD) via Lufthansa or Iberia, then Iberia's direct MAD-MVD service to Montevideo Carrasco. Iberia operates cargo connections on this transatlantic route.

        An alternative is FRA to Sao Paulo Guarulhos (GRU) via Lufthansa's direct South America service, then LATAM Airlines to MVD from GRU. Both routings involve a single transfer and are well-established for cargo. Confirm live animal acceptance on both legs before booking. Allow sufficient time at the connection point for live animal handling.

    - heading: "German export documentation"
      body: |
        Germany's export health certificates for pets are prepared by an official veterinarian (Amtstierarzt) and endorsed by the local Veterinäramt. The Veterinäramt checks the vaccination records, microchip, and the Praziquantel treatment record before endorsing the document.

        Allow three to five business days for the Veterinäramt endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. The Praziquantel treatment must be recorded within this 10-day window, and the timing must meet Uruguay's 72-hour to 30-day pre-arrival requirement.

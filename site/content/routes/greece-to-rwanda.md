        Rwanda's Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) is the authority responsible for live animal imports. Contact rab.gov.rw to start the permit process at least eight to ten weeks before your travel date, providing your pet's species, breed, microchip number and vaccination history.

        On arrival at Kigali International Airport, RAB's veterinary inspector checks the documentation against the animal. Rwanda is one of the more organised East African destinations for administrative processes generally, and a compliant, well-documented pet from Greece should clear without routine quarantine.

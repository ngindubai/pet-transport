content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Finland to Lebanon"
  overview: |
    Lebanon is one of the more involved international pet destinations from Finland. The route is not unmanageable, but it has more moving parts than most: an advance import permit that takes 10 to 12 weeks to obtain, a longer list of required vaccinations for both dogs and cats, a 30-day rabies minimum, and the Ruokavirasto endorsement step on the Finnish export certificate.

    Start the process early. The import permit from Lebanon's Ministry of Agriculture is the first task and the one with the longest lead time. Nothing else on the checklist matters if the permit is not in place. Do not book flights or start the vaccination schedule until you know the permit is being processed.

    The flights from Helsinki to Beirut all route via Istanbul or another European hub. Turkish Airlines is the primary option from HEL. The journey is well-established for cargo animals, and the documentation, once complete, is handled efficiently at Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY).

  sections:
    - heading: "The import permit: why it comes first and what to do"
      body: |
        Lebanon requires an advance import permit from its Ministry of Agriculture for all imported companion animals. This is not a formality that can be obtained quickly: allow 10 to 12 weeks from application to receipt. Some applications have taken longer depending on the period and staffing at the Ministry.

        Apply directly to the Lebanese Ministry of Agriculture through their official website at agriculture.gov.lb. The application will typically ask for details of your pet (species, breed, age, microchip number), your travel dates, and the health documentation you plan to present on arrival. The permit is issued before travel and must be presented at BEY on arrival alongside the health certificate.

        Do not book your flight or commence the vaccination schedule until the permit application is in progress and you have a confirmed expected receipt date. If the permit is delayed, you need time to adjust your travel dates. Starting the vaccination course before the permit is confirmed risks locking yourself into a timeline that cannot be met.

    - heading: "Vaccination requirements: more than just rabies"
      body: |
        Lebanon's vaccination requirements go beyond the standard rabies certificate that most international pet moves require. The full list, according to the Lebanese Ministry of Agriculture (agriculture.gov.lb), is:

        For dogs: rabies, distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, parvovirus, and parainfluenza. For cats: rabies, viral rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia, calicivirus, and leukemia.

        Many Finnish pets travelling with up-to-date vaccination records will already have most of these, particularly dogs on annual combination vaccines. Check your pet's existing records against this list before your vet appointment. Any gap in the record will need to be addressed, and some of the core vaccines require a primary course and a waiting period before the animal is considered fully protected.

        All vaccinations must be listed on the official health certificate. The Ruokavirasto-authorised vet will record them when issuing the certificate. Bring your pet's full vaccination history to the appointment.

    - heading: "Routing from Helsinki to Beirut"
      body: |
        The most practical routing for cargo from Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) to Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY) is via Istanbul (IST) with Turkish Airlines. Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animals, and Istanbul to Beirut is a regularly served route. Total transit time from Helsinki to Beirut, including the Istanbul stopover, is typically 10 to 14 hours.

        MEA (Middle East Airlines), Lebanon's national carrier, operates between Beirut and several European hubs. If MEA's routing from a hub airport suits your connection, the airline's close familiarity with Lebanese Ministry of Agriculture import documentation can reduce friction on the final leg into BEY. Check MEA's live animal cargo policy directly, as it can change.

        Finnair can connect to hub airports including Istanbul and Paris for onward cargo arrangements. For a high-complexity route like Lebanon, an IPATA-registered agent who manages both the import permit process and the cargo booking coordination is worth the cost.

  sections:
    - heading: "Applying for the Tanzania import permit"
      body: |
        Tanzania requires an advance import permit issued by the Director of Veterinary Services, within the Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries Development. The application is made by letter or email, not through an online portal. The letter should include: the species and breed of your pet, the pet's age, your planned port of entry into Tanzania, and copies of vaccination certificates.

        Contact the Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries Development (mifugo.go.tz) or the nearest Tanzanian Embassy to get the current contact details for submitting your permit application. Tanzanian embassies can also process these requests on behalf of applicants. Allow 6 to 8 weeks from application to receiving the permit.

        An import fee of approximately TZS 30,000 (around GBP 8) is payable on arrival. Keep this fee in local currency or as a small USD/GBP amount to exchange at the airport.

    - heading: "Choosing between Dar es Salaam and Kilimanjaro"
      body: |
        Julius Nyerere International Airport in Dar es Salaam (DAR) is Tanzania's main international cargo hub. Most cargo connections from Europe and the Gulf route through here. Kenya Airways via Nairobi, Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa, KLM via Amsterdam, and Qatar Airways via Doha all serve DAR.

        Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) serves the northern safari circuit, including Arusha, Moshi, and the national parks around Mount Kilimanjaro. JRO has fewer direct international connections and cargo options than DAR, but if you are relocating to northern Tanzania, it avoids a long overland journey from Dar es Salaam.

        When applying for the import permit, specify your intended port of entry. If you change your routing, update the permit authority and confirm whether an amendment is needed.

    answer: "The standard Tanzania requirements do not specifically mandate a titre test for pets from rabies-controlled countries such as the UK. However, you must carry full vaccination documentation and an APHA-endorsed health certificate. It is advisable to verify current requirements with the Tanzanian Veterinary Division (mifugo.go.tz) before travel, as requirements can be updated."
  - question: "How do I apply for a Tanzania pet import permit?"
    answer: "The application is by letter or email to the Director of Veterinary Services, Ministry of Livestock and Fisheries Development, Tanzania. Include your pet's species, breed, age, planned port of entry, and vaccination certificates. Contact the Tanzanian Embassy in the UK or the Ministry directly for current submission details. Allow 6 to 8 weeks."

    answer: "Kenya Airways via Nairobi, Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa, KLM via Amsterdam, and Qatar Airways via Doha all offer cargo connections to Dar es Salaam (DAR). Some also serve Kilimanjaro (JRO). There are no direct UK-to-Tanzania flights; all routes involve at least one connection."
  - question: "Is there quarantine for pets arriving in Tanzania from the UK?"

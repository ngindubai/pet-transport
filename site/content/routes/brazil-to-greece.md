  h1: "Pet Transport Brazil to Greece: MAPA Certificate, Titre Test and the 2026 EU Process"
  overview: |
    Brazil is not on the EU approved list for non-commercial pet movement, so the FAVN titre test and 90-day waiting period are mandatory for pets entering Greece from Brazil. MAPA endorses the health certificate. Emirates via Dubai, Air France/KLM via Paris/Amsterdam, and Lufthansa via Frankfurt all serve the Brazil-to-Athens corridor. Total minimum timeline is 6 to 7 months.

    - heading: "MAPA and the EU health certificate format"
      body: |
        MAPA is Brazil's competent authority for pet export health documentation. The certificate must use the EU format required by the European Commission for third-country pets entering the EU, endorsed by MAPA. It must include the ISO microchip number, vaccination history, titre test result (lab name, blood draw date, result in IU/ml, date 90 days from draw), and fitness to travel declaration.

        The certificate is valid for 10 days from examination to EU entry at the Athens BIP. Contact MAPA (mapa.gov.br) for authorised vets and the current EU certificate format.

Latvia to Sweden is one of the better-served Baltic routes for pet travel. airBaltic flies directly from Riga (RIX) to Stockholm Arlanda (ARN), which removes the layover that most other Baltic departures require. The documentation is standard EU: microchip, valid rabies vaccination, and an EU pet passport or AHC. There is one extra step for dogs: a tapeworm treatment that must be given in the days before you arrive in Sweden.

## Tapeworm Treatment: The Requirement That Catches Owners Out

Sweden requires dogs arriving from Latvia to be treated for Echinococcus (fox tapeworm) before entry. The Swedish Board of Agriculture (Jordbruksverket) publishes and maintains the list of countries subject to this requirement, and Latvia is on it.

The treatment must use praziquantel, must be administered by a vet, and must be recorded in the dog's EU pet passport or AHC with the date, dose, and product name. The timing is strict: between 24 and 120 hours (1 to 5 days) before the dog arrives in Sweden. A treatment given six days before arrival is outside the window and will not be accepted.

Cats are completely exempt. For cats from Latvia, the standard EU documentation is all that is needed.

## Core Requirements for Dogs and Cats

**Microchip:** ISO 11784/11785 standard. Must be in place before or on the same date as the first rabies vaccination.

**Rabies vaccination:** Required. A 21-day wait applies to first vaccinations. Boosters given within the valid period carry no waiting period.

**EU pet passport or AHC:** The EU pet passport is the simplest document for EU-to-EU moves. An AHC is the alternative; it must be endorsed by a Latvian official vet (PVD: pvd.gov.lv) within 10 days of your arrival in Sweden.

No titre test, no quarantine, no import permit.

## The Direct Riga to Stockholm Flight

airBaltic's direct service from Riga (RIX) to Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) is the most convenient option for Latvia-to-Sweden pet moves. Small pets (up to 8kg including carrier) travel in the cabin. Larger dogs go in the pressurised hold or via cargo.

Cabin pet spaces are limited. Book the pet reservation at the same time as your ticket, not afterwards. Once the cabin slots for pets are filled, you may be redirected to hold or cargo at short notice.

SAS also connects Riga and Stockholm on some schedules, and Finnair via Helsinki is a third option.

## Timing the Vet Appointment

The tapeworm treatment window is measured against your arrival time in Stockholm, not your departure from Riga. On a direct flight, the journey is around 80 minutes, so the treatment given the morning before a lunchtime flight will typically fall within the 24-120 hour window.

Book the vet appointment for 1-4 days before your planned arrival in Sweden. Confirm that the vet records the full details in your dog's EU passport. If your travel plans change after the appointment, check whether the new arrival date still falls within the 120-hour window.

For questions about Latvian export documentation, contact the Food and Veterinary Service (Partikas un veterinarais dienests, PVD) at pvd.gov.lv. For Swedish import requirements, the Jordbruksverket publishes its rules at jordbruksverket.se.

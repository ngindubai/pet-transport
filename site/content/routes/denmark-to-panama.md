content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Denmark to Panama"
  overview: |
    Denmark to Panama is a manageable route with two documentation steps that need to happen in sequence: first the Foedevarestyrelsen endorsement of the health certificate (1-2 working days), then the Panamanian Embassy authentication (2-3 working days). Both must be completed within the 10-day validity window of the health certificate.

    The substantive requirements for Panama are: a microchip, a rabies vaccination at least 30 days old, current additional vaccinations, and the health certificate. No titre test, no quarantine, no import permit for personal pets. Four to six weeks preparation is enough for pets with current vaccinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "Two authentication steps: Foedevarestyrelsen and the Panamanian Embassy"
      body: |
        Denmark's process involves two authentication steps before the health certificate is ready for Panama. First, a Foedevarestyrelsen-authorised official vet conducts the clinical examination and issues the certificate. Then the Foedevarestyrelsen regional office endorses it, which takes 1-2 working days.

        Once the endorsed certificate is returned, it must be taken to the Panamanian Embassy in Copenhagen for authentication. This second step verifies the Danish vet's official credentials for MIDA officers at Tocumen Airport. Allow 2-3 working days at the Embassy. Contact the Embassy in advance to confirm the procedure and current fee.

        The health certificate is only valid for 10 days from clinical examination. Book the vet appointment so all three steps (exam, endorsement, Embassy) are completed before departure, with at least 1-2 days remaining in the validity window as a buffer.

    - heading: "MIDA arrival and Panama entry requirements"
      body: |
        MIDA (Ministerio de Desarrollo Agropecuario) inspects all incoming animals at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) in Panama City. On arrival, MIDA veterinarians check the authenticated health certificate and vaccination records, register your pet in the MIDA system, and charge a fee of approximately USD 20.

        Required vaccinations for dogs: distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, and parvovirus in addition to rabies. For cats: viral rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia, and feline leukaemia. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. Pets under 4 months of age cannot enter.

    - heading: "Cargo from Copenhagen to Panama City"
      body: |
        Copenhagen Kastrup (CPH) does not have direct cargo connections to Tocumen International Airport (PTY). The main option routes via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS). SAS connects CPH to AMS, and from there KLM or Copa Airlines (which is headquartered at Tocumen) provides cargo service to Panama City.

        Iberia via Madrid is an alternative. Connect CPH to MAD via SAS or Iberia, then Iberia cargo to PTY. Book through a cargo agent on either routing and confirm live animal acceptance on all segments before finalising the travel date.

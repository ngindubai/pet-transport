  overview: |
    Somon Air flies Dushanbe to Dubai, the only direct link Tajikistan's national carrier has into the Gulf. It won't carry your pet on that flight. The airline's own published policy says so plainly, citing the UAE's strict animal import conditions as the reason, even though the same carrier does accept pets on several of its other routes.

    That single fact does more to shape this route than the paperwork does. A titre test applies, since Tajikistan isn't on MOCCAE's low-risk list, but there's no fixed waiting period afterwards, unlike the 90-day hold this pet would face moving to the UK. The airline gap, not the blood test, is what forces the detour through Istanbul.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why the obvious flight doesn't work"
      body: |
        Dushanbe to Dubai is a real, regularly scheduled Somon Air service. It's also, for a pet, a dead end. Somon Air's stated policy is that it does not accept animals for transport on this specific route, a restriction the airline attributes directly to the UAE's import conditions rather than any general company-wide pet ban.

        That leaves Turkish Airlines via Istanbul as the practical alternative, using the carrier's dedicated live animal facility at its hub before the onward leg into Dubai or Abu Dhabi. Etihad Airways is worth comparing for the final sector once a connecting flight is booked, since it has no Dushanbe service of its own.

    - heading: "The titre test, without the long wait"
      body: |
        Tajikistan sits outside MOCCAE's low-risk country list, so the standard route applies: microchip, vaccination, a 21-day wait, then a FAVN or ELISA blood draw showing at least 0.5 IU/ml at an accredited lab. No lab exists in Tajikistan itself, so the sample typically ships abroad, adding real weeks to the process.

        Once the result clears, MOCCAE doesn't impose the kind of fixed post-titre wait the UK applies to this same pet. That's a genuine timeline advantage over the UK route, even accounting for the airline detour this move also needs.

    Indonesia to Malaysia is one of the shorter international pet transport routes in Southeast Asia, with the CGK-KUL flight taking under two hours. Confirm live animal acceptance on the specific service and check crate dimension limits before booking.

    - heading: "BARANTAN export and DVS Malaysia import processes"
      body: |
        Exporting a pet from Indonesia requires a BARANTAN export permit and BARANTAN-issued health certificate, along with BARANTAN clearance at Jakarta Soekarno-Hatta on the day of travel. The process takes several weeks, so apply for the export permit early.

        On the Malaysia side, a DVS Malaysia import permit must be obtained before the pet departs Indonesia. The permit specifies the entry conditions and the health certificate format required. On arrival at Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KUL), DVS Malaysia inspects the pet and documentation.

        Note that Peninsular Malaysia has been declared rabies-free since 1999. Indonesia (in parts) has recorded rabies. DVS Malaysia may apply specific requirements for Indonesia-origin pets. Contact DVS Malaysia (dvs.gov.my) and BARANTAN (pertanian.go.id) for the current requirements.

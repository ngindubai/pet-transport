  overview: |
    Taking your pet from India to the Philippines is one of the more manageable international moves in the region. No titre test is required, no quarantine applies if the paperwork is in order, and direct flights run from Delhi (DEL) to Manila (MNL) daily via Philippine Airlines and Air India.

    The sequence matters. Get the microchip first -- Philippine authorities require it to be in place before the rabies vaccination, and a vaccination given before microchipping is invalid. After the chip and vaccination, apply for the Bureau of Animal Industry (BAI) import permit (allow 2-4 weeks), then have your government-endorsed health certificate issued within 10 days of your travel date.

    A pet relocation agent with Philippines experience can handle the BAI permit application and the Indian government endorsement on your behalf. Allow 6-10 weeks from starting the process to travel day, though the paperwork itself is not complicated for this route.

  sections:
    - heading: "Flight options from Beirut to Rome or Milan"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul offers the widest choice of connections into either Rome Fiumicino or Milan Malpensa, and is usually the default starting point for Beirut departures. Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt is a strong alternative, particularly with its dedicated Animal Lounge facility for the connecting leg.

        Emirates SkyCargo via Dubai and Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha both serve the route too. Neither is the fastest option given the extra distance to the Gulf hub, but both are well established for live animal cargo. Doha routing can be affected by summer heat restrictions, so check timing carefully if you're travelling between May and September.

    - heading: "No breed ban, but full document checks at the border"
      body: |
        Here's a genuine point of difference: Italy removed its breed-specific legislation in 2009, so there's no list of restricted or banned breeds to worry about on the Italian side. That said, this has nothing to do with the airline's own breed policies for brachycephalic dogs, which still apply separately and are worth confirming when booking cargo space.

        On arrival, Italian Ministry of Health vets inspect all documentation at Rome Fiumicino or Milan Malpensa. A missing endorsement, an out-of-date health certificate, or a titre test result that hasn't cleared the 90-day wait will all cause problems at this checkpoint regardless of the breed involved.

    - heading: "The titre test sequence and the 90-day trap"
      body: |
        Lebanon is not on the EU's approved list, so Italy applies the same unlisted-country rules as its neighbours: microchip before vaccination, then a minimum 30-day gap before blood is drawn for the titre test. A passing result of at least 0.5 IU/ml starts a 90-day clock, and the pet cannot legally enter Italy until that period has fully elapsed from the draw date. Add Lebanese export processing time on top and 20 weeks is the realistic floor.

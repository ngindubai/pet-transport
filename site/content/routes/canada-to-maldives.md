  overview: |
    A dog cannot legally enter the Maldives. Not with a health certificate, not with a clean bill of health from a Canadian vet, not under any standard process this site can find documented anywhere. The Maldives Customs Service lists dogs among prohibited import items, full stop, and no titre-test-and-wait workaround exists the way it would almost anywhere else in the world.

    A cat is a different story entirely. It's a normal, if long-haul, move once the CFIA export certificate is endorsed, the rabies and feline vaccine schedule is started with 30 days to spare, and a fresh health certificate is signed within 10 days of travel. What actually drives the cost and the stress on this route is the flight: nothing goes direct from Canada to Male, so every booking runs through a Gulf hub.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why the dog ban comes first"
      body: |
        Plenty of Canadian owners relocating for work assume a rabies-free province of origin and a thick folder of vaccination records will get a dog through. It won't. The prohibition holds regardless of where in Canada the dog lived or how well documented its health history is. If a dog is part of your move, get that confirmed directly with Maldivian authorities before you plan anything else around it, not from a standard shipping quote.
    - heading: "The CFIA side for a cat"
      body: |
        A licensed Canadian vet completes the export certificate first. An official CFIA veterinarian then has to endorse it, in person, at a booked appointment, while the cat is still in the country. That last part trips people up: CFIA has no mechanism to endorse a certificate retroactively once the animal has already flown. The fee sits around CAD 20 to 40 per certificate, and vaccination timing sets the rest of the calendar, rabies at least 30 days before arrival in the Maldives, standard feline vaccines alongside it.
    - heading: "What is thin on the Maldives side"
      body: |
        Import permit or no import permit, titre test or none: these are points where published sources genuinely disagree, and we're not going to invent a confident answer where none exists. Confirm the current position with your vet, your agent, or the Maldives Embassy before assuming either way.
    - heading: "Routing and cost"
      body: |
        Toronto Pearson is the more practical Canadian gateway for this trip; Vancouver adds several extra hours on every one of the three Gulf routings. Doha, Abu Dhabi and Dubai are the realistic connection points, with Qatar Airways and Etihad running the most weekly frequencies against Emirates' comparatively thinner schedule on this specific pairing. Price all three. Heat-related cargo restrictions during peak summer can bump a booking for reasons that have nothing to do with your paperwork.

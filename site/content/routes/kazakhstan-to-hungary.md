  sections:
    - heading: "Crate welfare for the Almaty-to-Budapest journey"
      body: |
        The cargo journey from Almaty to Budapest via Istanbul takes approximately 8-10 hours from check-in to delivery. This is a longer journey than a typical European-origin pet cargo move and requires more careful crate preparation.

        IATA Live Animals Regulations (LAR) specify minimum crate dimensions based on pet size: the pet must be able to stand, turn, and lie naturally. For journeys over 8 hours, food and water access fittings (fitted to the outside of the crate door) are required for longer trips. Check the specific IATA requirements for your pet's size and weight before purchasing the crate.

        At Istanbul Airport during transit, Turkish Airlines Cargo holds live animals in a temperature-controlled area. For journeys above a certain duration, an IATA-compliant water and food pouch may be attached to the crate by cargo staff during transit. Your cargo agent should coordinate this with Turkish Airlines Cargo in advance.

    - heading: "Summer heat considerations for Almaty departures"
      body: |
        Almaty's continental climate produces summer temperatures regularly above 30 degrees Celsius in June, July, and August. Many airlines apply temperature embargo conditions when ambient temperatures at origin, transit, or destination exceed 29-30 degrees Celsius. Turkish Airlines Cargo, for example, will refuse live animal cargo at certain temperatures.

        If your travel window overlaps with the Central Asian summer, discuss the temperature embargo policy with your cargo agent before confirming dates. Spring (April to May) and autumn (September to October) are generally the most practical windows for Almaty departures. Winter departures are possible but cold temperature embargoes at certain airlines also apply below 0-7 degrees Celsius.

        An IPATA-registered agent with experience on Kazakhstan routes can advise on the best months for your move and help confirm cargo acceptance for your specific dates.

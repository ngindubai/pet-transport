content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Slovenia to Uganda"
  overview: |
    Uganda's pet import process requires an import permit from the Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) before travel. Uganda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Entebbe International Airport.

    For Slovenia-based owners, this is a long-haul route best routed via Vienna Airport. Ljubljana Airport is small and most international long-haul cargo originates at Vienna, about two hours away. Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa and Qatar Airways via Doha both serve Entebbe and have live animal cargo programmes. Allow at least six to eight weeks for the process.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Uganda requires at the border"
      body: |
        Uganda's pet import requirements are administered by MAAIF (Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries), maaif.go.ug. Officers inspect documentation at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid MAAIF import permit (obtained before travel), a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Uganda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for arrivals with a valid permit and compliant documentation.

        Apply for the MAAIF permit first. Processing times vary and you need it before your pet can travel. Contact maaif.go.ug directly to confirm current processing times and required documentation.

    - heading: "UVHVVR health certificate process from Slovenia"
      body: |
        Slovenia's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate signed by a UVHVVR-authorised official vet and endorsed by UVHVVR. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. Allow 1-2 working days for UVHVVR endorsement. Check uvhvvr.gov.si for authorised official vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Slovenia to Entebbe"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Ljubljana (LJU) to Entebbe (EBB). Most owners originate at Vienna Airport (VIE), which is about two hours from Ljubljana and has established connections to Ethiopian Airlines and Qatar Airways.

        Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the primary option for East Africa. ADD is Ethiopian Airlines' main continental hub with direct connections to EBB. Qatar Airways via Doha (DOH) is a practical alternative, with good live animal facilities at Hamad International Airport.

        Book through a cargo agent and confirm acceptance on all legs before committing to a travel date.

content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Denmark to Jamaica"
  overview: |
    Jamaica is one of the most preparation-intensive Caribbean destinations. A Veterinary Import Permit from the VSD must be in hand before travel, and all incoming pets may spend up to 14 days in quarantine at the Plumb Point National Quarantine Facility in Kingston.

    Allow 10 to 12 weeks from starting preparations to travel day. The permit application is the first priority. Cargo from Copenhagen goes via Amsterdam with KLM or via London with British Airways. A specialist pet transport agent is strongly recommended given the permit, treatment schedule, and quarantine coordination involved.

  sections:
    - heading: "VSD import permit: start here"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division (VSD) requires a Veterinary Import Permit for all incoming companion animals. The permit must be secured before your pet travels. Apply to the VSD at vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm or call +1 876-977-2489 with your pet's details, proposed travel date, and the health documentation you hold. Processing can take several weeks.

        Do not book cargo or arrange the health certificate until you have the permit confirmed and a firm travel date. Breed restrictions apply: Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Japanese Tosa, and Pit Bull Terrier types cannot enter Jamaica. Confirm your dog is not in a restricted category when you apply.

    - heading: "Parasite treatment schedule and health certificate"
      body: |
        Jamaica requires two internal and two external parasite treatments. Both internal treatments must be at least 14 days apart, with the second within 7 days of transport. Both external treatments must be at least 14 days apart, with the second within 48 hours of transport. Work backwards from your exact departure date to set these treatment dates.

        The health certificate must be issued by a Foedevarestyrelsen-authorised official vet in Denmark and endorsed by the Foedevarestyrelsen regional office. Allow 1-2 working days for the endorsement after the vet signs. Issue the certificate within 10 days of travel. The certificate must include all vaccination and parasite treatment records, plus microchip confirmation.

    - heading: "Quarantine at Plumb Point and cargo from Copenhagen"
      body: |
        All pets arriving at Norman Manley International Airport (KIN) in Kingston may be directed to the Plumb Point National Quarantine Facility for up to 14 days. The VSD supervises the quarantine and charges a daily fee. Plan your own accommodation in Jamaica to account for this period.

        From Copenhagen Kastrup (CPH), the main cargo option is KLM via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS). SAS connects CPH to AMS, and KLM operates to KIN. British Airways via London Heathrow is an alternative: connect CPH to LHR, then BA World Cargo to Kingston. Book through a cargo agent and confirm live animal acceptance on every segment.

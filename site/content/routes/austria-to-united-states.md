  sections:
    - heading: "Austrian official vets and the health certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        The health certificate for US import from Austria must be issued by an Austrian official vet. Austrian official vets are authorised by the national veterinary authority, distinct from standard private practice vets. Most Austrian veterinary practices in cities can refer you to an official vet or have one on staff.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (including current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm with USDA APHIS (usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel) whether authentication of the Austrian-issued certificate is required before travel.

    - heading: "Austrian Airlines direct routes to the USA"
      body: |
        Austrian Airlines, part of the Lufthansa Group, operates direct long-haul cargo flights from Vienna International Airport (VIE) to New York JFK, Chicago O'Hare (ORD), Los Angeles (LAX), and Miami (MIA). Austrian Cargo handles pets. United Airlines also flies VIE to Newark (EWR) directly.

        Direct flights from Vienna to the USA are a practical advantage, avoiding the EU hub connections that most Central and Eastern European countries require. Confirm live animal acceptance, crate dimension requirements, and seasonal restrictions with Austrian Cargo or United Cargo before booking.

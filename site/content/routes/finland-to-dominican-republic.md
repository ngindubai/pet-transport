content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Finland to Dominican Republic"
  overview: |
    Finland to the Dominican Republic is one of the more accessible Caribbean routes for Finnish pet owners. No titre test is required, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals. The main requirements are a microchip, a rabies vaccination administered at least 30 days before entry, current DHPP vaccination for dogs, and a health certificate from a Ruokavirasto-authorised official veterinarian.

    Cargo routing goes from Helsinki-Vantaa via Amsterdam Schiphol to Las Americas Airport in Santo Domingo, primarily via KLM. Four to six weeks is enough preparation time for most pets with current vaccinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "Dominican Republic entry requirements"
      body: |
        DGAG (Direccion General de Ganaderia), part of the Ministerio de Agricultura, administers pet import rules and conducts veterinary inspections at Las Americas International Airport (SDQ) in Santo Domingo.

        Requirements for personal companion pets from Finland: an ISO-compliant microchip, a valid rabies vaccination (30 days to 12 months before entry), current DHPP vaccination for dogs or panleukopenia/rhinotracheitis/calicivirus for cats, and a health certificate issued within 15 days of travel. No titre test is required. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. Up to five personal pets can travel without an import permit.

    - heading: "Health certificate from Finland"
      body: |
        The EU pet passport is not valid for the Dominican Republic. You need an official health certificate from a Ruokavirasto (Finnish Food Authority)-authorised official veterinarian. Contact Ruokavirasto (ruokavirasto.fi) to identify authorised official vets in your region.

        The certificate must be issued within 15 days of travel and include: owner details, microchip number, full vaccination history, and the vet's authorisation. If the signing vet is accredited but not a government official vet, a countersignature from an official vet is required. Arrange this before departure.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Helsinki to Santo Domingo"
      body: |
        Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) does not have direct cargo connections to Las Americas Airport (SDQ). The main routing goes via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) with KLM. Finnair operates HEL to AMS, and KLM has cargo services to the Dominican Republic from Amsterdam.

        An alternative routes via Madrid (MAD) with Iberia. Finnair or SAS connects Helsinki to Madrid, and Iberia has established routes to Santo Domingo. Book through a cargo agent for either option and confirm live animal acceptance on all segments.

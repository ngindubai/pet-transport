  h1: "Pet Transport from New Zealand to United Kingdom"
  overview: |
    New Zealand is one of the cleanest starting points for UK entry: it's on the UK's approved country list, so no titre test is required. The main thing to plan around is the rabies vaccination. New Zealand is rabies-free, which is wonderful for the country's biosecurity, but it means most dogs and cats there have never been vaccinated against rabies. You will need to arrange the vaccination and then wait the mandatory 21 days before your pet can enter the UK.

    Beyond that, the practical challenge of this route is distance. There are no direct flights between New Zealand and the UK. Your pet will travel via Dubai, Singapore, Hong Kong, Seoul, or an Australian hub. The connection adds journey time, and you need to account for it when calculating the tapeworm treatment window for dogs.

    For most healthy, microchipped pets the preparation takes four to eight weeks from the point of vaccination. Use a pet transport specialist who knows the current routing options and seasonal embargoes.

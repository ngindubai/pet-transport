  overview: |

  sections:
    - heading: "Kazakhstan's entry requirements for Swedish pets"
      body: |
        Personal companion pets from Sweden can enter Kazakhstan with a valid EU health certificate endorsed by Jordbruksverket, current ISO microchip, and up-to-date rabies vaccination. No titre test is required under standard personal import rules.

        Kazakhstan's Veterinary Committee inspects incoming pets at Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ). Compliant arrivals are released without quarantine.

        One point to clarify before departure: Kazakhstan is part of the EAEU and uses the EAEU-format veterinary certificate for intra-EAEU movements. For EU-origin pets, the Jordbruksverket-endorsed EU certificate is generally accepted, but confirm the preferred format with the Kazakhstani Embassy in Stockholm before travel.

    - heading: "Routing options from Stockholm to Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        Air Astana from Amsterdam (AMS) is arguably the most attractive option for Sweden-to-Kazakhstan pet cargo. Air Astana operates direct flights from Schiphol to both Almaty and Astana, and their cargo service accepts live animals. The AMS connection from Stockholm is short: a domestic or EU-internal flight on SAS, Norwegian, or KLM.

        Turkish Airlines from Stockholm Arlanda is the simpler logistics choice. ARN to Istanbul (IST) with onward Turkish Airlines Cargo to Almaty or Astana. No separate hub connection needed, and Turkish Airlines Cargo is experienced on the IST-Kazakhstan corridor.

        Finnair via Helsinki is a useful alternative for pets where Turkish Airlines schedules do not suit. Helsinki-to-Kazakhstan connections via Finnair or partners are possible, though routing options are more limited.

    - heading: "What to expect at the Almaty or Astana cargo terminal"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan's Ministry of Agriculture Veterinary Committee inspects all incoming pets at the cargo terminal. Inspectors check the EU health certificate, verify the microchip reading against the documentation, and assess the pet's condition.

        Almaty (ALA) has a longer track record for pet imports and is generally smoother for cargo handling. Astana (NQZ) is more modern as an airport but handles lower volumes of pet cargo. Either is acceptable. Choose based on your destination within Kazakhstan.

        All inspections are conducted in the cargo/airfreight area. Your IPATA-registered agent (if you use one) can help coordinate the handover process and any customs declaration requirements.

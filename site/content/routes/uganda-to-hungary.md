  sections:
    - heading: "Why the timeline matters more than the flight"
      body: |
        Flight time from Entebbe to Budapest is a matter of hours. The regulatory sequence is a matter of months. Vaccination has to come after the microchip, then a minimum 30-day gap before blood is drawn for the titre test. A result of at least 0.5 IU/ml starts a 90-day countdown, and NEBIH will not admit the pet a day earlier, regardless of how eager the owner is to travel.

        Hungary does allow conditional quarantine in some circumstances, but relying on it as a backstop for an incomplete wait is a costly gamble, not a plan. Treat the 90 days as a hard floor, not a target to aim close to.

    - heading: "Routing and MAAIF timing"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines' non-stop Entebbe-Istanbul service usually gives the shortest overall journey into Budapest, given how densely Istanbul connects into Central Europe. Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa is a strong alternative with deep East Africa cargo experience, and Qatar Airways via Doha is a further option worth pricing if the first two are unavailable on your dates.

        On the paperwork side, start the MAAIF endorsement request while the 90-day wait is still running, not after it finishes. Since the certificate itself is valid for only 10 days, timing the endorsement close to, but not before, the end of the wait keeps the certificate valid through to the actual flight.

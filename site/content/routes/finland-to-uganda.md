  sections:
    - heading: "Ethiopian Airlines HEL-ADD-EBB: the most direct routing from Finland"
      body: |
        Ethiopian Airlines operates a direct service between Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) and Addis Ababa (ADD). From ADD, Ethiopian Cargo connects regularly to Entebbe International Airport (EBB) on a short East African hop. For Finnish pet owners moving to Uganda, this HEL-ADD-EBB chain is the most efficient routing available, avoiding a European hub transfer entirely.

        Contact Ethiopian Cargo to confirm live animal acceptance on the HEL-ADD service, applicable crate sizes, and current booking procedures. Ethiopian Cargo has the widest live animal programme in Africa.

        Brussels Airlines via Brussels Zaventem (BRU) is an effective alternative: Finnair connects HEL to BRU, then Brussels Airlines operates the established BRU-EBB East Africa service. If Ethiopian Cargo is unavailable for your dates, this routing is a practical second choice.

    - heading: "Ruokavirasto export health certificate for Uganda"
      body: |
        The Finnish EU pet passport is valid for intra-EU travel only. For Uganda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by Ruokavirasto (Finnish Food Authority) at ruokavirasto.fi.

        Contact Ruokavirasto to identify an authorised official vet. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for Ruokavirasto endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Uganda's requirements, which differ from EU-format documents. A pet transport agent with experience in Finnish exports to Africa can help ensure correct formatting for the MAAIF inspection.

    - heading: "MAAIF: Uganda's import permit requirement"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug manages companion animal imports. The MAAIF permit is the prerequisite for Uganda entry. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Follow up after submitting to confirm progress. On arrival at Entebbe, MAAIF inspects the permit alongside the Ruokavirasto health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

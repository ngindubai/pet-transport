content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Belize to the United Arab Emirates"
  overview: |
    A titre test applies on this route because Belize sits outside MOCCAE's roughly 50-country low-risk list. That much is standard for an unlisted origin. What isn't standard, at least compared with a UK-bound move from the same starting point, is what happens after a passing result: nothing. No fixed three-month wait, no further clock. Once the blood draw clears 21 days post-vaccination and hits 0.5 IU/ml or above, the pet is ready to fly as soon as the permit and flights line up.

    Getting there means routing through Miami, where American Airlines hands off to Emirates SkyCargo's near-daily nonstop run to Dubai. Belize's own paperwork is comparatively simple, though the export side isn't published in the same detail as the rules for bringing a pet into the country, so confirm directly with BAHA rather than assuming.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why this titre test moves faster than the UK equivalent"
      body: |
        Owners who've already looked at a UK-bound move sometimes assume every unlisted-country titre test comes with a long wait built in. It doesn't here. MOCCAE only requires the blood sample to sit at least 21 days after a first vaccination, or to be drawn the same day as a valid booster. Once a passing result of 0.5 IU/ml or higher is confirmed, there's no additional mandatory wait before the pet can travel.

        That difference alone means this route can realistically move in 8 to 10 weeks, where the equivalent Belize-to-UK trip needs four to five months, purely because of the UK's fixed post-titre wait.

    - heading: "A 30-day permit and a Miami connection"
      body: |
        MOCCAE's import permit is comparatively short-lived, valid for just 30 days from issue, so apply once the titre result is confirmed rather than earlier. There's also a higher minimum age for a high-risk-country pet, 15 weeks rather than the 12 weeks that applies for low-risk origins, worth knowing if you're planning around a young dog or cat.

        The flight itself runs through Miami on American, a short hop of under two hours from Belize City, then Emirates SkyCargo nonstop into Dubai, a sector of around 14 hours 45 minutes. Book through an IATA-certified pet shipping agent specifically for the Dubai leg; every pet enters the UAE as manifest cargo, and a standard cargo booking without that certification won't clear.

  sections:
    - heading: "Italy's ASL export certificate for non-EU destinations"
      body: |
        When moving a pet from Italy to a non-EU country such as Armenia, the EU pet passport that covers intra-EU movement does not serve as the export document. You need a specific export health certificate issued by a veterinario ufficiale at your local ASL (Azienda Sanitaria Locale).

        The ASL is Italy's local health authority. Every Italian province has an ASL office with a veterinary department. Call your local ASL to book an appointment with the official vet. They examine your pet, verify microchip and vaccination records, and issue the export health certificate. It is then endorsed by the competent authority for export.

        The certificate must be issued within the time window Armenia requires, which is typically within 10 days of travel. Allow 2-3 working days for the endorsement step and plan your vet appointment accordingly.

    - heading: "Rome or Milan to Yerevan: routing via Istanbul"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines connects both Rome Fiumicino (FCO) and Milan Malpensa (MXP) to Istanbul Ataturk (IST). From IST, Turkish Airlines operates multiple daily services to Yerevan Zvartnots International Airport (EVN). Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animal freight on this corridor and IST is a well-used transit hub for Italy-to-Caucasus pet cargo.

        The total transit time from FCO or MXP to EVN via IST is approximately 6-10 hours. This is a comfortable transit time for healthy dogs and cats in compliant crates.

        ITA Airways (formerly Alitalia) connects to Istanbul and may offer alternative routing options via Turkish partners. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance on any ITA Airways segments before planning around them.

    - heading: "Armenia's import process from Italy"
      body: |
        Armenia's State Service for Food Safety (SSFS) at ssfs.am handles companion animal imports. The SSFS import permit is the prerequisite for entry. Apply with your pet's microchip number, vaccination history, breed, and travel date.

        Armenia does not apply EU-style titre test requirements for pets arriving from EU member states such as Italy. Current rabies vaccination, ISO microchip, and the ASL-endorsed health certificate are the core requirements alongside the SSFS permit.

        The SSFS inspection at Yerevan's Zvartnots International Airport (EVN) checks documents and carries out a health assessment. Compliant arrivals from Italy proceed without quarantine.

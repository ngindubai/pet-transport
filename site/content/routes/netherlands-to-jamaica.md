  h1: "Pet Transport Netherlands to Jamaica: 2026 Guide"
  overview: "Netherlands to Jamaica is a route with two fixed requirements that cannot be bypassed: a VSD import permit (8-12 weeks processing) and a mandatory 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station on arrival. Both apply to every pet, regardless of documentation completeness. Two rounds of parasite treatment at precise intervals before the Jamaican arrival date add scheduling precision.   sections:
    - heading: "Jamaica's fixed requirements for pets from the Netherlands"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division (VSD), Ministry of Agriculture (moa.gov.jm), controls all pet imports. For pets from the Netherlands, the requirements are: VSD import permit (apply to vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm, allow 8-12 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; current rabies vaccination within the last 12 months; two rounds each of internal and external parasite treatment at precise timing before arrival; an official health certificate endorsed by the NVWA; and 14 days of mandatory quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station in Kingston on arrival.

        Restricted breeds (Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Japanese Tosa, Pit Bull Terrier) cannot enter Jamaica.

    - heading: "VSD permit and parasite treatment scheduling"
      body: |
        Email vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm with your pet's species, breed, sex, age, microchip number, and proposed travel date. Allow 8-12 weeks for processing. Do not finalise cargo bookings or health certificate preparation until the permit is confirmed.

        Both rounds of parasite treatment are calculated from the arrival date in Jamaica. Round 1 (internal deworming and external de-flea/de-tick, both on the same occasion) is 10-30 days before arrival. Round 2 (both treatments again) is 1-10 days before arrival. A veterinarian must administer and record all four treatments.

    - heading: "Routing from Amsterdam to Jamaica"
      body: |
        Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) is KLM's main hub with extensive international cargo coverage. The most common routing to Jamaica is AMS to Miami (MIA) via KLM Cargo, then a Caribbean carrier to Kingston (KIN) or Montego Bay (MBJ). British Airways via London Heathrow (LHR) is also a well-established option: AMS to LHR, then BA direct to KIN or MBJ.

        Both KIN and MBJ are accepted entry airports for pets. Plumb Point Quarantine Station is in Kingston, so pets arriving at MBJ are transported to Kingston for the quarantine period.

    - heading: "NVWA export endorsement from the Netherlands"
      body: |
        The Nederlandse Voedsel- en Warenautoriteit (NVWA, nvwa.nl) endorses official export health certificates for pets leaving the Netherlands for non-EU destinations. An official veterinarian prepares the certificate in the format required by Jamaica, and the NVWA reviews and endorses it.

        Allow three to five business days for the NVWA endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. It must include the VSD permit reference, microchip, rabies vaccination details, and both rounds of parasite treatment records.

faqs:
  - question: "Can I send my pet to Montego Bay instead of Kingston?"
    answer: "Yes. Sangster International Airport (MBJ) is an accepted entry point alongside Norman Manley International (KIN). Plumb Point Quarantine Station is in Kingston, so pets arriving at MBJ will be transferred to Kingston for the 14-day quarantine period. Confirm the logistics with VSD when applying for the permit."
  - question: "Does the 14-day quarantine apply even if all my documentation is perfect?"
    answer: "Yes. The 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point is mandatory for all pets entering Jamaica regardless of documentation quality. It is not a penalty for non-compliance; it is a fixed import requirement."
  - question: "Can my pet's EU pet passport replace the official health certificate for Jamaica?"
    answer: "No. The EU pet passport is a document for movement within the EU/EEA, not an official export health certificate for third countries. Jamaica requires a specific export health certificate endorsed by the NVWA. The EU pet passport may be used as a reference for vaccination history, but the dedicated health certificate is required."
  - question: "What are the quarantine fees at Plumb Point?"
    answer: "VSD publishes its current fee schedule. Contact VSD at moa.gov.jm or via vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm for up-to-date quarantine fees. Fees can be paid in USD or Jamaican dollars."

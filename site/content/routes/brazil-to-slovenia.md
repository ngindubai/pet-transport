  h1: "Pet Transport Brazil to Slovenia: 2026 Guide"
  overview: |
    TAP Air Portugal's Lisbon hub is the natural gateway for this move, and it stays that way whether your pet is a nine-kilo terrier or a Great Dane, because it's one of the few European connections built around Brazilian traffic rather than added onto it.

    Brazil does not appear on the EU's low-rabies-risk list under Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2026/131. That means the FAVN titre test and the 90-day wait apply in full, and Slovenia's UVHVVR checks for both without exception on arrival.

  sections:
    - heading: "MAPA's CVI: a 10-day clock, not a formality"
      body: |
        The CVI (Certificado Veterinário Internacional) only stays valid for 10 days after MAPA endorsement, not from when your vet first signs it. That window has to line up with your actual departure, so book the MAPA appointment once your flight is confirmed, not before.

        On a GRU-Lisbon-Ljubljana routing the total flight time runs 12 to 15 hours including the connection, so the gap between endorsement and arrival is manageable if the paperwork is planned properly. Leave it too late and you will be re-booking the vet appointment rather than the flight.

    - heading: "The FAVN test and the fixed 90-day wait"
      body: |
        The sequence is microchip first, then rabies vaccination, then a FAVN blood draw at least 30 days later at an EU-approved laboratory. A result of 0.5 IU/ml or higher starts a 90-day countdown from the draw date, not from when the paperwork comes back.

        There is no way to shorten this. Owners who leave the titre test until close to their preferred moving date are the ones who end up delaying the flight by three months, not the ones who plan around it from the start.

    - heading: "Landing in Slovenia: Lisbon, Istanbul, or a road transfer"
      body: |
        TAP's Lisbon route is the most established option for Brazilian pet cargo heading into Europe, and it connects reasonably well toward Ljubljana. Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul, with a direct GRU-IST leg, is a solid second choice if Lisbon slots are tight.

        Because Ljubljana Airport takes a smaller volume of long-haul cargo than the bigger EU hubs, some routings finish with a road transfer from Vienna or Frankfurt rather than a direct flight into LJU. Confirm which applies to your booking before setting a firm arrival date, particularly if you plan to meet the flight yourself.

    answer: "10 days from the date MAPA endorses it, not from the date your vet signs it. Book the MAPA appointment once your flight is confirmed so the two dates line up."

    answer: "No, provided the microchip, vaccination, titre test and 90-day wait are all in order, and the EU health certificate is current. Slovenia's UVHVVR does not hold compliant pets."

  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Riga to Kigali"
      body: |
        Riga International Airport (RIX) is airBaltic's main hub and has broader European connections than the other Baltic airports. AirBaltic operates from RIX to Amsterdam (AMS), Frankfurt (FRA), and other European cargo points.

        From AMS, a connection to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) gives access to Brussels Airlines' direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This multi-leg routing is the most direct available from Latvia to Kigali. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance at each stage with your cargo agent.

        From FRA, Ethiopian Airlines or Lufthansa Cargo connects to Addis Ababa (ADD), and Ethiopian Cargo covers ADD-KGL. This is the alternative routing if the BRU connection is not suitable for your travel dates or pet size. A pet transport agent experienced in Baltic exports will know which routing works most reliably from RIX.

    - heading: "PVD export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Latvian EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by the Pārtikas un veterinārais dienests (PVD - Food and Veterinary Service) at pvd.gov.lv.

        Contact PVD to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for PVD endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific requirements. Your pet transport agent can help verify this for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. On arrival at Kigali International Airport, RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the PVD health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

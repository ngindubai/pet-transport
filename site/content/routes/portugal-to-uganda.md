  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Lisbon to Entebbe"
      body: |
        Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport (LIS) has no direct service to Entebbe. Two main routings are practical for Portugal-to-Uganda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: TAP Air Portugal or another carrier connects LIS to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its established BRU-EBB East Africa service. This is a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-EBB leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from LIS to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-EBB. Ethiopian Cargo is the dominant carrier for Africa-bound live animal freight and ADD-EBB is a regular short East African hop. Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO-EBB) is an additional option accessible via Gulf or European connections from Lisbon.

    - heading: "DGAV export health certificate for Uganda"
      body: |
        The Portuguese EU pet passport is for intra-EU travel only. For Uganda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by DGAV (Direção-Geral de Alimentação e Veterinária) at dgav.gov.pt.

        Contact DGAV or your regional veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow 2-3 working days for DGAV endorsement to fall within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Uganda's specific import requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Portuguese exports to Africa can verify the correct format for the MAAIF inspection at Entebbe.

    - heading: "MAAIF: Uganda's import permit requirement"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug manages companion animal imports. The MAAIF permit is the authorising document for Uganda entry; without it, MAAIF officers at Entebbe cannot clear your animal.

        Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date. Follow up after submitting to confirm progress. On arrival at Entebbe International Airport, MAAIF inspects the permit alongside the DGAV health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

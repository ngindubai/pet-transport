  sections:
    - heading: "SAG and the Chile export health certificate"
      body: |
        SAG (Servicio Agricola y Ganadero) is Chile's agricultural and livestock authority, responsible for animal health documentation for exports. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a SAG-authorised official vet and endorsed by SAG.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, species and breed, vaccination history (including rabies), owner details, and fitness to travel. SAG offices operate throughout Chile, with major offices in Santiago and regional cities. Contact SAG (sag.gob.cl) for the current authorised vet list and health certificate format for US export.

        USDA APHIS may require authentication of the SAG-endorsed certificate. Confirm the current endorsement and authentication process at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Chile, as requirements can change.

    - heading: "LATAM direct from Santiago to the USA"
      body: |
        LATAM Airlines is Chile's principal long-haul carrier and operates direct cargo flights from Santiago Arturo Merino Benitez International Airport (SCL) to Miami (MIA) and New York JFK. This direct routing is a practical advantage: it avoids additional transit hub complications for live animal cargo.

        LATAM Cargo handles live animals on suitable aircraft. Confirm current live animal acceptance, crate dimension requirements, and seasonal restrictions directly with LATAM Cargo (latamcargo.com) before booking. American Airlines via Miami and Copa Airlines via Panama are alternatives if direct services are unavailable on your preferred dates.

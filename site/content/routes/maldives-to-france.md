  overview: |
    French Bee's announcement landed in June: twice-weekly Paris Orly to Male flights from 19 December 2026, flown in a triangle pattern onward to Colombo. It's real news for anyone reading Maldives travel headlines. It's less useful news for anyone trying to ship a cat, since the airline hasn't confirmed live animal cargo terms on a brand-new long-haul route flown without fifth-freedom rights on its second leg.

    The regulatory side hasn't moved regardless of which airline eventually carries the crate. France treats the Maldives as an EU-unlisted country, so the standard sequence applies in full: microchip, rabies vaccination, an FAVN titre test at an EU-approved lab, and a 90-day wait from the blood draw date.

  sections:
    - heading: "A cat route, not a dog ban"
      body: |
        Nothing in Maldivian law stops a resident dog leaving the country. The reason this route, like every Maldives guide on this site, is written around a cat is that dogs cannot legally be imported into the Maldives in the first place, so there's barely a resident dog population to fly anywhere. If you're the rare exception with a dog already living in Male, treat it as its own case, not a standard export job.
    - heading: "The Male side: a health certificate with no ministry to sign it"
      body: |
        There's no export permit process and no government counter-signature built into a routine cat export from the Maldives. A private vet in Male writes the certificate, and agents and airlines generally accept it as issued. Finding cargo capacity out of Velana International, which handles far more resort freight than live pets, is usually the harder part.
    - heading: "The FAVN titre test and France's 90-day rule"
      body: |
        Because the Maldives sits outside Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/636's approved list, Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2026/131 requires blood drawn at least 30 days after vaccination to clear 0.5 IU/ml at an EU-approved lab, and only then does a 90-day clock start, counted from the draw itself, not the result date. Get that wrong and a flight gets booked before the cat is legally cleared to travel.
    - heading: "French Bee, and the routing to actually book"
      body: |
        Once French Bee confirms whether its Orly-Male-Colombo triangle can carry live cargo, and at what price, it may become the fastest option for this specific corridor. Until then, Qatar Airways via Doha remains the steadier choice, landing pets with Air France Cargo taking over for the final leg into Paris Charles de Gaulle.

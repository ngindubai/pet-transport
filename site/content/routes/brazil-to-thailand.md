content:
  h1: "Pet Transport Brazil to Thailand: MAPA Certificate, DLD Import and the Bangkok Quarantine"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Brazil to Thailand requires a DLD (Department of Livestock Development) import permit, a MAPA-endorsed health certificate, and a 30-day quarantine at a government livestock farm on arrival in Bangkok. This is one of the longer quarantine periods in Southeast Asia, making it a more demanding destination than Malaysia or the Philippines. Emirates via Dubai and Qatar Airways via Doha are the main cargo routing options. Apply for the DLD permit well in advance and budget for the full quarantine period.

  sections:

      body: |
        | Requirement | Brazil to Thailand | Brazil to Indonesia | Brazil to Malaysia |
        |---|---|---|---|
        | Import permit | Yes (DLD) | Yes (BARANTAN) | Yes (DVS Malaysia) |
        | Quarantine | 30 days (DLD) | 14 days minimum | No routine (Peninsular) |
        | Export authority | MAPA | MAPA | MAPA |
        | Entry airport | BKK (primary) | CGK or DPS | KUL (primary) |
        | Complexity | Very high | Very high | High |

        Thailand's 30-day DLD quarantine is notably longer than Indonesia's 14-day BARANTAN period and significantly longer than Malaysia's inspection-based entry for Peninsular Malaysia. If quarantine duration is a key concern, Malaysia may be a more manageable Southeast Asian destination from Brazil.

    - heading: "Airline comparison: Brazil to Thailand"
      body: |
        | Airline | Hub | Route | Notes |
        |---|---|---|---|
        | Emirates | DXB | GRU-DXB-BKK | SkyCargo. Dubai-Bangkok connections are frequent |
        | Qatar Airways | DOH | GRU-DOH-BKK | QR Cargo handles live animals |
        | Singapore Airlines | SIN | GRU (via connection) to SIN-BKK | Short SIN-BKK hop. SQ Cargo handles live animals |

        Emirates via Dubai is a practical option given the frequency of Dubai-Bangkok services. Singapore Airlines via Singapore involves an additional connection from Brazil but offers excellent cargo facilities and a very short final leg to Bangkok.

    - heading: "DLD import permit and Thailand quarantine process"
      body: |
        The Department of Livestock Development (DLD) under Thailand's Ministry of Agriculture and Cooperatives is the authority for pet imports. An import permit must be obtained from DLD before the pet departs Brazil. The permit specifies entry conditions, the approved entry airport, and documentation requirements.

        On arrival at Bangkok Suvarnabhumi (BKK), pets are transferred to a DLD-designated government livestock farm for a 30-day quarantine period. Owners or representatives may typically visit during quarantine. Fees vary. After clearance, the pet is released with a DLD health certificate.

        Contact DLD (dld.go.th) for the current import permit application procedure and the MAPA-to-DLD health certificate format required from Brazil.

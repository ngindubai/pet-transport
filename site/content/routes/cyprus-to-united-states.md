  sections:
    - heading: "Department of Veterinary Services and the health certificate"
      body: |
        The Department of Veterinary Services (DVS) in Cyprus is the competent authority for animal health documentation. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a DVS-authorised official vet.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication is required at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Cyprus. Cyprus is an EU member state, and the standard EU health certificate format applies.

    - heading: "Routing from Larnaca to the USA via European hubs"
      body: |
        Larnaca International Airport (LCA) has no direct transatlantic service. The main cargo routing options are via Frankfurt (Lufthansa), Amsterdam (KLM), Athens (Aegean Airlines, connecting to Delta's ATH-JFK/ATL service), and London Heathrow (British Airways).

        Lufthansa via Frankfurt and KLM via Amsterdam are typically the most practical options, offering multiple daily connections and extensive US destination coverage. Confirm live animal acceptance and crate requirements with your chosen carrier's cargo desk before booking.

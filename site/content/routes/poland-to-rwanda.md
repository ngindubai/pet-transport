  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Warsaw to Kigali"
      body: |
        Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routings are practical for Poland-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: LOT Polish Airlines or another carrier connects WAW to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This gives a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Frankfurt and Addis Ababa: LOT or Lufthansa connects WAW to Frankfurt (FRA), then Ethiopian Airlines or Lufthansa Cargo connects to ADD, and Ethiopian Cargo completes the ADD-KGL hop. LOT Polish Airlines operates from WAW as its home hub and connects to most major European cargo hubs. Frankfurt is the most commonly used European hub for Polish-to-Africa cargo.

    - heading: "GIW export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Polish EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by the Główny Inspektorat Weterynarii (GIW - Chief Veterinary Inspectorate) at wetgiw.gov.pl.

        Contact GIW or your regional veterinary inspectorate to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for GIW endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Polish exports to Africa can verify the correct format for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. On arrival at Kigali International Airport, RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the GIW health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

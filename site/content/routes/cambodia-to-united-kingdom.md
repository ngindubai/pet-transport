    - heading: "Bangkok vs Singapore: two Asia-UK routing options compared"
      body: |
        From Phnom Penh, two main Asia-Pacific hub options connect to London for pet cargo. Bangkok Suvarnabhumi (BKK) is the nearest major hub, with Bangkok Airways or Thai Airways operating the short PNH-BKK segment and Thai Cargo connecting to London from Bangkok. This is the most commonly used routing for Cambodia to UK pet moves.

        Singapore Changi (SIN) via Singapore Airlines is the premium alternative. Singapore Airlines Cargo sets the standard for live animal handling in the Asia-Pacific region, with temperature-controlled holding areas and experienced staff at Changi. The Singapore routing is longer in distance but brings better guaranteed animal care standards during the transit.

        Cathay Pacific via Hong Kong is a third option, particularly practical for Phnom Penh given its direct PNH-HKG service and onward Heathrow cargo connections.

    - heading: "Finding the right vet in Cambodia for the titre test process"
      body: |
        Not all vets in Cambodia are familiar with the UK's FAVN titre test requirements. The process requires a specific type of blood sample, sent to an APHA-approved laboratory, which then measures rabies antibodies to a specific threshold. If the vet does not know this process, the blood sample may be taken incorrectly, sent to the wrong lab, or the timing may not be right.

        Phnom Penh has several veterinary practices with experience working with expat pet owners and international pet transport. Finding one of these, rather than using a general local veterinary practice, is the first practical step in the process. Ask the vet directly whether they have sent blood samples to an APHA-approved laboratory before.

content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Uganda to the UAE"
  overview: |
    Entebbe International sits close enough to Lake Victoria that departure day often means an early start and a short ferry-adjacent drive for owners on the Ssese Islands side of the country. What matters more for the move itself is that Emirates SkyCargo flies EBB to Dubai direct, no connection required.

    Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries, MAAIF, handles the export side: an authorised vet examines the pet and MAAIF endorses the health certificate on MOCCAE's own model format. No separate export permit is needed for a personal companion animal.

    On arrival, MOCCAE treats Uganda as outside its low-risk list, so the standard sequence applies: microchip, rabies vaccination, titre test, import permit. The one detail worth knowing before anyone books a flight is that there's no fixed wait after the titre test blood draw, unlike the EU's 90-day rule. The result itself, once it clears 0.5 IU/ml, is what counts, and it stays valid for a year.

  sections:
    - heading: "What MOCCAE asks of a pet leaving Uganda"
      body: |
        Uganda sits outside MOCCAE's short low-risk country list. A pet travelling from Entebbe needs a microchip fitted before vaccination, a rabies vaccination current for at least 21 days if it's the primary dose, and a rabies antibody titre test at an accredited laboratory returning at least 0.5 IU/ml. The online import permit runs for 90 days from issue.

        Where this differs sharply from an EU-bound move: MOCCAE does not add a fixed countdown after the blood draw. It treats the titre test result as valid for 365 days, provided the rabies vaccination remains current throughout. Minimum age for entry from Uganda's risk category is 15 weeks, three weeks above the 12-week floor MOCCAE sets for its low-risk list.

    - heading: "Export paperwork and flight choices from Entebbe"
      body: |
        No formal export permit is required to send a personal pet out of Uganda. The export health certificate is the document that matters, completed by a MAAIF-authorised vet on MOCCAE's model format and endorsed close to travel, usually within 5 to 10 days of departure.

        Entebbe International (EBB) handles departure for both airline options. Emirates SkyCargo runs a direct EBB-DXB cargo service, keeping the journey to one leg. RwandAir via Kigali (KGL) is a fair alternative if Emirates' direct capacity is booked out.

    - heading: "BAI import permit and NQAD quarantine process"
      body: |
        The Bureau of Animal Industry (BAI) under the Philippines Department of Agriculture is the authority for pet imports. An import permit must be obtained from BAI before the pet leaves Brazil. The permit is required for entry and specifies the documentation and health conditions.

        On arrival at Manila Ninoy Aquino International Airport (MNL), pets undergo a 7-day quarantine at the National Quarantine and Animal Disease Control (NQAD) facility. The owner (or a representative) can typically visit during quarantine. Fees apply.

        Contact BAI (bai.gov.ph) for the current permit application process and the health certificate format required from Brazil.

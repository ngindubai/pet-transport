    - heading: "Securing the Azerbaijani import permit"
      body: |
        Azerbaijan's State Veterinary Service, under the Ministry of Agriculture, requires a veterinary import permit before any pet can enter the country. Start this process at least eight to ten weeks ahead of your intended travel date, providing your pet's species, breed, microchip number and full vaccination record.

        The EU export health certificate from Greece has to be adapted specifically for Azerbaijan's requirements, not simply a generic EU document. Your Greek vet, together with the Directorate General of Veterinary Services, handles this endorsement.

    - heading: "Flying from Athens to Baku"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines is the dominant carrier on the Istanbul-Baku corridor and connects easily from Athens, with total transit typically running six to eight hours including the connection. Azerbaijan Airlines (AZAL) is worth checking as an alternative, though its live animal cargo policy needs direct confirmation from the airline rather than assumption.

        On arrival, Baku's growing cargo infrastructure means handling has improved in recent years, though it remains worth briefing your cargo agent in advance about the specific requirements for live animal shipments into Azerbaijan.

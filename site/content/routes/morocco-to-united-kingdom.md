  sections:
    - heading: "Royal Air Maroc cargo: the direct Casablanca-London option"
      body: |
        Royal Air Maroc (RAM) is Morocco's national carrier and operates direct cargo flights from Casablanca Mohammed V International Airport (CMN) to London Heathrow (LHR) and London Gatwick (LGW). This direct routing is a practical advantage for pet transport: it avoids the complexity of transit country veterinary requirements and minimises total travel time for your pet.

        Contact RAM Cargo (royalairmaroc.com) directly to confirm their current live animal policy, crate size requirements, and booking process. RAM Cargo procedures can change, so confirm the details specific to your dates and routing.

        British Airways also serves the Heathrow-Casablanca route and accepts pets via IAG Cargo. Either carrier provides a reasonably straightforward routing for this corridor.

    - heading: "The ONSSA health certificate in Morocco"
      body: |
        ONSSA (Office National de Securite Sanitaire des produits Alimentaires) is Morocco's food and agricultural safety authority, with responsibility for animal health documentation. The health certificate for UK import must be issued by an ONSSA-authorised official vet, not just a private practice vet.

        The certificate must include: ISO microchip number, species and breed, vaccination history (including titre test result with laboratory name, blood draw date, and result in IU/ml), and for dogs the tapeworm treatment details. ONSSA then endorses the certificate with an official stamp.

        Contact ONSSA (onssa.gov.ma) for the list of authorised vets and the current certificate format for UK export.

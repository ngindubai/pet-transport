    Lebanon accepts EU-origin pets without a titre test requirement, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport.   sections:
    - heading: "What Lebanon requires at the border"
      body: |
        Lebanon's veterinary import requirements are administered by the Ministry of Agriculture. Officers inspect documentation at Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY) on arrival.

        The core requirements are a valid rabies vaccination administered at least 30 days before entry, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Lebanon does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

        For 1-2 personal companion animals, no import permit is required. Verify the current requirements with the Ministry of Agriculture Lebanon before booking, particularly if your travel date is some way off, as requirements can change.

    - heading: "DGAV health certificate process from Portugal"
      body: |
        Portugal's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate. It must be signed by a DGAV-authorised official vet and then endorsed by DGAV (Direcao-Geral de Alimentacao e Veterinaria). Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Schedule the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, submit the certificate to DGAV for endorsement and allow 1-2 working days. Your regular vet can administer vaccinations and implant microchips, but the official health certificate must come from a DGAV-authorised official vet. Check dgav.pt for the list of authorised official vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Lisbon to Beirut"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Lisbon (LIS) to Beirut (BEY). Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul (IST) is the primary established route. Turkish Airlines operates Lisbon-Istanbul regularly, and IST to BEY is a well-served route.

        Book through an approved Turkish Airlines cargo agent. Confirm pet acceptance on both the LIS-IST and IST-BEY legs before committing to a travel date. If travelling June to September, ask specifically about temperature embargoes on the Istanbul connection. Brachycephalic breed restrictions apply year-round on Turkish Airlines cargo.

        TAP Air Portugal connects Lisbon to various European hubs and can be used for the first leg, but the onward routing to Lebanon will require a separate carrier. Your cargo agent can advise on the most practical chain for your dates.

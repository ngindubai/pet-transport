  sections:
    - heading: "DAHP and the Cambodia export certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        The Department of Animal Health and Production (DAHP), under Cambodia's Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries, is the authority responsible for animal health documentation for exports. The health certificate for US import must be endorsed by DAHP.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history with titre test result (USDA-recognised laboratory, blood draw date, result in IU/ml), owner details, and fitness to travel. Contact DAHP in Phnom Penh for the list of authorised vets and the current certificate format.

        For the titre test, blood samples from Cambodia typically need to be sent to USDA-recognised laboratories outside the country. A specialist pet transport agent familiar with Cambodian logistics can advise on sample shipping.

    - heading: "Asian hub routing from Cambodia to the USA"
      body: |
        No airlines operate direct flights from Cambodia to the USA. The main routing options are via Bangkok Suvarnabhumi (Thai Airways or Bangkok Airways), Singapore Changi (Singapore Airlines), or Seoul Incheon (Korean Air). Bangkok is the closest and most frequent hub from both Phnom Penh (PNH) and Siem Reap (REP).

        For CDC compliance, confirm that the specific US destination airport has a CDC-registered animal care facility before booking. LAX, JFK, ORD, MIA, and IAD all qualify. Singapore Airlines and Korean Air both have good transpacific networks connecting to multiple qualifying US airports.

  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Prague to Kigali"
      body: |
        Prague Václav Havel Airport (PRG) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routings suit Czech Republic-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: Czech Airlines, Lufthansa, or other carriers connect PRG to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This is a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Frankfurt and Addis Ababa: Czech Airlines or Lufthansa connects PRG to Frankfurt (FRA), then Lufthansa Cargo or Ethiopian Airlines connects to ADD, and Ethiopian Cargo completes the ADD-KGL hop. FRA is a well-established cargo hub for African routes. Frankfurt is the most commonly used European hub for Czech Republic-to-Africa pet cargo.

    - heading: "SVA export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Czech EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by the Státní veterinární správa (SVA - State Veterinary Administration) at svscr.cz.

        Contact SVA or your regional veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow time for SVA endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific import requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Czech exports to Africa can verify the correct format for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date. Apply early and follow up to confirm progress.

        On arrival at Kigali International Airport (KGL), RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the SVA health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

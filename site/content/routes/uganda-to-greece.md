      body: |
        Greece's Ministry of Rural Development and Food, through its Directorate General of Veterinary Services, applies the same unlisted-country rules to Uganda as any EU member state does. Microchip first, always before the rabies vaccination. A minimum 30-day gap, then blood drawn for a titre test at an approved laboratory. A result of at least 0.5 IU/ml starts the 90-day countdown from the draw date, and the pet cannot legally enter Greece before that period ends.

        The MAAIF-endorsed EU-format certificate that finalises the paperwork is valid for only 10 days, so timing its issue close to the actual travel date, once the wait is nearly finished, matters as much as the biological sequence itself.

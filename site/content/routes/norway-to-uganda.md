  overview: |
    Uganda's pet import process is administered by the Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF). It requires an import permit obtained from MAAIF before travel, and the long haul from Oslo to Entebbe means six to eight weeks preparation is realistic.

    Uganda does not require a titre test for EEA-origin pets, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Entebbe International Airport. The MAAIF permit is the first priority: apply early, as processing times can affect your travel timeline. Norwegian pets need the Mattilsynet-endorsed health certificate rather than an EU pet passport.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Uganda requires at the border"
      body: |
        Uganda's pet import requirements are administered by MAAIF (Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries), maaif.go.ug. Officers inspect documentation at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid MAAIF import permit (obtained before travel), a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Uganda does not require a titre test for EEA-origin pets. No quarantine applies for arrivals with a valid permit and compliant documentation.

        Apply for the MAAIF permit early and confirm the current processing time when you submit the application. Contact maaif.go.ug directly for the application procedure and any supporting documentation they require.

    - heading: "The Norwegian health certificate process"
      body: |
        Norway's health certificate for non-EU destinations must be signed by a Mattilsynet-authorised official vet and endorsed by the Mattilsynet regional office. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, submit the certificate to the regional Mattilsynet office and allow 1-2 working days for endorsement. Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the health certificate must come from a Mattilsynet-authorised official vet. Check mattilsynet.no for authorised vets in your region.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Oslo to Entebbe"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Oslo (OSL) to Entebbe (EBB). Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the primary option. Addis Ababa is Ethiopian Airlines' main East Africa hub with direct connections to EBB. Oslo to ADD operates as a direct or one-stop service.

        Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha (DOH) is a practical alternative. Hamad International Airport in Doha has good live animal facilities, and Qatar Airways serves Entebbe from there. Book through a cargo agent and confirm acceptance on all legs before committing to a travel date.

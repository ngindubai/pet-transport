  sections:
    - heading: "What Azerbaijan requires for Portuguese pets"
      body: |
        Azerbaijan accepts personal companion pets from EU countries including Portugal with a valid DGAV-endorsed EU health certificate, current microchip, and up-to-date rabies vaccination. No titre test is required.

        On arrival at Heydar Aliyev International Airport (GYD), Azerbaijani Ministry of Agriculture vets conduct a documentary inspection. Compliant pets are released without quarantine.

        Confirm current requirements at agro.gov.az or through the Azerbaijani Embassy in Lisbon before travel.

    - heading: "Cargo options from Lisbon to Baku"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines is the most straightforward cargo option for this route. Lisbon (LIS) to Istanbul (IST) to Baku (GYD) is a single-carrier journey with established cargo facilities on both segments. Turkish Airlines Cargo accepts live animals and has experience on the Istanbul-to-Baku corridor.

        Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt is an alternative. Connect from Lisbon to Frankfurt (via TAP or Lufthansa Europe), use Frankfurt's Animal Lounge for the transit, then proceed to GYD via Lufthansa or a partner carrier.

        AZAL (Azerbaijan Airlines) operates GYD from several European hubs but not from Lisbon directly. If you prefer AZAL's direct Baku service, you can connect from LIS to Amsterdam or Frankfurt and pick up AZAL from there.

    - heading: "Getting your DGAV certificate right"
      body: |
        The EU export health certificate for Portugal must be issued by a Médico Veterinário Oficial (MVO) registered with DGAV and then endorsed by DGAV. This two-step process takes one to three working days from the examination date for the endorsement alone.

        Schedule the MVO examination within 10 days of your travel date, and factor in the endorsement processing time. If you are travelling on a Monday, a Thursday examination gives just enough time for a Friday endorsement. For greater confidence, aim for a Tuesday examination and a Friday travel date.

        Find DGAV-authorised MVOs through the DGAV regional directorates listed at dgav.pt.

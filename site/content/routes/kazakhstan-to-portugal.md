  overview: |
    Kazakhstan to Portugal requires five to six months of preparation. Kazakhstan is EU-unlisted, triggering the full EU import process: microchip first, vaccination, FAVN titre test, 90-day wait, and Kazakh Veterinary Committee-endorsed EU health certificate.

    Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is the most practical single-carrier cargo option from Almaty or Astana to Lisbon.   sections:
    - heading: "Portugal's requirements for pets from Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        Portugal, as an EU member state, applies EU Regulation 576/2013 to pets from unlisted third countries. Kazakhstan is unlisted. Requirements: ISO microchip before vaccination, rabies vaccination, FAVN titre test at EU-approved laboratory (minimum 0.5 IU/ml, blood drawn 30+ days after vaccination), 90-day wait from blood draw date, and Kazakh Veterinary Committee-endorsed EU-format health certificate valid 10 days.

        DGAV (dgav.pt) inspects incoming pets at Lisbon Airport (LIS) cargo terminal. Compliant pets are released without quarantine.

    - heading: "The EU certificate format challenge from Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan's standard veterinary export certificate is the EAEU format, used for movements within the Eurasian Economic Union (Russia, Belarus, Armenia, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan). This format is not valid for EU entry.

        For your pet to enter Portugal, your Kazakh vet must prepare an EU-format health certificate that complies with EU Regulation 2020/692 for non-commercial pet movements from unlisted third countries. This is a specific format that not all Kazakh vets routinely prepare. Identifying a vet with EU export certificate experience, or working with an IPATA-registered agent, is strongly recommended.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Kazakhstan to Lisbon"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines is the most direct cargo option. Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ) to Istanbul (IST), then IST to Lisbon (LIS). Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animals on both segments.

        Air Astana from Almaty to Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) is an alternative for the long-haul leg. From Amsterdam, a short feeder flight to Lisbon on TAP Air Portugal or another carrier completes the routing. The advantage of Air Astana is using Kazakhstan's national carrier for the main leg.

        Emirates via Dubai is a longer option. Confirm all legs accept live animal cargo before booking any routing.

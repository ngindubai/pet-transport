  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Copenhagen to Entebbe"
      body: |
        Copenhagen Kastrup Airport (CPH) has no direct service to Entebbe. Two main routings are practical for Denmark-to-Uganda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: SAS or another carrier connects CPH to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its established BRU-EBB East Africa service. This is a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-EBB leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from CPH to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-EBB. Ethiopian is the dominant carrier for Africa-bound live animal freight and ADD-EBB is a regular short East African hop. Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO-EBB) is an additional option via Gulf or European connections from Copenhagen.

    - heading: "Fødevarestyrelsen export health certificate for Uganda"
      body: |
        The Danish EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Uganda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by Fødevarestyrelsen (Danish Veterinary and Food Administration) at foedevarestyrelsen.dk.

        Contact Fødevarestyrelsen to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Uganda's specific requirements, which differ from EU-format documents. A pet transport agent experienced in Danish exports to Africa can help ensure correct formatting for the MAAIF inspection at Entebbe.

    - heading: "MAAIF: Uganda's import permit requirement"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug manages companion animal imports. The MAAIF permit is the prerequisite document for Uganda entry. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Follow up after submitting to confirm progress. On arrival at Entebbe International Airport, MAAIF inspects the permit alongside the Fødevarestyrelsen health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

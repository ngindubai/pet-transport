content:
  h1: "Pet Transport Belgium to Jamaica: 2026 Guide"
  overview: "Belgium to Jamaica is a route with two fixed requirements that cannot be avoided: a VSD import permit (8-12 weeks processing) and a 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station in Kingston on arrival. Both apply to every pet, regardless of documentation. Two rounds of parasite treatment at specific intervals before the Jamaican arrival add scheduling complexity. FASFC/AFSCA in Belgium endorses the official export health certificate. From Brussels Airport, British Airways via London Heathrow is the most established routing for Jamaica-bound cargo."

  sections:
    - heading: "Jamaica's fixed requirements for pets from Belgium"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division (VSD), Ministry of Agriculture (moa.gov.jm), controls all pet imports. For pets from Belgium, the requirements are: VSD import permit (apply to vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm, allow 8-12 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; current rabies vaccination within the last 12 months; two rounds each of internal and external parasite treatment at precise timing before arrival; an official health certificate endorsed by FASFC/AFSCA; and 14 days of mandatory quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station on arrival.

        Restricted breeds (Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Japanese Tosa, Pit Bull Terrier) cannot enter Jamaica.

    - heading: "VSD permit and parasite treatment scheduling"
      body: |
        Email vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm with your pet's species, breed, sex, age, microchip number, and proposed travel date. Allow 8-12 weeks for processing. Do not finalise cargo bookings or begin the health certificate process until the permit is confirmed.

        Both rounds of parasite treatment are calculated from the arrival date in Jamaica. Round 1 (internal deworming and external de-flea/de-tick, both on the same occasion) is 10-30 days before arrival. Round 2 (both treatments again) is 1-10 days before arrival. All four treatments must be by a veterinarian and documented in the health certificate.

    - heading: "Routing from Brussels to Jamaica"
      body: |
        Brussels Airport (BRU) has good European hub connectivity. The most established routing to Jamaica is via London Heathrow (LHR) with British Airways: BRU to LHR, then British Airways direct to Kingston (KIN) or Montego Bay (MBJ). British Airways operates Caribbean cargo services from LHR.

        Alternatives include via Frankfurt (FRA) with Lufthansa Cargo to Miami (MIA), then an onward connection to KIN or MBJ. Given Belgium's proximity to both the UK and France, connections via CDG with Air France are also available. Book cargo space early and confirm live animal acceptance on all segments.

    - heading: "FASFC/AFSCA export endorsement from Belgium"
      body: |
        Belgium's Federal Agency for the Safety of the Food Chain (FASFC/AFSCA, favv-afsca.be) endorses official export health certificates for pets leaving Belgium for non-EU destinations. An official veterinarian prepares the certificate in the format required by Jamaica. FASFC/AFSCA reviews and endorses it.

        Allow three to five business days for the FASFC/AFSCA endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. It must include the VSD permit reference, microchip, rabies vaccination, and both rounds of parasite treatment records.

faqs:
  - question: "Does my pet have to go to Kingston even if I am travelling to Montego Bay?"
    answer: "Plumb Point Quarantine Station is in Kingston. Pets arriving at Sangster International Airport (MBJ) in Montego Bay will be transported to Plumb Point in Kingston for the mandatory 14-day quarantine. Contact VSD when applying for the permit to confirm the logistics of MBJ arrivals and quarantine transfer."
  - question: "Does the 14-day quarantine apply to cats as well as dogs?"
    answer: "Yes. The 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point applies to all companion animals entering Jamaica, including cats. It is not species-specific."
  - question: "Can my Belgian vet's EU pet passport records be used on the Jamaican health certificate?"
    answer: "The vaccination records in your pet's EU pet passport can be referenced by your official veterinarian when preparing the Jamaican health certificate, but the EU pet passport itself is not an accepted entry document for Jamaica. A dedicated health certificate endorsed by FASFC/AFSCA is required."
  - question: "What if the VSD permit specifies conditions that differ from the standard requirements?"
    answer: "Read the VSD permit carefully when it arrives. If it includes any specific conditions, these must be met in addition to the standard requirements. Contact VSD at moa.gov.jm if any permit conditions are unclear before arranging travel."

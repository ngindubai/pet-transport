  h1: "Pet Transport from United Kingdom to Angola"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from the UK to Angola is, on paper, simpler than the reverse journey. Angola does not require a rabies titre test from any country, UK included. No quarantine applies to a compliant pet.

    The catches are different from the ones UK owners usually expect. The health certificate is only valid for 14 days before entry, tighter than many destinations. Angola's published guidance also states rabies vaccination must fall between 4 and 11 months before arrival, an unusual window worth confirming directly rather than assuming. And wolf hybrids, along with certain Savannah and Bengal cats, cannot enter the country at all.

    There is no direct flight either. Expect to route through Lisbon or Frankfurt, and allow 10 to 16 weeks to get everything lined up.
  sections:
    - heading: "What Angola actually asks for, and what it doesn't"
      body: |
        Compared with many destinations on this site, Angola's import rules are relatively light. There's no titre test, no import permit for a pet travelling with its owner, and no quarantine if the paperwork is correct. The microchip standard is the familiar ISO 11784/11785.

        The health certificate is where the timing gets tight. It has to be completed within 14 days of entering Angola, cover the pet's vaccination and treatment history, and carry a vet's attestation that the animal is healthy and free of contagious disease. Book the vet appointment around your confirmed flight date, not the other way round, or you risk the certificate expiring before you travel.

    - heading: "The vaccination window, and the species that can't come at all"
      body: |
        One detail stands out from the published guidance: the primary rabies vaccination is stated to need to fall between 4 and 11 months before entry. That's an unusually specific window, and it comes from a secondary source rather than a live ISV page, so it deserves a direct check with Angola's veterinary authority or an experienced agent before you lock in a vaccination date.

        Separately, and firmly: Angola does not permit wolf hybrids to enter under any circumstances, and Savannah or Bengal cats are barred unless they are at least five generations removed from a wild ancestor. If either applies to your pet, confirm the position with ISV well before you plan the move, since this is not a rule with exceptions for accompanying pets.

  h1: "Pet Transport United Arab Emirates to Uganda"
  overview: |
    Emirates SkyCargo flies non-stop from Dubai to Entebbe, which is a rarer thing than it sounds. Most UAE-to-East Africa pet moves in this build route through a connecting hub, so a direct service is a genuine timesaver for anyone relocating with a dog or cat.

    The process itself splits cleanly in two. Leaving the UAE always means the same MOCCAE export certificate, whatever the destination. Entering Uganda means an import permit from the Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries, plus a straightforward arrival check at Entebbe, no titre test, no quarantine for a pet travelling with the right paperwork.

  sections:
    - heading: "The MOCCAE certificate: fixed regardless of destination"
      body: |
        Every pet leaving the UAE needs a MOCCAE export health certificate. Apply online through the MOCCAE portal using a UAE Pass account, then attend an in-person exam at an approved clinic in Dubai or Abu Dhabi. A vet there confirms vaccination status and general health before endorsing the certificate. One further check happens at the airport itself, immediately before the flight. The certificate expires 30 days after issue, so line up your travel date before starting the paperwork.

    - heading: "MAAIF's import permit and what happens at Entebbe"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries issues the import permit required before a pet can enter the country, applied for through maaif.go.ug. Build in several weeks' lead time; this isn't a same-week process.

        On arrival at Entebbe International Airport, present the MOCCAE-endorsed certificate alongside the MAAIF permit. There's no titre test requirement and no quarantine for pets arriving from the UAE with complete documentation, which keeps this route simpler than several other African corridors in this build.

    - heading: "Direct Emirates service, with Doha and Nairobi as backups"
      body: |
        Emirates SkyCargo's non-stop Dubai-Entebbe route is the standout option here. Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha and Kenya Airways Cargo via Nairobi both offer workable alternatives, useful if Emirates' live animal cargo allocation is already full on your preferred date. Nairobi in particular is a short additional hop, so the Kenya Airways routing rarely adds much time.

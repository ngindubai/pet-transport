      body: |
        The Bulgarian Food Safety Agency (BFSA) is the competent authority responsible for animal health documentation for exports. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a BFSA-authorised official vet and endorsed by BFSA.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication of the BFSA-endorsed certificate is required for the USA at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel.

        Sofia Airport (SOF) has no direct transatlantic flights to the USA. The most practical cargo routing options are via Frankfurt (Lufthansa), Amsterdam (KLM), and Vienna (Austrian Airlines). Frankfurt and Amsterdam both have extensive US connections including JFK, LAX, ORD, MIA, and IAD.

        Vienna is particularly convenient given its relatively short flight time from Sofia, and Austrian Airlines flies direct from VIE to JFK, ORD, LAX, and MIA. Confirm live animal acceptance, crate dimension requirements, and seasonal restrictions with the chosen carrier before booking.

        Latvia's PVD (Pārtikas un veterinārais dienests) handles companion animal imports. For an intra-EU arrival from Finland, the requirement is the EU pet passport with a valid ISO microchip and current rabies vaccination. PVD inspectors may conduct spot checks at Riga Airport, particularly for cargo arrivals.

        The check is quick for a compliant passport. Microchip scan matched against the passport record, vaccination dates verified against the travel date. No additional forms are required.

        Latvia has breed-specific legislation covering certain dog types. For the vast majority of pets this is irrelevant, but if you own a dog that might fall under Latvian breed regulations, check PVD's guidance at pvd.gov.lv before finalising travel arrangements.

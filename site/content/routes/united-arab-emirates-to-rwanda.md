content:
  h1: "Pet Transport United Arab Emirates to Rwanda"
  overview: |
    RwandAir flies straight from Dubai into Kigali, which puts this route ahead of most UAE-to-Africa corridors in this build. Add Rwanda's genuinely well-run import permit system, run through the Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board, and this becomes one of the more manageable long-haul pet moves out of the Gulf.

    The UAE side is fixed regardless of where the pet is heading: a MOCCAE export certificate, issued after an online application and an in-person clinic exam, is compulsory for every departure. Rwanda's side adds an import permit and a straightforward vet check on arrival at Kigali, no titre test, no quarantine.

  sections:
    - heading: "MOCCAE's export process, the same for every route out of the UAE"
      body: |
        No matter the destination, a pet cannot leave the UAE without a MOCCAE export health certificate. It starts with an online application through the MOCCAE portal, which requires a UAE Pass account, followed by an in-person examination at an approved clinic in Dubai or Abu Dhabi where a vet confirms the pet's vaccination status and general health. A final check happens at the airport itself before boarding. The certificate runs for 30 days once issued, so book the flight first.

    - heading: "Why Rwanda's import process stands out regionally"
      body: |
        The Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) handles pet import permits through a clear online process at rab.gov.rw. Compared with several neighbouring countries where paperwork can be slow or inconsistently enforced, RAB's system is comparatively predictable, which matters when you're trying to plan a move with a fixed travel date.

        Once the import permit and MOCCAE-endorsed health certificate are in hand, there's no quarantine and no titre test required for a pet arriving from the UAE. Kigali International Airport handles the arrival inspection efficiently by regional standards.

    - heading: "RwandAir's direct Dubai service"
      body: |
        RwandAir operates a non-stop Dubai to Kigali route and has an established process for handling live animal cargo into its home hub. That's a genuine advantage over routes elsewhere in Africa that require two or three connections. Emirates SkyCargo and Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha both offer solid backup options if RwandAir's live animal capacity on a given date is already booked.

    - heading: "Choosing your routing: via Addis Ababa, Brussels, or Nairobi"
      body: |
        Germany to Uganda offers more routing options than many African destinations, which is good news for pet owners who need flexibility. Ethiopian Airlines Cargo via Addis Ababa (ADD) is widely regarded as the strongest option for East Africa cargo movements. Ethiopian's FRA-ADD-EBB routing is reliable, and the airline has substantial experience with live animal movements across Africa.

        Brussels Airlines via Brussels (BRU) is another solid choice, particularly if you value the Belgium connection for mid-journey welfare. Brussels Airlines serves Entebbe and handles live animal cargo. Getting from Germany to BRU is easy via Lufthansa or other connections from FRA or MUC.

        Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO) adds a short NBO-EBB hop but connects well from European hubs. Turkish Airlines via Istanbul (IST) is a fourth option that also serves EBB; Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animals on many African routes. Your pet transport agent can advise on which routing has the most reliable live animal capacity on your travel dates.

    - heading: "Uganda's MAAIF and what to expect at Entebbe"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) is the competent authority for live animal imports. You need a MAAIF import permit before your pet travels, and you should start the application process at least eight to ten weeks in advance. Contact maaif.go.ug for the current application process and requirements.

        On arrival at Entebbe International Airport (EBB), MAAIF veterinary officers carry out a documentation and health check. For pets arriving from Germany, a recognised rabies-controlled country, the inspection is a compliance check rather than a lengthy quarantine procedure. Compliant arrivals with correct documents are cleared without routine quarantine.

        One practical note: EBB is located about 40 kilometres south of Kampala, on the northern shore of Lake Victoria. Factor the transfer logistics into your plans. If you are using a pet transport agent who has local contacts in Kampala, they can often arrange for someone to receive the pet at the airport and handle the final delivery.

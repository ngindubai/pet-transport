content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Poland to Panama"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Poland to Panama is a well-defined process with one step that trips people up: the health certificate must be authenticated by the Embassy of Panama in Warsaw (or the nearest Panamanian consular representation) after GIW endorsement and before departure. That authentication must fit within the 10-day validity window of the EU-format health certificate. Getting this timing right is the central challenge of the route.

    Panama's MIDA (Ministerio de Desarrollo Agropecuario, mida.gob.pa) administers all pet imports at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) in Panama City. There is no import permit, no quarantine, and no titre test. A MIDA inspection on arrival takes around 20 minutes if documents are complete, and a registration fee of approximately USD 20 applies.

    Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) connects to Panama City (PTY) via Copa Airlines, Iberia via Madrid, and Air France via Paris CDG. LOT Polish Airlines operates from Warsaw to several European hubs, making cargo transfers to Copa and Iberia straightforward. Allow five to seven weeks from start to departure to account for vaccinations, GIW endorsement, and Embassy authentication.

  sections:
    - heading: "Embassy of Panama Authentication"
      body: |
        Panama's health certificate requirement includes a step that many owners are not aware of until they begin the process. After the GIW endorses the official health certificate, it must be authenticated by the Embassy of Panama in Warsaw (or the nearest Panamanian consular representation if Warsaw does not have a resident Embassy at the time of travel). MIDA inspectors at PTY will not accept a certificate without this authentication.

        Contact the Embassy of Panama well before your travel date to find out current processing times and any required supporting documents for the authentication. Typical processing takes two to five working days, but this varies. The GIW endorsement and Embassy authentication must both be completed within the 10-day validity window of the certificate. If the Embassy requires five working days, the official vet appointment must be at least six working days before departure.

    - heading: "Polish Export Requirements"
      body: |
        Poland's official export health certificate must be issued by the Powiatowy Lekarz Weterynarii, the district-level official vet authorised by GIW (Glowny Inspektorat Weterynarii, wetgiw.gov.pl). This is distinct from a standard private vet appointment; contact your district veterinary office directly to identify the right official and arrange the pre-export examination. GIW endorsement after the vet's signature takes one to three working days.

        The EU-format certificate is valid for 10 days from signing. Given the additional Embassy authentication step, the official vet appointment needs to be carefully timed. Book it early enough in the 10-day window to allow for GIW endorsement, Embassy processing, and travel. Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) is the cargo departure point.

    - heading: "Panama Import Rules at PTY"
      body: |
        MIDA inspectors at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) check the microchip, rabies vaccination record (minimum 30 days old, pet must be over 4 months), and the authenticated health certificate. No import permit is required for personal pets, and there is no quarantine. The registration fee on arrival is approximately USD 20.

        Documents in Spanish are preferred by MIDA; English documents are generally accepted but a Spanish translation helps. If your private vet's vaccination records are in Polish, arrange a certified translation before travel. The original authenticated health certificate must be presented; a photocopy will not suffice at the MIDA desk.

    - heading: "Cargo Options from Warsaw"
      body: |
        Copa Airlines offers direct cargo service from Warsaw Chopin (WAW) to Tocumen International Airport (PTY), making it the most efficient choice for this route. LOT Polish Airlines' network from WAW connects to Madrid and other European hubs, where cargo can transfer to Iberia or other carriers serving Panama. Air France via Paris CDG also serves Panama City from Warsaw on a cargo basis.

        Cargo costs from Warsaw to PTY run EUR 800-2,000 depending on animal size and crate. Factor in the GIW official vet and health certificate (EUR 100-250), IATA crate (EUR 60-220), Embassy authentication (variable), and agent fees if used (EUR 250-600). A well-prepared single-pet move from Warsaw to Panama City typically costs EUR 1,300-3,000 all-in.

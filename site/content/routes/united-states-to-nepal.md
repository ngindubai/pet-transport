  sections:
    - heading: "Selecting a carrier and managing long crate times"
      body: |
        The connection requirement is the most important logistical factor for US to Nepal pet transport. Qatar Airways via Doha and Turkish Airlines via Istanbul are the most commonly used options, both with good connections to Kathmandu. Emirates via Dubai is another route. Total crate time from a US East Coast airport to Kathmandu is typically 20-24 hours; from the West Coast or via longer routings it can be 24-28 hours.

        Before booking, confirm with the airline's cargo team that live animals are accepted on both the US-to-hub and hub-to-Kathmandu legs. Aircraft type restrictions mean live animal acceptance varies by flight. A pet transport agent with Nepal routing experience can advise on current carrier reliability.

    - heading: "DLS permit, titre test and quarantine in sequence"
      body: |
        Nepal's Department of Livestock Services requires a titre test for most countries, and the USA is expected to be included. Start the vaccination and titre test process as early as possible. The blood draw must be at least 30 days after your pet's most recent rabies vaccination, and results take 2-3 weeks.

        Run the DLS permit application in parallel. Apply at least 6 weeks before travel with microchip details, vaccination history, titre test result, and planned entry date. On arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, your pet goes into a 7-day quarantine facility managed by DLS. The owner pays all quarantine costs for the full period.

faqs:
  - question: "How do I get my pet from the USA to Nepal without a direct flight?"
    answer: "All US to Nepal routing involves a connection, most commonly via Doha (Qatar Airways), Istanbul (Turkish Airlines), or Dubai (Emirates). These carriers all handle live animal cargo. Book with the airline's cargo team directly for both legs. Pet transport agents specialising in Asia-Pacific routing know which carriers consistently accept live animals on the Kathmandu leg, which changes by aircraft type. Total crate time is 20-28 hours from the US."

  - question: "Does my pet need a titre test to enter Nepal from the USA?"
    answer: "Nepal requires titre tests for pets from most countries, and the USA's non-rabies-free status means US pets are expected to need one. The blood draw must be at least 30 days after the most recent rabies vaccination. Verify the current requirement with Nepal's Department of Livestock Services when applying for the import permit. Laboratory results take 2-3 weeks after the blood draw."

  - question: "How long is quarantine when my pet arrives in Nepal?"
    answer: "7 days at a quarantine facility managed by Nepal's Department of Livestock Services, starting on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu. The quarantine cannot be shortened for any origin country. The owner pays facility fees for the full period. Confirm current rates and visiting arrangements with DLS when applying for the permit."

  - question: "Is altitude a concern for pets flying to Kathmandu?"
    answer: "Kathmandu sits at approximately 1,400 metres above sea level. For most healthy dogs and cats, this altitude is not a significant problem, but brachycephalic (flat-faced) breeds such as French Bulldogs, Pugs, Persian cats, and similar types may face additional stress. Consult your vet before booking, particularly if your pet has any respiratory history. Some airlines also restrict brachycephalic breeds in cargo holds regardless of destination."

  h1: "Pet Transport from Italy to India"
  overview: |
    Italy to India is one of the more procedurally complex pet transport routes for European owners. India requires a pre-approved import permit from its Animal Quarantine and Certification Service (AQCS), the health certificate must be endorsed by the Indian Embassy in Italy, and arrival must be through the specific port declared on the permit.

    Start at least twelve weeks before your travel date. The permit application, embassy endorsement, and official health certificate all have fixed processing times that you cannot compress.

    This route is manageable, but it rewards careful preparation. A pet transport agent is worth serious consideration if this is your first time.
  sections:
    - heading: "India AQCS import permit: how to apply"
      body: |
        India's Animal Quarantine and Certification Service (AQCS) at aqcsindia.gov.in issues import permits for all pet animals. You must apply before travel and specify your port of entry. The permit is port-specific. If you later change your arrival airport, you need a new permit.

        Applications typically take 4-6 weeks. Apply as early as possible. The permit will list the documentation your pet must arrive with.

    - heading: "Italian health certificate and embassy endorsement"
      body: |
        Your pet's health certificate must be issued by an official veterinarian (veterinario ufficiale) authorised by the Italian Ministry of Health. A regular private vet cannot issue the official certificate.

        After the official vet signs the certificate, take it to the Indian Embassy in Rome or the Indian Consulate in Milan for endorsement. Allow a week for this and book an appointment rather than turning up.

    - heading: "Arrival process in India"
      body: |
        On arrival, you will be directed to the AQCS animal health checkpoint at the declared port. Your pet and all documents will be inspected. A short arrival inspection period (24-48 hours) is normal. A longer stay (up to 7 days) is possible if documentation is queried.

        Your agent or someone receiving the pet in India should be aware of the AQCS process and be ready to respond quickly to any queries.

    - heading: "Airlines flying Italy to India with pets"
      body: |
        Most carriers fly Rome Fiumicino or Milan Malpensa to major Indian cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore). Direct Air India flights carry cargo pets. Emirates, British Airways, Lufthansa, and Qatar Airways all route via their hubs.

        Pets travel as manifested cargo on nearly all Italy-India routes. Cabin pet acceptance is limited. Air India is the main carrier that sometimes permits small pets in cabin on India-origin flights.

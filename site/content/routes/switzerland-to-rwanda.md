  sections:
    - heading: "BLV/FSVO export health certificate: Switzerland's non-EU process"
      body: |
        Switzerland is not a member of the EU and does not participate in the EU pet passport system. The correct export document for a companion animal leaving Switzerland for Rwanda is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by BLV/FSVO (Bundesamt für Lebensmittelsicherheit und Veterinärwesen / Office fédéral de la sécurité alimentaire et des affaires vétérinaires).

        Contact BLV/FSVO at blv.admin.ch or your cantonal veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement procedure. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the veterinary appointment and allow time for BLV/FSVO endorsement within that window.

        Confirm that the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific import requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Swiss exports to Africa can help ensure the certificate is correctly formatted for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "Routing from Zurich to Kigali"
      body: |
        Zurich International Airport (ZRH) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routing options apply for Switzerland-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: a connection from ZRH to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) on SWISS or another carrier, then Brussels Airlines' direct BRU-KGL service to Rwanda. Brussels Airlines operates this route as part of its established East Africa network. Confirm live animal acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from ZRH (or via FRA/AMS on SWISS/Lufthansa) to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on the ADD-KGL hop. Ethiopian is the dominant carrier for Africa-bound live animal cargo from Europe and its ADD hub is the main East Africa transit point. Geneva (GVA) is an alternative Swiss departure airport with similar connection options.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal imports. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        The permit is the authorising document for entry into Rwanda. Apply early and follow up to confirm your application is progressing. Present the RAB permit alongside the BLV/FSVO health certificate at the inspection desk at Kigali International Airport (KGL) on arrival. No quarantine applies for pets arriving with a valid permit and compliant documentation.

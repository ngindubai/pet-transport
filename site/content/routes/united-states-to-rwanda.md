  sections:
    - heading: "RAB permit: Rwanda's professional import system"
      body: |
        The Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) processes companion animal imports. Apply at rab.gov.rw with your pet's details: microchip number, vaccination history, breed, species, and intended travel date.

        The RAB is considered one of the more organised import authorities in East Africa. Its online presence is functional, its requirements are published, and it processes applications in a reasonable timeframe. Apply 8-10 weeks before travel to give yourself room for any queries and to ensure the permit arrives before your cargo booking is due.

        At Kigali International Airport (KGL), the RAB inspection team checks the permit and health certificate. Compliant arrivals from the USA proceed without quarantine.

    - heading: "Washington Dulles to Addis Ababa: Ethiopian's direct option"
      body: |
        Ethiopian Airlines operates a direct service between Washington Dulles (IAD) and Addis Ababa (ADD). For USA-to-Rwanda pet cargo, this is an efficient routing: fly directly IAD-ADD, then connect to Ethiopian's KGL service. Ethiopian Cargo handles live animals across its African network and the ADD-KGL segment is a regular short hop.

        From New York JFK, the routing via Brussels Airlines is practical. JFK-Brussels on a transatlantic carrier (Delta, United, or European partners), then Brussels Airlines BRU-KGL direct. Brussels Airlines has a strong BRU-Kigali operation as part of its East Africa network.

        KLM via Amsterdam (AMS) is a third option for SkyTeam-aligned routings. JFK-AMS on KLM or Delta, then connecting services from Amsterdam to KGL.

        Total transit from the US East Coast to Kigali runs 16-24 hours via Brussels, or slightly longer depending on the Addis connection. Discuss the transit time with your vet when assessing your pet's fitness.

    - heading: "USDA APHIS endorsement: what the process looks like"
      body: |
        USDA APHIS endorses health certificates for US pet exports. Your USDA-accredited vet issues the certificate after examining your pet. You then take it (or send it) to a USDA APHIS regional office for endorsement, or use the VSPS (Veterinary Services Process Streamlining) system online.

        For Rwanda, the certificate format covers Rwanda's import requirements. APHIS endorsement is approximately USD 38 per certificate. Regional office processing takes 1-3 business days; VSPS may be faster. The endorsed certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel.

        Plan the vet appointment and APHIS endorsement so the endorsed certificate reaches you before your departure date with time to spare.

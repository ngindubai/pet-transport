    - heading: "CFIA endorsement: what Canadian owners need to do"
      body: |
        The CFIA endorsement confirms the EU AHC is issued by a legitimate licensed Canadian vet. Without this endorsement, the VMVT border vet at Vilnius Airport will not accept the document. The CFIA endorsement is obtained by submitting the vet-signed AHC form to a CFIA office in your province.

        Processing times vary. In Toronto and Vancouver, turnaround is often 1-2 business days for standard service. In smaller centres, allow up to 4 days. CFIA also offers expedited processing in some regions. Check current processing times and fees at inspection.canada.ca before planning your schedule.

        Your vet must use the current EU AHC template. As of April 2026, updated EU AHC requirements apply. The CFIA's instruction page (inspection.canada.ca/en/animal-health/terrestrial-animals/exports/pets/eu-non-commercial) has current templates and completed-form examples.

faqs:
  - question: "Does Canada being listed mean no waiting period at all for Lithuania?"
    answer: "Canada being listed waives the titre test requirement. The 21-day post-vaccination waiting period still applies for newly administered rabies vaccinations. If your pet's current rabies vaccination is more than 21 days old and within its valid period, no additional wait is needed. If a new vaccination is given, wait 21 days before the Lithuanian entry date."
  - question: "What does the VMVT check at Vilnius Airport?"
    answer: "The VMVT (State Food and Veterinary Service of Lithuania) border vet at VNO checks the EU AHC (including the CFIA endorsement), scans the microchip, and confirms the vaccination date is current and at least 21 days old. Ensure the chip number in the AHC matches the chip in your pet. For correctly documented Canadian pets, the check is typically brief."
  - question: "Can I use an Air Canada international health certificate instead of the EU AHC?"
    answer: "No. Air Canada issues its own travel documentation for pets, but this does not replace the EU AHC required for Lithuanian border entry. The EU AHC is a specific EU-format document, completed by a licensed vet and endorsed by CFIA. Air Canada's documentation is for airline purposes only."
  - question: "Is there a direct flight from Toronto to Vilnius?"
    answer: "No direct Toronto-to-Vilnius service exists. LOT Polish Airlines via Warsaw (WAW) is the most direct option for cargo. The total journey is two legs: YYZ to WAW (approximately 8-9 hours), then WAW to VNO (approximately 1 hour). Total journey time: 13-16 hours including connection time."

        For up to five personal pets, the Dominican Republic requires no import permit at all. No titre test applies, since that requirement is reserved for countries DGAG classifies as high-rabies-risk, and Greece is not one of them.

Given the certificate's 15-day validity, book the vet appointment once your cargo or cabin space is confirmed, not before.

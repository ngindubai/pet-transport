  overview: |
    Moving a pet from the Philippines to India is a moderate-complexity route with direct flights available and no titre test requirement -- but the DAHD import clearance from India's Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying adds meaningful lead time to the process.

    Start the DAHD clearance application as early as possible. Processing typically takes 4-6 weeks, and you cannot travel without it. On the Philippines side, the BAI export permit and health certificate can be arranged concurrently, which helps compress the overall timeline.

    Philippine Airlines and Air India both fly direct from Manila (MNL) to Delhi (DEL), accepting small pets in cabin and larger pets as cargo. Delhi is the recommended entry point -- other Indian airports may have limited DAHD inspection capacity. Allow 8-12 weeks from starting the process to travel day. India's import rules can change, so verify current DAHD requirements at the start with a licensed pet relocation agent.

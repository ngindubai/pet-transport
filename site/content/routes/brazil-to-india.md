  sections:
    - heading: "Brazil to India vs Brazil to Malaysia: the key difference"
      body: |
        Both India and Malaysia have quarantine requirements for pets from Brazil, but the processes differ significantly.

        India has no titre test requirement and a 15-day facility quarantine. The minimum preparation timeline from vaccination to travel is around 6-8 weeks. The cost is lower because there is no titre test.

        Malaysia requires the RNATT titre test (3 months before entry) and a minimum 7-day quarantine. The minimum preparation timeline is around 5 months. The cost is higher due to the titre test.

        For owners choosing between destinations, India's process is considerably shorter and less expensive on the documentation side. Both destinations have a facility quarantine that the owner cannot skip or shorten.

    - heading: "AQCS entry ports: which station for your arrival city?"
      body: |
        India's AQCS has quarantine stations at five major entry airports: Delhi Indira Gandhi (DEL), Mumbai Chhatrapati Shivaji (BOM), Chennai (MAA), Kolkata Netaji Subhas (CCU), and Hyderabad Rajiv Gandhi (HYD).

        Your NOC application must be directed to the AQCS station at your specific arrival airport. If your cargo pet arrives at Mumbai, the Mumbai AQCS handles the quarantine. If you plan to arrive in Delhi, apply to the Delhi station.

        A local agent in India can navigate the AQCS application and advise on which entry city has the most available quarantine capacity at your travel time. For most Brazil-to-India moves, Mumbai and Delhi handle the majority of international pet arrivals.

faqs:
  - question: "Does my Brazilian pet need a titre test to enter India?"
    answer: "No. India does not require a titre test. You need a microchip, current rabies and core vaccines, antiparasitic treatment 2-7 days before travel, a MAPA health certificate, and an AQCS NOC lodged at least 15 days before arrival. Source: AQCS India (aqcsindia.gov.in), 2026."
  - question: "How do I apply for the India AQCS NOC from Brazil?"
    answer: "Apply through the AQCS portal at aqcsindia.gov.in to the station serving your entry airport. Include your pet's microchip number, vaccination records, and intended arrival date. Allow at least 15 days from application to approval, and submit well before that minimum."
  - question: "How much does the AQCS quarantine cost in India?"
    answer: "Quarantine fees are set by AQCS and are modest compared to some other countries. Current rates are around INR 1,500-5,000 for the 15-day period, depending on the species and the specific station. Confirm current fees with the relevant AQCS station before travel."

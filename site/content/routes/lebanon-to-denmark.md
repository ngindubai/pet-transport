content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Lebanon to Denmark"
  overview: "Twenty weeks is the realistic floor for this route, not a worst-case figure. Denmark is an EU member state, and under EU Regulation 2020/692 that means any pet arriving from an unlisted country such as Lebanon needs a titre test and a 90-day wait counted from the blood draw, enforced by Fodevarestyrelsen, the Danish Veterinary and Food Administration. Add Lebanese export processing time and the full window runs 20 to 26 weeks. Cats and dogs go through the same steps."

  sections:
    - heading: "What Fodevarestyrelsen actually requires"
      body: |
        Fodevarestyrelsen applies the standard EU unlisted-country sequence to every pet arriving from Lebanon: microchip before vaccination, a 30-day gap, a titre test at an EU-approved lab reaching at least 0.5 IU/ml, and a 90-day wait from the day blood was drawn. There's no discretion here. Documentation showing the steps in the wrong order, or a wait counted from the wrong date, gets rejected on arrival, and non-compliant pets can be detained or returned at the owner's cost.

        The final document, the EU-format health certificate, is only valid for 10 days, so it needs to be issued close to the actual travel date, ideally once the 90-day wait is nearly complete and a flight is booked.

    - heading: "Routing from Beirut to Copenhagen, and the Lebanese side"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul is generally the most consistent option into Scandinavia from Beirut, with Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt a strong alternative built specifically for Northern European connections. Qatar Airways via Doha and Emirates via Dubai both work as backups, though either adds hours to total transit and Gulf carriers occasionally suspend live animal cargo in the hottest summer weeks.

        On the Lebanese side, the export permit and health certificate come from the Ministry of Agriculture, and the certificate needs converting to EU format before it's any use in Denmark. Ministry endorsement takes 2 to 4 weeks in practice, so this step needs starting well before the 90-day wait ends, not after.

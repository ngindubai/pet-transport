content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Finland to Panama"
  overview: |
    Finland to Panama is a manageable route with one step that catches many owners out: the health certificate must be authenticated by the Panamanian Embassy or Consulate in Finland before departure. MIDA officers at Tocumen International Airport require the Embassy stamp. Without it, the documentation is incomplete.

    Aside from the consulate step, the requirements are: a microchip, a rabies vaccination at least 30 days old, current vaccinations, and an official health certificate from a Ruokavirasto-authorised vet. No titre test, no quarantine, no import permit for personal pets. Four to six weeks is enough preparation time for pets with current vaccinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "The consulate authentication requirement"
      body: |
        Panama requires that all official veterinary health certificates from abroad be authenticated by a Panamanian Embassy or Consulate in the country of origin. In Finland, the Panamanian Embassy is in Helsinki. Contact the Embassy in advance to confirm their current process and any fee.

        After your Ruokavirasto-authorised official vet issues the health certificate, take or send the original to the Embassy for authentication. Allow 2-3 working days. The health certificate is only valid for 10 days from the date of clinical examination, so book the vet appointment so the Embassy step can be completed and you still have time to travel.

        A pet transport agent experienced with Panama can manage the Embassy coordination on your behalf, which is worth considering if you are not based in Helsinki.

    - heading: "MIDA arrival and entry requirements"
      body: |
        MIDA (Ministerio de Desarrollo Agropecuario) handles all animal imports at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) in Panama City. On arrival, MIDA veterinarians inspect the authenticated health certificate and vaccination records. They register your pet in the MIDA system and charge a fee of approximately USD 20 (20 balboas).

        For dogs, required vaccinations are distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, and parvovirus in addition to rabies. For cats: viral rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia, and feline leukaemia. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. Pets under 4 months of age are not permitted entry.

    - heading: "Cargo from Helsinki to Panama City"
      body: |
        Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) does not have direct cargo connections to Tocumen International Airport (PTY). The most practical routing goes via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS). Finnair connects Helsinki to Amsterdam, and from there KLM or Copa Airlines provides cargo service to Panama City.

        Copa Airlines is headquartered at Tocumen and operates services from Amsterdam to PTY. KLM also serves the Amsterdam-Panama City route. Connect from Helsinki to AMS on Finnair, then book through a cargo agent for the long-haul segment. Confirm live animal acceptance and any breed restrictions before finalising your departure date.

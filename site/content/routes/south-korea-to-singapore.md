  sections:
    - heading: "Verify the AVS Category before planning"
      body: |
        AVS Singapore classifies countries based on rabies risk. Category A (rabies-free) gets simplified entry with a shorter quarantine. Category C triggers the full protocol: titre test, 6-month wait, 30-day quarantine. South Korea is likely Category C, but classifications can change.

        Go to nparks.gov.sg/avs and confirm South Korea's current category before committing to any timeline or spending any money on tests. If the classification has changed to something more favourable, your preparation time may be significantly shorter.
    - heading: "Managing the dual health certificate window"
      body: |
        The APQA health certificate must meet two simultaneous windows: issued within 10 days of travel from South Korea, AND within 7 days of Singapore arrival. The ICN-SIN flight is approximately 6 hours. With that flight time, both windows overlap cleanly if the APQA appointment is in the 7 days before departure. Plan accordingly.
    - heading: "Korean Air vs Singapore Airlines on ICN-SIN"
      body: |
        Korean Air and Singapore Airlines both operate direct daily ICN-SIN services. Korean Air brings APQA Incheon export familiarity; Singapore Airlines brings AVS Changi import expertise. On this short route, both are capable choices. Book based on availability and cargo space on your travel date.

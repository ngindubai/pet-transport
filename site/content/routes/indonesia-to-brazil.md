content:
  h1: "Pet Transport Indonesia to Brazil: BARANTAN Export Certificate and the VIGIAGRO Arrival Process"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Indonesia to Brazil is a very long-haul journey with a documentation-intensive export process at the Indonesian end. BARANTAN (the Agriculture Quarantine Agency) issues the export health certificate and provides clearance at Jakarta Airport. On arrival in Brazil, VIGIAGRO inspects the documentation but does not impose routine quarantine on compliant pets. Emirates via Dubai is the most practical routing option, though the total journey duration from Jakarta to São Paulo will be significant.

        Brazil is a more accessible destination from Indonesia than either Malaysia or India, in terms of arrival requirements: no import permit and no quarantine. However, the sheer distance makes it one of the longer pet transport routes globally.

    - heading: "Airline comparison: Indonesia to Brazil"
      body: |
        | Airline | Hub | Route | Notes |
        |---|---|---|---|
        | Emirates | DXB | CGK-DXB-GRU | SkyCargo. Dubai has South America cargo connections |
        | Singapore Airlines | SIN | CGK-SIN then onward to GRU | SQ Cargo handles live animals |
        | Air France | CDG | CGK via hub to CDG-GRU | AFKLMP Cargo handles live animals |

        Emirates via Dubai is the most practical routing for Indonesia-to-Brazil pet cargo, given Dubai's range of South America connections. This is an extremely long journey: ensure the travel crate dimensions, water provisions, and welfare arrangements are appropriate for an extended multi-leg trip.

    - heading: "BARANTAN export process and VIGIAGRO arrival"
      body: |
        Exporting a pet from Indonesia requires obtaining a BARANTAN export permit and a BARANTAN-issued health certificate before departure. BARANTAN inspectors at Jakarta Soekarno-Hatta (CGK) carry out a final check and provide export clearance on the day of travel.

        On arrival in Brazil at São Paulo Guarulhos (GRU) or Rio de Janeiro Galeão (GIG), VIGIAGRO inspects the export health certificate, microchip, and vaccination records. For compliant pets from Indonesia with complete BARANTAN documentation, this is a documentary inspection rather than a quarantine hold.

        Contact BARANTAN (pertanian.go.id) for the current export permit process and the health certificate format required for pets leaving Indonesia for Brazil.

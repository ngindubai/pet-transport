content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from the United Kingdom to Belize"
  overview: |
    BZ$50. That's the entire BAHA import permit fee for up to 10 cats and dogs travelling together, valid 90 days from issue. Compared with what a UK pet needs to enter most countries, Belize's paperwork is genuinely light.

    The two things that actually need managing are timing, not volume. The health certificate has to be issued within 14 days of arrival, tighter than the UK's own outbound rules elsewhere, and BAHA needs 2 days' notice of your exact flight before your pet lands. Miss either window and a straightforward move gets complicated fast.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why 90 days on the permit doesn't mean you have 90 days of slack"
      body: |
        The BAHA import permit itself runs for 90 days once issued, plenty of runway on paper. The problem is that the health certificate behind it only lasts 14 days, and the rabies vaccination has to sit at least 30 days before your pet actually touches down in Belize, not before you leave London.

        Work the dates in this order: confirm the vaccination timing first, then book flights, then apply for the permit, then get the certificate signed last, close to departure. Reverse that order and you risk a certificate that's expired before your pet's even at the airport.

    - heading: "Miami is the connection, and it's a short second leg"
      body: |
        British Airways flies London Heathrow to Miami, the natural first leg for this route, booked as cargo through IAG Cargo since BA carries no animal in the cabin on any of its services. From Miami, American Airlines covers Belize City in under two hours, one of several new direct routes American has added out of Belize through 2025 and 2026.

        It's worth flagging that American's cargo pet programme has had gaps in availability in the past, so confirm space on that second leg specifically before you commit to a date. A UK vet appointment timed around a flight that later falls through is the single most common way this route gets delayed.

    This is the one Russia route in this guide where the flight isn't the hard part. Aeroflot restarted its Sheremetyevo-Dubai service on 1 June 2026, moving to twice a day from 1 July. FlyDubai runs Dubai to Vnukovo, also stepped up to two flights daily through the summer. Emirates covers Dubai to Domodedovo directly. The UAE's usual export paperwork is written for EU or US destinations, so the certificate has to be reformatted for Rosselkhoznadzor's model, and that's not a form you'll find pre-printed at every clinic.

    - heading: "The Russian import side, and the certificate that needs confirming"

        Russia doesn't ask for a titre test from any origin country, the UAE included, which puts this route ahead of most non-EU destinations for paperwork weight. What it does need precisely: a rabies vaccination at least 20 days old and never more than 12 months old, plus the full core vaccine set for dogs or cats, and a matching export certificate.

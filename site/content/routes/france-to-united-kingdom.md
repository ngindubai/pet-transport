content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from France to United Kingdom"
  overview: |
    France and the UK share a border, but since Brexit they no longer share the same pet travel rules. The EU Pet Passport your vet issued in France is not accepted at UK entry points. What you need now is an Animal Health Certificate, issued by an official veterinarian, within 10 days of travel.

    For most dogs and cats the preparation takes three to six weeks. If your pet is not yet vaccinated against rabies, allow at least 21 days after the vaccination before travel. If your dog needs a tapeworm treatment, that has to be done inside a strict 24-120 hour window before the UK arrival time, so timing matters.

    The route itself is short. Direct flights from Paris Charles de Gaulle, Lyon, and Nice to London Heathrow, London Gatwick, or Manchester are straightforward once the paperwork is right.

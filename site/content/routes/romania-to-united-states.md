    - heading: "ANSVSA and the Romanian health certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        ANSVSA (Autoritatea Nationala Sanitara Veterinara si pentru Siguranta Alimentelor) is Romania's national veterinary and food safety authority. The health certificate for US import must be issued by an ANSVSA-authorised official vet and endorsed by ANSVSA.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication of the ANSVSA-endorsed certificate is required at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Romania.

    - heading: "Routing from Bucharest to the USA"
      body: |
        Bucharest Henri Coanda International Airport (OTP) has no direct transatlantic US service. The main cargo routing options are via Frankfurt (Lufthansa), Amsterdam (KLM), and Vienna (Austrian Airlines), all of which have extensive US connections including JFK, LAX, ORD, MIA, and IAD.

        TAROM (Romania's national carrier) connects Bucharest to Frankfurt, Amsterdam, and other European hubs. Confirm live animal acceptance and crate requirements with your chosen carrier before booking.

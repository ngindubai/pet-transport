  sections:
    - heading: "FAVN titre test and Panama's SITERPA timing"
      body: |
        EU Implementing Regulation 2026/636 classifies Panama as an unlisted third country, making the FAVN titre test mandatory for Finland. Blood must be drawn at least 30 days after rabies vaccination and must show at least 0.5 IU/ml at an EU-approved laboratory. The 90-day waiting period runs from the blood draw date; Ruokavirasto grants no exemptions.

        Panama's export health certificate is issued through the SITERPA portal by the veterinarian and countersigned by DECA within MIDA. It is valid for 72 hours from the DECA countersignature. Schedule the appointment so DECA countersigns within 72 hours of your actual departure from Tocumen (PTY). The EU-format health certificate for Finland is then valid for 10 days from the MIDA endorsement date.

    - heading: "Tapeworm treatment and the HEL entry requirement"
      body: |
        All dogs entering Finland must receive tapeworm treatment using praziquantel between 1 and 5 days before arrival at Helsinki-Vantaa. The vet records the product, dose, and date in the health certificate. Coordinate the treatment timing carefully against your flight schedule; the window is measured to actual arrival at HEL.

        Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) is the only Finnish airport with a veterinary Border Control Post for non-EU arrivals. No other Finnish airport will work. Iberia's PTY-MAD service followed by a Finnair connection to HEL is the most practical routing; KLM via Amsterdam is an alternative. Cats are not subject to the tapeworm requirement.

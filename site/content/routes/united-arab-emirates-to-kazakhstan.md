  sections:
    - heading: "MOCCAE's export process out of Dubai or Abu Dhabi"
      body: |
        Every pet leaving the UAE needs a MOCCAE export health certificate, applied for online through the MOCCAE portal with a UAE Pass login, followed by an in-person clinic exam in Dubai or Abu Dhabi. A vet there checks vaccination and general health before endorsing the certificate, and a final inspection happens at the airport right before departure. The certificate lasts 30 days.

    - heading: "Why the KVCS permit deserves extra lead time"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan's Committee for Veterinary Control and Supervision issues the import permit required before a pet can enter the country. Unlike Georgia's simple notification system or Rwanda's comparatively smooth online process, KVCS's timeline can vary, and a registered Kazakhstani import agent often makes the difference between a permit that arrives on schedule and one that doesn't. No titre test is required for a UAE-origin pet, and there's no quarantine for compliant arrivals, but building in extra buffer time around the permit stage is the sensible approach.

    - heading: "Two direct carriers, two Kazakhstani hubs"
      body: |
        FlyDubai flies non-stop into Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest city and commercial centre. Air Astana, the national carrier, covers both Almaty and the capital, Astana, direct from Dubai, with an established live animal handling process at each end. Whichever city is your actual destination, a direct flight is available rather than a routing through a third country.

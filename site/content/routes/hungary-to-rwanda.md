  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Budapest to Kigali"
      body: |
        Budapest Ferenc Liszt International Airport (BUD) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routings suit Hungary-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: Lufthansa, Austrian Airlines, or another full-service carrier connects BUD to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This gives a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Frankfurt and Addis Ababa: Lufthansa connects BUD to Frankfurt (FRA), then Ethiopian or Lufthansa Cargo to ADD, and Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-KGL. Frankfurt is the most accessible major European cargo hub from Budapest. Note that Wizz Air, a major carrier at BUD, does not accept pets; use full-service carriers for the connecting leg.

    - heading: "NÉBIH export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Hungarian EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by NÉBIH (Nemzeti Élelmiszerlánc-biztonsági Hivatal - National Food Chain Safety Office) at nebih.gov.hu.

        Contact NÉBIH or your regional veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for NÉBIH endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Hungarian exports to Africa can verify the correct format for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. On arrival at Kigali International Airport, RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the NÉBIH health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

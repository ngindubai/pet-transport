  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Vilnius to Kigali"
      body: |
        Vilnius Airport (VNO) has connections to Warsaw (WAW) and other European hubs via LOT Polish Airlines and airBaltic. From Warsaw, LOT or other carriers connect to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) for Brussels Airlines' direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This is the most direct multi-leg routing available from Lithuania to Kigali.

        Alternatively, VNO can connect via Riga (RIX) on airBaltic to Amsterdam (AMS) or Frankfurt (FRA), then Ethiopian Cargo via ADD to KGL. FRA is the most commonly used European cargo hub for Central and Eastern European-to-Africa routes.

        A pet transport agent experienced in Baltic or Central Eastern European exports is recommended. They will identify which routing offers reliable live animal cargo acceptance for your travel dates and manage the multi-carrier documentation chain.

    - heading: "VMVT export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Lithuanian EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by the Valstybinė maisto ir veterinarijos tarnyba (VMVT - State Food and Veterinary Service) at vmvt.lt.

        Contact VMVT to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the appointment and allow time for VMVT endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific requirements. Your pet transport agent can help verify this for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. On arrival at Kigali International Airport, RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the VMVT health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

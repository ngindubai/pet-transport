  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Stockholm Arlanda to Kigali"
      body: |
        Stockholm Arlanda Airport (ARN) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routings suit Sweden-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: SAS or another carrier connects ARN to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This is a two-leg journey with a single European hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from ARN (or via FRA/AMS on SAS/Lufthansa) to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-KGL. Ethiopian Cargo has the widest live animal programme in Africa and ADD-KGL is a short East African hop. Gothenburg Landvetter (GOT) is an alternative departure airport with similar connection options for Swedish pet owners in western Sweden.

    - heading: "Jordbruksverket export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Swedish EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the correct document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by Jordbruksverket (Swedish Board of Agriculture) at jordbruksverket.se.

        Contact Jordbruksverket or your regional veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement procedure for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow time for Jordbruksverket endorsement within that window.

        Your regular private vet handles vaccinations and microchipping. The official export health certificate must be issued by a Jordbruksverket-authorised official vet and endorsed accordingly.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and intended travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm your application is progressing. On arrival at Kigali International Airport (KGL), RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the Jordbruksverket health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Lisbon to Kigali"
      body: |
        Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport (LIS) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routings are practical for Portugal-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: TAP Air Portugal or another carrier connects LIS to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. Brussels Airlines has a well-established Kigali route and BRU is a practical transit point. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from LIS (or via a European hub) to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-KGL. Ethiopian Cargo has the widest live animal programme in Africa and ADD-KGL is a short East African connection. Porto (OPO) is an alternative Portuguese departure point with similar hub connection options.

    - heading: "DGAV export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Portuguese EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only and will not be accepted at Kigali for Rwanda entry. The correct document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by DGAV (Direção-Geral de Alimentação e Veterinária) at dgav.gov.pt.

        Contact DGAV or your regional veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement procedure. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow time for DGAV endorsement within that 10-day window. Allow 2-3 working days for endorsement after the vet appointment.

        Your regular private vet handles vaccinations, microchipping, and routine checks, but the export health certificate must be issued and endorsed through the official DGAV process.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up after submission to confirm your application is in progress. Present the RAB permit alongside the DGAV health certificate at the inspection desk at Kigali International Airport on arrival. No quarantine applies for pets with a valid permit and compliant documentation.

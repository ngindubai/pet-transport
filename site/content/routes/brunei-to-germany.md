content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Brunei to Germany"
  overview: |
    No airline flies a pet cargo booking straight out of Bandar Seri Begawan. That single fact shapes everything else on this route: before the EU paperwork even starts, the pet needs a road or regional transfer to Singapore, since Royal Brunei Airlines has never run a standard live animal cargo service for privately owned dogs and cats.

    Once that transfer is arranged, the destination side is a familiar EU sequence. Brunei sits outside the bloc's list of low-risk third countries, so a rabies titre test and a fixed 90-day wait from the blood draw both apply, on top of the Department of Agriculture and Agrifood's own export sign-off.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why this route starts in Singapore, not Brunei"
      body: |
        Brunei International Airport has limited long-haul cargo infrastructure, and DAA's export process assumes the pet will clear customs at whichever airport actually flies the transfer. Most owners route through Singapore Changi by road across the border, a trip of a few hours, though Kuala Lumpur is a workable second option using Malaysia Airlines Cargo for the domestic leg.

        Once in Singapore, Lufthansa Cargo runs a direct service to Frankfurt, with the airport's dedicated Animal Lounge handling live cargo inspection on arrival. That facility matters on a route like this one, where the pet has already had a long day before the transatlantic sector even begins.

    - heading: "The EU's titre test sequence, and what it means for the calendar"
      body: |
        Because Brunei isn't on the EU's low-risk list, Germany applies the standard unlisted-country protocol under Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2026/131 and Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/636: microchip before vaccination, a 30-day gap before the titre test blood draw, a passing result of at least 0.5 IU/ml, then a 90-day wait counted from the draw date itself.

        Add the Singapore transfer logistics and DAA's own processing time, and a realistic floor for this route sits at 22 to 28 weeks. Start the vaccination and titre test steps as early as possible; everything else can be arranged in parallel once that clock is running.

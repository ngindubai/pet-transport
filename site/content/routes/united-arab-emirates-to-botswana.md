  overview: |
    A Botswana-origin dog needs a rabies titre test to clear MOCCAE on the way into the UAE. Coming back the other way, a UAE-origin pet needs none at all. Botswana's Department of Veterinary Services doesn't ask for a blood test from any country of origin, which strips out the single biggest timeline driver most owners expect on a route like this.

    What replaces it is a document that rarely shows up on a Gulf owner's radar: a signed attestation confirming no animal disease movement restriction currently applies in the emirate the pet is leaving from. Add Emirates SkyCargo's recently expanded Johannesburg schedule and a MOCCAE export permit that runs on its own 30-day clock, and seven to ten weeks is a realistic planning window.

  sections:
    - heading: "No titre test, but an attestation Gulf owners rarely expect"
      body: |
        Botswana sorts import requirements the same way regardless of origin: a current rabies vaccination at least 30 days old on arrival, no titre test, no fixed post-vaccination wait beyond that. For a UAE-origin pet, that's considerably lighter than the reverse journey, where MOCCAE requires a titre test from any Botswana-origin animal since Botswana isn't on the UAE's own low-risk list.

        DVS's own paperwork adds one thing most other import checklists this site tracks don't: written confirmation that no animal disease control zone currently applies where the pet is departing from. It isn't a difficult document to obtain, but it's easy to miss if nobody flags it before the DVS import permit application goes in, since it isn't something MOCCAE's own export process asks for as standard.

    - heading: "Dubai to Gaborone by way of Johannesburg"
      body: |
        Emirates SkyCargo added a fourth daily Dubai-Johannesburg flight on 1 July 2026, meaningful extra belly-hold capacity for the long-haul leg of this route. From Johannesburg, Airlink or Air Botswana finishes the journey into Sir Seretse Khama International Airport, the entry point consistently used for pet imports into Botswana.

        As with every UAE departure, the pet travels as manifest cargo through an IATA-certified pet shipping agent rather than a standard cargo booking. Confirm both legs align before locking in a date, since a pet transferring between a long-haul carrier and a regional one needs both bookings to work together, not just the first.

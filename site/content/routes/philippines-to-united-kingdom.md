    - heading: "BAI NVQS: the Philippines export authority and what it requires"
      body: |
        The Bureau of Animal Industry (BAI) and its National Veterinary Quarantine Services (NVQS) are the Philippines authority for live animal exports. The NVQS-endorsed health certificate is the core export document.

        Your vet must be BAI NVQS-accredited to issue the certificate. The certificate must be endorsed by the NVQS office before travel. The NVQS endorsement process typically takes 1-3 working days, but allow more time if you are not using an agent familiar with the Manila NVQS office.

        The certificate must be in GB-compatible format and cover: microchip number and implantation date, full vaccination history including titre test result, and a statement that the pet was examined and found healthy within 10 days of the planned GB arrival date.

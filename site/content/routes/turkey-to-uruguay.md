content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Turkey to Uruguay"
  overview: |
    Istanbul and Montevideo aren't connected by a single non-stop flight, so this route runs through a partner hub, either Sao Paulo or Panama City, adding real hours to the journey. The regulatory side is lighter than the flight: no titre test, no quarantine, just an MGAP import permit and, for dogs, a tightly timed parasite treatment.

    That combination, simple paperwork against a genuinely long flight path, is the defining feature of this corridor. Get the permit and the treatment timing right, and the main remaining risk is logistical rather than regulatory.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why this route has no non-stop option"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines doesn't fly Istanbul to Montevideo direct. The realistic routings run via Sao Paulo Guarulhos (GRU) or Panama City Tocumen (PTY), each adding a connection and pushing total travel time past 21 hours. For a pet in cargo, that means confirming the second carrier's own live-animal handling separately, since an interline transfer doesn't automatically guarantee the same standard of care as the originating airline.

        LATAM Cargo, which runs its own strong Montevideo network out of Sao Paulo, is worth checking as an alternative if a Turkish Airlines connection doesn't line up with a suitable transfer window.

    - heading: "MGAP's import permit comes before the flight, not after"
      body: |
        Uruguay's Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca issues an import permit that sets out the conditions a pet must meet, and it's meant to be obtained before travel is booked, not arranged in parallel. There's no titre test and no quarantine on arrival at Carrasco International, a genuinely light regulatory load once the permit is in hand.

    - heading: "The dog-only parasite treatment, timed to arrival"
      body: |
        Dogs need a praziquantel-based treatment against Echinococcus, given no less than 72 hours and no more than 30 days before arrival in Uruguay. Given the long connecting flight time on this route, count the window against the Montevideo arrival date, not the Istanbul departure, or the treatment can fall outside the compliant range without anyone realising until check-in.

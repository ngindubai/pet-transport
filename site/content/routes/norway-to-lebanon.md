  sections:
    - heading: "What Lebanon requires at the border"
      body: |
        Lebanon's veterinary import requirements are administered by the Ministry of Agriculture. Border officers inspect documentation at Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY) on arrival.

        The core requirements are a valid rabies vaccination administered at least 30 days before entry, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Note the 30-day window: this is longer than the 21-day minimum that applies in many other countries. If your pet was vaccinated less than 30 days before your planned departure, you cannot enter Lebanon until the 30-day period has passed.

        Lebanon does not require a titre test for EEA-origin pets. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. For 1-2 personal companion animals, no import permit is required.

    - heading: "The Norwegian health certificate process"
      body: |
        Norway's health certificate for non-EU destinations must be signed by a Mattilsynet-authorised official vet and then endorsed by the Mattilsynet regional office. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Time the vet examination to fall within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, submit the certificate to the Mattilsynet regional office and allow 1-2 working days for endorsement. If travelling on a Monday, book the vet appointment no later than the Wednesday before.

        Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the official health certificate must come from a Mattilsynet-authorised official vet. Check mattilsynet.no for authorised vets in your region.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Oslo to Beirut"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Oslo (OSL) to Beirut (BEY). Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul (IST) is the main established route. Turkish Airlines operates Oslo-Istanbul regularly, and IST to BEY is a well-served route. Book through an approved cargo agent and confirm pet acceptance on both legs.

        During summer months (June to September), Turkish Airlines applies temperature embargoes on certain connections. If your travel date falls in this period, confirm acceptance of your pet's breed and size early. Brachycephalic breed restrictions apply year-round.

        For alternative routing, some owners use a connection via London Heathrow (LHR) to Middle East Airlines (MEA) services. This adds complexity and an extra handling step, but may be worth exploring if Turkish Airlines embargoes are an issue. Coordinate the full chain with your cargo agent before committing to any travel date.

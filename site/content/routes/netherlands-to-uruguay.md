  sections:
    - heading: "Uruguay's requirements for pets from the Netherlands"
      body: |
        Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca, gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) governs pet imports. The requirements are: MGAP import permit (apply well in advance, allow 6-10 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; rabies vaccination administered between 30 days and 12 months before arrival; and an NVWA-endorsed health certificate issued within 10 days of travel.

        For dogs, one additional requirement applies: Praziquantel treatment against Echinococcus administered between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival. A veterinarian records the treatment. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. MGAP inspects pets on arrival at Carrasco International Airport (MVD).

    - heading: "The MGAP import permit"
      body: |
        The MGAP import permit is the critical path item for this route. Apply to MGAP (gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) with your pet's details and proposed travel date. Processing takes 6-10 weeks. Do not book cargo or finalise dates until the permit is confirmed.

        The permit must accompany the health certificate on arrival at MVD.

    - heading: "Praziquantel treatment for dogs: timing from Uruguay arrival"
      body: |
        Dogs entering Uruguay must be treated with Praziquantel against Echinococcus between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival at MVD. Calculate the window from your scheduled arrival time in Montevideo, not from your departure time from Amsterdam. A treatment given 71 hours before arrival is outside the window and non-compliant.

        Your veterinarian in the Netherlands records the treatment: product name, dosage, date and time. Cats do not require the treatment.

    - heading: "Routing from Amsterdam to Montevideo"
      body: |
        Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) has good South American cargo connectivity. KLM operates direct services to Buenos Aires Ezeiza (EZE) and Sao Paulo Guarulhos (GRU), from where LATAM Airlines connects to Montevideo Carrasco (MVD).

        Iberia via Madrid (MAD) is another strong option, with Iberia's direct MAD-MVD service. Air France via Paris CDG also has South American transatlantic routes. Confirm live animal cargo availability on the specific flight before booking. The Iberia MAD-MVD direct service is generally among the most efficient for cargo into Montevideo.

    - heading: "NVWA export endorsement from the Netherlands"
      body: |
        The NVWA (Nederlandse Voedsel- en Warenautoriteit, nvwa.nl) endorses official export health certificates for pets leaving the Netherlands for non-EU destinations. An official veterinarian prepares the certificate referencing the MGAP permit, microchip, vaccination details, and Praziquantel treatment record.

        Allow three to five business days for the NVWA endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel.

    answer: "Yes. The Praziquantel treatment requirement applies to dogs only. Cats from the Netherlands need the MGAP import permit, microchip, current rabies vaccination, and the NVWA-endorsed health certificate."

    answer: "KLM operates direct flights from AMS to EZE (Buenos Aires) and GRU (Sao Paulo), which then connect to MVD via LATAM. There is no AMS-MVD direct service as of 2026. The Iberia routing via MAD with a direct MAD-MVD service offers a single-transfer option."
  - question: "What happens at MGAP inspection on arrival at MVD?"
    answer: "MGAP inspectors at Carrasco Airport check the import permit, NVWA-endorsed health certificate, vaccination records, Praziquantel treatment record for dogs, and scan the microchip. For compliant pets, the inspection is routine and the animal is released after clearance."

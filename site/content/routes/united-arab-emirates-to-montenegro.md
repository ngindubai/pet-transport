  h1: "Pet Transport from United Arab Emirates to Montenegro"
  overview: |
    MOCCAE puts Montenegro on its low-risk rabies list, the same roughly 50-country group that lets a Montenegro-origin pet skip the titre test entering the UAE. Coming the other way, Montenegro's own veterinary authority applies no such courtesy.

    AFSVPA wants a FAVN or RNATT titre test drawn at least 30 days after vaccination, then a further wait of more than 3 calendar months before the pet can travel. That single fact turns what MOCCAE's own classification might suggest is a light route into one of the longer timelines on this site for a UAE departure. Nothing about Montenegro's welcome for a UAE-vaccinated pet mirrors the welcome the UAE extends to a Montenegrin one.

  sections:
    - heading: "Two regulators, two opposite judgements about the same country"
      body: |
        MOCCAE runs its own low-risk list, and Montenegro is on it: no titre test, no fixed wait, just a standard microchip, vaccination and import permit for a pet entering the UAE from Montenegro. AFSVPA, Montenegro's own authority, has published no equivalent exemption for UAE-origin arrivals in any guidance this site could locate.

        That's not a contradiction so much as two independent classification systems reaching different conclusions about the same rabies risk. Neither one defers to the other. Plan this route around AFSVPA's requirement, not MOCCAE's.

    - heading: "The 3-month wait sets the whole calendar"
      body: |
        Everything else on this route, the MOCCAE export permit, the flight booking, the health certificate, fits into a few weeks. The titre test result and its mandatory 3-calendar-month wait do not. Start the vaccination and blood draw the moment a Montenegro move is confirmed, since that clock can't be compressed by paying for faster service anywhere else in the process.

        Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is the more direct routing once the wait has passed, and its well-publicised refusal to carry pets, familiar from the UK-Montenegro route on this site, doesn't apply to Gulf-origin bookings. Lufthansa via Frankfurt or Munich is the alternative if Istanbul capacity or timing doesn't line up.

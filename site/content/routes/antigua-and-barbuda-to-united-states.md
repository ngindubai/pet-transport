content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Antigua and Barbuda to the United States"
  overview: |
    The CDC doesn't count Antigua and Barbuda among the 100-plus countries it treats as high risk for dog rabies. That single omission strips out most of the friction this site flags on other routes into the US: no titre test, no CDC-registered facility airport requirement, no restriction on which airport the flight lands at.

    What's left is close to a formality. A dog needs a free CDC Dog Import Form and, ideally, a current rabies vaccination, though CDC itself doesn't insist on one. A cat needs even less. Antigua and Barbuda's own export side is a single 7-day health certificate from a local vet.

    Two nonstop options exist, American Airlines from Miami and Caribbean Airlines from New York JFK, and either can have a pet moving within 3 to 5 weeks.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why this route sits at the light end of the scale"
      body: |
        Most of the routes on this site into the US involve at least checking whether the origin country triggers the CDC's high-risk pathway, with its microchip-before-vaccine sequence, titre test and restricted list of arrival airports. Antigua and Barbuda doesn't trigger any of it. The dog can arrive at any US airport, seaport or land crossing.

        Cats have it easier still. There's no federal rabies vaccination requirement, no titre test and no import form for cats, whatever their country of origin.

    - heading: "Two nonstop routes, cabin or cargo"
      body: |
        American Airlines flies Miami to Antigua nonstop as AA2579/AA2580, about 3 hours 23 minutes. Because the route doesn't touch the UK or Ireland, a small pet under 9kg including carrier can fly in the cabin, a cheaper and simpler option than cargo for owners with a cat or a small dog.

        Caribbean Airlines runs a nonstop New York JFK to Antigua service and handles pet cargo through a dedicated Antigua desk. Its cargo service into Antigua was briefly suspended in 2025 during a change of ground handler and has since resumed, so confirm current capacity when booking rather than assuming it from an older quote.

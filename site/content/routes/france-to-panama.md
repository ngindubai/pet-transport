  sections:
    - heading: "MIDA requirements: what Panama checks on arrival"
      body: |
        Panama's Ministry of Agricultural Development (MIDA) handles pet import controls at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) in Panama City. MIDA veterinarians check your pet's microchip, vaccination records, health certificate, and antiparasitic treatment documentation on arrival. The registration fee is approximately USD 20 per pet, paid in cash at the MIDA airport office.

        Your pet must be over 4 months old to enter Panama. Pets younger than this are not admitted, regardless of vaccination status. The rabies vaccination must have been given at least 30 days before entry and must be current on arrival. Dogs need current distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, and parvovirus vaccinations. Cats need rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia, and feline leukaemia on record.

        Panama does not require a quarantine period for compliant arrivals, and no import permit is needed for personal companion animals. The antiparasitic treatment must be carried out within 30 days of departure and documented with the product name, dose, and application date.

    - heading: "The consulate authentication step: do not underestimate it"
      body: |
        The process for France is one step longer than many EU-to-Latin America routes because Panama requires the health certificate to be authenticated by a Panamanian diplomatic mission in the origin country.

        The sequence is: veterinaire officiel examination and issue of AHC, DGAL (or DDSPP) endorsement, then submission to the Embassy of Panama in Paris for authentication. The embassy charges a consulate fee (confirm the current amount when you book your appointment) and typically takes 2-3 working days to process the stamp.

        Given that the AHC is valid for 10 days from issue, the timing is tight. If your cargo departure is on a Friday, your vet appointment should be no later than the previous Monday, so the DGAL endorsement and embassy appointment can complete by Wednesday or Thursday. Book the embassy appointment before finalising your cargo date so you are not working backward under pressure.

  sections:
    - heading: "Ethiopian Airlines HEL-ADD: Finland's direct link to East Africa"
      body: |
        Ethiopian Airlines operates a direct service between Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) and Addis Ababa (ADD). This makes Ethiopia's main hub directly accessible from Finland without a European transit stop. From ADD, Ethiopian Cargo operates regular connections to Kigali International Airport (KGL), a short East African hop.

        Contact Ethiopian Cargo to confirm live animal acceptance on the HEL-ADD service, applicable crate sizes, and booking requirements. Ethiopian Cargo has the most extensive live animal programme in Africa, and the HEL-ADD-KGL routing is among the most efficient available from Finland.

        Brussels Airlines via Brussels Zaventem (BRU) is an alternative: Finnair connects HEL to BRU, then Brussels Airlines operates BRU-KGL directly. This is a practical option if Ethiopian Cargo is not available on your preferred dates.

    - heading: "Ruokavirasto export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Finnish EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by Ruokavirasto (Finnish Food Authority) at ruokavirasto.fi.

        Contact Ruokavirasto to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow time for Ruokavirasto endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific import requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Finnish exports to Africa can help verify the correct format for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. Present the RAB permit alongside the Ruokavirasto health certificate at Kigali International Airport on arrival. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

  h1: "Pet Transport from Denmark to Indonesia"
  overview: |
    Taking a pet from Denmark to Indonesia requires a BARANTAN import permit, a DVFA-endorsed health certificate, and a 7-day mandatory quarantine on arrival in Jakarta. The import permit process is the main timing constraint: allow at least 60 days for processing, which puts the minimum preparation window at around 10-12 weeks.

    Entry for air-freighted pets is restricted to Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK). If your final destination in Indonesia is outside Jakarta, plan for a domestic connection after your pet clears quarantine.

    This route is well within reach for families who plan ahead. Indonesia's animal import system, while bureaucratic, follows a clear process once the permit is issued.
  sections:
    - heading: "BARANTAN import requirements: what Indonesia demands"
      body: |
        Indonesia's Agricultural Quarantine Agency (BARANTAN), part of the Ministry of Agriculture, controls all animal imports. The core requirements for dogs and cats from Denmark are:

        Microchip: ISO 11784/11785, 15-digit transponder. Must be recorded in all documentation.

        Rabies vaccination: current and valid. Full vaccination history required. Administered after microchipping.

        Import permit: mandatory. Issued by BARANTAN. Apply at least 60 days before travel -- processing varies, and some applications take longer than others. The permit specifies the approved entry port (Jakarta CGK) and conditions your pet must meet.

        Health certificate: official DVFA-endorsed certificate covering microchip, vaccinations, and health status. Must be issued within 14 days of travel.

        Quarantine: 7 mandatory days at the BARANTAN quarantine station at Soekarno-Hatta Airport on arrival. Quarantine fees are charged to the owner.

        Entry port: Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK) in Jakarta is the designated entry point for pet cargo arriving in Indonesia from Europe. Routings that arrive at other Indonesian airports are not accepted.
    - heading: "Planning your routing and cargo connection"
      body: |
        Copenhagen (CPH) to Jakarta (CGK) has no direct air service. All pet cargo will travel via at least one hub.

        Lufthansa via Frankfurt (FRA) is a reliable and commonly used European option. Singapore Airlines via Singapore (SIN) is another strong choice -- the final leg from Singapore to Jakarta is only about 90 minutes, keeping your pet's total transit time down.

        Emirates via Dubai and Qatar Airways via Doha are also frequently used for this corridor. All operate temperature-controlled cargo holds.

        Book cargo space at least 3 weeks in advance. Notify your cargo agent that entry must be through Jakarta CGK -- this affects routing choices.
faqs:
  - question: "How long does the BARANTAN import permit take?"
    answer: "BARANTAN import permits typically take 4-8 weeks to process. Apply at least 60 days before your planned travel date -- earlier if possible. The permit is issued by the Indonesian Ministry of Agriculture's Agricultural Quarantine Agency. Some pet transport agents who regularly handle Indonesia-bound pets have established processes for submitting applications efficiently."
  - question: "Can my pet fly directly to Bali or another Indonesian city?"
    answer: "No. For international pet imports, entry must be through Jakarta Soekarno-Hatta Airport (CGK). After your pet completes the 7-day quarantine in Jakarta, you can then arrange a domestic flight to Bali (DPS), Surabaya (SUB), or wherever your final destination is within Indonesia."
  - question: "Is quarantine mandatory in Indonesia even for vaccinated pets?"
    answer: "Yes. The 7-day quarantine at the BARANTAN station at CGK applies to all incoming dogs and cats regardless of vaccination status or country of origin. This is a biosecurity measure rather than a penalty for incomplete documentation. Factor quarantine facility fees into your overall transport budget."
  - question: "Which airlines handle pet cargo from Copenhagen to Jakarta?"
    answer: "No airline offers direct pet cargo from CPH to CGK. The main hub routing options are: Lufthansa via Frankfurt, Emirates via Dubai, Qatar Airways via Doha, Singapore Airlines via Singapore, and KLM via Amsterdam. Singapore Airlines via SIN gives the shortest final leg into Jakarta."
  - question: "Does Denmark's EU status simplify the Indonesian import process?"
    answer: "EU membership does not significantly simplify Indonesia's pet import requirements. BARANTAN applies its own classification system independent of EU pet travel rules. The import permit requirement and quarantine period apply to all incoming pets regardless of their country's regulatory status."

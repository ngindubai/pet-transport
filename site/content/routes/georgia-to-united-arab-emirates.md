  h1: "Pet Transport from Georgia to the UAE"
  overview: |
    The National Food Agency in Tbilisi signs off on more pet export certificates each year than most people would guess, and the process it runs is straightforward if the timing is right. No export permit is needed for a companion animal leaving Georgia, only the NFA-endorsed health certificate, written on MOCCAE's model format.

    On the UAE side, MOCCAE treats Georgia as sitting outside its short low-risk list. That means the standard sequence: a microchip before vaccination, a rabies vaccination current for the right number of days, a titre test, and an online import permit valid for 90 days.

    What separates this corridor from an EU-bound one is simple but easy to miss. There's no fixed wait after the titre test blood draw here. Once the result clears 0.5 IU/ml, it holds for 365 days, full stop, no extra countdown attached the way the EU's 90-day rule works.

  sections:
    - heading: "Where Georgia sits in MOCCAE's risk categories"
      body: |
        Georgia doesn't appear on MOCCAE's short low-risk list, so pets travelling from Tbilisi go through the full sequence. Microchip first, then rabies vaccination, current for at least 21 days if it's a first dose. Then the rabies antibody titre test at an accredited laboratory, minimum result 0.5 IU/ml.

        Unlike routes into EU countries, there's no separate waiting period once that blood draw is done. MOCCAE checks the result itself: valid for 365 days as long as vaccination stays current. Minimum age from Georgia's risk category is 15 weeks, and the online import permit runs for 90 days once granted.

    - heading: "Departing Tbilisi: paperwork and flight choices"
      body: |
        No export permit stands between a companion pet and departure from Georgia. The document that does matter is the health certificate, examined and completed by an NFA-authorised vet on MOCCAE's own format, endorsed within 5 to 10 days of the flight.

        Tbilisi International (TBS) is the departure airport for both routings here. FlyDubai flies direct to Dubai, the simplest option available. Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul (IST) is a dependable second choice, particularly useful if FlyDubai's direct schedule doesn't line up with your travel window.

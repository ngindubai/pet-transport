content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Turkey to Jamaica"
  overview: |
    Turkey's own export rules are about as light as they get, no export permit, just a vet-issued certificate. None of that shortens the Jamaican side. VSD runs the same sequential process for every origin country: a Preliminary Application Form has to clear before a single qualifying vaccination or titre test can be administered, and skipping ahead risks the whole file.

    Add a connecting flight, since Istanbul and Kingston aren't linked non-stop, and this becomes one of the longer processes on the Turkey cluster, closer to five to six months than the six to nine weeks typical of this corridor's other destinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "VSD's Preliminary Application: the same first step for every origin"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division requires this form submitted and approved before an owner begins the rabies vaccination and titre test steps that count towards Jamaican entry. That rule doesn't bend for Turkey's own lighter export process, vaccinating ahead of approval still risks the vaccination not counting. Build in 4-5 months from the very start of the process to a realistic travel date.

    - heading: "FAVN titre test and Plumb Point quarantine, regardless of origin"
      body: |
        A FAVN titre test, drawn 30 days after the qualifying rabies vaccination at a VSD-approved laboratory, needs to clear 0.5 IU/ml. A pet over 12 months old receiving its first rabies vaccination doesn't qualify for entry until three months after that dose, on top of the 30-day titre wait. On arrival, pets go to the National Quarantine Facility at Plumb Point, Kingston, for up to 14 days, with early release at VSD's discretion, not guaranteed.

    - heading: "No direct flight adds a genuine second layer of planning"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines doesn't fly Istanbul to Kingston non-stop. The realistic options connect via a US hub, commonly New York, or via London Gatwick, pushing total travel time to 17-27 hours depending on the routing. JetBlue's direct JFK-Kingston service is a strong choice for the final leg of a US-routed itinerary.

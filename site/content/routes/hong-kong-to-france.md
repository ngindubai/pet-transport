  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Hong Kong to France means navigating two sets of official requirements. On the Hong Kong side, an AFCD export permit and an endorsed health certificate in the EU format. On the French side, a FAVN titre test and a mandatory 3-month wait before your pet can cross the EU border.

    Both Cathay Pacific and Air France operate direct HKG-CDG services, which keeps the logistics clean once the paperwork is sorted. Preparation runs 22-26 weeks.
  sections:
    - heading: "Living with the 3-month wait"
      body: |
        France applies EU Regulation 576/2013 in full. Hong Kong isn't on the EU approved country list, which means the non-listed protocol kicks in: FAVN titre test, minimum 0.5 IU/ml at an EU-approved laboratory, blood drawn at least 30 days after the last rabies vaccination, then a mandatory 3-month wait calculated from that blood draw date.

        Three months from the blood draw, not from when results arrived, not from when you paid the lab invoice. The date that blood left your pet's vein is what matters. Mark it on the calendar.

        During those three months, you book the flight, apply for the AFCD export permit, and arrange the health certificate appointment. The wait is the constraint; the administrative tasks run alongside it.
    - heading: "Two direct carriers, two advantages"
      body: |
        Cathay Pacific operates direct HKG-CDG cargo. As Hong Kong's own airline, their Kowloon Cargo Centre handles AFCD export documentation daily. The cargo team knows what AFCD expects and the documents are processed smoothly at departure.

        Air France operates the same route from the opposite direction. Their Charles de Gaulle cargo team handles regular EU BCP arrivals and has established relationships with the DGAL inspectors who check pets at CDG. Both carriers are strong options; the choice often comes down to schedule fit and cargo availability.
    - heading: "The health certificate timing challenge"
      body: |
        The health certificate must be issued within 14 days of Hong Kong departure AND within 10 days of France arrival. With a HKG-CDG flight time of roughly 13 hours, both windows overlap comfortably if you time the vet appointment correctly. Book the AFCD-authorised vet appointment in the week before travel, not the week before that.

        AFCD endorsement adds processing time on top of the vet appointment. In busy periods, allow a few days for AFCD to process the endorsement. Your agent will know the current lead times.

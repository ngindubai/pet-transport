    Kazakhstan does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ).   sections:
    - heading: "What Kazakhstan requires at the border"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan's veterinary import requirements are administered by the Committee of Veterinary Control and Supervision under the Ministry of Agriculture. Officers inspect documentation at Almaty International Airport (ALA) or Astana International Airport (NQZ) on arrival.

        The core requirements are a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Kazakhstan does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. For 1-2 personal companion animals, no import permit is required.

        Choose your entry airport based on your final destination within Kazakhstan. Almaty has more international cargo connections; Astana (NQZ) is convenient if you are based in or near the capital.

    - heading: "DAFM health certificate process from Ireland"
      body: |
        Ireland's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate signed by a DAFM-registered official vet and endorsed by DAFM. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Schedule the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, allow 1-2 working days for DAFM endorsement. Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the official health certificate must come from a DAFM-registered official vet. Check gov.ie/agriculture for authorised vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Dublin to Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Dublin (DUB) to Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ). Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul (IST) is the most established option, with onward connections to both ALA and NQZ.

        Book through an approved cargo agent. Confirm pet acceptance on all legs before committing to a travel date. If travelling June to September, ask specifically about temperature embargoes on the IST connection. Air Astana serves both Kazakh airports from European hubs and is worth checking for alternative routing if Turkish Airlines restrictions apply.

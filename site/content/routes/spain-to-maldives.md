  h1: "Pet Transport from Spain to Maldives: 2026 Guide"
  overview: |
    Spain's PPP rules, the ones requiring EUR 120,000 civil liability insurance and a muzzle in public for breeds like Rottweilers and American Pit Bull Terriers, are a genuine feature of Spanish pet law. They're also entirely beside the point on this route, since import of dogs into the Maldives is prohibited outright, PPP-listed or not.

    Cats have a workable path instead: microchip, rabies vaccination given 30 days to 12 months before arrival, the core feline vaccines, and a health certificate signed within 10 days of travel. Permits and titre tests are where the published guidance thins out, so confirm those specific points before booking.

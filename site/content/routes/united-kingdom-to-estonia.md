  h1: "Pet Transport from the United Kingdom to Estonia"
  overview: |
    UK to Estonia is one of the simpler international pet routes. The United Kingdom is a listed third country under EU Regulation 576/2013, which means UK pets can enter Estonia without a titre test or quarantine.

    You need an Animal Health Certificate from an APHA-authorised UK Official Vet in the EU-approved format for listed third countries. Current rabies vaccination and ISO microchip are required.

    airBaltic via Riga and Ryanair direct are the most practical flight options from the UK to Tallinn (TLL). Total preparation time: 2-4 weeks.

faqs:
  - question: "Does my pet need a titre test to travel from the UK to Estonia?"
    answer: "No. The United Kingdom is a listed third country under EU Regulation 576/2013. No titre test is required. You need a current rabies vaccination, ISO microchip, and a UK Animal Health Certificate in the correct format for EU listed third countries."
  - question: "What format does the UK health certificate need to be in for Estonia?"
    answer: "Your APHA-authorised Official Vet issues the AHC in the EU-approved format for listed third countries (under EU Regulation 576/2013). This is different from the AHC used for pets entering the UK from the EU. Tell your OV the destination is Estonia (EU) so they issue the correct certificate."
  - question: "Can I take my pet in the cabin on a flight from the UK to Estonia?"
    answer: "Yes. Ryanair flies direct to Tallinn from several UK airports and accepts very small cabin pets within their weight limits. airBaltic allows small cabin pets on the Riga-Tallinn leg. Check each airline's current cabin pet weight limits before booking."
  - question: "My pet has an EU pet passport issued in 2019. Is it valid for UK-to-Estonia travel?"
    answer: "An EU pet passport issued before 1 January 2021 may be valid for EU travel purposes. However, since your pet is travelling from the UK (now a third country), you need a UK AHC regardless of any existing passport. Confirm with your OV what documentation is required for the direction of travel."
  - question: "How long does it take to prepare a pet for UK-to-Estonia travel?"
    answer: "Allow 2-4 weeks. The main steps are confirming microchip and vaccination records, booking your OV appointment to issue the AHC (valid 10 days), booking flights, and allowing time for any cargo booking. This is one of the faster international pet routes to prepare."

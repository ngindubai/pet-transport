  overview: |
    Denmark is an EU member state and applies EU Regulation 576/2013 for all incoming pets from non-listed third countries. Thailand falls into the non-listed category, which means a rabies titre test and a mandatory 3-month waiting period are required before your pet can enter.

    Allow at least 22 weeks from starting preparations to travel day. The titre test and waiting period are the rate-limiting steps.

    Thai Airways operates a direct BKK-CPH service, which makes the flight logistics cleaner than most Southeast Asia to Europe routes. The DVFA inspection at Copenhagen Airport is thorough, and all documentation must be in order before you board.
  sections:
    - heading: "EU requirements for pets from Thailand"
      body: |
        Thailand is not on the EU's list of approved non-rabies-controlled countries (Commission Implementing Decision 2013/519/EU). This means the titre test protocol applies in full.

        The sequence: microchip first, then vaccinate, then titre test at an EU-approved laboratory (blood drawn at least 30 days after last vaccination), then a 3-month wait from the blood draw date. After the wait, you can travel.

        The FAVN titre test is the specific method required. It measures the level of rabies antibodies in your pet's blood. A result below 0.5 IU/ml means you must re-vaccinate and restart the 30-day wait and re-test. This can add several months to your timeline. Get your pet properly vaccinated and in good health before the test.
    - heading: "Practical points for the Bangkok to Copenhagen route"
      body: |
        Thai Airways is the standout option for this route. The direct BKK-CPH service removes the complexity of a mid-journey pet cargo transfer, which is a meaningful risk reduction. THAI Cargo handles live animal transport professionally, but check the current schedule as routes can change.

        Copenhagen Airport (CPH) has a well-organised DVFA border inspection process. Bring originals of everything: the EU Annex IV health certificate, vaccination records, titre test result certificate, microchip documentation, and DLD endorsement paperwork. Copies will not substitute for originals at the BCP.

        The DLD health certificate validity window is 10 days from issue. The BAI endorsement in Bangkok takes time -- book the government vet appointment for the week before travel, not the day before. DLD offices can be busy.

        If you are moving to Denmark long-term, check Danish DVFA guidance on re-registration of pets after arrival. Denmark has its own domestic dog registration requirements separate from EU entry rules.

        Portuguese pet owners moving within the EU are used to the EU pet passport covering everything: no titre test, no import permit, no quarantine. Indonesia sits well outside that system. A DGAV-authorised vet still issues the health certificate, but it needs to follow the format specified in the Barantin rekomendasi permit, which is closer to a government-endorsed certificate than a passport stamp.

        Confirm with your vet early that they can produce this format. A vet who only handles routine intra-EU pet passports may need a template from your Indonesia-side agent before the appointment.

faqs:
  - question: "Can I use my pet's existing EU pet passport for Indonesia?"
    answer: "No. Indonesia is outside the EU pet passport system. You need a fresh health certificate from a DGAV-authorised vet, issued in the format Barantin's rekomendasi permit specifies, alongside the FAVN titre test and the import permit itself."
  - question: "Is there a direct flight from Lisbon to Jakarta?"
    answer: "No. Qatar Airways via Doha, Turkish Airlines via Istanbul, and TAP Air Portugal connecting into KLM's Amsterdam hub are the three main routings. All involve at least one connection and a full day or more of total transit."
  - question: "How long does the rekomendasi permit take for a Portuguese pet?"
    answer: "Plan for 4 to 8 weeks across both stages: the provincial recommendation and the national approval from the Directorate of Animal Health in Jakarta. Start the application once your FAVN titre result of 0.5 IU/ml or above is confirmed."
  - question: "Does my dog need to be a specific age to enter Indonesia from Portugal?"
    answer: "Yes. Indonesia requires dogs to be at least 6 months old at the point of entry, regardless of the origin country's own age rules. Confirm this before booking, since a younger dog cannot be imported even with complete paperwork."
  - question: "What if I am moving to Bali rather than Jakarta?"
    answer: "Your pet still enters through a mainland port, typically Jakarta, and completes Barantin quarantine there before an onward domestic transfer to Bali. Name the Bali province as your destination address in the rekomendasi application; the entry point itself will still be the mainland port."
  - question: "Which lab in Portugal can run the FAVN titre test for Indonesia?"
    answer: "The blood draw is done by your DGAV-authorised vet in Portugal, but the sample must be analysed at a laboratory Barantin recognises. Confirm with your agent or DGAV which currently approved laboratories accept samples from Portugal before scheduling the blood draw."

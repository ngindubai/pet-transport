    - heading: "AGROCALIDAD permit: what it requires and how to apply"
      body: |
        Ecuador's AGROCALIDAD is the Agencia de Regulación y Control Fito y Zoosanitario. The zoosanitary import permit is required before travel and asks for the animal's species, breed, age, vaccination history, and origin country. Apply four to six weeks before your planned departure.

        The permit is obtainable directly through the AGROCALIDAD online portal or via an Ecuador-based agent. Once granted, it accompanies your USDA health certificate on arrival for AGROCALIDAD inspection.

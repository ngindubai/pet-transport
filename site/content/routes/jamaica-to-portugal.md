  h1: "Pet Transport Jamaica to Portugal"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Jamaica to Portugal requires the full EU unlisted country process. Jamaica is not on the EU listed country register, which makes a FAVN titre test and a 90-day wait from the blood draw date mandatory before EU entry. The VSD-endorsed EU-format health certificate is required within 10 days of travel. Total preparation time is 20 to 24 weeks.

    The most practical routing is via London Heathrow with British Airways, then connecting to Lisbon. DGAV veterinary officers inspect pets at Lisbon Airport's border post on arrival. Plan the titre test first: everything else fits around the 90-day wait.

  sections:
    - heading: "The EU unlisted country process for Jamaica"
      body: |
        Portugal applies EU Regulation 2020/692 for pets from unlisted countries. Jamaica is classified as unlisted, which means every dog or cat entering Portugal from Jamaica must have a successful FAVN (or ELISA) titre test result of at least 0.5 IU/ml from an EU-approved laboratory, regardless of vaccination history.

        The sequence is fixed: microchip before vaccination, then rabies vaccination, then blood draw at least 30 days after vaccination. The 90-day wait runs from the blood draw date. International sample shipping from Jamaica to a European laboratory adds approximately 2-4 weeks for transit and results. The minimum total preparation time from vaccination to Portugal entry is around 20 weeks.

    - heading: "Cargo from Kingston to Lisbon"
      body: |
        Norman Manley International Airport (KIN) in Kingston is the main departure point. British Airways World Cargo serves KIN with transatlantic services to London Heathrow (LHR). From Heathrow, TAP Air Portugal or other carriers connect to Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport (LIS). This two-leg routing, via London, is the most practical option from Jamaica to Portugal.

        KLM via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) is a second option for the transatlantic leg, with onward connections to Lisbon from AMS. Book the cargo during the 90-day wait and confirm live animal acceptance on every segment of the journey before finalising the travel date.

    - heading: "VSD health certificate and DGAV inspection"
      body: |
        The export health certificate must be in EU format, issued by a VSD-authorised vet in Jamaica and endorsed by the Veterinary Services Division. The certificate is valid for 10 days from issue and must include microchip number, vaccination history, titre test result, and laboratory report reference. Confirm with VSD whether an export permit is also required: vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm.

        DGAV (Direcao-Geral de Alimentacao e Veterinaria) officers inspect arriving pets at the border inspection post at Lisbon Airport. Compliant arrivals are released without quarantine. Check dgav.pt for the current non-commercial movement requirements for unlisted countries.

    - heading: "The DALRRD import permit: allow 4-8 weeks"
      body: |
        South Africa requires a Veterinary Import Permit from DALRRD for all live animal imports. The permit specifies the animals, the origin country, the entry port, and the health conditions that must be met. Without it, your pet cannot clear customs at JNB.

        Apply through DALRRD's Directorate of Animal Health. Processing times vary between 4 and 8 weeks and can be unpredictable. Apply as soon as you have a confirmed move date. Your agent can manage this.

        The health certificate must match the conditions specified in the DALRRD permit exactly. Any discrepancy between the certificate and the permit causes delays at the South African border inspection point.
    - heading: "The vaccination window: 30 days to 12 months"
      body: |
        South Africa, like Hong Kong, applies a dual vaccination window. The rabies vaccination must be at least 30 days old and not more than 12 months old at the time of South Africa arrival. A booster given yesterday won't work. A vaccination given 13 months ago won't work either, even if it's still within the German manufacturer's validity period.

        Plan your travel date with this window explicitly in mind. If your pet's vaccination will be over 12 months old when you arrive, get a booster at least 30 days before departure.
faqs:
  - question: "Does my pet need a titre test to enter South Africa from Germany?"
    answer: "No. Germany has a bilateral veterinary agreement with South Africa that removes the titre test requirement. The main requirements are microchip, rabies vaccination within the 30-day to 12-month window, a DALRRD import permit, and an official health certificate."
  - question: "How do I get a DALRRD import permit?"
    answer: "Apply directly to the DALRRD Directorate of Animal Health at dalrrd.gov.za, or through an experienced pet transport agent. Allow 4-8 weeks for processing. The permit must be in hand before your pet travels."
  - question: "Is there quarantine when bringing a pet from Germany to South Africa?"
    answer: "No routine quarantine for properly documented pets from Germany. DALRRD conducts an inspection at the Port of Entry at Johannesburg O.R. Tambo International Airport. With complete documentation including the DALRRD permit, your pet is released the same day."
  - question: "How long does preparation take for Germany to South Africa?"
    answer: "6-10 weeks. The DALRRD import permit is the main timeline driver, taking 4-8 weeks. If your pet's vaccinations are current and within the required window, no additional waiting periods apply."

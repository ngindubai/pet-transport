  sections:
    - heading: "Uruguay's requirements for pets from France"
      body: |
        Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca, gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) governs pet imports. For pets arriving from France, the requirements are: MGAP import permit (apply well in advance, allow 6-10 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; rabies vaccination administered between 30 days and 12 months before arrival; and a DDPP-endorsed health certificate issued within 10 days of travel.

        For dogs, there is one additional requirement: Praziquantel treatment against Echinococcus administered between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival in Uruguay. A veterinarian must record the treatment. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. MGAP inspects pets on arrival at Carrasco International Airport (MVD).

    - heading: "The MGAP import permit"
      body: |
        The MGAP import permit is the item with the longest lead time on this route. Apply to MGAP (gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca) with your pet's details and proposed travel date. Processing takes 6-10 weeks. Do not book cargo or finalise dates until the permit is confirmed.

        The permit must accompany the health certificate on arrival. MGAP inspectors at MVD check the permit reference before releasing the animal.

    - heading: "Praziquantel treatment for dogs"
      body: |
        Dogs entering Uruguay must be treated with Praziquantel against Echinococcus between 72 hours and 30 days before arrival at MVD. Calculate the window from your scheduled arrival time in Montevideo, not departure from CDG. A treatment given 71 hours before arrival is outside the window.

        Your veterinarian in France records the treatment: product name, dosage, date and time, and the dog's microchip number. This record accompanies the health certificate. Cats do not require the Praziquantel treatment.

    - heading: "Routing from Paris to Montevideo"
      body: |
        Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) has one of Europe's best connections to Montevideo. Air France operates transatlantic South American services from CDG, including connections to Montevideo Carrasco (MVD). This makes CDG-MVD cargo relatively straightforward compared to other European origins.

        Iberia via Madrid (MAD) is an alternative, with Iberia's direct MAD-MVD service. LATAM Airlines handles the South American domestic segment if the routing goes via Sao Paulo (GRU) or Buenos Aires (EZE). Confirm live animal cargo acceptance on the specific flight before booking.

    - heading: "DDPP export endorsement from France"
      body: |
        France's DDPP (Direction Departementale de la Protection des Populations) endorses official export health certificates at the department level. An official veterinarian prepares the certificate referencing the MGAP permit, microchip, vaccination details, and Praziquantel treatment record for dogs.

        Allow three to five business days for the DDPP endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Coordinate the endorsement timing carefully given the 10-day validity window.

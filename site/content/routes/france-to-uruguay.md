  sections:
    - heading: "Uruguay's import requirements for EU pets"
      body: |
        Uruguay's animal health authority, MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca), handles pet imports through its DIGEGRA division. MGAP veterinary officers inspect arriving pets at Carrasco International Airport (MVD) in Montevideo, which is the only air entry point for companion animals.

        The core requirements are: a rabies vaccination given at least 30 days before entry and not more than 12 months before arrival, a health certificate from the competent authority in France (the DGAL-endorsed AHC), and an import permit obtained from MGAP before travel. No titre test is required from France.

        Dogs have one additional requirement: praziquantel treatment against Taenia echinococcus (hydatic cyst). This treatment must be given between 72 hours and 30 days before admission to Uruguay, and a vet must document the product used, the dose, and the date. The window matters: treatment given more than 30 days before arrival is outside the valid period.

        Uruguay also has breed restrictions. Pit Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Bull Terrier, and Dogo Argentino face import restrictions. Confirm your breed's status with MGAP before applying for the permit.

    - heading: "Getting the MGAP import permit from France"
      body: |
        The MGAP import permit is a prerequisite for travel, not something you arrange on arrival. Apply to MGAP (mgap.gub.uy) directly, or through the Uruguayan consulate in France if you prefer to handle it from Paris. Allow at least six to eight weeks for the process.

        The permit specifies conditions that must be met: the vaccinations required, the health certificate format, and the antiparasitic requirements. Read it carefully when it arrives, as the conditions may differ slightly from the general requirements listed here.

        Once the permit is confirmed, finalise your travel date and book cargo from Paris CDG to Carrasco Airport (MVD) in Montevideo. The main routing options are Iberia via Madrid or connections through Buenos Aires via Air France or LATAM. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance on all legs before committing to a date.

  sections:
    - heading: "What the UK requires from pets arriving from Azerbaijan"
      body: |
        Azerbaijan is not on the UK's approved country list for simplified pet travel. The full APHA protocol applies: ISO microchip before vaccination, current rabies vaccination, titre test (blood drawn at least 30 days post-vaccination, result at least 0.5 IU/ml from an EU/UK approved laboratory), 3-month wait from blood draw date, tapeworm treatment for dogs 24-120 hours before arrival, and an Azerbaijan State Veterinary Service-endorsed health certificate valid 10 days.

        The 3-month wait starts on the day blood was drawn for the titre test. Calculate this date precisely. Arriving one day early means UK border force will refuse entry.

        Source: UK APHA, gov.uk/bring-pet-to-great-britain.
    - heading: "Flight options from Baku to London: what works best for pets"
      body: |
        AZAL direct from Baku (GYD) to London Heathrow is the cleanest option. Direct means no intermediate handling, no connection risk, and the shortest total journey time for your pet in cargo. Confirm with AZAL Cargo that pet shipments are being accepted on your travel dates.

        Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is a popular alternative and has good schedule frequency from Baku. The Istanbul connection is typically 2-4 hours. Small pets may travel in cabin on some Turkish Airlines legs within Europe.

        Emirates via Dubai adds Gulf connection time but Emirates SkyCargo is one of the largest and most experienced live animal cargo operations globally. Temperature embargoes during summer months in Dubai may affect June-September bookings.

        Lufthansa via Frankfurt offers the additional option of cabin travel for small pets on the Frankfurt-London leg.

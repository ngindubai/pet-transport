  h1: "Pet Transport Uruguay to Belgium"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Uruguay to Belgium requires the EU unlisted country process. Uruguay is not on the EU listed country register, which means a FAVN titre test, a mandatory 90-day wait from the blood draw date, and a MGAP-DGSG endorsed EU-format health certificate are all required. The total preparation time is 20 to 24 weeks.

    Iberia via Madrid is the main cargo routing from Carrasco International Airport in Montevideo. FASFC veterinary officers inspect arriving pets at Brussels Airport. Start the titre test process first: the 90-day wait is the controlling factor and cannot be shortened.

  sections:
    - heading: "FAVN titre test and the 90-day wait"
      body: |
        Belgium applies EU Regulation 2020/692 for pets from unlisted countries. Uruguay is classified as unlisted, which means every dog or cat entering Belgium from Uruguay must have a successful FAVN (or ELISA) titre test from an EU-approved laboratory. The minimum acceptable result is 0.5 IU/ml.

        The sequence is: microchip before vaccination, rabies vaccination, blood draw at least 30 days after vaccination, then 90-day wait from the blood draw date. Shipping a blood sample from Uruguay to a European laboratory and receiving results typically adds 2-4 weeks. The minimum total preparation time from vaccination to Belgium entry is around 20 weeks.

        Uruguay has veterinary infrastructure experienced with South American animal movement. Confirm with your vet whether they have handled FAVN test sample shipping to EU-approved laboratories before starting the process. If not, a specialist pet transport agent can coordinate this step.

    - heading: "Cargo from Montevideo to Brussels"
      body: |
        Carrasco International Airport (MVD) in Montevideo is the main cargo departure point. Iberia operates cargo services from MVD to Madrid Barajas (MAD), and from Madrid, connections to Brussels Airport (BRU) are available via Iberia partner carriers or Brussels Airlines. Iberia is one of the most established European cargo carriers to Uruguay and has experience with live animal shipments on this route.

        LATAM Airlines is an alternative, connecting from MVD via South American hubs to European cities. From a European hub, onward cargo connections to Brussels are available. Book during the 90-day wait period and confirm live animal acceptance on all segments.

    - heading: "MGAP-DGSG health certificate and FASFC inspection at Brussels"
      body: |
        The export health certificate must be in EU format, issued by a MGAP-registered vet and endorsed by the Direccion General de Servicios Ganaderos (DGSG) of Uruguay's MGAP. This certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel to Belgium and include the microchip number, vaccination records, titre test result, and laboratory report reference.

        FASFC (Federal Agency for the Safety of the Food Chain) veterinary officers inspect arriving pets at the border inspection post at Brussels Airport. Compliant arrivals are released without quarantine. Check favv-afsca.be for the current requirements for non-commercial pet movement from unlisted countries.

  sections:
    - heading: "ONSSA and the Morocco export certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        ONSSA (Office National de Securite Sanitaire des produits Alimentaires) is Morocco's food and agricultural safety authority, with responsibility for animal health documentation. The health certificate for US import must be issued by an ONSSA-authorised official vet and endorsed by ONSSA.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history with titre test result (laboratory name, blood draw date, result in IU/ml), owner details, and fitness to travel. Contact ONSSA (onssa.gov.ma) for the list of authorised vets and the current certificate format.

        For US travel, the titre test documentation must also satisfy CDC/USDA standards. The ONSSA-endorsed certificate and the titre test result from a USDA-recognised laboratory are both required for the CDC inspection on arrival.

    - heading: "Royal Air Maroc direct to JFK and the CDC facility advantage"
      body: |
        Royal Air Maroc operates direct cargo flights from Casablanca Mohammed V International Airport (CMN) to New York JFK. This is a notable advantage: JFK has a CDC-registered animal care facility, which is required for dogs from CDC high-risk countries including Morocco.

        A direct flight from Casablanca to JFK avoids transit hub complications and minimises total journey time for your pet. Contact RAM Cargo (royalairmaroc.com) directly to confirm live animal acceptance, crate dimension requirements, and seasonal restrictions for your travel dates.

        Air France via Paris CDG provides an alternative connection to multiple US airports. Iberia or British Airways via Madrid or London are further options. Confirm that the US destination airport has a CDC-registered facility for whichever routing you choose.

        Greece follows the same EU rulebook as every other member state. Uruguay has not been assessed as having equivalent rabies control, so it falls into the unlisted group: FAVN or ELISA titre test at an EU-approved laboratory, a result of at least 0.5 IU/ml, blood drawn 30 or more days after vaccination, then a 90-day wait from that draw date.

        Nothing about Greece's own entry process shortens this. The 90 days is an EU-wide minimum, identical whether the pet lands in Athens, Berlin or Copenhagen.

        Luxembourg Findel Airport (LUX) is a small international airport with limited direct UK connections. Luxair from Heathrow and British Airways from Heathrow are the most reliable direct options for UK to Luxembourg pet transport. Both accept pets in the hold.

        For owners who are driving within Europe or whose pet is travelling separately as cargo, routing to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) or Frankfurt (FRA) with an onward road journey is practical. Brussels is approximately 220 km from Luxembourg, Frankfurt around 200 km. This gives access to the full cargo networks of Brussels Airlines and Lufthansa respectively, which have more capacity for large dogs.

    - heading: "What Luxembourg requires from UK pets"
      body: |
        Luxembourg applies standard EU pet travel rules through its Administration des Services Vétérinaires. The process on arrival is the same as any EU border inspection: the microchip is scanned, the AHC reviewed, and the tapeworm treatment date verified for dogs.

        Bring the original AHC, not a photocopy. The document is a controlled veterinary certificate and border staff need to see the original. Luxembourg inspectors are thorough but efficient for compliant arrivals.

      body: |
        TAP's only Mexican destination is Cancun, flown non-stop from Lisbon. If your actual move is to Mexico City, that flight doesn't help you directly, and the practical option is a connection through Madrid, where Iberia operates a well-used Mexico City service and handles pet cargo from across the Iberian peninsula in volume.

        Amsterdam with KLM is a workable second option if Madrid space is tight on your dates. Either way, this route needs a connection booked and confirmed on both legs, unlike the more direct KLM service some other European countries get straight into Mexico City.

        Worth checking early: is Cancun actually close enough to your final Mexican address that TAP's direct flight makes more sense than a Mexico City routing? For some relocations, particularly to the Yucatan or Riviera Maya, it genuinely does.
    - heading: "DGAV's role and what SENASICA wants on arrival"
      body: |
        Portugal doesn't require an export permit for a companion pet. A DGAV-authorised vet issues the health certificate, or in some cases an EU pet passport, recording the microchip and rabies vaccination.

        On arrival, whether that's Mexico City or Cancun, SENASICA inspects the pet physically rather than processing paperwork ahead of time. Present the health certificate, vaccination record and completed FF-SENASICA-003 form together. No titre test, no quarantine, provided everything's in order and the certificate is still within its 15-day window.

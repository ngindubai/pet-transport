content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Uganda to Turkey"
  overview: |
    Entebbe to Istanbul runs as a direct flight on Turkish Airlines, one of the airline's more established East African connections. That removes the mid-journey cargo transfer that complicates a lot of routes into Turkey from this part of the world.

    Uganda's own export side has one extra step most of its regional neighbours skip: a formal export permit from MAAIF, on top of the health certificate. Build that into the timeline early, since it's not something that can be rushed alongside the titre test wait.

  sections:
    - heading: "Uganda's export permit, a step Rwanda doesn't require"
      body: |
        MAAIF, Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries, requires a formal export permit for a pet leaving the country, in addition to the standard health certificate. That's a genuine point of difference from Rwanda, just across the border, where RAB's endorsement of the health certificate is the only export document needed. Apply for the Ugandan permit early; it's a separate process from the certificate itself and shouldn't be left until the final week before travel.

        Entebbe International (EBB) has Turkish Airlines' own direct cargo service to Istanbul, so once the paperwork is in order, the flight itself is a single non-stop leg rather than a connection through the Gulf or Europe.

    - heading: "Turkey's titre test still governs the timeline"
      body: |
        The permit and certificate on Uganda's side are straightforward once you know to plan for them. The real driver of how long this move takes is Turkey's own titre test requirement: blood drawn at least 30 days after the rabies vaccination, a result of at least 0.5 IU/ml, and a 90-day wait from that draw date, not from the date the lab confirms the result.

        A booster vaccination given after the test resets its own 21-day wait, so if your pet's rabies protection is due for renewal close to your travel date, sequence it carefully. Arriving without a compliant test means 21 days of quarantine in Turkey at the owner's expense.

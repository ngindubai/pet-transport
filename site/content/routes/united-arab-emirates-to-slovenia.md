content:
  h1: "Pet Transport United Arab Emirates to Slovenia"
  overview: |
    Ljubljana's airport is small by EU standards, and that shapes this route more than the regulatory side does. Direct long-haul cargo capacity into Slovenia itself is limited, so many pet transport agents route through Vienna, Frankfurt or Munich and finish the journey by road, a detail worth confirming before booking rather than after.

    On the regulatory side, the UAE's Annex II classification under EU rules means the titre test and 90-day wait apply here just as they do for most non-EU countries. Slovenia's UVHVR enforces this without exception.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why Ljubljana's size matters for routing"
      body: |
        Unlike Vienna or Frankfurt, Ljubljana Joze Pucnik Airport handles a smaller volume of long-haul cargo traffic, which means fewer live animal slots on any given connection. Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul is generally the most practical single-connection option. Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt or Munich is a workable alternative, though it may mean a ground transfer for the final leg into Slovenia rather than a direct onward flight. Ask your cargo agent to confirm the actual routing before committing to a date.

    - heading: "The titre test, applied without exception"
      body: |
        A pet needs an ISO microchip before vaccination, a current rabies vaccination, and a FAVN or equivalent titre test drawn at least 30 days later at an EU-approved laboratory, clearing 0.5 IU/ml. From the date of that blood draw, 90 days must pass before the pet can enter Slovenia. UVHVR applies this to the UAE because of its Annex II status, the same treatment given to most countries outside the EU's short low-risk list.

    - heading: "MOCCAE's fixed export process"
      body: |
        Every pet leaving the UAE needs the standard MOCCAE export certificate: an online application, an in-person clinic exam in Dubai or Abu Dhabi, and a final airport check before the flight. For Slovenia, an EU-format health certificate is also required, and it's only valid for 10 days, so time it to your actual departure.

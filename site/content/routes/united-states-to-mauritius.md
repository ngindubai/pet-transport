  sections:
    - heading: "Paris vs Dubai: two transatlantic options compared"
      body: |
        No airline flies direct from the USA to Mauritius. The two most practical routing options are via Paris and via Dubai.

        Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) is served by Air France from major US cities. Air France connects to Mauritius via Air Mauritius from Paris. This routing avoids summer Gulf heat embargoes and is generally the more straightforward option for US East Coast departures.

        Emirates via Dubai connects to Mauritius from JFK, LAX, IAH, and ORD. Emirates SkyCargo has strong Mauritius connections. The Dubai transit introduces summer heat embargo risk (June to September), which can prevent live animal cargo from transiting DXB in extreme heat. Avoid the Dubai routing in summer if possible.

    - heading: "The quarantine: what owners need to know"
      body: |
        Mauritius's mandatory quarantine is non-negotiable. It is not possible to bring a dog or cat into Mauritius without completing the quarantine period at the government facility, regardless of vaccination status, titre test results, or origin country.

        Contact the Mauritius National Parks and Conservation Service (NPAS) well before travel (three to six months minimum) to understand the current process, reserve a space, and pay the quarantine fee. The facility provides basic care during the quarantine. Visiting arrangements vary.

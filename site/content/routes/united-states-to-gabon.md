  h1: "Pet Transport from the United States to Gabon"
  overview: |
    Four to six months. That's how far ahead Gabon's own Direction Generale de l'Elevage recommends starting the import permit application, and nothing about a US departure shortens it. The permit runway sets the pace of this entire route far more than the USDA's own, comparatively quick, export process does.

    Rabies vaccination has its own window too, at least 30 days before travel and within a year of it, but published sources don't confirm a titre test requirement one way or the other for Gabon, US-origin or otherwise. What is confirmed is the language: every document needs to arrive in French, which means budgeting translation time for a USDA certificate that starts out entirely in English.

    No airline connects the US to Libreville nonstop. Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa, Royal Air Maroc via Casablanca, or Air France via Paris are the three realistic paths, and with the permit's own lead time stacked on top, 20 to 26 weeks is a realistic planning window rather than a worst case.

  sections:
    - heading: "The permit runway that dictates everything else"
      body: |
        Most destinations on this site work to a lead time measured in weeks. Gabon works in months. The import permit application, filed with the Direction Generale de l'Elevage, needs 4 to 6 months to process, according to Gabon's own guidance, and there's nothing route-specific about a US departure that changes that figure.

        Rabies vaccination, given 30 days to a year before travel, has to sit comfortably inside that longer window rather than being planned as a separate, later deadline. Miss the permit's own timing and the vaccination schedule becomes irrelevant.

    - heading: "French paperwork, whatever the departure country"
      body: |
        Gabon's import documentation runs in French, and that applies just as much to a USDA-issued certificate from an American vet as it does to any other country's export paperwork. Translation has to happen before the certificate is finalised, not after, so the wording matches what Gabon's authorities actually expect to see.

        A titre test isn't confirmed as required in the guidance reviewed for this page, a genuine gap in what's published rather than a firm answer either way. Given how much of Gabon's process runs outside the templates this site sees on better-documented routes, confirm that point directly before ruling it out.

    - heading: "Three ways in, none of them short"
      body: |
        Ethiopian Airlines offers the most direct single-carrier option, a nonstop Washington Dulles-Addis Ababa service connecting onward to Libreville. Royal Air Maroc runs roughly 13 weekly flights between New York JFK and Casablanca, with its own nonstop Casablanca-Libreville leg picking up the connection. Air France routes through Paris instead, joining its established nonstop CDG-Libreville service.

        None of the three is short, and none removes the need to plan around Gabon's own multi-month permit process running in parallel with the flight booking.

        Bratislava Airport (BTS) has fewer direct UK connections than many other European capitals. Wizz Air from Luton is the main direct option accepting pets. Ryanair flies the same route but does not accept live animals.

        Slovakia applies standard EU pet travel rules for pets arriving from the UK. The State Veterinary and Food Administration of the Slovak Republic oversees compliance. On arrival, border inspection staff check the microchip, verify the AHC, and confirm the tapeworm treatment record for dogs.

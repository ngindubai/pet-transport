content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Philippines to Indonesia"
  overview: |
    Two archipelago nations in the same region don't add up to a simple move. Manila and Jakarta have no non-stop flight between them, and Indonesia's own entry process, a FAVN titre test, a two-stage rekomendasi permit, and mandatory quarantine, runs on a much longer clock than the short regional hop might suggest.

    Owners moving pets to Bali specifically need to plan for mainland entry first; direct arrival into Denpasar isn't permitted for a pet clearing quarantine.

  sections:
    - heading: "The rekomendasi permit comes before anything else"
      body: |
        Barantin's two-stage import permit, a provincial recommendation followed by national approval from the Directorate of Animal Health in Jakarta, needs to be secured before vaccination or titre testing even begins. It's valid 30 days from issue, so timing the application against the rest of the process matters.

    - heading: "No direct flight, and mainland-only entry"
      body: |
        Philippine Airlines and Garuda Indonesia both route through a Southeast Asian hub, commonly Singapore or Kuala Lumpur, rather than flying Manila to Jakarta non-stop. On arrival, entry has to be through a mainland port, Jakarta being the most common, since direct Bali arrival isn't available for a pet completing quarantine.

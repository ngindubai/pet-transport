  h1: "Pet Transport from UAE to South Korea: QIAS Quarantine and MOCCAE Export"
  overview: |
    South Korea's QIAS (Quarantine Inspection and Approval Service) classifies the UAE as a rabies-endemic country. This means pets from the UAE require an FAVN titre test at a QIAS-approved laboratory and a 5-10 day quarantine inspection at the QIAS facility at Seoul Incheon International Airport on arrival. The total preparation time from starting vaccinations to arriving in South Korea is approximately 12-20 weeks. Source: QIAS, qia.go.kr.

    The UAE export process requires a health certificate endorsed by the relevant municipal authority and by MOCCAE (Ministry of Climate Change and Environment), in the format specified by QIAS. Emirates SkyCargo and Korean Air Cargo both operate direct services from Dubai (DXB) to Seoul Incheon (ICN), making this a well-served route with practical airline options.

    Comparing UAE-to-South Korea with UAE-to-Japan: Japan requires two vaccinations, a titre test, and a mandatory 180-day pre-travel wait, with total preparation of 32-40 weeks. South Korea requires a titre test and imposes a 5-10 day arrival quarantine but no pre-travel 180-day wait, making it approximately 8-10 months faster to prepare for than Japan.

  sections:
    - heading: "QIAS requirements: what South Korea needs from UAE pets"
      body: |
        QIAS manages all animal quarantine and inspection for South Korea. For pets from the UAE (classified as rabies-endemic), the requirements are: ISO microchip; current rabies vaccination; FAVN titre test at a QIAS-approved laboratory with a result of 0.5 IU/ml or above; advance QIAS notification before travel; QIAS-format health certificate endorsed by municipal authority and MOCCAE; and 5-10 day quarantine at the QIAS facility at Incheon on arrival. The quarantine duration depends on documentation completeness and QIAS's assessment. Source: QIAS, qia.go.kr.

        The health certificate must be in the format specified by QIAS for UAE pets, not a general MOCCAE export certificate. The QIAS notification must be submitted before travel arrangements are finalised. A specialist agent familiar with QIAS documentation for UAE-origin pets is valuable, as the specific format requirements can differ from what the MOCCAE endorsement process normally produces for other destinations.

    - heading: "UAE export: municipal endorsement and MOCCAE"
      body: |
        The UAE export process for live animals involves two endorsement steps. First, the health certificate is issued by an accredited vet and endorsed by the relevant municipal authority: Abu Dhabi Agriculture and Food Safety Authority (ADAFSA) for Abu Dhabi, Dubai Municipality for Dubai, or Sharjah Municipality for Sharjah. Second, the certificate is submitted to MOCCAE for the formal national-level endorsement, which typically takes 48 hours.

        The health certificate must be in QIAS-specified format and must be issued within 14 days of the South Korea arrival date. Emirates SkyCargo from Dubai (DXB) to Incheon (ICN) is the most direct routing. Etihad Cargo from Abu Dhabi (AUH) to ICN is an option for Abu Dhabi-based owners. Korean Air Cargo also operates this route from DXB.

    - heading: "UAE-South Korea versus UAE-Japan: a timeline comparison"
      body: |
        For UAE-based owners comparing Japan and South Korea as destinations, the timeline difference is substantial. UAE-to-Japan: two rabies vaccinations after microchipping, titre test at 30 days post-second vaccination, mandatory 180-day wait from blood draw, 40-day advance AQS notification. Total preparation: 32-40 weeks. UAE-to-South Korea: one rabies vaccination, titre test at 30 days post-vaccination, QIAS advance notification, 5-10 day quarantine at Incheon on arrival. Total preparation: approximately 12-20 weeks.

        The key difference is that South Korea imposes its quarantine on arrival rather than requiring a pre-travel waiting period. Japan's 180-day wait dominates the timeline for Japan-bound moves. For families who have a choice between Japan and South Korea as a destination, or whose relocation date is fixed and cannot accommodate a 32-40 week preparation window, South Korea is considerably more accessible.

  overview: |
    Moving a pet from the Dominican Republic to Portugal requires the EU unlisted country process. The Dominican Republic is not on the EU listed country register, making a FAVN titre test and a 90-day wait from the blood draw date mandatory. The EU-format health certificate must be endorsed by DIGEGA. Total preparation time is 20 to 24 weeks.

    TAP Air Portugal is the most practical routing, with services connecting Santo Domingo and Lisbon. DGAV veterinary officers inspect arriving pets at Lisbon Airport's border inspection post. Plan the titre test well in advance: the 90-day wait controls the whole timeline.

  sections:
    - heading: "The FAVN titre test: the controlling step"
      body: |
        Portugal follows EU Regulation 2020/692 for non-commercial pet movement from unlisted countries. Because the Dominican Republic is classified as unlisted, every dog or cat entering Portugal from the Dominican Republic must have a successful FAVN (or ELISA) titre test at an EU-approved laboratory. The minimum acceptable result is 0.5 IU/ml.

        The sequence is: microchip implantation, then rabies vaccination, then blood draw at least 30 days after vaccination. The 90-day wait runs from the blood draw date. Shipping a Dominican Republic blood sample to a European laboratory and receiving the result adds approximately 2-4 weeks on top of this. The practical minimum from vaccination to Portugal entry is around 20 weeks.

        Confirm your vet's capacity to handle international FAVN sample shipping before starting the process. The sample requires sodium azide preservative medium and international courier experience with biological samples.

    - heading: "TAP Air Portugal and cargo to Lisbon"
      body: |
        TAP Air Portugal is the most direct cargo option on this route. TAP operates transatlantic services connecting Santo Domingo Las Americas (SDQ) to Lisbon Humberto Delgado Airport (LIS). Contact TAP Cargo to confirm live animal acceptance and freight requirements for your specific travel date.

        If TAP schedules do not suit, Iberia via Madrid Barajas (MAD) is a practical alternative. SDQ to MAD on Iberia, then MAD to LIS, is a well-established hub routing for Caribbean-to-Portugal cargo. KLM via Amsterdam is a third option. Book during the 90-day wait period and confirm live animal acceptance on every segment.

    - heading: "DIGEGA certificate and DGAV inspection at Lisbon"
      body: |
        The export health certificate for Portugal must be in EU format, issued by a DIGEGA-authorised vet in the Dominican Republic and endorsed by DIGEGA. This certificate is valid for 10 days from issue as the EU entry document and must include the microchip number, rabies vaccination and titre test records, and laboratory report reference.

        At Lisbon Airport, DGAV (Direcao-Geral de Alimentacao e Veterinaria) officers inspect arriving pets at the border inspection post. Compliant arrivals are released without quarantine. Check dgav.pt for the most current requirements for non-commercial pet movement from unlisted countries.

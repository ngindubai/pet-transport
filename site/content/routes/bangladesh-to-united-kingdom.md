  sections:
    - heading: "The titre test: the step most owners underestimate"
      body: |
        The FAVN titre test is the most time-sensitive step in this process and the one most likely to cause delays if not planned correctly.

        The blood must be drawn at least 30 days after the pet's most recent rabies vaccination. If you draw the blood on day 29, the test is invalid. After the blood draw, the 90-day wait begins immediately, from the blood draw date, regardless of when you receive the result.

        If the result is below 0.5 IU/ml (the pet has not mounted an adequate immune response), the entire process resets. The vaccination course must be completed again, and a new blood draw must wait another 30 days. This is why it is important to start the process at the right time, with vaccinations properly spaced, to give the pet the best chance of achieving a good titre test result.

        Samples from Bangladesh can be sent to APHA-approved laboratories. Your vet in Bangladesh or a specialist pet transport agent can advise on which laboratories accept international samples and how to arrange the shipping of the blood sample.

    - heading: "Tapeworm treatment timing for dogs"
      body: |
        Dogs entering the UK from Bangladesh must receive tapeworm treatment using a product containing praziquantel. The treatment must be administered by a vet and must take place no earlier than 120 hours (5 days) and no later than 24 hours before the dog arrives in the UK.

        This timing is strict. Treatment given outside this window does not count. To calculate the correct window: take your flight arrival time in the UK and count back 120 hours (the earliest treatment time) and 24 hours (the latest treatment time). The vet appointment must fall within that window.

        The vet must record the treatment on the health certificate or a separate signed treatment certificate: product name, dose, microchip number, and date and time of administration. Bring this documentation as part of your travel paperwork.

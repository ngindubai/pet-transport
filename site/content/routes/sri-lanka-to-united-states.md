  sections:
    - heading: "DAPH and the Sri Lanka export certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        DAPH (Department of Animal Production and Health), under Sri Lanka's Ministry of Agriculture, is responsible for endorsing health certificates for pet exports. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a DAPH-authorised official vet and endorsed by DAPH.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history with titre test result (USDA-recognised laboratory, blood draw date, result in IU/ml), owner details, and fitness to travel. Contact DAPH (daph.gov.lk) for the current list of authorised vets and certificate format.

        For the US titre test requirement, the blood sample needs to go to a USDA-recognised laboratory. Your specialist pet transport agent can advise on shipping blood samples from Colombo.

    - heading: "Routing from Colombo to the USA"
      body: |
        No airlines fly direct from Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) in Colombo to the USA. The main cargo routing options are Emirates via Dubai (DXB), Qatar Airways via Doha (DOH), and Singapore Airlines via Singapore (SIN). All connect to multiple US gateway airports with CDC-registered animal care facilities, including JFK, LAX, and ORD.

        Emirates and Qatar Airways offer the best frequency from Colombo to Gulf hubs. Singapore Airlines via Singapore provides an alternative Pacific routing. Confirm live animal acceptance and crate requirements directly with the chosen carrier's cargo desk before booking.

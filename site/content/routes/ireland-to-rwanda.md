  h1: "Pet Transport from Ireland to Rwanda"
  overview: |
    Rwanda has a well-organised pet import system administered by the Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB). The process is manageable, but it requires an import permit from RAB before travel, and the long haul from Dublin to Kigali means preparation of at least six to eight weeks is realistic.

    Rwanda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Kigali International Airport. The permit and the documentation need to align before travel, and both must be in hand before your pet boards.

    Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa and Qatar Airways via Doha are the main cargo options from Dublin to Kigali. Both carriers have experience with live animal cargo and serve Kigali on scheduled services.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Rwanda requires at the border"
      body: |
        Rwanda's pet import requirements are administered by RAB (Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board), rab.gov.rw. Officers inspect documentation at Kigali International Airport (KGL) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid RAB import permit (obtained before travel), a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Rwanda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for arrivals with a valid import permit and compliant documentation.

        The import permit is the first thing to organise. Apply to RAB well before your travel date, as processing can take several weeks. Without the permit, your pet cannot enter Rwanda regardless of how complete the other documentation is.

    - heading: "DAFM health certificate process from Ireland"
      body: |
        Ireland's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate signed by a DAFM-registered official vet and endorsed by DAFM (Department of Agriculture, Food and the Marine). Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, allow 1-2 working days for DAFM endorsement. Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the official health certificate must come from a DAFM-registered official vet. Check gov.ie/agriculture for authorised vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Dublin to Kigali"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Dublin (DUB) to Kigali (KGL). Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the primary option: Dublin to ADD, then ADD to KGL on Ethiopian Airlines. Ethiopian Airlines has an established live animal cargo programme and Addis Ababa is their major hub for East Africa connections.

        Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha (DOH) is a practical alternative. Qatar Airways serves Kigali from Doha and has good live animal facilities at Hamad International Airport in Doha. Book through a cargo agent and confirm acceptance on both legs.

        RwandAir also operates services to/from Kigali, but connections from Dublin require a European hub transfer first. Coordinate all legs with your cargo agent before committing to a travel date.

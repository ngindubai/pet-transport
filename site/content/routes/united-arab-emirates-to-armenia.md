    FlyDubai's route network across the Caucasus makes this one of the tidier corridors in this build: a direct Dubai-Yerevan cargo service, no connection required. That single fact removes a lot of the scheduling complexity that affects other UAE-outbound routes.

  sections:
    - heading: "MOCCAE's export process, same for every UAE departure"
      body: |
        A MOCCAE export health certificate is required for every pet leaving the UAE, regardless of destination. It starts with an online application through the MOCCAE portal, requiring a UAE Pass login, followed by an in-person clinic exam in Dubai or Abu Dhabi where a vet confirms vaccination status and general health. A final check at the airport happens right before the flight. The certificate is valid for 30 days, so confirm your flight before applying.

    - heading: "Armenia's SSFS permit and arrival at Zvartnots"
      body: |
        The State Service for Food Safety issues Armenia's import permit, applied for through ssfs.am ahead of travel. Once granted, and with a current rabies vaccination and MOCCAE-endorsed health certificate in hand, there's no titre test and no routine quarantine for a pet arriving at Zvartnots International Airport in Yerevan.

    - heading: "FlyDubai's direct Caucasus network"
      body: |
        FlyDubai serves an unusually wide spread of destinations across the Caucasus and Central Asia directly from Dubai, and Yerevan is one of them. That means no connecting flight, no second airport to plan around, and a shorter overall journey for the pet. Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha remains a solid fallback if FlyDubai's live animal capacity is booked out on your preferred date.

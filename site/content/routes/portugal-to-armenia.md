  sections:
    - heading: "Armenia's requirements for Portuguese pets"
      body: |
        Armenia accepts personal companion pets from EU countries including Portugal with a valid EU health certificate endorsed by DGAV, current microchip, and up-to-date rabies vaccination. No titre test is required.

        On arrival at Zvartnots International Airport (EVN), the State Service for Food Safety (SSFS, ssfs.am) inspects the health certificate and checks the pet's condition. Healthy pets with complete documentation are released without quarantine.

        Verify requirements with the Armenian Embassy in Lisbon or at ssfs.am before finalising your plans. Import rules can change.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Lisbon to Yerevan"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service between Lisbon (LIS) and Yerevan (EVN). The most practical routing is Turkish Airlines via Istanbul.

        Turkish Airlines operates daily flights from Lisbon to Istanbul Atatürk (IST), with connecting Turkish Airlines Cargo services to Yerevan. The IST-EVN segment is a regular route and Turkish Airlines Cargo has experience with live animal movements on this corridor.

        Emirates via Dubai (DXB) is an alternative, though it involves a longer total journey. Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt is another option, using European hub infrastructure before connecting to Yerevan.

        When booking cargo, confirm live animal acceptance on both legs of the journey, not just the long-haul segment.

    - heading: "Portugal's DGAV export process"
      body: |
        Portugal's veterinary export system requires an official vet (Médico Veterinário Oficial, MVO) registered with DGAV to issue the EU health certificate. Not every private vet is an MVO. Check your vet's authorisation status before booking the appointment.

        The MVO examines the pet, completes the EU-format health certificate, and submits it for DGAV endorsement. The endorsed certificate is valid for 10 days from the examination date. Allow one to three working days for DGAV to complete the endorsement.

        DGAV's offices and regional contacts are listed at dgav.pt. If you are unsure which office covers your location, start at dgav.pt and search for the relevant regional veterinary directorate (DSAVR).

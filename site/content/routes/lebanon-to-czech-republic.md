  sections:
    - heading: "Why the routing takes longer than a straight-line flight suggests"
      body: |
        Prague sits outside the network of any airline flying direct from Beirut, so a connection is unavoidable. Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul is the most reliable first option, given how densely Turkish's IST hub connects into Central Europe. Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt runs a close second, and its Frankfurt Animal Lounge gives a pet a proper rest during the layover rather than sitting in a holding area.

        Emirates SkyCargo via Dubai works too, though the added distance means longer total transit, which matters more for older pets or brachycephalic breeds. When comparing quotes, don't just look at price, check total door-to-door time and how each carrier handles the connection itself, since a badly timed layover can add hours a pet spends in a crate on the ground rather than moving.

    - heading: "Lebanese export paperwork before departure"
      body: |
        Before any flight is booked, Lebanon requires an export permit and an official health certificate from the Ministry of Agriculture, reformatted to EU standard for entry into an EU member state. This has to go through a vet the Ministry recognises.

        Lebanese government processing for this endorsement runs 2 to 4 weeks in practice, sometimes longer around public holidays. Since the certificate itself is only valid for 10 days before travel, timing the endorsement request near the end of the 90-day wait, not at the start, avoids having a valid certificate expire before the flight is confirmed.

    - heading: "The titre test sequence, briefly"
      body: |
        Microchip first, then rabies vaccination, then a 30-day gap before blood is drawn for the FAVN or ELISA test at an EU-approved lab. A result of 0.5 IU/ml or above starts a 90-day countdown from the draw date. The SVA, the Czech Republic's State Veterinary Administration, enforces this at the point of entry and will not accept documentation showing the steps out of order.

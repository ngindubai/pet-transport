  sections:
    - heading: "BLV/FSVO export health certificate: Switzerland's non-EU process"
      body: |
        Switzerland is not a member of the EU, so the EU pet passport system does not apply for exports to Uganda. The correct document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by BLV/FSVO (Bundesamt für Lebensmittelsicherheit und Veterinärwesen / Office fédéral de la sécurité alimentaire et des affaires vétérinaires).

        Contact BLV/FSVO at blv.admin.ch or your cantonal veterinary authority to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement procedure. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Allow time after the vet appointment for BLV/FSVO endorsement within that window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Uganda's specific requirements, which differ from EU-format documents. A pet transport agent with experience in Swiss exports to Africa can help ensure correct formatting for the MAAIF inspection at Entebbe.

    - heading: "Routing from Zurich to Entebbe"
      body: |
        Zurich International Airport (ZRH) has no direct service to Entebbe. Via Brussels is the most direct multi-leg option: a connection from ZRH to Brussels Zaventem (BRU) on SWISS or another carrier, then Brussels Airlines' established BRU-EBB service. Confirm live animal acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-EBB leg.

        Via Addis Ababa on Ethiopian Airlines is the established alternative: Ethiopian connections from ZRH to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-EBB. Ethiopian Cargo has the widest live animal programme in Africa and ADD-EBB is a short regional hop. Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO) is a third option, with NBO-EBB a short regional service, accessible via Gulf or European connections from Zurich.

        Geneva (GVA) offers similar connection options and can be a practical alternative departure airport for Swiss pet owners in western Switzerland or French-speaking regions.

    - heading: "MAAIF: Uganda's import permit requirement"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug manages companion animal imports. The MAAIF permit is the prerequisite document for Uganda entry; without it, officers at Entebbe cannot clear your animal.

        Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and intended travel date. Follow up after submitting to confirm progress. On arrival at Entebbe International Airport (EBB), MAAIF inspects the permit alongside the BLV/FSVO health certificate. No quarantine applies for pets arriving with a valid permit and compliant documentation.

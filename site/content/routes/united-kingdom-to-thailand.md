    - heading: "DLD import permit: how the email process works"
      body: |
        The DLD import permit is applied for by email, not through an online portal. Send your request to qsap_bkk_import@dld.go.th at least 30-60 days before travel. Include your pet's microchip number, species and breed, vaccination and titre test records, your planned arrival date and airport, and your contact details in Thailand.

        The DLD replies with a permit number, which must be included on your AHC. Without this permit number, DLD customs staff at the airport will not process the import and the 30-day quarantine booking cannot be confirmed.

        Allow at least 30 days for the DLD to respond and issue the permit. If you need to change your travel date, email the DLD to amend the permit. Keep the original email chain as part of your documentation.

        The original AHC (with OV signature and APHA endorsement stamp) must travel with your pet and be presented to DLD staff on arrival. Electronic versions and photocopies are explicitly not accepted.

    answer: "Yes. All dogs and cats arriving in Thailand from international origins must complete a mandatory 30-day quarantine at a DLD facility. This applies regardless of vaccination history, documentation quality, or country of origin. There is no waiver."
  - question: "How do I apply for the DLD import permit?"
    answer: "Email qsap_bkk_import@dld.go.th at least 30-60 days before your planned arrival date. Include your pet's microchip number, species, breed, vaccination and titre test records, and planned arrival date and airport. The DLD will issue a permit number by return email. This number must be on your AHC."

  h1: "Pet Transport from Denmark to Dominican Republic"
  overview: |
    Denmark to the Dominican Republic is one of the more straightforward Caribbean routes. The Dominican Republic does not require a titre test or quarantine for compliant arrivals. The main requirements are a microchip, a valid rabies vaccination at least 30 days before entry, current DHPP vaccination for dogs, and a Foedevarestyrelsen-endorsed health certificate.

    The Foedevarestyrelsen endorsement process adds 1-2 working days to the health certificate timeline, so plan the vet appointment with this in mind. Cargo goes via Amsterdam Schiphol with KLM. Four to six weeks is enough preparation time for most pets with current vaccinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "Dominican Republic entry requirements"
      body: |
        DGAG (Direccion General de Ganaderia), part of the Ministerio de Agricultura, conducts veterinary inspections at Las Americas International Airport (SDQ) in Santo Domingo. Officers check documentation before releasing your pet.

        For personal companion pets from Denmark: a microchip (ISO 11784/11785), a valid rabies vaccination (30 days to 12 months before entry), current DHPP vaccination for dogs or panleukopenia/rhinotracheitis/calicivirus for cats, and a health certificate issued within 15 days of travel. No titre test is required. No quarantine for compliant arrivals. Up to five personal pets travel without an import permit.

    - heading: "The Foedevarestyrelsen endorsement process"
      body: |
        Denmark's official veterinary health certificate for non-EU travel must be signed by a Foedevarestyrelsen-authorised official vet and then endorsed by the Foedevarestyrelsen regional office. This two-step process takes 1-2 working days for the endorsement to be returned.

        Book your official vet appointment so the examination falls within 10 days of your travel date, and allow 1-2 working days after the signature for the Foedevarestyrelsen regional office to endorse the certificate. Check fvst.dk for the list of authorised official vets in your region and the regional office responsible for endorsement. If your departure is on a Monday, book the vet appointment no later than the preceding Tuesday to be safe.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Copenhagen to Santo Domingo"
      body: |
        Copenhagen Kastrup (CPH) does not have direct cargo connections to Las Americas Airport (SDQ). The main routing is via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) with KLM. SAS provides regular connections from Copenhagen to Amsterdam, and KLM operates established cargo services to the Dominican Republic.

        An alternative routes via Madrid with Iberia. SAS connects CPH to MAD, and Iberia has cargo services to Santo Domingo. Book through a cargo agent for either option and confirm live animal acceptance before finalising the travel date.

faqs:
  - question: "Can I visit my pet during quarantine at Soekarno-Hatta?"
    answer: "BARANTAN quarantine facilities have visitor protocols that vary. Some facilities permit owner visits at set times; others restrict access during the quarantine period. Confirm with the specific CGK facility through your Indonesian agent before your pet's arrival. An agent who works regularly with the CGK quarantine facility will have current information on visiting arrangements."
  - question: "How does the NVWA endorsement work for the Indonesia health certificate?"
    answer: "NVWA endorses the health certificate after reviewing it for accuracy. The certificate must be in the format specified by BARANTAN in the import permit. Your vet and agent need to confirm the correct format before issuing, as BARANTAN's requirements may differ from the standard EU travel health certificate. Contact NVWA in advance to confirm the processing time for the specific BARANTAN certificate format."
  - question: "Does the FAVN titre test need to be done at a specific laboratory for Indonesia?"
    answer: "Yes. The laboratory must be approved by BARANTAN (Badan Karantina Indonesia) for the titre test result to be accepted. The approved laboratory list can change. Confirm with BARANTAN or your Indonesian agent which laboratories are currently approved for samples from the Netherlands. Your Dutch vet may need to send the sample to an approved laboratory by courier."
  - question: "What documents does my pet need at CGK on arrival?"
    answer: "The BARANTAN import permit (original), the NVWA-endorsed health certificate, the FAVN titre test result, the vaccination certificate, and the microchip record. The BARANTAN officer at CGK checks all of these before transferring the animal to the quarantine facility. Have digital copies as backup, but originals are required for the official inspection."

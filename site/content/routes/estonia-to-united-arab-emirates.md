  h1: "Pet Transport from Estonia to the UAE"
  overview: |
    Skip the blood test. Estonia sits on MOCCAE's short list of low-risk rabies countries, alongside the rest of the EU and the UK, so a dog or cat travelling from Tallinn to Dubai needs no titre test at all, just a current vaccination and the right paperwork. That single fact shaves weeks off what unlisted-country routes in this build require.

    The remaining steps are still real. Estonia's Veterinary and Food Board endorses a MOCCAE-format export certificate close to travel, and MOCCAE's own import permit has to be applied for online before departure. Neither step is optional, but neither is slow.

    There's no direct flight from Tallinn, so the route runs through Riga with airBaltic, or via Istanbul on Turkish Airlines Cargo for owners of larger dogs.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why Estonia skips the titre test"
      body: |
        MOCCAE sorts source countries into two groups: a short low-risk list, and everywhere else. Estonia, as an EU member state, sits in the low-risk group. So does the UK, Ireland, France, and every other EU country. That classification means no rabies antibody titre test, no accredited-lab blood draw, and no waiting period attached to one.

        What's still required: a current rabies vaccination (21 days after a first dose, no extra wait after a booster), a MOCCAE import permit valid for 90 days, and a MOCCAE-format health certificate endorsed on the Estonian side. Minimum age for entry is 12 weeks, lower than the 15-week threshold applied to countries outside the low-risk group.

    - heading: "Routing through Riga or Istanbul"
      body: |
        airBaltic operates Tallinn to Riga on its own schedule, connecting from there to Dubai with a partner carrier. It's a natural fit for owners already flying airBaltic for the first leg. Turkish Airlines Cargo, routed through Istanbul, is the sturdier choice for a larger dog needing dedicated cargo space rather than a cabin booking.

        Book the connecting leg early. Live animal cargo allocations on both routings can fill up around school holidays and the winter peak into Dubai.

  overview: |
    Kazakhstan does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ). Slovenia's EU membership means the export process uses the standard UVHVVR health certificate route.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Kazakhstan requires at the border"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan's veterinary import requirements are administered by the Committee of Veterinary Control and Supervision under the Ministry of Agriculture. Officers inspect documentation at Almaty International Airport (ALA) or Astana International Airport (NQZ) on arrival.

        The core requirements are a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Kazakhstan does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. For 1-2 personal companion animals, no import permit is required.

    - heading: "UVHVVR health certificate process from Slovenia"
      body: |
        Slovenia's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate signed by a UVHVVR-authorised official vet and endorsed by UVHVVR. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. Allow 1-2 working days for UVHVVR endorsement. Check uvhvvr.gov.si for authorised official vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Slovenia to Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Ljubljana (LJU) to Kazakhstan. The standard routing is Austrian Airlines LJU to Vienna (VIE), then Turkish Airlines VIE to Istanbul (IST), then IST to Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ).

        Many owners originate the journey at Vienna Airport for a two-leg booking rather than three. Vienna has direct cargo connections to Istanbul, and Turkish Airlines then connects to both ALA and NQZ.

        Air Astana connects Almaty and Astana to European hubs and may offer an alternative routing. Ask your cargo agent to compare options for your travel dates, particularly if Turkish Airlines summer restrictions apply.

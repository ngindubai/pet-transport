content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Sweden to Panama"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Sweden to Panama is straightforward once the consulate authentication step is understood. Panama requires that the official veterinary health certificate be stamped and signed by the Panamanian Embassy or Consulate in Sweden before the pet travels. This is the most important step that owners miss.

    Beyond that, the requirements are: a microchip, a rabies vaccination at least 30 days old, current additional vaccinations, and an official health certificate from a Jordbruksverket-authorised vet. No titre test, no quarantine, no import permit for personal pets. Four to six weeks is enough preparation time for most pets with current vaccinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "Panama's consulate authentication requirement"
      body: |
        Panama requires all official veterinary health certificates issued abroad to be authenticated by a Panamanian Embassy or Consulate in the country of origin. This authentication confirms the issuing vet's credentials and official status to the MIDA veterinarians at Tocumen International Airport.

        In Sweden, the Panamanian Embassy is in Stockholm. After your Jordbruksverket-authorised official vet issues the health certificate, take or send the original to the Embassy for authentication. Contact the Embassy in advance to confirm the current procedure and fee. Allow 2-3 working days for the authentication.

        The certificate is only valid for 10 days from the date of clinical examination. Plan the vet appointment so that the Embassy process can be completed and you still have time to travel before the 10-day window closes. If the certificate expires before authentication is complete, you will need a new examination.

    - heading: "MIDA requirements and arrival at Tocumen"
      body: |
        MIDA (Ministerio de Desarrollo Agropecuario) handles all incoming animal movements at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) in Panama City. On arrival, MIDA veterinarians inspect the authenticated health certificate, vaccination records, and microchip confirmation. A registration fee of approximately USD 20 (20 balboas) is charged at the MIDA desk.

        No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. Core vaccination requirements for dogs are distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, and parvovirus in addition to rabies. For cats: viral rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia, and feline leukaemia. Your Swedish vet should record all vaccinations on the health certificate so MIDA can verify them on arrival.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Stockholm to Panama City"
      body: |
        Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) does not have direct cargo connections to Tocumen International Airport (PTY). The most practical option routes via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) with KLM. SAS provides regular connections from Stockholm to Amsterdam, and KLM operates cargo to Panama City.

        Copa Airlines, which is headquartered at Tocumen, also operates services from Amsterdam to PTY. Contact Copa Cargo for live animal acceptance procedures. Iberia via Madrid is a third option if AMS capacity is limited. Whichever routing you choose, confirm live animal acceptance on all segments before finalising your departure date.

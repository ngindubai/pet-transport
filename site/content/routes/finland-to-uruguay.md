  overview: |
    Relocating a pet from Finland to Uruguay involves two steps that require careful timing: an MGAP import permit that must be secured before travel, and a Praziquantel antiparasitic treatment for dogs within a specific window before arrival.   sections:
    - heading: "MGAP permit and what Uruguay requires"
      body: |
        Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca) handles all incoming animal movements and inspects pets at Carrasco International Airport (MVD) in Montevideo. The MGAP import permit must be obtained before travel. Contact MGAP through gub.uy/ministerio-ganaderia-agricultura-pesca to start the application. Processing takes several weeks, so begin this step first.

        Requirements for personal companion pets from Finland: ISO-compliant microchip, rabies vaccination (30 days to 12 months old), full vaccination records, and an official health certificate. Dogs must also receive Praziquantel treatment. No quarantine for compliant arrivals. No titre test required for Finland-origin pets.

    - heading: "Praziquantel treatment for dogs"
      body: |
        Uruguay requires all incoming dogs to be treated against Taenia Echinococcus (hydatid tapeworm) using a Praziquantel-containing antiparasitic. The treatment must be administered between 72 hours and 30 days before your dog's arrival at Carrasco Airport. The date, product, dose, and microchip number must be recorded on the health certificate.

        Calculate the treatment window from your specific arrival date. If you arrive on a Monday, the earliest the treatment can be given is the Monday 30 days before, and the latest is the Friday before departure (at least 72 hours before arrival). Work with your Finnish vet to schedule the treatment within this window.

        Cats are not subject to this requirement.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Helsinki to Montevideo"
      body: |
        Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL) does not have direct cargo connections to Carrasco International Airport (MVD). The two main options are via Madrid (MAD) with Iberia or via Lima (LIM) with LATAM.

        Via Madrid: connect from Helsinki to MAD on Finnair, then Iberia cargo to Montevideo. Iberia is one of Europe's most established carriers to Uruguay and has live animal cargo procedures. Via Lima: LATAM operates from European hubs to MVD through Peru. Connect from Helsinki to a hub via Finnair, then LATAM Cargo to Montevideo.

        Journey times via South America run 18-24 hours including connections. Ensure the crate meets IATA standards and has adequate ventilation for an extended trip.

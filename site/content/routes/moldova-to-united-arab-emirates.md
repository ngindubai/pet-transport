  h1: "Pet Transport from Moldova to the United Arab Emirates"
  overview: |
    Ukraine's UAE page on this site opens with a hard fact: no flight has left a Ukrainian airport since February 2022, so every pet crosses overland into Poland or Romania first. Moldova, its neighbour, has no such problem. flydubai and FLYONE both fly Chisinau to Dubai direct, several times a week between them, with no overland leg and no third-country hub.

    The regulatory side is less forgiving than the flight schedule. Moldova sits outside MOCCAE's roughly 50-country low-risk list, so a titre test applies, minimum age for the pet rises to 15 weeks, and the sample has to clear an accredited laboratory before the import permit application makes sense. Budget 8 to 12 weeks, most of it the titre test's own turnaround.

  sections:
    - heading: "A direct flight most owners don't expect from Chisinau"
      body: |
        Chisinau International Airport isn't a major hub, but it has more direct long-haul capacity than its size suggests, largely because of Gulf carrier competition. flydubai, part of the Emirates Group, flies the route several times a week; FLYONE, Moldova's own airline, competes on the same city pair. Either one clears a pet on a single sector, no connecting flight and no change of aircraft or airline partway through the journey.

    - heading: "Why the titre test still applies despite the easier flight"
      body: |
        MOCCAE splits the world into two groups: named on its low-risk list of roughly fifty countries, or everyone else. Moldova falls into the second group, alongside most of Eastern Europe and Africa. That means a titre test at an accredited laboratory, result at least 0.5 IU/ml, applies regardless of how convenient the flight is. MOCCAE doesn't attach a fixed wait once the result clears, unlike Great Britain's 3-month rule, so the pet can travel as soon as the test passes and the import permit comes through.

    - heading: "What actually sets the pace on this route"
      body: |
        Not the flight, which is straightforward, and not MOCCAE's own permit processing, which moves quickly online. The titre test's laboratory turnaround, typically 3 to 5 weeks once the blood is drawn, is the genuine bottleneck. Book that early, keep the MOCCAE certificate's short validity window in mind, and the rest of the process on this route moves faster than almost any other Eastern European origin on this site.

    - heading: "Georgia's advance notification, not a full import permit"
      body: |
        Unlike neighbouring Armenia and Azerbaijan, Georgia's National Food Agency (NFA) works through an advance notification process rather than a formal import permit application. It is a relatively light-touch requirement compared to some other Caucasus destinations, but it still needs to happen before travel, not on arrival.

        Contact nfa.gov.ge at least eight to ten weeks ahead to confirm the current notification process. Alongside it, the standard requirements apply: microchip, current rabies vaccination, and an EU export health certificate specifically adapted for Georgia.

    - heading: "Athens to Tbilisi via Istanbul"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines is the dominant carrier on the Istanbul-Tbilisi leg, and the Athens-Istanbul connection itself is short and well served, including by Aegean Airlines. Total transit typically runs five to seven hours including the connection.

        On arrival at Tbilisi International Airport, the NFA veterinary inspection unit checks documentation. For a well-documented pet arriving from Greece, a recognised rabies-controlled country, this inspection is typically brief, and compliant arrivals are not placed in quarantine.

  overview: |
    Sweden to Azerbaijan is a manageable route. Azerbaijan accepts EU health certificates without requiring a titre test from Swedish pets. The two main cargo paths are Turkish Airlines via Istanbul or AZAL via Frankfurt.

  sections:
    - heading: "Azerbaijan's requirements for Swedish pets"
      body: |
        Azerbaijan accepts personal companion pets from EU countries including Sweden with a valid Jordbruksverket-endorsed EU health certificate, current microchip, and current rabies vaccination. No titre test is required.

        On arrival at Heydar Aliyev International Airport (GYD, Baku), Azerbaijani Ministry of Agriculture vets inspect the certificate and the pet. Compliant arrivals are released without quarantine. Confirm current requirements at agro.gov.az before travel.

    - heading: "AZAL and Turkish Airlines: the two main cargo paths"
      body: |
        Azerbaijan Airlines (AZAL) operates a direct service from Frankfurt (FRA) to Baku (GYD). From Stockholm Arlanda (ARN), this means connecting to Frankfurt first via SAS or Lufthansa, then continuing on AZAL. AZAL Cargo accepts live animals on their Frankfurt-Baku route, though you should confirm this directly with AZAL Cargo before booking.

        Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is the simpler single-carrier option. Daily ARN to IST flights connect with Turkish Airlines Cargo services to GYD. This keeps your pet on one carrier system from Stockholm to Baku.

        Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt is a third option, connecting ARN to FRA and then using Lufthansa Cargo or a partner to reach GYD. Frankfurt's Animal Lounge is a reliable transit facility.

    - heading: "Jordbruksverket OV: Sweden's official vet process"
      body: |
        The EU health certificate for Sweden must be issued by a Jordbruksverket-authorised Officiell veterinär (OV). The OV examines your pet, completes the EU-format certificate, and submits it for Jordbruksverket endorsement. Endorsement usually takes one to three working days.

        The certificate validity begins from the examination date and runs 10 days. Schedule carefully: if your OV appointment is on a Monday and endorsement takes two working days, the certificate is live from Wednesday, giving you until the following Friday to travel.

        Jordbruksverket's website (jordbruksverket.se) provides guidance on finding authorised OVs in your region.

    Fiji Airways started flying Vancouver to Nadi direct on 18 June 2026, three times a week, ending the days when every Canada-Fiji pet move needed a connection through Los Angeles or Auckland. That's a genuine change to the logistics side of this route.

  sections:
    - heading: "A new direct flight changes the logistics, not the paperwork"
      body: |
        Until mid-2026, a Canada-Fiji pet move meant routing through a US or Pacific hub, adding a live-animal transfer on top of an already long process. The Fiji Airways-WestJet codeshare, announced in May 2026, changed that: a direct, year-round Vancouver-Nadi service, upgraded to an Airbus A350 from September 2026, plus WestJet's own code reaching Toronto, Edmonton, Winnipeg and Halifax for owners starting further inland.

        That's a real logistical improvement. It just doesn't shorten the quarantine, since Koronivia's stay length is set by BAF's own risk tier for Canada, not by how the flight is booked.

    - heading: "Why Canada still gets the 30-day tier"
      body: |
        BAF's shortest quarantine track, 7 days, is reserved for a small group of countries it treats as genuinely rabies-free: Australia, New Zealand and a handful of Pacific territories. Canada isn't among them, because rabies persists in Canadian wildlife, mainly bats, foxes and raccoons, even though domestic dog rabies has been eliminated for decades.

        That places Canada in the same Group B bracket BAF applies to the USA and most of Europe: a titre test with a 60-day-to-12-month window, and a 30-day minimum PEQ stay regardless of how clean the pet's own paperwork is. Budget for this from the outset rather than treating it as an edge case.

    - heading: "Budgeting for the move"
      body: |
        The Koronivia stay and the titre test at an approved laboratory are the two costs owners underestimate most. Neither is negotiable, and both need paying regardless of whether you fly direct on the new Vancouver service or connect through WestJet's wider Canadian network.

        Get a specialist agent to confirm the current PEQ facility rate and the cargo fare together before booking flights. Prices on a route this new can shift as demand for the direct Vancouver-Nadi service settles in.

content:
  h1: "Pet Transport France to Jamaica: 2026 Guide"
  overview: "France to Jamaica is a route with a fixed quarantine period and a mandatory advance permit process. Every pet entering Jamaica spends 14 days at Plumb Point Quarantine Station in Kingston, regardless of how complete the documentation is. The VSD import permit (apply to vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm) takes 8-12 weeks and must be in hand before any other step. Two rounds of parasite treatment at precise intervals before arrival add further scheduling precision. Total preparation is 16-20 weeks. From Paris CDG, Air France provides Caribbean connectivity, and British Airways via London Heathrow is the most established routing for Jamaica-bound cargo."

  sections:
    - heading: "Jamaica's fixed requirements for pets from France"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division (VSD), Ministry of Agriculture (moa.gov.jm), controls all pet imports. For pets from France, the requirements are: VSD import permit (apply to vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm, allow 8-12 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; current rabies vaccination within the last 12 months; two rounds each of internal and external parasite treatment at precise timing before arrival; an official health certificate endorsed by the DDPP; and 14 days of mandatory quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station on arrival.

        Restricted breeds (Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Japanese Tosa, Pit Bull Terrier) cannot enter Jamaica.

    - heading: "VSD permit and parasite treatment scheduling"
      body: |
        The VSD import permit is the critical path item. Email vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm with your pet's species, breed, sex, age, microchip number, and proposed travel date. Allow 8-12 weeks for a response. Do not finalise cargo bookings or begin the health certificate process until the permit is confirmed.

        Once you have a travel date, work backwards from the scheduled Jamaican arrival to set the parasite treatment windows. Round 1 (both internal deworming and external de-flea/de-tick) must be given 10-30 days before arrival. Round 2 (both treatments again) must be given 1-10 days before arrival. All four treatments must be administered by a veterinarian and recorded in the health certificate.

    - heading: "Routing from Paris to Jamaica"
      body: |
        Paris Charles de Gaulle (CDG) is the primary departure point. British Airways via London Heathrow (LHR) is one of the most established routings for Jamaica cargo from Europe: Air France or British Airways CDG to LHR, then British Airways LHR to Kingston (KIN) or Montego Bay (MBJ). British Airways operates regular cargo services on its Caribbean routes.

        Air France's own Caribbean network also provides connectivity, with connections to MIA or other Caribbean hubs where onward services to KIN or MBJ are available. Book cargo space early and confirm live animal acceptance on each segment.

    - heading: "DDPP export endorsement from France"
      body: |
        France's official export health certificates for pets going to non-EU countries are endorsed by the Direction Departementale de la Protection des Populations (DDPP) of the relevant French department (usually the department where the vet is based). An official veterinarian prepares the certificate in the format required by Jamaica.

        Allow three to five business days for the DDPP endorsement. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel and must include the VSD permit reference number, microchip, rabies vaccination, and both rounds of parasite treatment records.

faqs:
  - question: "Is Plumb Point Quarantine Station comfortable for pets?"
    answer: "Plumb Point Quarantine Station is Jamaica's official quarantine facility. VSD staff manage the animals during the 14-day stay. Contact VSD at moa.gov.jm for information about the conditions and any personal item allowances (bedding, toys). Many owners send familiar items with the animal to help reduce stress during the quarantine period."
  - question: "Does the 14-day quarantine count from the day of arrival or the day after?"
    answer: "Contact VSD directly for the precise calculation, as this can affect release dates and owner pickup arrangements. VSD staff will advise on the quarantine completion date when the permit is issued."
  - question: "Can I fly my pet directly from Paris to Montego Bay?"
    answer: "Direct flights from CDG to MBJ are unlikely. The most common routing is via London Heathrow (LHR) with British Airways to MBJ, or via Miami (MIA) to MBJ. Confirm available cargo routings with your freight forwarder. Both KIN and MBJ are accepted entry airports for pets."
  - question: "What if my pet is a mixed breed that might resemble a restricted breed?"
    answer: "If there is any uncertainty about whether your dog falls under a restricted breed type, contact VSD at vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm before applying for the permit. VSD makes the final determination. Dogs refused entry on breed grounds at the airport are in a very difficult situation, so clarifying this in advance is strongly recommended."

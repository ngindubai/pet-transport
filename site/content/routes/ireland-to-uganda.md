  overview: |
    Uganda's pet import process is administered by the Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF). The process requires an import permit obtained from MAAIF before travel, and the long haul from Dublin to Entebbe means six to eight weeks preparation is realistic.

    Uganda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Entebbe International Airport. The permit is the first priority: apply through maaif.go.ug as early as possible in your planning.

    Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa and Qatar Airways via Doha are the main cargo options from Dublin. Both carriers serve Entebbe on scheduled services and have experience with live animal cargo.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Uganda requires at the border"
      body: |
        Uganda's pet import requirements are administered by MAAIF (Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries), maaif.go.ug. Officers inspect documentation at Entebbe International Airport (EBB) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid MAAIF import permit (obtained before travel), a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Uganda does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for arrivals with compliant documentation and a valid import permit.

        Apply for the import permit early. Processing times at MAAIF vary, and delays in permit approval will delay your pet's travel. Contact maaif.go.ug directly to confirm current processing times and required documentation for the permit application.

    - heading: "DAFM health certificate process from Ireland"
      body: |
        Ireland's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate signed by a DAFM-registered official vet and endorsed by DAFM. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, allow 1-2 working days for DAFM endorsement. Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the official health certificate must come from a DAFM-registered official vet. Check gov.ie/agriculture for authorised vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Dublin to Entebbe"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Dublin (DUB) to Entebbe (EBB). Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the primary option: Dublin to ADD, then ADD to EBB. Addis Ababa is the main East Africa hub for Ethiopian Airlines, and live animal cargo on this route is well-established.

        Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha (DOH) is a practical alternative. Qatar Airways serves Entebbe from Doha and Hamad International Airport has good live animal facilities. Book through a cargo agent and confirm acceptance on both legs before committing to a date.

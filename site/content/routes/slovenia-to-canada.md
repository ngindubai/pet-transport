  sections:
    - heading: "Why the certificate window matters more than almost anything else"
      body: |
        The official health certificate for Canada is valid for 10 days from the date the vet signs it. In those 10 days, you need to get the UVHVVR endorsement (1-3 working days), travel from Ljubljana to the European hub, and complete the transatlantic leg to Canada. That is achievable, but the timing needs to be deliberate.

        Book your vet appointment on a Monday or Tuesday at the start of a week if you are travelling later in the week or the following Monday. This gives enough time for UVHVVR processing and the flights. The vet appointment should be the last major step, not the first, which means your cargo booking and routing should already be confirmed before you book the vet.

    - heading: "Routing from Ljubljana to Canada"
      body: |
        Ljubljana Airport (LJU) is Slovenia's main airport. It is small, and long-haul or transatlantic connections require a European hub. Vienna (VIE) is the closest and most practical hub, served by Austrian Airlines from Ljubljana daily. Frankfurt (FRA) and Amsterdam (AMS) are reachable via Munich or Vienna connections.

        From Vienna, Lufthansa Group or Air Canada Cargo operate to Toronto, Montreal, and Vancouver. From Amsterdam, KLM Cargo is well established for transatlantic pet moves.

        Confirm pet cargo acceptance at every point in the routing: Ljubljana to Vienna, and Vienna to Canada. Do not assume the cargo booking covers all legs without written confirmation from the cargo agent.

faqs:
  - question: "What is UVHVVR and why does my pet's certificate need their endorsement?"
    answer: "UVHVVR is the Administration of the Republic of Slovenia for Food Safety, Veterinary Sector and Plant Protection, the official government authority responsible for certifying animal health exports. The CFIA requires that health certificates for pets entering Canada from Slovenia be endorsed by this authority, confirming the issuing vet is officially authorised and the certificate meets international standards."
  - question: "Is there a direct cargo route from Ljubljana to Canada?"
    answer: "No direct route exists. All pet cargo from Ljubljana routes via a European hub, typically Vienna (VIE), Frankfurt (FRA), or Amsterdam (AMS). The full journey time to Toronto is typically 14-18 hours including connections."
  - question: "Do I need any Canadian provincial permits on top of CFIA requirements?"

Check the provincial rules for your destination province before travel, particularly if you are moving to Ontario, British Columbia, or Quebec."

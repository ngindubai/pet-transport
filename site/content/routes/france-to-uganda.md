  sections:
    - heading: "MAAIF permit: the document that drives the timeline"
      body: |
        Uganda's import authority for companion animals is the Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries, known as MAAIF (maaif.go.ug). The import permit from MAAIF is mandatory and must be in your possession before your pet's departure.

        Apply early, at least eight to ten weeks before travel. Provide your pet's microchip number, vaccination records including rabies, your intended travel date, and the entry airport (Entebbe International, EBB). If there are any queries on your application, you want time to resolve them without rushing.

        Once the permit arrives, your timeline becomes clear. You can finalise your cargo booking and schedule the DGAL health certificate appointment to fall within the required window before departure.

    - heading: "Routing from Paris to Entebbe"
      body: |
        Brussels Airlines via Brussels is the strongest option for pet cargo from France to Uganda. CDG connects within the Air France-KLM group to Brussels (BRU), and Brussels Airlines runs BRU-EBB direct as part of its East Africa network. The airline has long-standing experience with cargo on this corridor.

        Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is a well-established alternative with good live animal cargo procedures. CDG-ADD-EBB is a reliable routing with Ethiopian's extensive African connections handling the Addis to Entebbe leg.

        Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO-EBB) adds a short regional leg but is a viable option when other routes are full. Confirm live animal transfer procedures at Nairobi before booking via this option.

    - heading: "The DGAL certificate: fitting the 10-day window"
      body: |
        France's health certificate for companion animal export is issued by a DGAL-authorised official vet and endorsed by the DDPP. The certificate is typically valid when issued within 10 days of your pet's arrival in Uganda.

        Book your vet appointment to fall within that window but also allow enough time for the DDPP endorsement, which can take a day or two. If your travel date is firm, count back 10 days and identify the earliest acceptable appointment date, then book as close to the 10-day mark as practical to maximise freshness.

        On arrival at Entebbe, the MAAIF inspection team checks the permit, health certificate, microchip, and vaccination records. Compliant arrivals are cleared without quarantine. Uganda applies a standard veterinary entry inspection rather than a quarantine regime for properly documented pets from countries like France.

  sections:
    - heading: "Kazakhstan's entry rules for companion pets"
      body: |
        Pet entry into Kazakhstan is administered by the Committee of Veterinary Control and Supervision under the Ministry of Agriculture. For personal companion pets from Estonia, the requirements are a current rabies vaccination and a health certificate from the Estonian competent authority. There is no titre test requirement and no quarantine for compliant arrivals.

        Kazakhstan has two main international airports that handle live animal cargo: Almaty International Airport (ALA) and Astana International Airport (NQZ). Your routing decision affects which entry point you use, and it is worth confirming with the carrier which city the live cargo will arrive in. Kazakhstani border veterinary services inspect documentation on arrival.

        Like many Central Asian destinations, Kazakhstan's enforcement of rules is thorough at the border. An accurate, properly formatted health certificate reduces the chance of delays. A pet transport agent experienced in Central Asian corridors is helpful for this route.

    - heading: "Exporting from Estonia to a non-EU destination"
      body: |
        The EU pet passport that covers intra-EU travel does not extend to Kazakhstan. For this move, you need an official export health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian in Estonia and endorsed by the Pohllumajandus- ja Toiduamet (Agricultural Board) at agri.ee.

        The Agricultural Board can point you to authorised vets in your area. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel, so schedule the vet appointment close to your departure. Confirm with the Agricultural Board whether Kazakhstan has any specific format requirements for the certificate before the vet drafts it.

    - heading: "Routing from Tallinn to Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        Tallinn does not have direct connections to Almaty or Astana, so this journey involves at least two legs. The most commonly used routings for Estonia-to-Kazakhstan pet cargo are:

        Via Istanbul (IST): airBaltic or connections from TLL to a European hub, then Turkish Airlines Cargo from IST to ALA or NQZ. Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animals at IST and has regular cargo flights to Kazakhstan.

        Via Frankfurt (FRA): airBaltic from TLL to FRA (or via RIX), then Air Astana cargo from FRA to ALA or NQZ. Air Astana's cargo service from Frankfurt to Kazakhstan is a direct Central Asia option worth considering.

        Both routings are viable. The choice often comes down to which carrier has live cargo capacity on the dates you need. Book well in advance and get written confirmation on every segment.

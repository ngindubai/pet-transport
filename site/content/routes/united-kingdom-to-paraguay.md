content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from the UK to Paraguay"
  overview: |
    Paraguay does not run the elaborate rabies-titre pathway that GB applies in the other direction. SENACSA's companion animal entry process centres on a CVI, current vaccinations and a clinical check on arrival, which makes this route lighter on paperwork than the reverse trip.

    Two things catch owners out. First, a short list of banned breeds, Pit Bull types, American Bully, Cane Corso and several mastiff breeds among them, are refused entry outright regardless of documentation. Second, there is no nonstop UK-Paraguay flight, so the plan has to route through Madrid, the one European city with a nonstop link to Asuncion.

  sections:
    - heading: "What SENACSA actually asks for"
      body: |
        A dog or cat arriving in Paraguay needs an ISO microchip, current core vaccinations, and a CVI issued by APHA on behalf of a UK Official Veterinarian. Dogs need distemper, parvovirus, infectious hepatitis and leptospirosis cover alongside rabies; cats need panleukopenia, rhinotracheitis and calicivirus cover.

        SENACSA's published guidance does not set out a mandatory titre test for pets entering from the UK, unlike the reverse journey. That is a meaningful difference in workload, but it is also the kind of detail that is worth double-checking directly with SENACSA close to your travel date. Requirements for Latin American import authorities are not always updated in English at the same pace as the Spanish original, and Paraguay's rules can move.

        SENACSA also caps non-commercial pet imports at five animals per owner. Bring a sixth, and the shipment shifts into commercial import territory, with its own separate process and paperwork.

    - heading: "Routing through Madrid, and the breed list that ends a booking"
      body: |
        Air Europa is the only airline flying nonstop into Asuncion from Europe, out of Madrid Barajas, several times a week on a Boeing 787. From London, British Airways or Iberia to Madrid, then the Air Europa connection south, is the routing most agents book for this move. There is no shorter path.

        Before any of that gets booked, check the dog's breed against Paraguay's import ban. Pit Bull-type dogs, American Staffordshire Terrier, American Bully, Cane Corso, Boerboel and several named mastiff breeds are refused entry outright, no exceptions for documentation or temperament assessments. This is not a quarantine or extra-paperwork situation. It is a straight refusal, so it needs settling before anything else on this list.

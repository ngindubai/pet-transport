  overview: |
    Turkish Airlines flies Istanbul to Panama City non-stop, a genuine rarity on this site's Latin America corridors, most of which route through a third hub. Panama's own entry rules are equally straightforward: no titre test, no quarantine, no import permit for a personal pet.

    The one step that trips owners up is consular. Panama requires its own diplomatic mission in Turkey to stamp and sign the health certificate before departure, on top of TRGM's endorsement, and that's a different office with its own hours and processing time.

This is a separate appointment from the vet visit, and it needs to happen after the certificate is issued but before departure, so build it into the 10-day certificate validity window rather than treating it as an afterthought.

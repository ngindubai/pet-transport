  overview: |
    Lebanon accepts EU-origin pets without a titre test requirement, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport. Sweden's export process follows the standard EU official health certificate route through Jordbruksverket.

    The timing requirement that most commonly causes delays on this route is Lebanon's 30-day pre-entry rabies vaccination rule. Check your pet's vaccination date and confirm it will be at least 30 days before your planned arrival in Lebanon. The standard 21-day minimum used by many other countries does not apply here.

    Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) connects to Istanbul via Turkish Airlines, and IST is the transfer point for the final leg to Beirut. Plan cargo booking at least 4-6 weeks ahead.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Lebanon requires at the border"
      body: |
        Lebanon's veterinary import requirements are administered by the Ministry of Agriculture. Officers inspect documentation at Beirut Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid rabies vaccination administered at least 30 days before entry, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Lebanon does not require a titre test for EU-origin pets. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. For 1-2 personal companion animals, no import permit is required.

        The 30-day vaccination window is the most frequently misunderstood requirement on Lebanon routes from Northern Europe. Confirm the date before booking.

    - heading: "Jordbruksverket health certificate process from Sweden"
      body: |
        Sweden's export health certificate for non-EU destinations is the EU-format official veterinary certificate. It must be signed by a Jordbruksverket-authorised official vet and then endorsed by Jordbruksverket (Swedish Board of Agriculture). Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Schedule the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, submit the certificate to Jordbruksverket for endorsement. Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the official health certificate must come from a Jordbruksverket-authorised official vet. Check jordbruksverket.se for the list of authorised vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Stockholm to Beirut"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) to Beirut (BEY). Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul (IST) is the primary established route. Turkish Airlines operates ARN-IST regularly, and IST to BEY is a well-served route.

        Book through an approved Turkish Airlines cargo agent. Confirm pet acceptance on both the ARN-IST and IST-BEY legs. If travelling June to September, check for temperature embargoes on the IST connection. Brachycephalic breed restrictions apply year-round.

        SAS Cargo can connect Stockholm to European hubs with onward routing to Lebanon, but this adds an extra handling step. Your cargo agent can advise on the most practical routing for your travel dates.

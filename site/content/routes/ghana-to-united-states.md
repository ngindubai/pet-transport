  sections:
    - heading: "VSD and the Ghana export certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        Ghana's Veterinary Services Directorate (VSD), under the Ministry of Food and Agriculture, endorses health certificates for pet exports. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a VSD-authorised official vet and endorsed by VSD with an official stamp.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history with titre test result (laboratory name, blood draw date, result in IU/ml), owner details, and fitness to travel. Contact VSD (vetagri.gov.gh) for the list of authorised vets and the current certificate format for US export.

        For US CDC purposes, the titre test result from a USDA-recognised laboratory must be included in the documentation package alongside the VSD-endorsed certificate. Both are required at the CDC inspection on arrival.

    - heading: "Delta direct from Accra to JFK: the CDC facility advantage"
      body: |
        Delta Air Lines operates direct flights from Kotoka International Airport (ACC) in Accra to New York JFK. Delta Cargo accepts live animals on suitable aircraft. JFK has a CDC-registered animal care facility, which is required for dogs from CDC high-risk countries including Ghana.

        A direct Accra-to-JFK flight avoids transit hub complications and provides a relatively straightforward routing for this corridor. Confirm live animal acceptance, crate dimension requirements, and seasonal restrictions directly with Delta Cargo before booking.

        British Airways via London Heathrow and Air France via Paris CDG are alternatives, both connecting to multiple US airports with CDC-registered facilities. Confirm the specific US destination airport has a qualifying facility for whichever routing you choose.

  h1: "Pet Transport from Australia to Botswana"
  overview: |
    Two airlines cover the only direct long-haul link this route depends on: Qantas and South African Airways both fly Johannesburg to Perth, between them most days of the week but neither one daily. That scheduling reality shapes the booking timeline more than anything on Botswana's own import checklist.

    The regulatory side is comparatively light. Botswana doesn't ask for a rabies titre test from any country of origin, Australia included, just a vaccination at least 30 days old on arrival and a signed attestation that no animal disease movement restrictions apply where the pet is leaving from. DAFF's own export process on the Australian side is the more paperwork-heavy half of the journey. Realistically, budget 8 to 12 weeks.

  sections:
    - heading: "Two carriers, neither one daily"
      body: |
        Owners researching this route sometimes assume a Perth-Johannesburg flight is available whenever they need it, since two airlines cover the sector. In practice, Qantas runs it three times a week and South African Airways around five, and the two schedules don't necessarily fill every gap between them. Check both directly rather than assuming daily availability.

        Anyone flying from Sydney or Melbourne needs a domestic connection to Perth first, an extra step this route requires that some other Australia-outbound journeys through Johannesburg avoid.

    - heading: "The attestation Australian owners tend to miss"
      body: |
        Botswana's Department of Veterinary Services doesn't stop at a health certificate and an import permit. It also wants a signed statement confirming that no animal disease movement restriction currently applies in the Australian state the pet is leaving from. Most owners researching this route haven't encountered a requirement quite like it on other destinations, so it's worth raising with your DAFF-accredited vet at the first appointment rather than discovering the gap once the import permit application is already underway.

        Beyond that single document, Botswana's import side is genuinely straightforward: no titre test, no fixed multi-month wait, just a vaccination timed correctly and a permit that DVS typically processes quickly once the file is complete.

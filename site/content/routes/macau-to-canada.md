  h1: "Pet Transport from Macau to Canada: 2026 Guide"
  overview: |
    Here's the detail that changes this route's shape: the Canadian Food Inspection Agency's tougher "high-risk country" rabies protocol, the one that adds a 7-month minimum age and a strict microchip-before-vaccination rule, applies to mainland China. It specifically excludes Macau, Hong Kong and Taiwan. A pet leaving Macau's Special Administrative Region enters Canada under the same straightforward path as a pet from almost anywhere else: a rabies vaccination certificate, and nothing more exotic than that.

    The friction on this route isn't the destination, it's the middle. Macau International Airport carries no transpacific cargo, so every pet makes a road or ferry transfer to Hong Kong first, adding a leg that needs its own timing and its own handler.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why Macau is not treated as high-risk by CFIA"
      body: |
        CFIA's high-risk list covers mainland China but names Macau, Hong Kong and Taiwan as exclusions. That distinction is easy to miss if you're reading a generic "China" guide rather than one written for this specific SAR, and it has real consequences: no 7-month minimum age, no forced microchip-before-vaccine timing rule, no restriction to a handful of designated entry airports, and no CFIA import permit requirement. Source: Canadian Food Inspection Agency, 'Countries at high-risk for dog rabies', inspection.canada.ca, 2026.

        Cats have it simpler still. Canada asks for a rabies vaccination certificate only once a cat is 3 months or older, no titre test, no quarantine, no permit.
    - heading: "The Hong Kong bottleneck, not the Canadian border"
      body: |
        Macau's own airport, MFM, doesn't run transpacific cargo, so Air Macau's regional network is no use for this leg. The workable path is a road transfer across the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macau Bridge, or a ferry, about an hour door to door, into Hong Kong International Airport, then a direct Cathay Pacific Cargo flight to Vancouver or Toronto.

        Book that transfer as its own line item. It's short, but it involves clearing a second jurisdiction's cargo terminal with a live animal, and slots fill fast around Lunar New Year and the summer travel peak. Get the crate specification approved by Cathay Cargo before you commit to a flight date, not after.

  overview: |
    Uruguay requires an MGAP import permit before travel and a Praziquantel treatment for dogs within a specific window before arrival. Neither step is complicated, but both require advance planning. There is no quarantine for compliant arrivals and no titre test is required for Sweden-origin pets.

  sections:
    - heading: "MGAP permit and import requirements"
      body: |
        Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca) administers animal import rules and inspects incoming pets at Carrasco International Airport (MVD) in Montevideo. Before travel, you need an import permit from MGAP. Apply early: processing takes several weeks.

        The requirements for personal companion pets from Sweden are: an ISO-compliant microchip, a valid rabies vaccination (30 days to 12 months old), current full vaccination records, and an official health certificate. For dogs, Praziquantel treatment against Taenia Echinococcus is also required. No titre test is required, and no quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

    - heading: "Praziquantel treatment for dogs"
      body: |
        Uruguay requires all incoming dogs to be treated against Taenia Echinococcus (hydatid tapeworm) with a Praziquantel antiparasitic. The treatment must be administered between 72 hours and 30 days before the dog's arrival in Uruguay.

        Work backwards from your scheduled arrival at Carrasco Airport. If you arrive on a Monday, the treatment window opens 30 days before (10 weeks earlier) and closes 72 hours before (the Friday before travel). The MGAP veterinary certificate records the product name, dose, date, and the dog's microchip number. MGAP officers check this on arrival.

        Cats are not subject to the Praziquantel requirement.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Stockholm to Montevideo"
      body: |
        There are no direct cargo flights from Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) to Carrasco International Airport (MVD). The two main European routing options are via Madrid with Iberia or via Lima with LATAM.

        Via Madrid: SAS connects ARN to MAD, and Iberia cargo serves Montevideo from Madrid. Iberia is one of Europe's most established carriers to Uruguay. Contact Iberia Cargo for live animal procedures.

        Via Lima: LATAM connects European hubs to MVD via Lima (LIM). The connection from Stockholm requires a European hub leg before the transatlantic LATAM flight. Journey times via South America typically run 18-24 hours. Ensure the crate size and construction meet IATA requirements for this duration.

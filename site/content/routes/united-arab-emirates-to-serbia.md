content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from the United Arab Emirates to Serbia"
  overview: |
    Leaving the UAE with a pet takes more paperwork than arriving in Serbia does. MOCCAE requires an export health certificate through its own online portal, a clinical exam by an approved vet, and then a second, separate inspection at the airport's Cargo Village before boarding is confirmed. Two vet visits, not one, and both need to land inside a 30-day certificate window.

    Serbia's side, by contrast, is one of the lighter entries on this route list. The UAE sits on the Veterinary Directorate's Annex 2, so only a 21-day wait after rabies vaccination applies, no titre test needed at all.

  sections:
    - heading: "MOCCAE's two-vet-visit export process"
      body: |
        The export certificate itself is only step one. A MOCCAE-approved vet in Dubai or Abu Dhabi handles the clinical exam and vaccination check, and the certificate that comes out of it is valid for 30 days. Before the pet actually boards, though, a second and separate MOCCAE vet inspection happens at the airport's Cargo Village, the final clearance point. Skip the timing on that second visit and the certificate being valid on paper doesn't help; the pet still doesn't fly.

    - heading: "Why the flight, not the paperwork, is the harder part"
      body: |
        Air Serbia connects Dubai and Belgrade directly, in under six hours, and refuses every pet on that route regardless of size or class, the same rule it applies to UK and Qatar sectors. Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul is the standard workaround, with Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt as a second option for owners already working with a forwarder set up on that network.

    - heading: "Serbia's side of the border: comparatively light"
      body: |
        Once the pet lands in Belgrade, Serbia's own requirements are comparatively light. The UAE's Annex 2 status means a current rabies vaccination and a 21-day wait cover the whole of it, no titre test, no import permit for a personal move of one or two animals. The contrast with the two-vet-visit MOCCAE export process on the way out is stark; getting into Serbia is the easy half of this particular round trip.

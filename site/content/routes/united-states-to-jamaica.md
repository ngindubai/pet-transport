content:
  h1: "Pet Transport United States to Jamaica"
  overview: |
    Miami to Kingston takes under two hours in the air. Getting a pet cleared to make that flight takes closer to five months. Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division runs a sequential process: a Preliminary Application Form has to be approved before an owner can even start the vaccination and FAVN titre test sequence that counts towards entry, and doing those steps early risks having the whole application rejected.

    None of this is unique to US-origin pets; it's simply how Jamaica handles every arrival. What changes for American owners is the ease of the journey itself, direct flights from three major hubs, once the regulatory side finally clears.

  sections:
    - heading: "The Preliminary Application: Jamaica's first, non-negotiable step"
      body: |
        VSD requires this form submitted and approved before an owner begins the rabies vaccination and titre test steps that count towards Jamaican entry. Vaccinating ahead of approval, hoping it will retroactively qualify, is explicitly discouraged by VSD's own guidance. Build in 4-5 months from the very start of the process to a realistic travel date, not from when you'd like to book the flight.

    - heading: "FAVN titre test and Plumb Point quarantine"
      body: |
        A FAVN titre test, drawn 30 days after the qualifying rabies vaccination at a VSD-approved laboratory, needs to clear 0.5 IU/ml. A pet over 12 months old receiving its first rabies vaccination doesn't qualify for entry until three months after that dose, on top of the 30-day titre wait.

        On arrival, pets go to the National Quarantine Facility at Plumb Point, Kingston, for up to 14 days. VSD inspectors can release a healthy, fully documented pet earlier, but that's discretionary, and planning around it is a mistake.

    - heading: "The USDA APHIS side"
      body: |
        A USDA-accredited vet examines the pet and prepares the health certificate, which then needs APHIS endorsement, ideally issued within 10 days of the actual travel date to satisfy Jamaica's own validity window.

    - heading: "Three direct US gateways into Kingston"
      body: |
        American Airlines Cargo flies non-stop from Miami, JetBlue covers both Miami and JFK, and Delta operates direct from Atlanta. Whichever US city you're departing from, a non-stop option is realistically available, a genuine advantage once the long regulatory runway is behind you.

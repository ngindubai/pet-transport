  sections:
    - heading: "DGAV: Portugal's competent authority for pet export"
      body: |
        DGAV (Direção-Geral de Alimentação e Veterinária) is Portugal's General Directorate of Food and Veterinary, the authority that endorses export health certificates for animals leaving Portugal. Your vet issues the CVI in the MAPA format, DGAV reviews it and stamps it with the official endorsement.

        DGAV regional offices operate around Portugal. Turnaround is typically 2 to 5 working days. Contact the nearest DGAV office before booking travel to confirm their current schedule. The endorsed CVI is valid for 10 days from the endorsement date, so plan the endorsement appointment to land within 10 days of your pet's arrival in Brazil.
    - heading: "TAP and the Lisbon-Brazil corridor"
      body: |
        TAP Air Portugal operates multiple weekly direct flights from Lisbon to São Paulo Guarulhos, Rio de Janeiro Galeão, Brasília, Recife, Fortaleza, and other Brazilian cities. For a Portugal-to-Brazil pet move, this frequency gives flexibility when booking cargo space.

        TAP Cargo handles live animal cargo on its Brazil routes. Contact TAP Cargo well in advance, provide the crate dimensions and estimated pet weight, and confirm acceptance before finalising travel plans. For large dogs in big crates, early booking matters.

faqs:
  - question: "Does Brazil quarantine pets arriving from Portugal?"
    answer: "No. Brazil does not require quarantine for dogs and cats from Portugal. The MAPA inspection at Guarulhos or Galeão is a documentary verification, not a quarantine hold. If the microchip, vaccination, antiparasitic treatment record, and DGAV-endorsed CVI are all in order, the pet is cleared for entry on arrival."
  - question: "Does the CVI need to be in Portuguese for Brazil?"
    answer: "The MAPA format for the CVI can be in Portuguese or English. Since both Portugal and Brazil are Portuguese-speaking, a Portuguese-language CVI is well-understood by MAPA inspectors. However, the standard EU health certificate format that DGAV endorses for international travel is typically bilingual or in English. Confirm with your vet and DGAV that the format aligns with MAPA's current requirements."
  - question: "Can I bring my pet as cabin baggage from Lisbon to Brazil?"
    answer: "TAP Air Portugal allows small pets in the cabin on some routes within Europe, but for transatlantic flights from Lisbon to Brazil, pets travel as cargo in the hold, not in the passenger cabin. Confirm the current TAP policy for the specific flight route and date, as policies do change."
  - question: "How early should I book TAP cargo for a Lisbon to São Paulo pet move?"
    answer: "Book at least 3 to 4 weeks before travel, and earlier for peak summer months (July-August) and the Christmas-New Year period when both passenger and cargo capacity tightens. Provide the exact crate dimensions and total weight when making the enquiry, as hold space allocation depends on aircraft type and specific flight loading."

  sections:
    - heading: "FAVN titre test: the 90-day constraint for Panama to Sweden"
      body: |
        Panama is classified as an unlisted third country under EU Implementing Regulation 2026/636. This means the FAVN titre test is mandatory before Jordbruksverket (the Swedish Board of Agriculture) can clear your pet for entry. Blood must be drawn at least 30 days after the most recent rabies vaccination. The result must show a neutralising antibody level of at least 0.5 IU/ml at an EU-approved laboratory.

        The 90-day waiting period starts on the blood draw date. Jordbruksverket states explicitly that this wait cannot be shortened. After the 90 days, the MIDA/DECA export health certificate can be issued and the EU-format certificate becomes valid for 10 days from the DECA endorsement date to the documentary checks at ARN or GOT. Keep the original FAVN laboratory report: it must accompany the health certificate throughout the journey.

    - heading: "Panama's SITERPA export process and Iberia's PTY-MAD route"
      body: |
        Panama's export health certificates for companion animals are processed through the SITERPA portal (Sistema de Información y Trazabilidad en Exportación de Productos y Recursos Agropecuarios). The certificate is completed by the veterinarian online and then countersigned by DECA (Dirección Ejecutiva de Cuarentena Agropecuaria) within MIDA.

        The critical constraint is validity: the MIDA/DECA certificate is valid for only 72 hours from the DECA countersignature. This means the certificate appointment must be booked within 72 hours of the actual departure time from Tocumen International Airport (PTY). Coordinate the SITERPA appointment and the flight departure carefully.

        Iberia operates direct PTY-MAD services, making Madrid the most practical European hub for Panama-to-Sweden pet cargo. From Madrid, Iberia and SAS connect to ARN and GOT. If your routing involves a connection at Amsterdam, KLM offers AMS-ARN and AMS-GOT services. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance on all legs.

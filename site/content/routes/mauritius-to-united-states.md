  sections:
    - heading: "MAIFS and the Mauritius export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Mauritius Ministry of Agro-Industry and Food Security (MAIFS) is the competent authority for animal health documentation for exports. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a MAIFS-authorised vet.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication is required at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Mauritius.

        Mauritius is known for having strict import requirements for pets arriving INTO the island (including FAVN titre test, neutering requirement, and quarantine). These requirements apply in the opposite direction of travel: they do not affect pets being exported FROM Mauritius to the USA.

    - heading: "Routing from Mauritius to the USA via European or Gulf hubs"
      body: |
        Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport (MRU) has no direct transatlantic US service. Air Mauritius connects the island to London Heathrow, Paris CDG, and regional hubs. British Airways flies direct from London Heathrow to multiple US cities. Air France connects Paris to numerous US gateways. Emirates via Dubai provides an alternative route.

        The total journey time from Mauritius to the USA is substantial (typically 18 to 25 hours with connections), which is worth factoring into the welfare planning for your pet. IATA crate sizing and temperature limits at each hub should be confirmed with your cargo agent.

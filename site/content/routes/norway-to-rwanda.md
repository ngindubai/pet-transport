    Rwanda's pet import system requires a permit from the Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) before travel. Rwanda does not require a titre test for EEA-origin pets, and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals at Kigali International Airport.   sections:
    - heading: "What Rwanda requires at the border"
      body: |
        Rwanda's pet import requirements are administered by RAB (Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board), rab.gov.rw. Officers inspect documentation at Kigali International Airport (KGL) on arrival.

        The requirements are a valid RAB import permit, a valid rabies vaccination, an official health certificate issued within 10 days of travel, and an ISO-compliant microchip. Rwanda does not require a titre test for EEA-origin pets. No quarantine applies for arrivals with a valid permit and compliant documentation.

        Apply for the import permit early: it must be in hand before travel, and RAB processing can take several weeks.

    - heading: "The Norwegian health certificate process"
      body: |
        Norway's health certificate for non-EU destinations must be signed by a Mattilsynet-authorised official vet and then endorsed by the Mattilsynet regional office. Both steps must complete within 10 days of departure.

        Book the vet examination within 10 days of your travel date. After signing, submit the certificate to the Mattilsynet regional office and allow 1-2 working days for endorsement. Your regular vet can handle vaccinations and microchipping, but the health certificate must come from a Mattilsynet-authorised official vet. Check mattilsynet.no for authorised vets.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Oslo to Kigali"
      body: |
        There is no direct cargo service from Oslo (OSL) to Kigali (KGL). Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the primary option. Addis Ababa is Ethiopian Airlines' main hub for East Africa, with direct connections to KGL. Oslo to ADD is a direct or one-stop flight depending on the schedule.

        Qatar Airways Cargo via Doha (DOH) is a practical alternative, with Hamad International Airport providing good live animal facilities. Turkish Airlines via Istanbul (IST) also connects to Kigali and may offer competitive rates. Book through a cargo agent and confirm acceptance on all legs.

content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Panama to Turkey"
  overview: |
    Panama City to Istanbul is a genuine outlier in this build: a real, non-stop cargo flight between Latin America and Turkey. Turkish Airlines flies it direct, which most of Panama's Caribbean and South American neighbours simply don't have. That's the good news.

    The rest of the process is standard for an unlisted origin. Turkey requires the full FAVN titre test from Panama, with a 90-day wait counted from the blood draw. The direct flight shortens the journey itself; it doesn't shorten the paperwork timeline in front of it.

  sections:
    - heading: "A rare direct flight, and what it changes"
      body: |
        Most Latin American and Caribbean routes in this build need at least one connection to reach Istanbul, through Madrid, Miami or a South American hub. Panama is the exception. Turkish Airlines operates Tocumen International (PTY) to Istanbul non-stop, which cuts out a cargo handover and the associated transfer risk that comes with switching carriers mid-journey.

        Copa Airlines, based at the same Tocumen hub, offers a fallback routing through a European partner if the direct Turkish Airlines cargo slot isn't available on your preferred date, though it means the same two-handover process most other origins on this list face by default.

    - heading: "The titre test still sets the real timeline"
      body: |
        Panama's own export side is genuinely simple: MIDA's DINASA division endorses the health certificate, no separate export permit is needed, and there's no titre test requirement leaving Panama itself. The complexity sits entirely on Turkey's side of the process.

        A FAVN or RNATT titre test is required, blood drawn at least 30 days after your pet's rabies vaccination, and a 90-day wait counted from that draw date, not from when the result comes back. Arriving without a valid test means 21 days of quarantine at the owner's expense, a costly way to find out the direct flight didn't save you the preparation time.

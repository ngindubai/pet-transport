content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Finland to Jamaica"
  overview: |
    Jamaica is one of the more demanding Caribbean destinations for pet import. A Veterinary Import Permit from the VSD must be in hand before travel, and all incoming pets may face up to 14 days of quarantine at the Plumb Point National Quarantine Facility in Kingston.

    Allow 10 to 12 weeks from starting preparations to travel day. Apply for the VSD import permit first: it is the most time-consuming step. Cargo from Helsinki-Vantaa goes via Amsterdam with KLM or via London with British Airways.

  sections:
    - heading: "Import permit and VSD requirements"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division (VSD) issues Veterinary Import Permits for all incoming companion animals. The permit must be obtained before your pet travels. Apply to the VSD at vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm or by phone at +1 876-977-2489, providing your pet's details, travel date, and health status. Allow 8-12 weeks for permit processing.

        Full vaccination requirements for dogs include distemper, parvovirus, leptospirosis, hepatitis, and parainfluenza in addition to rabies. Cats require herpesvirus 1, calicivirus, and panleukopenia. The microchip must be implanted before any rabies vaccination for the vaccination to be valid under Jamaican rules.

    - heading: "Parasite treatment schedule"
      body: |
        Jamaica requires two rounds of internal parasite treatment and two rounds of external parasite treatment. The timing is specific.

        For internal parasites: two treatments at least 14 days apart. The second treatment must fall within 7 days of transport. For external parasites: two treatments at least 14 days apart. The second must be within 48 hours of transport.

        Start planning the treatment schedule once your travel date is confirmed. The first internal treatment happens 21 or more days before travel. External treatments follow the same logic with a tighter 48-hour final window. All treatments are recorded on the health certificate, which must be issued by a Ruokavirasto-authorised official vet within 10 days of travel.

    - heading: "Quarantine at Plumb Point and cargo from Helsinki"
      body: |
        On arrival at Norman Manley International Airport (KIN) in Kingston, your pet will be taken to the National Quarantine Facility at Plumb Point for up to 14 days under VSD supervision. The quarantine fee is charged per day by the VSD. Plan your own stay in Jamaica around the quarantine period, as you will not have access to your pet until it ends.

        From Helsinki-Vantaa (HEL), the main cargo routing is via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) with KLM. Finnair connects HEL to AMS, and KLM operates cargo to Kingston. An alternative routes via London Heathrow with British Airways World Cargo. Book through a cargo agent and confirm live animal acceptance on all segments before finalising your travel date.

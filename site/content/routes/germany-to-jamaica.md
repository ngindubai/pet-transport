  overview: "Germany to Jamaica is a route with a fixed quarantine period and a mandatory advance permit process. Every pet entering Jamaica undergoes a 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station in Kingston regardless of compliance.   sections:
    - heading: "Jamaica's import requirements for pets from Germany"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division (VSD), Ministry of Agriculture (moa.gov.jm), governs all pet imports. The fixed requirements are: a VSD import permit (apply to vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm, allow 8-12 weeks); ISO 11784/11785 microchip; current rabies vaccination within the last 12 months; two rounds each of internal and external parasite treatment at specific intervals before arrival; an official health certificate endorsed by Germany's Veterinäramt; and a mandatory 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point Quarantine Station in Kingston on arrival.

        Restricted breeds (Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Japanese Tosa, Pit Bull Terrier) cannot enter Jamaica under any circumstances.

        Arrival airports are Norman Manley International (KIN) in Kingston and Sangster International (MBJ) in Montego Bay. VSD inspects pets on arrival before transferring them to quarantine.

    - heading: "The VSD import permit and parasite treatment schedule"
      body: |
        The VSD import permit is the first step. Email vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm with your pet's species, breed, sex, age, and microchip number, and your proposed travel date. VSD responds within 8-12 weeks with the permit or a request for additional information. Do not book cargo or start the health certificate process until the permit is confirmed.

        The parasite treatment schedule is calculated from your pet's arrival date in Jamaica. Round 1 (both internal deworming and external de-flea/de-tick treatment) must be given between 10 and 30 days before arrival. Round 2 (both treatments again) must be given between 1 and 10 days before arrival. A veterinarian must administer all treatments and record them. The health certificate must reference all four treatments with dates.

    - heading: "Routing from Germany to Jamaica"
      body: |
        There are no direct cargo routes from Germany to Jamaica. The most common routing is via London Heathrow (LHR) with British Airways: FRA or MUC to LHR via Lufthansa, then LHR to KIN or MBJ with British Airways Cargo. British Airways operates regular passenger and cargo services from London to Kingston.

        An alternative is via Miami (MIA): Lufthansa Cargo from FRA to MIA, then an American Airlines or other carrier connection to KIN or MBJ. Each routing option has different transit times, which affects how you plan the parasite treatment timing relative to the Jamaican arrival date.

    - heading: "German export documentation: Veterinäramt endorsement"
      body: |
        Germany's export health certificates for pets are prepared by an official veterinarian (Amtstierarzt) and endorsed by the local Veterinäramt (district veterinary office). The Veterinäramt operates at district (Kreis) or city level across Germany. Contact your local Veterinäramt to understand their process and timeline for non-EU destination health certificates.

        Allow three to five business days for the endorsement. The health certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. It must include the microchip number, current rabies vaccination details, both rounds of parasite treatment records, and the VSD import permit reference number.

faqs:
  - question: "Can I send my pet directly to Montego Bay or only Kingston?"
    answer: "Both Norman Manley International Airport (KIN) in Kingston and Sangster International Airport (MBJ) in Montego Bay are accepted entry points for pets. Plumb Point Quarantine Station is in Kingston, so pets arriving at MBJ will be transported to Plumb Point for the 14-day quarantine. Confirm the logistics with VSD when you apply for the import permit."
  - question: "Can my pet avoid the 14-day quarantine if all documents are correct?"
    answer: "No. The 14-day quarantine at Plumb Point is mandatory for all pets arriving in Jamaica regardless of documentation status. It is not a penalty for non-compliance; it is a fixed import requirement. All pets entering Jamaica must complete the 14 days."
  - question: "Does Germany's EU pet passport satisfy Jamaica's health certificate requirement?"
    answer: "No. Jamaica requires a specific export health certificate endorsed by the official German Veterinäramt. The EU pet passport is not a substitute. Your official vet (Amtstierarzt) prepares the Jamaica-specific health certificate, which the Veterinäramt then endorses. The EU pet passport may be used as a vaccination record reference but is not the certificate of entry for Jamaica."
  - question: "What are the Plumb Point quarantine fees?"
    answer: "VSD publishes quarantine fees, which are subject to change. Contact VSD directly at moa.gov.jm or via vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm for the current fee schedule at the time of your travel. Fees are typically paid in USD or Jamaican dollars on arrival or in advance by arrangement."

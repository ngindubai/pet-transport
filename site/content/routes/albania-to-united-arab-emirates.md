  sections:
    - heading: "Why does Albania skip the titre test that Algeria and Algeria's neighbours don't?"
      body: |
        MOCCAE runs one list, low-risk or not, and Albania is on it. That single classification removes the rabies antibody titre test entirely for an Albania-origin pet, along with the three-week gap it usually forces between blood draw and travel elsewhere. A dog or cat needs the standard microchip, a rabies vaccination at least 21 days old if it's a first course, and the MOCCAE import permit, nothing more.

        It's worth double-checking with MOCCAE close to travel regardless. Low-risk lists get reviewed, and a reclassification would change this page's whole shape.

    - heading: "How does the flight actually work?"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines flies Tirana to Istanbul on its regular Balkan network, then Istanbul to Dubai roughly 14 times a week each direction, effectively twice a day. That frequency turns what could be a two-carrier handover into a single booking, with the same Pet Lounge Turkish already runs at Istanbul for animals on a longer layover.

        Qatar Airways also serves Tirana from Doha and offers a genuine second option if the Turkish schedule doesn't fit. Neither route is nonstop end to end, so confirm cargo space on the connecting sector specifically, since passenger seat availability and live-animal cargo capacity aren't the same thing.

    - heading: "What does the whole move cost?"
      body: |
        This is one of the cheaper UAE-bound routes on this site precisely because there's no lab fee, no courier charge for a blood sample, and no extended wait to fund. Vet visits and the certificate in Albania typically land under EUR 350, and the MOCCAE permit itself is a modest fixed online fee.

        Air cargo makes up most of the spend, typically EUR 900 to EUR 2,200 depending on crate size and whether the flight runs through Istanbul or Doha. All in, most owners land somewhere between EUR 1,600 and EUR 3,900, a noticeably shorter list of costs than the titre-test routes elsewhere in this build.

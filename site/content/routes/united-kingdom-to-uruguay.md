    UK to Uruguay requires an Animal Health Certificate from a UK Official Vet and an import permit from Uruguay's MGAP (Ministerio de Ganaderia, Agricultura y Pesca). No titre test is required for UK-origin pets entering Uruguay, which keeps the preparation timeline to around 8-12 weeks.

    There are no direct flights from the UK to Uruguay. The most practical routings are Iberia via Madrid or TAP Air Portugal via Lisbon, both of which have direct service to Montevideo's Carrasco International Airport (MVD).

    Uruguay's expat community is significant, particularly around Montevideo and Punta del Este. This is a well-travelled relocation route for diplomatic and professional households.

faqs:
  - question: "Does my pet need a titre test to travel from the UK to Uruguay?"
    answer: "No. Uruguay does not require a titre test for pets from the United Kingdom. You need a current rabies vaccination, ISO microchip, UK Animal Health Certificate, and a MGAP import permit from Uruguay's DGSG."
  - question: "How do I get the Uruguay MGAP import permit?"
    answer: "Apply to Uruguay's Direccion General de Servicios Ganaderos (DGSG), a division of MGAP. Allow 3-4 weeks for processing. An IPATA-registered agent with South America experience can manage the application. The permit must be in hand before your pet travels."
  - question: "Are there direct flights from the UK to Uruguay?"
    answer: "No direct UK-Uruguay scheduled flights currently operate. Iberia via Madrid and TAP Air Portugal via Lisbon are the most practical options. Both carriers connect directly to Montevideo (MVD) with good frequency and established pet cargo operations."
  - question: "Does Uruguay require health certificates in Spanish?"
    answer: "MGAP may require the health certificate in Spanish or with a certified translation. Confirm this with MGAP or an IPATA agent before your UK Official Vet appointment, so the AHC is issued in the correct format."
  - question: "What happens when my pet arrives at Carrasco airport?"
    answer: "A DGSG/MGAP veterinarian inspects your pet on arrival at Carrasco International Airport (MVD). They check the import permit, AHC, microchip, and vaccination records. With correct documentation, there is no extended quarantine. Bring originals of everything."

  h1: "Pet Transport from Sweden to Jamaica"
  overview: |
    Jamaica requires more preparation than most Caribbean destinations. A Veterinary Import Permit from the VSD must be in place before travel, and all arriving pets may face up to 14 days of quarantine at the Plumb Point National Quarantine Facility in Kingston.

    Allow 10 to 12 weeks from starting preparations to travel day. The permit application takes the longest, so start that step first. Cargo from Stockholm goes via Amsterdam with KLM or via London with British Airways.

    Using a specialist pet transport agent is strongly recommended for this route, given the permit, parasite treatment schedule, and quarantine coordination involved.

  sections:
    - heading: "The Veterinary Import Permit: the first and most important step"
      body: |
        Jamaica requires a Veterinary Import Permit (VIP) for all incoming companion animals. It is issued by the Veterinary Services Division (VSD) of Jamaica's Ministry of Agriculture, Fisheries and Mining. Without the permit, your pet will be refused at Kingston Airport (KIN).

        Apply to the VSD at vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm or by phone at +1 876-977-2489. Include your pet's details (species, breed, age, microchip number) and your intended travel date. Processing takes time, so apply 8-12 weeks before your planned departure.

        Once the permit is confirmed, you can commit to a travel date and start the veterinary preparation. The permit and your travel timeline must be aligned before you book flights.

    - heading: "Parasite treatment schedule"
      body: |
        Jamaica's VSD requires two rounds of internal parasite treatment and two rounds of external parasite treatment. The timing is specific and must be recorded on the health certificate.

        For internal parasites: two treatments at least 14 days apart. The second must be administered within 7 days of transport. For external parasites (fleas and ticks): two treatments at least 14 days apart. The second must be administered within 48 hours of transport.

        Work backwards from your scheduled departure. The second internal treatment needs to fall in the 1-7 day window before travel. The first internal treatment therefore happens 15-21 days before departure. External treatments follow the same logic but with the tighter 48-hour window for the final dose.

        Your Swedish veterinarian will record each treatment on the health certificate. Issue the certificate within 10 days of travel, ensuring all treatments are complete.

    - heading: "Quarantine at Plumb Point and cargo routing"
      body: |
        On arrival at Norman Manley International Airport (KIN) in Kingston, your pet will be directed to the National Quarantine Facility at Plumb Point. The VSD manages the facility and the quarantine period runs for up to 14 days. You will not be able to collect your pet during this time. Quarantine costs are charged by the VSD directly.

        For cargo, the main option from Stockholm Arlanda (ARN) is via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) with KLM. SAS connects ARN to AMS, and KLM operates to KIN. British Airways via London Heathrow (LHR) is an alternative. Book through a cargo agent and confirm live animal acceptance and any breed or size restrictions before finalising your travel date.

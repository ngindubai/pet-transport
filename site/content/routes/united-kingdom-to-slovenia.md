    answer: "No scheduled direct UK-Ljubljana flights currently operate. Austrian Airlines via Vienna is the most practical routing. Some owners also drive to Vienna (2 hours) or Munich (3.5 hours) and fly from those hubs, which avoids a connecting domestic flight."

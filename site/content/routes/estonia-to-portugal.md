    - heading: "Portugal's clear EU entry requirements"
      body: |
        Portugal administers pet imports through the General Directorate of Food and Veterinary (DGAV, dgav.pt). For personal companion animals from other EU member states, the requirements are simple: a valid EU pet passport with an ISO microchip and a current rabies vaccination given at least 21 days before arrival.

        There is no titre test, no quarantine, and no tapeworm treatment requirement. Portugal does not sit on the list of EU countries with additional dog entry conditions (that list is Ireland, Finland, Malta, and Norway). For a dog or cat from Estonia, arriving in Portugal is a routine documentary check.

    - heading: "Tallinn to Lisbon: routing your pet"

        Tallinn International Airport (TLL) does not have direct connections to Lisbon (LIS) or Porto (OPO). Standard routings go via Frankfurt, Amsterdam, or Helsinki, with connecting flights to Portugal.

        TAP Air Portugal connects from major European hubs to Lisbon and Porto, and accepts small pets in the cabin. For live animal cargo, TAP Air Portugal Cargo handles incoming shipments at Lisbon. Lufthansa Group connections via Frankfurt are another option for cargo coordination.

        Tallinn to Lisbon travel time with a typical connection is six to nine hours.

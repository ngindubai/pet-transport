  overview: "For most dogs and cats, moving from Latvia to Hungary is a manageable EU-to-EU transfer: microchip, valid rabies vaccination, EU pet passport or AHC, and a direct airBaltic flight from Riga to Budapest.   sections:
    - heading: "Hungary's breed ban: the first thing to check"
      body: |
        Hungary operates a breed ban under Government Decree 35/1997. The American Pit Bull Terrier and American Staffordshire Terrier are prohibited outright. These breeds cannot enter Hungary regardless of origin country or documentation status.

        Beyond the outright ban, several other breeds face special conditions. Rottweilers, Bull Terriers, and Staffordshire Bull Terriers can be kept in Hungary but are subject to specific muzzling, registration, and insurance requirements. If you are travelling with any of these breeds, contact NEBIH (Hungary's National Food Chain Safety Office) before making any bookings.

        For all other breeds, and for all cats, Hungary applies the standard EU rules. The breed check is the starting point; once cleared, the process follows the same path as any other EU-to-EU move.

    - heading: "Direct flight: Riga to Budapest with airBaltic"
      body: |
        airBaltic operates direct flights from Riga (RIX) to Budapest (BUD), which is the most convenient routing for this move. Small pets (up to 8kg including carrier) travel in the cabin. Larger dogs travel in the cargo hold.

        Note that Wizz Air, which has a strong Budapest presence, does not accept pets under any circumstances. Do not book Wizz Air for this journey. airBaltic and Lufthansa are the practical options.

        For larger dogs travelling in the hold, Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt is an alternative route if airBaltic's cargo service does not suit your dates. Book at least 4-6 weeks ahead.

    - heading: "Documentation from Latvia"
      body: |
        Latvia's PVD authorises vets to issue EU pet passports. Once your pet has a valid microchip and current rabies vaccination recorded in the passport, you are set for this journey and for future EU travel.

        The AHC is a valid alternative for a one-way move. It must be endorsed within 10 days of your arrival in Hungary. If documentation is incomplete or out of date at the Hungarian border, NEBIH can impose conditional quarantine. This is uncommon for owners who prepare properly, but worth knowing.

        Hungary's border veterinary service may check documents at Budapest Airport or at land crossings. Have everything ready and accessible.

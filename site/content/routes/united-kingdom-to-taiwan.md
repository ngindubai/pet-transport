  overview: "Moving a pet from the UK to Taiwan involves a BAPHIQ import permit applied for at least 60 days in advance and mandatory quarantine on arrival. The UK is classified as a Group 1 country by BAPHIQ, which means the standard quarantine period is 7 days.   sections:
    - heading: "The BAPHIQ permit: start here, 60 days out"
      body: |
        The BAPHIQ import permit is the step that dictates the timeline for this entire move. The application must be submitted to BAPHIQ at least 60 days before your planned travel date, and the permit has a limited validity window. Missing this deadline means missing your travel date.

        The permit application requires your pet's microchip number, vaccination details, and owner information. A specialist agent in Taiwan or a UK-based agent with Taiwan experience can manage the BAPHIQ application on your behalf and track progress.

        Once the permit is approved, book your quarantine facility immediately. BAPHIQ-approved quarantine facilities in Taiwan have limited capacity and spaces fill up. Leaving the facility booking until after the permit arrives can leave you without a confirmed quarantine place.

    - heading: "Quarantine in Taiwan: 7 days for UK pets"
      body: |
        Taiwan imposes mandatory quarantine on all incoming pets. Countries are categorised into groups, and the quarantine duration depends on which group the origin country falls into. The UK is a Group 1 country, which gives UK pets the shortest standard quarantine period: 7 days.

        Quarantine takes place at a BAPHIQ-approved facility. The facilities are purpose-built for animal care during the quarantine period, and most allow owners to visit or receive updates. The cost of the quarantine stay varies by facility.

        BAPHIQ also operates a quarantine exemption programme. Under specific conditions, dogs from approved countries may qualify for a reduced or eliminated quarantine period. The eligibility criteria and application process for the exemption programme should be confirmed directly with BAPHIQ or your agent, as the programme's terms can change.

    - heading: "Direct flights from Heathrow to Taipei"
      body: |
        EVA Air and China Airlines both operate direct cargo services from London Heathrow to Taipei Taoyuan (TPE). For pet owners, a direct flight is preferable: it eliminates the complexity of a hub transfer and reduces total journey time.

        EVA Air is widely regarded as one of the better carriers for live animal cargo. China Airlines is a solid alternative. Both have experience with the UK-Taiwan corridor. Cathay Pacific via Hong Kong is a fallback option if direct services are unavailable on your preferred dates.

  h1: "Pet Transport from Denmark to Kazakhstan"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Denmark to Kazakhstan involves a longer preparation window than most Caucasus or eastern European routes. The key differences are the 30-day rabies vaccination minimum (compared to 21 days in EU rules) and a question mark over health certificate format: Kazakhstan is a member of the Eurasian Economic Union (EAEU), and while the EU-format certificate is generally accepted, an EAEU-format certificate may be preferred by some border inspectors. Confirming this with the Kazakhstani Embassy before your vet prepares the paperwork saves delays on arrival.

    The logistics require a connection. Air Astana, the national carrier, does not fly from Copenhagen. Passengers and cargo heading to Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ) on Air Astana need to connect through Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS). Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is the other established cargo option, serving both Almaty and Astana from IST with CPH connections from Copenhagen.

    Allow four to six weeks for preparation. The 30-day vaccination minimum, the Foedevarestyrelsen endorsement process, and the multi-leg cargo coordination all need time built in.

  sections:
    - heading: "Kazakhstan entry rules: where they differ from EU routes"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan's Veterinary Committee sits within the Ministry of Agriculture and sets the rules for companion animal imports. Inspection takes place at Almaty International Airport (ALA) or Astana International Airport (NQZ). You can enter through either city depending on your routing.

        The requirements are similar to other non-EU destinations in the region, with two details worth noting. First, the rabies vaccination must be at least 30 days before entry, not the 21 days that EU movement regulations use. If your pet was vaccinated 22 days before you plan to travel, that is not enough. Second, Kazakhstan's EAEU membership means their vets may be more familiar with the EAEU certificate format than the EU format. Both formats carry the same information, but checking with the embassy avoids any friction at the border.

        No titre test is required. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals. No import permit is needed for 1-2 personal companion animals.

    - heading: "Health certificate format: EU or EAEU?"
      body: |
        This is the question that most distinguishes the Denmark-to-Kazakhstan route from other non-EU moves from Denmark. Foedevarestyrelsen issues EU-format official health certificates, which are the standard for Danish pet export. These are generally accepted into Kazakhstan, and there is no formal prohibition on using them.

        However, Kazakhstan's Veterinary Committee operates under EAEU rules, and some inspectors on arrival may be more familiar with the EAEU-format veterinary certificate. The safest approach is to contact the Kazakhstani Embassy in Denmark before your vet appointment and ask which format they recommend. This can often be answered quickly and removes any ambiguity.

        If the embassy confirms that the EU-format certificate is fully accepted, proceed with the standard Foedevarestyrelsen-endorsed certificate. If they recommend EAEU format, your pet transport agent or official vet can advise on how to prepare one. Either way, the certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel and endorsed by Foedevarestyrelsen.

    - heading: "Getting your pet from Copenhagen to Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        The routing decision comes down to two main options. Air Astana is Kazakhstan's flagship carrier with direct services to Almaty and Astana from Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS). To use this routing, you need a feeder flight from Copenhagen to AMS, then Air Astana AMS to ALA or NQZ. Both legs must accept live animal cargo; confirm with a cargo agent before booking.

        Turkish Airlines offers the alternative: Copenhagen (CPH) to Istanbul (IST) then IST to Almaty (ALA) or Astana (NQZ). Turkish Airlines has an established cargo live animal programme and serves both Kazakhstani airports from IST. Summer temperature embargoes (June to September) may apply on some Turkish Airlines routes, so confirm availability for your specific dates if you are travelling in this period.

        For a route of this distance and complexity, using an IPATA-registered pet transport agent is strongly recommended. They coordinate the cargo booking across multiple carriers, manage documentation, and know the specific requirements at both Copenhagen and Almaty or Astana.

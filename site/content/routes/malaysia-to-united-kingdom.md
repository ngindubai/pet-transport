        Singapore is on the UK's GB listed country schedule. Malaysia is not. This single difference means a Singapore-based owner can move a pet to the UK with a straightforward AHC and arrive within a few weeks, while a Malaysia-based owner faces a 5-7 month process involving a titre test and a 90-day wait.

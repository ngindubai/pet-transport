  sections:
    - heading: "VRD and the Malta health certificate for US travel"
      body: |
        The Veterinary Regulation Directorate (VRD) is Malta's competent authority for animal health documentation and is part of the Animal Health Department under the Veterinary Affairs Division. The health certificate for US import must be issued by a VRD-authorised official vet.

        The certificate must include your pet's microchip number, vaccination history (current rabies vaccination), species and breed, owner details, and fitness to travel. Confirm whether USDA APHIS authentication is required at usda.gov/aphis/pet-travel for Malta.

    - heading: "Routing from Malta to the USA via European hubs"
      body: |
        Malta International Airport (MLA) has no direct transatlantic US service. The main cargo routing options are via Frankfurt (Lufthansa), Amsterdam (KLM), London Heathrow (British Airways), and Rome Fiumicino (ITA Airways). Frankfurt and London both offer the widest range of direct US connections.

        Air Malta connects Malta to major European hubs. Confirm live animal acceptance with each carrier, as policies can differ between Air Malta and the onward transatlantic carrier.

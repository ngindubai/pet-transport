  h1: "Pet Transport from the United Kingdom to El Salvador"
  overview: |
    Great Britain has a certificate built for exactly this journey. Export Health Certificate 3946 exists specifically to move cats and dogs from Great Britain to El Salvador, applied for through APHA's online export service and signed by an APHA-registered Official Veterinarian. That specificity is unusual: many destination countries share a generic EHC template, but El Salvador gets its own numbered certificate.

    On arrival, El Salvador does not ask for a titre test or impose routine quarantine. MAG, the Ministerio de Agricultura y Ganaderia, wants an import authorisation obtained in advance for USD 9.04, valid for 30 days and good for one entry only. The paperwork is lighter than the outbound APHA process, but the fixed 30-day validity window means it has to be timed carefully against your actual flight date.
  sections:
    - heading: "Export Health Certificate 3946: what makes it different"
      body: |
        Most APHA export certificates cover a group of similar destinations. Certificate 3946 is written for El Salvador specifically, which suggests El Salvador's import requirements have their own quirks that a generic certificate would not capture. Applying requires a Government Gateway account and a linked Defra account, then submission through APHA's online export health certificate service.

        Only an APHA-registered Official Veterinarian can sign the certificate. APHA's guidance notes for this certificate were last revised on 2 February 2024, so confirm with APHA directly, or check the current specimen on gov.uk, before your appointment in case a newer version has since replaced it.
    - heading: "MAG's import authorisation and why the 30-day window matters"
      body: |
        MAG requires an import authorisation before a pet enters El Salvador. You can apply in person at MAG's Santa Tecla headquarters, online, or at the point of entry itself, whether that is the international airport, a land border, or a port. The fee is USD 9.04 and the document is valid for 30 days from issue, for one entry only.

        Because it cannot be used for a later export back out of El Salvador, and because a 30-day validity window leaves little margin for a delayed flight, apply once your travel date is close to confirmed rather than months in advance. MAG's contact point for questions is its quarantine division in Santa Tecla, reachable by phone or at cuarentena@mag.gob.sv.
    - heading: "Getting from London to San Salvador"
      body: |
        No airline flies London to San Salvador direct. The practical options connect via Miami on American Airlines, via a Central or South American hub, or potentially via Madrid on the new seasonal Avianca-Wamos Air service that started in June 2026, four times weekly on an Airbus A330. That Madrid routing is worth asking about, since it would avoid a US transit altogether, but nobody has yet confirmed it carries manifest cargo pets rather than passengers only.

        British Airways can carry the outbound leg to a connecting hub, with IAG Cargo handling the animal under IATA Live Animal Regulations. Whichever routing you choose, confirm current pet cargo acceptance, seasonal heat embargoes, and breed restrictions with the specific carrier before booking, since policies on Central American connections change more often than the underlying import rules do.

  sections:
    - heading: "DLS and the Nepal export certificate"
      body: |
        Nepal's Department of Livestock Services (DLS) is the authority for animal health documentation. The health certificate for UK import must be issued by a DLS-authorised official vet and endorsed by DLS. The DLS is part of the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock Development. Contact DLS at dlsnep.gov.np.

        For FAVN titre testing, blood samples from Nepal are typically sent to laboratories in India, the UK, or other APHA-approved countries. Your specialist pet transport agent can advise on the practicalities of blood sample shipping from Kathmandu.

    - heading: "Kathmandu altitude and cargo routing"
      body: |
        Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM) in Kathmandu sits at approximately 1,400 metres above sea level. This affects aircraft performance and maximum cargo payloads. For most dogs and cats in standard crates, this is not a significant problem, but very large dogs in heavy crates may be affected by weight limits on certain aircraft.

        Most cargo routes to the UK connect through the Gulf (Dubai or Doha) or Istanbul. Qatar Airways, Emirates, and Turkish Airlines all serve Kathmandu with good frequency and accept live animals in cargo. Confirm the specific aircraft type and cargo weight limits before booking.

  h1: "Pet Transport from Belize to the United Kingdom"
  overview: |
    There's no nonstop flight between Belize and the UK, not close. Every pet on this route flies Belize City to Miami first, on American's new direct service, then picks up a transatlantic leg from there.

    Belize also falls into the UK's unlisted category, the same group as the rest of Central America. That means a rabies titre test at an approved laboratory, then a fixed three-month wait counted from the blood draw date rather than the day the result arrives. Add a Belize side where the export paperwork for a single pet isn't published in any real detail, and the practical planning window runs closer to four to five months.

  sections:
    - heading: "Why the blood draw date is the one date that matters"
      body: |
        The 90-day clock starts the moment your vet takes blood for the titre test, not the day the lab confirms a pass. Get that date wrong and you can miss a travel window by weeks without realising it until it's too late to fix.

        Work backwards from your target month. If you want to land in the UK in December, blood needs drawing by early September. The rabies vaccination behind it needs at least 30 days' lead time before that, so early August at the latest. Four to five months isn't padding. It's close to the floor.

        One thing that can shorten this: if your pet already carries a passing titre result from an earlier international move, and the vaccination behind it hasn't lapsed, that result may still count. Ask your vet to check the dates rather than assuming a fresh test is unavoidable.

    - heading: "BAHA's paperwork gap, and Miami's role in getting there"
      body: |
        Search BAHA's website for how to bring a pet into Belize and you'll find a detailed, fee-by-fee process. Search for how to take a pet out, and there's a lot less to go on. That isn't a reason to assume nothing's required. It's a reason to call the Animal Health Department at Central Farm directly and get it confirmed in writing before you build a schedule around it.

        On the flight side, American Airlines now flies Belize City to Miami nonstop in under two hours, part of the airline's expanded 2025 and 2026 Belize network. From Miami, American's own widebody service reaches London Heathrow directly, or British Airways covers the same sector through IAG Cargo. Either way, a Belize-origin pet is never on a single-airline itinerary for this route, so confirm cargo space on both legs before locking in dates.

  sections:
    - heading: "Routing from Copenhagen to Kigali"
      body: |
        Copenhagen Kastrup Airport (CPH) has no direct service to Kigali. Two main routings are practical for Denmark-to-Rwanda pet cargo.

        Via Brussels: SAS or another carrier connects CPH to Brussels Zaventem (BRU), where Brussels Airlines operates its direct BRU-KGL East Africa service. This is a two-leg journey with a single hub transfer. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance with Brussels Airlines Cargo for the BRU-KGL leg.

        Via Addis Ababa: Ethiopian Airlines connections from CPH (or via FRA on SAS/Lufthansa) to ADD, then Ethiopian Cargo on ADD-KGL. Ethiopian Cargo has the widest live animal programme in Africa and ADD-KGL is a short East African hop. Copenhagen has good European hub connections through SAS and multiple European carriers.

    - heading: "Fødevarestyrelsen export health certificate"
      body: |
        The Danish EU pet passport applies to intra-EU travel only. For Rwanda, the required document is an official health certificate issued by an authorised official veterinarian and endorsed by Fødevarestyrelsen (Danish Veterinary and Food Administration) at foedevarestyrelsen.dk.

        Contact Fødevarestyrelsen to identify an authorised official vet and understand the endorsement process for non-EU exports. The certificate must be issued within 10 days of travel. Book the vet appointment and allow time for endorsement within that 10-day window.

        Confirm the certificate format meets Rwanda's specific import requirements. A pet transport agent experienced in Danish exports to Africa can ensure the certificate is correctly formatted for the RAB inspection at Kigali.

    - heading: "RAB import permit for Rwanda"
      body: |
        Rwanda's Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB) at rab.gov.rw processes companion animal import permits. Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, species, breed, vaccination history, and travel date.

        Apply early and follow up to confirm progress. On arrival at Kigali International Airport (KGL), RAB officers inspect the permit alongside the Fødevarestyrelsen health certificate. No quarantine applies for compliant arrivals.

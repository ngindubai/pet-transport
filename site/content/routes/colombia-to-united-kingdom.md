  sections:
    - heading: "The ICA health certificate and what it needs to include"
      body: |
        ICA (Instituto Colombiano Agropecuario) is Colombia's agricultural health authority. The health certificate for UK entry must be issued by an ICA-authorised official vet, which is a different qualification from a standard private practice vet.

        The certificate must include: your pet's ISO microchip number, species and breed, date of birth, owner details, vaccination history (including the titre test result with laboratory name, sample date, result in IU/ml, and the date the 90-day waiting period ends), and for dogs, the tapeworm treatment details.

        APHA inspectors at Heathrow verify all of these details. The titre test result section is particularly important: inspectors check that the blood was drawn at least 30 days after the most recent vaccination, that the result is 0.5 IU/ml or above, and that 90 days have passed since the blood was drawn.

        ICA has offices in Bogota and most major Colombian cities. Contact ICA (ica.gov.co) to find an authorised vet in your area who can issue the certificate in the correct format.

    - heading: "Choosing a routing via Madrid, Miami or Amsterdam"
      body: |
        No direct flights connect Colombia to the UK, so all cargo routes involve a connection. The three main options are via Madrid (Iberia/British Airways), Miami (American Airlines with connection to IAG), and Amsterdam (KLM). Avianca connects Bogota to multiple hubs and is a practical option for the South America-to-Europe segment.

        Each routing has trade-offs. Via Madrid is the most direct to London for Colombian carriers. Via Amsterdam adds time but gives access to KLM's strong cargo network. Via Miami adds significant time and a North Atlantic crossing, but may have better cargo availability on some dates.

        For pets, transit country rules generally do not apply to animals remaining airside in the cargo facility. Confirm this with your airline for each specific routing before booking.

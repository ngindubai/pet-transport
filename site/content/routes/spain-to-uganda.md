  sections:
    - heading: "MAAIF import permit: the first step"
      body: |
        Uganda's Ministry of Agriculture, Animal Industry and Fisheries (MAAIF) at maaif.go.ug manages companion animal imports. The import permit is the document that enables entry: without it, no amount of correct health certification will clear your pet at Entebbe International Airport (EBB).

        Apply at least 8-10 weeks before travel with your pet's ISO microchip number, full vaccination history, breed, and intended travel date. Uganda's administrative processes can be unpredictable in timing. Apply as early as possible and follow up after submitting to confirm your application is being processed. Do not finalise cargo bookings until the permit is in hand.

        At Entebbe, the MAAIF inspection team checks the permit alongside the MAPA health certificate. A complete, consistent document set proceeds without quarantine.

    - heading: "Madrid to Entebbe: three routing options"
      body: |
        Madrid Barajas (MAD) to Entebbe International Airport (EBB) requires at least one hub transfer. Three established options cover this corridor.

        Ethiopian Airlines via Addis Ababa (ADD) is the most common routing for Spain-to-Uganda pet moves. Ethiopian operates from Madrid to ADD, and Ethiopian Cargo handles live animal freight to East Africa at scale. The ADD-EBB connection is short.

        Brussels Airlines via Brussels (BRU) connects BRU directly to EBB with regular frequency. Iberia from MAD-BRU (or another carrier) gets your pet to Brussels, and Brussels Airlines Cargo covers the BRU-EBB leg. Brussels Airlines has built a consistent record on Central and East Africa routes.

        Kenya Airways via Nairobi (NBO) is the third option. Nairobi is East Africa's other major hub, and NBO-EBB is a short final connection. Kenya Airways Cargo handles live animals.

        Confirm live animal acceptance, crate size limits, and current availability with each carrier before committing to a routing.

    - heading: "MAPA export health certificate"
      body: |
        For non-EU destinations, Spain's Ministerio de Agricultura, Pesca y Alimentación (MAPA) governs the export health certificate process through the DGSVA (Dirección General de Sanidad de la Producción Agraria). An official veterinarian (Servicio Oficial de Veterinaria) issues the certificate after a pre-export examination. The DGSVA endorses the document.

        The Spanish EU pet passport is not valid for Uganda. The MAPA-endorsed certificate is what Uganda's MAAIF inspection team at Entebbe expects.

        Book the pre-export examination with a Servicio Oficial de Veterinaria in Spain. The vet checks microchip, vaccination status, and general health. After endorsement by the DGSVA, the certificate is valid for 10 days. Time the appointment carefully so the certificate is issued within 10 days of your pet's departure from Spain.

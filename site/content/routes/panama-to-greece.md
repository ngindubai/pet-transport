        Greece applies the standard EU framework: countries assessed as having equivalent rabies control skip the titre test, and countries that have not been assessed, Panama included, do not. The sequence runs microchip before vaccination, a 30-day wait before the blood draw, a FAVN or ELISA result of at least 0.5 IU/ml, then 90 days from that draw date.

        This is identical whether the destination is Athens, Berlin or Copenhagen. Greece's own entry process adds nothing extra on top and takes nothing away.

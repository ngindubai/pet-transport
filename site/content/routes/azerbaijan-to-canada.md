## Pet Transport from Azerbaijan to Canada

Canada is one of the more welcoming destinations for pet owners relocating from Azerbaijan. For dogs over three months old, the Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA) requires a current rabies vaccination certificate in English or French. That is the core requirement. There is no titre test, no quarantine, and no lengthy waiting period after vaccination. If your dog is vaccinated and the certificate is correctly formatted, the regulatory side of this move is straightforward.

The main tasks are getting your export paperwork right in Azerbaijan, specifically the State Veterinary Service endorsement, and choosing a reliable routing from Baku (GYD) to Canada. With 4 to 8 weeks of lead time, this is achievable without drama.

---

### What Canada Actually Requires

Canada's rules for personal companion dogs arriving from Azerbaijan break down cleanly:

| Requirement | Required? | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| Rabies vaccination certificate | Yes | In English or French, for dogs over 3 months |
| Titre test (FAVN blood test) | No | Not required for personal companion dogs |
| Quarantine | No | None for compliant arrivals |
| Import permit | No | Not required for personal pets |
| Microchip | Not legally required | Strongly recommended to match documentation |

For cats, the federal position is lighter still: no rabies certificate is required at the CFIA level. Your cat still faces a CFIA health inspection at the port of entry and must be in good health. Check with your carrier for their own cat cargo rules.

The language requirement is the most common point of failure on this route. Azerbaijan vets may issue certificates in Azerbaijani by default. Confirm with your vet in Baku that the certificate will be produced in English, or arrange a certified translation before travel. A certificate in Azerbaijani only will be refused at the Canadian border.

---

### The Azerbaijan Export Process

Azerbaijan does not require a formal export permit for personal companion pets, which removes one layer of paperwork. What is needed is a two-stage document process run through the State Veterinary Service.

**Stage 1: Authorised vet certificate.** The vet issuing your export health certificate must be authorised by the State Veterinary Service, which sits under the Ministry of Agriculture of Azerbaijan. They carry out a full health examination and issue an official certificate covering your pet's species, microchip number, vaccination record, and fitness to travel. Not every clinic in Baku holds State Veterinary Service authorisation, so confirm before booking an appointment.

**Stage 2: State Veterinary Service endorsement.** The completed certificate is submitted to the State Veterinary Service for an official stamp and signature. This endorsement is what gives the document its legal standing at the Canadian border. Allow 3 to 5 working days. Book your flight only after the endorsed document is in your hands.

Your authorised vet will guide you through the submission process. Some vets handle the submission on your behalf; others ask you to deliver it in person to the State Veterinary Service office.

---

### Flying from Baku to Canada

There are no direct flights from Baku to Canada. Every pet on this route travels via at least one connecting hub in Europe. The most used options are:

**Turkish Airlines Cargo via Istanbul (GYD-IST-YYZ):** The main established option on this corridor. Turkish Airlines Cargo has a dedicated live animal handling process at Istanbul, and its cargo operation from Baku is well set up. This is the most frequently used routing for Baku-to-Canada pet shipments.

**Lufthansa Cargo via Frankfurt (GYD-FRA-YYZ):** The strongest alternative. Frankfurt is one of Europe's best-equipped live animal transit hubs, with dedicated animal holding facilities and welfare-trained ground staff. If Turkish cargo slots are full on your dates, Lufthansa is a solid second choice, particularly for larger dogs.

**Air France Cargo via CDG:** Involves more legs (GYD via IST then CDG-YYZ) and longer overall transit time. Connection timing at each hub needs careful checking to meet live animal welfare interval requirements.

**AZAL (Azerbaijan Airlines) to Frankfurt:** AZAL serves FRA, from where onward cargo connections to Canada are available via partner carriers. Worth comparing on price if the breed and size restrictions are acceptable.

Book cargo space as early as possible. Live animal slots on transatlantic cargo flights are limited, and Baku is not a high-frequency cargo origin point. Your pet relocation agent should have direct relationships with cargo booking desks at each carrier.

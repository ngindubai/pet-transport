content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Norway to Panama"
  overview: |
    Moving a pet from Norway to Panama is a manageable route, but it has one step that surprises many owners: the health certificate must be authenticated by the Panamanian Embassy or Consulate in Norway before departure. Without that consulate stamp, MIDA officers at Tocumen International Airport may not accept the documentation.

    Aside from that step, the requirements are straightforward: a valid microchip, a rabies vaccination given at least 30 days before entry, current additional vaccinations, and a health certificate from a Mattilsynet-authorised official veterinarian. No titre test is required and there is no quarantine for compliant arrivals. Four to six weeks is enough preparation time for pets with current vaccinations.

  sections:
    - heading: "What Panama requires at Tocumen Airport"
      body: |
        Panama's animal import rules are administered by MIDA (Ministerio de Desarrollo Agropecuario). MIDA veterinarians at Tocumen International Airport (PTY) inspect all incoming pets and their documentation. The inspection takes place at arrival before your pet is released to you, and a registration fee of approximately USD 20 (20 balboas) is charged.

        The requirements are: an ISO-compliant microchip, a valid rabies vaccination administered at least 30 days before entry, current DHPP-equivalent vaccinations for dogs (distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis, parvovirus), core vaccines for cats, and an official health certificate. No titre test is required and no import permit is needed for personal companion animals. There is no quarantine for compliant arrivals.

        Pets under 4 months of age are not permitted entry. This is enforced on arrival.

    - heading: "The consulate authentication step"
      body: |
        Panama requires that the official veterinary health certificate be authenticated by the Panamanian Embassy or Consulate in the country of origin before the pet travels. This authentication confirms the issuing vet's credentials to Panamanian authorities.

        In Norway, the Panamanian Embassy is in Oslo. After your Mattilsynet-authorised official vet issues the health certificate, you must take or send the original to the Embassy for stamping. Allow 2-3 working days for this process. Contact the Embassy in advance to confirm the current procedure and any fee.

        The health certificate is only valid for 10 days from the date of clinical examination, and the consulate authentication must be completed within that same 10-day window. Time the vet appointment so you have at least 5-6 days remaining after authentication before your departure date. Your pet transport agent can coordinate this step if you are working with one.

    - heading: "Cargo routing from Oslo to Panama City"
      body: |
        There are no direct cargo flights from Oslo Gardermoen (OSL) to Tocumen International Airport (PTY). The standard routing is via Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS), connecting with KLM cargo. SAS and Norwegian Air provide regular services from Oslo to Amsterdam, and KLM operates cargo capacity to Panama City.

        An alternative routes via Madrid (MAD) with Iberia or Copa Airlines, which is headquartered in Panama City and connects Amsterdam to PTY via its own network. Whichever route you choose, book through a cargo agent, confirm live animal acceptance on every segment, and allow the consulate authentication window when finalising your travel date.

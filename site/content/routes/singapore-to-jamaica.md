content:
  h1: "Pet Transport from Singapore to Jamaica"
  overview: |
    Fourteen days at Plumb Point in Kingston is non-negotiable, and getting there starts with a Jamaican government permit applied for from Singapore, months before departure. The VSD import permit takes 8-12 weeks on its own, runs entirely independently of Singapore's own AVS export process, and has to be confirmed in writing before a flight is even worth booking.

    Layer Singapore's AVS export licence and endorsed certificate, two rounds of precisely timed parasite treatment, and a cargo routing that has to change carriers in London, and this becomes one of the more involved Caribbean moves this site covers from Southeast Asia. Allow 12 to 16 weeks.

  sections:
    - heading: "The VSD permit: apply before anything else"
      body: |
        Jamaica's Veterinary Services Division issues the import permit that every arriving pet needs, and it has nothing to do with Singapore's own AVS process. Email vsdpermits@moa.gov.jm with species, breed, age, sex, microchip number and country of origin, and expect 8-12 weeks from application to a written confirmation. The fee is J$5,000, roughly USD 32-35.

        Jamaica maintains a restricted breeds list: Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro, Japanese Tosa and Pit Bull Terrier. If a dog could plausibly be read as one of these, or a cross, contact the VSD directly before applying. There's no appeal process at Norman Manley International if the breed turns out to be restricted on arrival.

    - heading: "Parasite treatment timing drives the whole calendar"
      body: |
        The VSD's protocol asks for two rounds each of internal and external parasite treatment, 14 days apart. The second internal round has to fall within 7 days of transport; the second external round within 48 hours. Work backward from a confirmed cargo departure date, not forward from when the first appointment happens to be available.

        If the flight date changes after treatments have started, the whole 14-day cycle can need to restart. Book the Singapore Airlines SIN-LHR and British Airways or Virgin Atlantic LHR-KIN cargo legs early enough that the parasite treatment schedule, not the flight schedule, becomes the fixed point everything else is planned around.

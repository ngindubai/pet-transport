  sections:
    - heading: "AQCS's two-exam process, and why the timing is unforgiving"
      body: |
        India's Animal Quarantine and Certification Service doesn't sign off a pet for export on a single vet visit. A first clinical examination happens around a week before the intended travel date, then a second within 2 days of departure, at which point the AQCS-endorsed certificate is actually issued.

        That's a tighter window than most European origins on this site, where a single vet visit close to departure is usually enough. Book both exam slots when you book the AQCS No-Objection Certificate application, which itself needs 4 to 6 weeks lead time. If your flight date shifts, both exam appointments may need to move with it.
      
    - heading: "Choosing a connection: Amsterdam, Frankfurt or Dubai"
      body: |
        Delhi to Mexico City via Amsterdam is generally the shortest overall journey among the common routings, with KLM Cargo carrying the pet on both sectors under one alliance. Frankfurt with Lufthansa is a close alternative and sometimes has better crate availability when Amsterdam space is tight.

        Dubai offers the shortest connection time from Delhi, which matters for reducing the time a pet spends in transit, though Emirates SkyCargo applies seasonal heat embargoes during the Gulf summer that can force a reschedule. Whichever hub you choose, confirm live animal acceptance on each individual flight number, not just on the route in general; acceptance can vary by aircraft type and by season even within the same airline.

    - heading: "What SENASICA checks on arrival in Mexico City"
      body: |
        Mexico doesn't require an advance import permit. SENASICA inspects the pet physically at the airport, checking the AQCS-endorsed health certificate, the rabies vaccination record and the completed FF-SENASICA-003 form.

        India's AQCS certificate carries its own 14-day validity from issue. Mexico separately treats an undated-expiry certificate as valid for 15 days to arrival. Build the connecting flight schedule around the shorter of the two, and leave a buffer in case the first or second leg is delayed.

    answer: "It's India's export authorisation for a companion pet, issued by the Animal Quarantine and Certification Service under the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying.

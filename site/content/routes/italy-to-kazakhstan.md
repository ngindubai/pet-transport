  sections:
    - heading: "EAEU format: the documentation challenge for Kazakhstan"
      body: |
        Kazakhstan is a member of the Eurasian Economic Union alongside Russia, Belarus, Kyrgyzstan, and Armenia. The EAEU operates its own veterinary standards for animal imports, which differ from EU veterinary certificate formats.

        When your Italian ASL official vet issues the health certificate for Kazakhstan, the document must comply with EAEU Integrated Sanitary and Phytosanitary Supervision rules (ISPS), not EU format. This means the certificate needs specific EAEU-required fields and wording that a vet unfamiliar with Central Asia routes may not know.

        Contact the Kazakh embassy in Rome, or the Committee on Veterinary Control and Supervision under Kazakhstan's Ministry of Agriculture, 10-12 weeks before travel to confirm the exact documentation format required. A pet transport agent with Central Asia experience will know this process and can guide both you and your vet.

    - heading: "Rome or Milan to Almaty: Turkish Airlines is the main option"
      body: |
        Turkish Airlines connects both Rome Fiumicino (FCO) and Milan Malpensa (MXP) to Istanbul (IST), with onward Turkish Airlines service to Almaty (ALA) and Nur-Sultan/Astana (NQZ). Turkish Airlines Cargo handles live animal freight on this corridor and IST is the primary European hub for Central Asia-bound cargo.

        The FCO or MXP to ALA routing via IST takes approximately 8-11 hours. This is a moderate transit time, suitable for healthy adult dogs and cats in compliant crates.

        Air Astana, Kazakhstan's national carrier, connects European cities to ALA and NQZ. Check whether Air Astana Cargo operates live animal freight from Italian airports or via Frankfurt connections. Frankfurt Animal Lounge at Frankfurt Airport handles live animal connections for pets transiting through FRA.

    - heading: "Italy's export certificate in context"
      body: |
        The Italian export health certificate from the ASL replaces the EU pet passport for international travel outside the European Union. For Kazakhstan, the content is the same (microchip, vaccination records, health attestation) but the format must comply with EAEU requirements rather than EU format.

        This is the specific challenge of the Italy-to-Kazakhstan route: the certificate is standard in content but non-standard in format requirements. Your local ASL veterinario ufficiale may need guidance on the EAEU-specific format. A pet transport agent with experience in EAEU routes is the most reliable way to ensure the certificate is correctly formatted and endorsed.

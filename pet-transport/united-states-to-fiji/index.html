Overview

Moving a dog or cat from United States to Fiji is very doable with the right preparation. Expect around 14-20 weeks of lead time to line up the documents and the fixed waiting periods. Factor in 30 days of quarantine at the destination.

The full checklist, requirements and airline options are set out below.

Step by step

The United States to Fiji process

Checklist

Fiji: entry requirements

RequirementDetail
MicrochipISO-compatible microchip (AVID, Trovan, Destron or ISO standard) required before any vaccination is given. Source: baf.com.fj, Bringing Cats, Dogs and Other Pets to Fiji, checked 14 July 2026.
Rabies vaccinationRequired at least 30 days before export. Dogs also need distemper, infectious canine hepatitis, parvovirus and parainfluenza vaccinations; cats need feline enteritis, rhinotracheitis and calicivirus vaccinations, all given at least 30 days and not more than 12 months before export. Source: baf.com.fj, checked 14 July 2026.
Rabies titre testThird-party relocation guidance describing BAF's own risk framework places US-origin pets in the same Group B tier as continental Europe, requiring a rabies neutralising antibody titre test. Sources differ on the exact window: some describe roughly 60 days to 12 months before export, one detailed third-party account instead ties the required blood-draw timing to a 180 to 365 day window before travel, with the resulting quarantine length shortening or lengthening depending on how close to 180 days the draw falls. BAF does not publish one single definitive table online, so confirm the current window directly with BAF when applying for the import permit. Source: BAF import permit guidance and third-party relocation sources describing the Group A/Group B framework, including catabroad.com (verified 2 June 2026) and tailwindglobalpet.com, checked 14 July 2026.
QuarantineMandatory. Group B-origin pets, the tier US animals fall into under this framework, face a minimum 30-day stay at BAF's government Post Entry Quarantine (PEQ) station at Koronivia, Nausori. Third-party guidance also describes the stay extending well beyond 30 days, to 45, 60, 90 or even 115 days, if the titre blood draw happened too close to the travel date. Monitored home quarantine with vet checks roughly every 10 days for six months follows release, then further checks at 9 months and 1 year. Guide and service dogs may be exempted from the PEQ stay on a case-by-case basis with full documentation. Source: baf.com.fj and the third-party relocation sources above, checked 14 July 2026.
Import permitImport permit required from BAF before departure, applied for via baf.com.fj or by emailing animalimports@baf.com.fj, with a fee due at submission. Processing takes around 5 working days. The permit is valid for 6 months from issue and must accompany the animal on arrival. Source: baf.com.fj, checked 14 July 2026.
Health certificateThe USDA-endorsed international health certificate, completed by a USDA-accredited vet and endorsed via APHIS eFile, is the document that travels with the pet and accompanies the BAF import permit application. Confirm with BAF exactly which certificate format its import office wants attached, since the sample form issued alongside the permit is a reference template rather than the document your vet actually completes. Source: aphis.usda.gov/pet-travel and baf.com.fj, checked 14 July 2026.

Leaving United States of America

Export requirements

RequirementDetail
Export permitNo general US export permit for pets. Fiji, like most destinations, works from a USDA-endorsed export health certificate rather than a separate permit.
Health certificateInternational health certificate issued by a USDA-accredited veterinarian, endorsed through the USDA APHIS eFile system. The endorsement fee is roughly USD 38 per certificate, as published on the APHIS pet travel pages. This is the certificate BAF expects to see alongside the Fiji import permit application.

Critical points

Read before you book

⚠ The 30-day Koronivia quarantine is not a penalty for weak paperwork. BAF's own risk framework groups US-origin pets with continental Europe, not with the 7-day tier used for Australia and New Zealand, so the stay applies even to a pet with a flawless titre result. Some third-party guidance describes the stay extending well past 30 days if the titre blood draw sits too close to the travel date, so build in margin rather than planning to the exact minimum.
⚠ The sample certificate issued with the BAF import permit is a reference template. Confirm with BAF, in writing, exactly which certificate your USDA-accredited vet needs to complete, rather than assuming the USDA international certificate and the BAF sample form are interchangeable.
⚠ Unlike a lot of Pacific routes, this one does have a direct flight: Fiji Airways runs Los Angeles to Nadi nonstop. That removes the transfer risk that complicates routes like UK to Fiji, but the animal still travels purely as manifest cargo, and Fiji Airways will not accept a dog or cat under 6 months old or under 6 lbs (3 kg).
Carriers

Airlines on this route

Fiji Airways
Flies Los Angeles (LAX) to Nadi (NAN) nonstop, daily, the same aircraft that runs the reverse direction. Pets travel purely as manifest cargo, never as checked baggage, and never in the cabin except a trained service dog. Fiji Airways will not carry a dog or cat under 6 months old or lighter than 6 lbs (3 kg). Book through the Fiji Freight Department or an IATA cargo agent. Source: fijiairways.com and PetTravel.com, checked 14 July 2026.

Cargo Only

What Fiji requires

Bringing a pet into Fiji involves a fair amount of documentation. Keep everything organised and check each item against the current requirements before you book.

Avoiding the common mistakes

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

Confirm live animal acceptance on every flight segment before booking. Not all aircraft or connections take pets, and cargo terms can differ leg to leg.

Keep every original document together and take copies. Border officials want originals, and a missing signature or date can hold up clearance.

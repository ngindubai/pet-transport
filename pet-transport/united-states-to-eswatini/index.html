Requirements

Eswatini entry requirements

Every item below must be in place before your pet can enter. We verify and track each one.

Microchip
Not legally mandated, but a 15-digit chip meeting ISO 11784 or Annex A to ISO 11785 is expected on the paperwork. Implant it before rabies vaccination so the certificate and chip number match. Source: licensed pet import documentation, checked July 2026.
Rabies vaccination
Required, given between 30 days and 12 months before arrival in Eswatini. Dogs also need current cover against distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis and parvovirus; cats need feline viral rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia and feline leukaemia.
Rabies titre test
Not required for a pet arriving from the United States, or from any other country, under Eswatini's own import rules. This is a genuinely different picture from the reverse direction: a pet leaving Eswatini for the US, if Eswatini is classified CDC high-risk for dog rabies, needs a serology titre or a 28-day quarantine on arrival. None of the CDC's machinery runs the other way; it governs entry to the US, not entry to Eswatini.
Quarantine
No quarantine for a pet arriving with complete, compliant paperwork. Non-compliant arrivals face quarantine where facilities allow, return to origin, or, in unresolved serious cases, euthanasia at the importer's expense.
Import permit
A Veterinary Import Permit from the Director of Animal Health is required, since the US is outside the SADC bloc that qualifies for a waiver. Apply well ahead of travel; the permit is valid for 6 months once issued.
Health certificate
USDA-endorsed international health certificate presented on arrival, completed within 10 days of entry, covering vaccination history, microchip number and a clean bill of health.
Leaving United States

Export requirements

Export permit
No export permit required from the US side for companion animals.
Health certificate
USDA APHIS Veterinary Health Certificate (VS Form 7001 or equivalent international health certificate) completed by a USDA-accredited veterinarian and endorsed by the USDA APHIS State Veterinarian. Allow 3-5 business days for endorsement.
Costs

What this route typically costs

IATA-compliant travel crate: USD 80-300
Pet transport agent for the US-to-Eswatini booking: USD 500-1,200
Cargo Atlanta or Newark to Johannesburg, plus the regional JNB-SHO leg: USD 1,800-3,600 combined
Total typical range: USD 2,500-5,300

Critical points

No CDC rule applies on this route. The CDC's high-risk-country machinery, titre tests, the six-facility-airport rule, governs animals entering the United States, not animals leaving it. Don't plan around it here; plan around Eswatini's own Director of Animal Health requirements instead.

There is no long-haul carrier at King Mswati III International Airport. Every pet connects through Johannesburg on Delta or United, then transfers to a regional Airlink or Eswatini Air flight. Book this as one interline cargo movement, with a ground agent managing the O.R. Tambo handover.

Eswatini's own rules currently ask for no titre test from any origin country, but that position is Eswatini's to change. Confirm with the Director of Animal Health close to your travel date.

The import permit fee for Eswatini is not published anywhere we could verify. Ask directly when submitting the application rather than budgeting on a guess.

Eswatini entry requirements at a glance

The path into Eswatini is straightforward to follow once you know the steps. Give the time-sensitive items priority and check the details below.

Getting the details right

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

Confirm live animal acceptance on every flight segment before booking. Not all aircraft or connections take pets, and cargo terms can differ leg to leg.

Keep every original document together and take copies. Border officials want originals, and a missing signature or date can hold up clearance.

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

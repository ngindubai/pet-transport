Step by step

The Serbia to United Kingdom pet import process

Each step must be completed in a precise sequence. Start early. We manage every stage and deadline.

1
Implant ISO-compliant microchip
Responsible: Your veterinarian in Serbia
Before or on the same day as the rabies vaccination. Do this first.
2
Complete rabies vaccination
Responsible: Your veterinarian in Serbia
After microchipping. This starts the clock for the titre test.
3
Titre test blood draw
Responsible: Veterinarian plus approved laboratory
At least 30 days after vaccination, sent to an EU/UK-approved laboratory. Results generally take 2-4 weeks, longer if the sample has to be couriered abroad.
4
Observe the 3-month post-titre wait
Responsible: Owner planning
Runs from the blood draw date, not the result date. This is the longest fixed block in the timeline and cannot be shortened.
5
Book cargo routing, since the direct Belgrade-London flight cannot carry the pet
Responsible: Owner or IPATA-registered pet transport agent
4-6 weeks before travel. Confirm space with Lufthansa Cargo or via the Austrian Airlines Vienna connection.
6
Government-endorsed Serbian export health certificate
Responsible: Serbian vet and Veterinary Directorate
Vet examination shortly before departure, endorsed by the Veterinary Directorate within 2-3 working days.
7
GB Pet Health Certificate and dog tapeworm treatment
Responsible: Official Veterinarian
Certificate issued by an Official Veterinarian, valid 10 days. Dogs treated for tapeworm 24-120 hours before GB arrival.
Requirements

What your pet needs to enter United Kingdom

Every item below must be in place before your pet can travel. We manage and verify each one.

Microchip
ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip required, and it must be implanted before or on the same day as the rabies vaccination that will be used for UK entry. A chip fitted after vaccination invalidates that vaccination for travel purposes.
Rabies vaccination
Full primary course required after microchipping. A simple 21-day wait is not enough on its own here, because Serbia sits outside both of the UK's listed country groups (see titre test below).
Rabies titre test
Required. Serbia does not appear on either Part 1 or Part 2 of the UK's listed-country annex (confirmed against the retained EU Regulation 577/2013 Annex II text on legislation.gov.uk), which makes it an unlisted country for GB entry purposes. Blood must be drawn at least 30 days after the rabies vaccination, tested at an EU or UK-approved laboratory, and show a result of 0.5 IU/ml or above. A 3-calendar-month wait then runs from the date of the blood draw, not the date the result arrives. Source: gov.uk/bring-pet-to-great-britain.
Quarantine
No routine quarantine for a fully compliant pet. A pet that arrives with a missing or incorrectly timed step can be detained at the owner's cost or refused entry, so the sequence above has to be followed exactly.
Import permit
No general import licence required for a non-commercial move of up to five pets travelling with their owner. The GB Pet Health Certificate itself is the travel document APHA checks on arrival.
Health certificate
GB Pet Health Certificate required, completed by an Official Veterinarian shortly before travel and valid for 10 days from issue to entry into Great Britain.
Export permit (Serbia)
No formal export licence needed for a non-commercial move of your own pet. Serbia's Veterinary Directorate handles export documentation on a per-certificate basis rather than a general permit system.

Critical points to read before you book

Air Serbia's direct Belgrade-Heathrow flight cannot carry your pet under any circumstances. Budget the extra time and cost of a connecting cargo route through Vienna or Frankfurt from the outset, not as a late discovery.

Serbia is unlisted for UK entry, not listed. The 3-month wait after the titre blood draw is fixed and cannot be reduced by paying for a faster lab or a rush booking.

The microchip must go in before the rabies vaccination that counts for the titre test. Get this order wrong and the whole vaccination-to-titre sequence has to restart.

Airlines

Carriers approved for this route

Not all airlines accept live animals on this route. We know which carriers to use and how to book.

AIR SERBIA
Air Serbia
Air Serbia flies Belgrade to London Heathrow directly, roughly ten times a week, but does not carry pets on any UK route in the cabin, hold or as cargo. This is a route restriction specific to the UK (and to the UAE and Qatar), not a general pet ban, so the direct flight cannot be used for a pet move whatever the crate size.
Cargo Only
AUSTRIAN
Austrian Airlines
Regional connection from Belgrade to Vienna (VIE), a short hop well suited to a nervous traveller. Pets that fit airline crate limits and travel with an accompanying adult go as accompanied checked baggage in the pressurised hold; larger pets or unaccompanied shipments transfer to Lufthansa Cargo for the onward Vienna-London leg, since Austrian routes true cargo consignments through its parent carrier.
Cargo Only
LUFTHANSA
Lufthansa
Cargo routing via Frankfurt (FRA) or Munich (MUC) from Belgrade, then on to London. Pets travel in a pressurised, temperature-controlled hold through Lufthansa Cargo, booked separately from any passenger ticket. Brachycephalic breeds face additional restrictions.
Cargo Only

The rules for bringing a pet into United Kingdom

United Kingdom runs a strict import regime, so every document is checked closely on arrival. Build your plan around the fixed waiting periods, because they set the pace for everything else.

How to keep this move on track

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

Confirm live animal acceptance on every flight segment before booking. Not all aircraft or connections take pets, and cargo terms can differ leg to leg.

Keep every original document together and take copies. Border officials want originals, and a missing signature or date can hold up clearance.

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

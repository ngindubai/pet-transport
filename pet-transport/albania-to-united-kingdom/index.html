Step by step

The Albania to United Kingdom import process

Responsible: Albania-based vet

02
As early as possible; the whole process is measured from this date.

No sooner than 30 full days after vaccination.

Responsible: Albania-based vet, shipping to an approved lab

04

Responsible: Owner tracks the dates

Requirements

United Kingdom entry requirements

Every item below must be in place before your pet can enter. We verify and track each one.

Microchip
ISO 11784/11785 microchip implanted before the rabies vaccination used for the titre test. A vaccination given first invalidates the process and it must be repeated after chipping. Source: GOV.UK, 'Bringing your pet dog, cat or ferret to Great Britain,' 2026.
Rabies vaccination
Current rabies vaccination required. Albania sits outside both of GB's listed-country tables, so vaccination alone does not clear a pet for travel; it starts the clock for the titre test. Source: GOV.UK, 'Bringing your pet dog, cat or ferret to Great Britain: Which pet travel document you need,' 2026.
Rabies titre test
Rabies antibody blood test taken no sooner than 30 full days after vaccination, result of at least 0.5 IU/ml, analysed by an approved laboratory. Source: GOV.UK, 'Bringing your pet dog, cat or ferret to Great Britain: Blood tests for rabies,' 2026.
Quarantine
No routine quarantine for a pet that meets the unlisted-country rules in full before travel. Quarantine only applies where an APHA import licence has been issued for a non-compliant animal. Source: GOV.UK, 2026.
Import permit
No APHA import licence needed for a compliant pet under the standard unlisted-country rules. One is only required alongside approved quarantine for an animal that hasn't met the titre test and waiting period. Source: GOV.UK, 2026.
Health certificate
Great Britain Pet Health Certificate, issued by an official or accredited vet, valid 10 days from issue to arrival in Great Britain. Source: GOV.UK, 2026.
Leaving Albania

Export requirements

Export permit
No separate export permit is published for pets leaving Albania. The export health certificate below, endorsed by AKU, is the operative document.
Veterinary inspection
A clinical examination by a licensed Albanian veterinarian confirming the animal is fit to travel and free of visible disease, carried out close to the travel date.
Government endorsement
The vet-issued certificate is endorsed by the Autoriteti Kombëtar i Ushqimit (AKU, National Food Authority), Albania's veterinary competent authority, before the pet leaves the country.
Health certificate
Bilingual (Albanian/English) veterinary health certificate covering identification, vaccination history and the fitness-to-travel declaration, issued shortly before departure.
Costs

What this route typically costs

Albanian vet visits, vaccination, blood draw and AKU-endorsed export certificate combined: roughly EUR 250-550, no fixed government schedule published
Rabies titre test and shipping the sample to an approved laboratory abroad: EUR 150-280
GB Pet Health Certificate: GBP 150-300
IATA-compliant travel crate: GBP 70-250
IPATA agent handling cargo booking, either direct BA/IAG Cargo or the Istanbul routing: GBP 700-1,800
Air cargo Tirana to Heathrow, direct or via Istanbul: GBP 900-2,500 depending on crate size and routing

Critical points

Albania has no widely available EU-recognised laboratory for the rabies titre test. Budget extra time and cost for the blood sample to travel abroad and come back with a result, on top of the 3-month wait that follows.

Wizz Air, the busiest carrier on this route by far, will not carry pets at all, in the cabin or the hold. Booking a cheap Wizz Air fare and assuming the pet can travel alongside it is a mistake that surfaces too late to fix quickly.

The 3-month wait is counted from the day blood was drawn, not the day the lab confirms a pass. Vaccinating and testing out of sequence, or miscounting from the result date, resets the whole timeline.

The AKU export certificate itself moves quickly once the vet paperwork is in order. It has no bearing on the GB side; the date that actually controls travel is the end of the UK's 3-month post-titre wait.

United Kingdom import rules, explained

United Kingdom has a detailed set of import rules, and each one needs to be satisfied before travel. None of it is unusual, but the order and timing are what catch people out.

Practical tips for this move

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Book the titre test laboratory well ahead. Approved labs have limited slots and the sample takes time to process, so this is not something to leave to the final weeks.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Pet Transport from Fiji to Canada

Bringing your dog or cat from Fiji to Canada takes some coordination, but the requirements are well defined. Around 4-7 weeks should do it, as this is an easier route to plan. No routine quarantine applies as long as every requirement is met. You will not need …

In short: Preparing to move a pet from Fiji to Canada typically takes around 4-7 weeks. There is no routine quarantine on arrival for compliant pets. A rabies titre test is not required.

Step by step

The Fiji to Canada import process

Every step must be completed in sequence. A single missed deadline can add months to your timeline. We own the entire process.

Start of planning.
Confirm the microchip is fitted (expected by the airline and BAF, though not a CFIA requirement).

Responsible: Your vet in Fiji

2
Close to the travel date, matching BAF's certificate timing.
Obtain the CFIA-format Rabies Country-Freedom Certificate for dogs 8 months or older, confirmed in English or French.

Send supporting paperwork to the BAF veterinary officer ahead of the booked final inspection.

Responsible: You or your agent

4
3-5 working days processing once documentation is complete.
Attend the BAF final pre-export inspection and receive the export health certification.

Responsible: BAF veterinary officer

5
4-6 weeks before travel.
Book cargo space on the direct Fiji Airways Nadi to Vancouver service, or a WestJet codeshare connection further inland.

Responsible: You or a pet transport agent

6
Travel day.
Fly Nadi to Vancouver direct, then any onward domestic connection.

Responsible: Airline cargo handlers

7
On arrival.
Clear CBSA at the port of entry; the certificate is checked and the animal collected from the cargo terminal. No quarantine for a compliant arrival.

Responsible: Canada Border Services Agency

Critical points

The rabies vaccine exemption applies to dogs at least 8 months old from a CFIA-recognised rabies-free country. A younger puppy, or a cat, should have its exact requirements confirmed directly with CFIA rather than assuming the same exemption applies without qualification.

The new Fiji Airways-WestJet direct service only started in June 2026, flying three times a week. Confirm current cargo capacity before assuming a booking slot is available on your preferred date.

This direction is genuinely the light one. A pet travelling from Canada into Fiji faces a mandatory minimum 30-day quarantine on arrival, because BAF's own risk framework, not the CFIA's, governs that direction. Fiji's rabies-free recognition by Canada has no bearing on how Fiji treats pets coming the other way.

Meeting Canada's entry requirements

Bringing a pet into Canada is one of the simpler processes. Cover the core requirements and you should have few surprises.

Advice worth following

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

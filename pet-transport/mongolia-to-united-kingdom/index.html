Checklist

What your pet needs

Every item below must be completed and verified before your pet can travel. Expand each category for the detail.

Microchip
Required (ISO 11784/11785). Must be implanted before the rabies vaccination, or the vaccination does not count towards UK entry.
Rabies vaccination
Required. Minimum age 12 weeks at first dose. The titre test blood draw cannot happen until at least 30 days after this vaccination.
Rabies titre test
Required for: pets from Mongolia, an unlisted country under UK APHA rules. Blood drawn at least 30 days after vaccination, sent to an EU or UK-approved laboratory, result must reach 0.5 IU/ml or above. A 3-calendar-month wait from the blood draw date applies before entry.
Quarantine
No routine quarantine if the microchip, vaccination, titre test and 3-month wait are all correctly sequenced. Pets arriving with incomplete or incorrect documentation can face penalty quarantine of up to 4 months at the owner's expense.
Import permit
No formal import permit. The GB Pet Health Certificate serves as the travel document.
Health certificate
GB Pet Health Certificate required, issued by a GAVS-registered official vet in Mongolia, valid 10 days from issue for entry into Great Britain. Dogs also need praziquantel tapeworm treatment 24-120 hours before arrival, recorded in the certificate.
Export permit
No standalone export permit for personal pets. The General Authority for Veterinary Services (GAVS), based in Ulaanbaatar, oversees veterinary export documentation.
Veterinary inspection
Clinical examination by a licensed Mongolian vet before travel, confirming fitness to fly and recording microchip and vaccination status.
Government endorsement
Health certificate processed through GAVS's digital veterinary certificate system, in nationwide use since May 2019, fully rolled out from early 2020.
Health certificate
GB Pet Health Certificate, completed by a GAVS-registered vet within 10 days of travel.

Airlines

Carriers on this route

Not all airlines accept live animals on this route. Confirm the operating carrier's current policy for every flight segment.

MIAT Mongolian Airlines
Ulaanbaatar's own national carrier operates Boeing 787 Dreamliners on its long-haul network to Frankfurt and Istanbul, with onward connections to London. Cabin pets under 5kg (with carrier). Checked hold pets up to 42kg combined weight. An unusually long brachycephalic exclusion list applies to the hold, covering 18 breeds including Boxer, Bulldog, Pug, Shih Tzu, Cane Corso, Dogue de Bordeaux and Mastiff. Hold and cargo suspended when forecast temperature exceeds 29.4C or falls below 7.2C at any airport on the route.
Cabin and Cargo
Turkish Airlines
Ulaanbaatar to Istanbul, then onward to London on a single ticket. Cabin pets under 8kg accepted; brachycephalic breeds excluded from both cabin and hold since October 2021. Cargo hold via Turkish Cargo, flat USD 150 fee up to 50kg. Ground temperature embargo when any airport on the route exceeds 29C, common from May to September.
Cabin and Cargo
Air China
Connects via Beijing to European gateways with onward London service. Snub-nosed and aggressive breeds are not accepted for carriage under Air China's general pet rules. Checked baggage limit 32kg on international sectors; heavier pets move via Air China Cargo. This routing is workable for the UK leg; it is not usable for dogs travelling onward to the US, where Air China suspended dog transport in February 2025.

Critical points

Mongolia is an unlisted country under UK APHA rules. The titre test and 3-month wait apply in full, with no exemption for shorter trips or urgent moves.

There is no direct flight between Ulaanbaatar and London. MIAT's own long-haul network reaches Frankfurt and Istanbul; from either hub you need an onward connection, which means the pet changes handlers mid-journey unless you route on a single Turkish Airlines or MIAT-interline ticket.

MIAT's brachycephalic exclusion list for the cargo hold is longer than most airlines', 18 breeds, and includes some dogs owners don't always think of as flat-faced, such as Cane Corso and Dogue de Bordeaux. Check your specific breed against the current list before booking, not after.

Verify current requirements directly with UK APHA (apha.service.gov.uk) before booking flights. Country classifications can change.

United Kingdom import rules, explained

United Kingdom has a detailed set of import rules, and each one needs to be satisfied before travel. None of it is unusual, but the order and timing are what catch people out.

Practical tips for this move

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Book the titre test laboratory well ahead. Approved labs have limited slots and the sample takes time to process, so this is not something to leave to the final weeks.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Checklist

What your pet needs

Every item below must be completed and verified before your pet can travel. Expand each category for the detail.

Microchip
Not legally mandated, but a 15-digit chip meeting ISO 11784 or Annex A to ISO 11785 is expected on the paperwork. Implant it before the rabies vaccination so the certificate and chip number match.
Rabies vaccination
Required, given between 30 days and 12 months before arrival. Dogs also need current cover against distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis and parvovirus; cats need feline viral rhinotracheitis, panleukopenia and feline leukaemia.
Rabies titre test
Not required for a pet arriving from Canada, or from any other country, under Eswatini's own import rules. Eswatini simply doesn't ask for a rabies antibody test, regardless of the direction of travel or either country's own high-risk classification for the reverse journey.
Quarantine
No quarantine for a pet arriving with complete, compliant paperwork. Non-compliant arrivals face quarantine where facilities allow, return to origin, or, in unresolved serious cases, euthanasia at the importer's expense.
Import permit
A Veterinary Import Permit from the Director of Animal Health is required, since Canada sits outside the SADC bloc that qualifies for a waiver. Apply well ahead of travel; the permit is valid for 6 months once issued.
Health certificate
CFIA-endorsed international health certificate presented on arrival, completed within 10 days of entry, covering vaccination history, microchip number and a clean bill of health.
Export permit
No CFIA export permit required for a personal pet leaving Canada.
Health certificate
Health certificate issued by a CFIA District Veterinarian or an accredited Canadian vet, endorsed by CFIA if the destination requires it.

Critical points

No CDC-style rule applies here; that machinery governs entry to the United States, not entry to Eswatini. Plan around the Director of Animal Health's own requirements instead, which are lighter than the US route's paperwork by a wide margin.

There is no long-haul carrier at King Mswati III International Airport. Every pet connects through Johannesburg on KLM or Lufthansa, then transfers to a regional Airlink flight. Book this as one interline cargo movement, with a ground agent managing the O.R. Tambo handover.

The import permit fee for Eswatini is not published anywhere we could verify. Ask directly when submitting the application rather than budgeting on a guess.

Eswatini's own rules currently ask for no titre test from any origin country, but that's Eswatini's policy to change. Confirm with the Director of Animal Health close to your travel date.

Meeting Eswatini's entry requirements

The path into Eswatini is straightforward to follow once you know the steps. Give the time-sensitive items priority and check the details below.

Advice worth following

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

Requirements

Kyrgyzstan entry requirements

Every item below must be in place before your pet can enter. We verify and track each one.

Microchip
Not required by Kyrgyzstan, though strongly recommended: a non-encrypted, ISO 11784/11785-compliant 15-digit microchip, with the number recorded on the health certificate if fitted.
Rabies vaccination
Required. Current rabies vaccination given no sooner than 21 days and no more than 11 months before entry. A multi-year vaccine needs the vet's confirmation of ongoing validity noted on the certificate.
Rabies titre test
Not required. Kyrgyzstan does not ask for a rabies antibody titre test on a pet arriving from any country, a genuine outlier given how strictly the reverse direction is treated: a Kyrgyzstan-origin pet entering the UK needs the full unlisted-country titre protocol and a 3-month wait. Nothing like that applies here.
Quarantine
No quarantine for pets arriving with complete, compliant documentation.
Import permit
Not required for an owner travelling with a small number of pets. Separate USDA export guidance for the same corridor cites a limit of up to five dogs and/or cats per traveller before extra permissions are needed; treat one or two animals as the safe, unambiguous case.
Health certificate
Government-endorsed veterinary health certificate issued within 5 days of travel, a noticeably tighter window than the 10-day certificate APHA typically issues for other destinations. Cats also get a clinical check for dermatophytosis (ringworm); a cat with active lesions cannot be imported.
Leaving United Kingdom

Export requirements

Export permit
An export application (EXA) is submitted to APHA alongside the certificate request; there is no separate standalone export permit for a companion pet.
Veterinary inspection
Clinical examination by an APHA-authorised Official Veterinarian (OV), confirming the pet is fit to travel and meets Kyrgyzstan's stated health conditions.
Government endorsement
Only an APHA-authorised OV can issue and sign the Export Health Certificate (EHC). Applications submitted more than 7 working days ahead are processed 7 working days before the certification date. Source: GOV.UK, 'Taking your pet dog, cat or ferret abroad: Getting an animal health certificate,' 2026.
Health certificate
Bilingual Export Health Certificate confirming microchip (if fitted), rabies vaccination and general health, valid for a limited window around the travel date, typically 10 days under APHA's own rules.

Critical points

Kyrgyzstan's 5-day health certificate validity window is tighter than the 10-day window APHA usually works to. Book the OV appointment against the confirmed flight date, not the other way round.

There is no direct flight between London and Bishkek. Every route needs one hub connection, either Istanbul on Turkish Airlines or Almaty on Air Astana.

Aeroflot has historically served Bishkek, but Western sanctions since March 2022 bar Russian carriers from EU and UK airspace and airports. It is not a routing option regardless of any headline fare you find.

Cats need a clinical dermatophytosis check before travel. A cat with active ringworm lesions cannot be imported into Kyrgyzstan under any circumstances.

Kyrgyzstan import rules, explained

Kyrgyzstan keeps its requirements reasonable, though a few steps have fixed timing. Map those out first and the rest falls into place.

Practical tips for this move

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

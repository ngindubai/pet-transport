In short: Preparing to move a pet from Belize to United Kingdom typically takes around 17-21 weeks. There is no routine quarantine on arrival for compliant pets. A rabies titre test is required, which adds a fixed waiting period, so start early.

Step by step

The Belize to United Kingdom pet import process

Each step must be completed in a precise sequence. Start early. We manage every stage and deadline.

Responsible: Your vet in Belize
Start of planning, 5-6 months before travel.
2
Draw blood for the titre test at least 30 days after vaccination, send to an APHA-approved laboratory, wait for a 0.5 IU/ml result or higher.
Responsible: Your vet and the approved laboratory
Roughly 4.5-5 months before travel.
3
The fixed 3 calendar month wait begins automatically from the blood draw date. Nothing shortens it, whatever the result timeline looks like.
Responsible: No action needed, just planning
Runs in the background from the blood draw date.
4
Contact BAHA's Animal Health Department directly to confirm what export documentation they issue for a single pet leaving Belize.
Responsible: You or your agent
6-8 weeks before travel.
5
Book the Belize City to Miami leg and the connecting Miami to Heathrow service.
Responsible: You or a pet transport agent
6-8 weeks before travel.

Responsible: Your vet in Belize
1-5 days before UK arrival.

Collect the GB Pet Health Certificate, valid only 10 days from signature.
Responsible: BAHA-registered vet
Within 10 days of travel.

Arrive at an approved GB pet entry point. Heathrow, Gatwick, Manchester and Edinburgh all handle pet arrivals.

Day of arrival.
Requirements

What your pet needs to enter United Kingdom

Every item below must be in place before your pet can travel. We manage and verify each one.

Microchip
ISO 11784/11785 microchip, implanted before the rabies vaccination. A vaccination given before the chip does not count and has to be repeated. Source: GOV.UK.
Rabies vaccination
Current rabies vaccination, given after the microchip. Source: GOV.UK.
Rabies titre test
Required. Belize does not appear on either Part 1 or Part 2 of the UK's listed countries, the same unlisted category as the rest of Central America. Blood drawn at least 30 days after the primary vaccination, tested at an APHA-approved laboratory, result of 0.5 IU/ml or higher. A fixed 3 calendar month wait then runs from the blood draw date itself, not the result date. Source: GOV.UK, 2026.
Quarantine
None for a pet that meets every requirement on arrival. Incomplete paperwork can mean up to 4 months in quarantine at the owner's cost. Source: GOV.UK.
Health certificate
GB Pet Health Certificate, completed by the BAHA-registered vet and valid for 10 days from signature. It has to still be valid on the day your pet actually lands.
Export permit (Belize)
No single-pet export permit process is published on BAHA's own site. What is published covers importing a pet into Belize in detail: BZ$50 permits, BZ$20 inspection fees, a 2-day arrival notice. For the outbound side, contact BAHA's Animal Health Department directly at Central Farm, Cayo District, and confirm exactly what paperwork they issue for a pet leaving the country, well before you plan to travel.

Critical points to read before you book

Belize is unlisted for UK pet travel purposes, the same category as every other Central American country. The titre test and the 3-month wait from the blood draw date are both fixed. There is no waiver and no way to shorten the clock once it starts.

BAHA's own published guidance covers importing a pet into Belize in real detail. It does not publish an equivalent single-pet export process. Confirm directly with BAHA's Animal Health Department what they will actually issue for an outbound pet, and build in extra time for that correspondence.

There is no direct flight between Belize and the UK. Every pet transits Miami, American's only nonstop option out of Belize City toward a hub with UK service. Confirm cargo acceptance on both legs before committing to a travel date.

Meeting United Kingdom's entry requirements

The requirements for United Kingdom are thorough rather than difficult. Work through them methodically and confirm anything you are unsure about with the official source.

Advice worth following

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

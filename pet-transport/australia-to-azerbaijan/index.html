Critical points

Both processes, DAFF export and Azerbaijan import permit, must run simultaneously. Do not wait for one before starting the other.

DAFF export requirements are strict. A DAFF-accredited vet with international export experience is essential.

The journey from Australia to Baku is 20-26 hours with connections. Confirm your pet is fit for this duration with your vet.

Emirates operates DXB-GYD directly and is a reliable option for the last leg into Baku. Confirm live animal cargo acceptance on the DXB-GYD segment.

Azerbaijan import rules, explained

Azerbaijan has a clear set of import requirements that are manageable with some planning. Follow them in order and keep an eye on the dates that cannot move.

Practical tips for this move

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

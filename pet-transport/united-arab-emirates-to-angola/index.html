Requirements

What your pet needs to enter Angola

Every item below must be in place before your pet can travel. We manage and verify each one.

Microchip
A 15-digit chip meeting ISO Standard 11784 or Annex A of ISO Standard 11785 is the accepted form of identification, though not stated as a hard entry requirement in the way the certificate and vaccination are. Source: PetTravel.com Angola Pet Import Requirements, cross-checked against the Instituto dos Serviços de Veterinária's published rules, 2026.
Rabies vaccination
Published guidance states the primary rabies vaccination must fall between 4 and 11 months before entry, an unusually tight and specific window compared with most countries on this site. This detail comes from a compiled secondary source rather than a live ISV page, so confirm it directly with ISV or an experienced agent before fixing a vaccination date for a UAE departure.
Rabies titre test
Not required. Angola doesn't apply a rabies antibody titre test to pets from any country of origin, including the UAE, the same position confirmed on the Angola-to-UAE route built alongside this one and on the UK-to-Angola route elsewhere on this site.
Quarantine
No quarantine for a pet that meets the documentation and vaccination requirements. A non-compliant pet can be quarantined, returned to the country of origin, or in the worst case euthanised, all at the importer's expense.
Import permit
Not required for a pet travelling with its owner.
Health certificate
Government-endorsed health certificate covering owner and pet details, vaccination and treatment history, and a vet's attestation of good health, completed within 14 days of entry into Angola.
Export permit (United Arab Emirates)
No separate export permit exists. MOCCAE's veterinary health certificate for export or re-export of live animals is itself the export clearance document, applied for through the MOCCAE portal, which requires a UAE Pass login.
Government endorsement
The MOCCAE-approved airport clinic vet endorses the certificate directly. No further government office visit is required.

Critical points to read before you book

The published rule that rabies vaccination must fall between 4 and 11 months before entry is unusual and comes from a compiled secondary source, not a live official ISV page. Confirm it directly with ISV or a transport agent before committing to a vaccination date; getting this wrong could mean starting the vaccination cycle over.

Wolf hybrids cannot enter Angola under any circumstances, and Savannah or Bengal cats need documented proof of at least five generations removed from a wild ancestor. Check this before booking, not after.

The Angola-side health certificate has a tight 14-day window before entry. Time that appointment against your confirmed flight date, since Angola's own export process for pets moving in either direction remains uncommon enough that standard turnaround isn't always fast.

Cabin and Cargo

Angola import rules, explained

Angola keeps its requirements reasonable, though a few steps have fixed timing. Map those out first and the rest falls into place.

Practical tips for this move

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

Confirm live animal acceptance on every flight segment before booking. Not all aircraft or connections take pets, and cargo terms can differ leg to leg.

Keep every original document together and take copies. Border officials want originals, and a missing signature or date can hold up clearance.

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

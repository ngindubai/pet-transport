In short: Preparing to move a pet from Brunei to United Arab Emirates typically takes around 10-14 weeks. There is no routine quarantine on arrival for compliant pets. A rabies titre test is required, which adds a fixed waiting period, so start early.

Requirements

United Arab Emirates entry requirements

Every item below must be in place before your pet can enter. We verify and track each one.

Microchip
ISO 11784/11785 required, implanted before the rabies vaccination and scannable on arrival.
Rabies vaccination
Required. If this is the pet's first vaccination, at least 21 days must pass before travel.
Rabies titre test
Required. Brunei does not appear on MOCCAE's published low-risk country list, so a rabies antibody titre test at an accredited laboratory applies, minimum result 0.5 IU/ml. There's no fixed waiting period after the blood draw; the test result itself, valid for 365 days provided vaccination stays current, is what MOCCAE checks. Minimum import age from this category of origin is 15 weeks.
Quarantine
No routine quarantine for pets arriving with complete, compliant documentation.
Import permit
Required. Applied for online through MOCCAE before departure, valid for 90 days from issue.
Health certificate
MOCCAE model veterinary health certificate, endorsed by Brunei's DAA, presented alongside the import permit on arrival.
Leaving Brunei

Export requirements

Export permit
Export permit from the Department of Agriculture and Agrifood (DAA), under Brunei's Ministry of Primary Resources and Tourism. Apply with microchip number, species, breed and date of birth.
Health certificate
Veterinary health certificate from a Brunei-licensed vet, endorsed by DAA, reformatted to meet the MOCCAE model.
Costs

What this route typically costs

Titre test and laboratory fee: USD 150-280.
DAA export permit and health certificate: USD 90-180.
MOCCAE import permit: fee set at booking through the online portal.
IATA-compliant travel crate: USD 90-320.
Cargo, Brunei to Dubai or Abu Dhabi via Singapore: USD 1,100-2,600 depending on crate size and season.
Agent coordination for the Singapore transfer: USD 200-450.
Total typical range: USD 1,600-3,800 over 10-14 weeks.

Critical points

MOCCAE sets no fixed post-titre wait, unlike the EU's 90-day rule, but the titre test itself still has to pass before the import permit application makes sense, so don't apply for the permit until the result is confirmed.

No airline flies pets directly out of Brunei. Every booking routes through Singapore, adding a transfer and a second set of crate checks to plan for.

The MOCCAE import permit's 90-day validity window runs from issue, not from the intended travel date. Time the application close to the actual flight.

What United Arab Emirates requires

The path into United Arab Emirates is straightforward to follow once you know the steps. Give the time-sensitive items priority and check the details below.

Avoiding the common mistakes

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

Confirm live animal acceptance on every flight segment before booking. Not all aircraft or connections take pets, and cargo terms can differ leg to leg.

Keep every original document together and take copies. Border officials want originals, and a missing signature or date can hold up clearance.

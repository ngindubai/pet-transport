Critical points

Brazil requires a specific MAPA trilingual certificate format. A standard DLD health certificate will not be accepted at the Brazilian border. The certificate must be in Portuguese, English, and Thai, and must follow MAPA's approved template. Source a vet in Thailand with experience completing MAPA-format documents, or use a specialist agent.

The MAPA import permit must be obtained before the health certificate is finalised. The permit number must appear on the certificate. Do not get the certificate before the permit is confirmed.

Bangkok to Brazil has no direct flights. The total cargo transit time is typically 24 to 40 hours via a European or Middle Eastern hub. Choose the routing with the shortest hub layover and confirm live animal cargo acceptance on every segment.

The antiparasitic treatment must be within 15 days of the health certificate issue date, not the travel date. Time this carefully.

Brazil import rules, explained

Brazil has a clear set of import requirements that are manageable with some planning. Follow them in order and keep an eye on the dates that cannot move.

Practical tips for this move

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

FAQ

Common questions

The MAPA import permit is an official Brazilian government authorisation to import a specific companion animal. You apply through MAPA’s SINDI platform. Processing typically takes 2 to 4 weeks. The permit must be in hand before you finalise the health certificate in Thailand, because the permit number must appear on the certificate.
Most vets in Bangkok do not routinely prepare MAPA certificates. The MAPA format is specific: trilingual (Portuguese, English, Thai), with particular sections and attestations. Using a Bangkok vet with MAPA experience or a specialist pet transport agent who coordinates the documentation is strongly recommended.
Major Brazilian international airports with MAPA inspection facilities include Sao Paulo Guarulhos (GRU), Rio de Janeiro Galeao (GIG), and Brasilia (BSB). Confirm that your chosen arrival airport has live animal inspection capacity before booking. Not all airports have active MAPA animal inspection services.
Brazil requires a valid rabies vaccination certificate from all origin countries, including Thailand. Carry the original vaccination record. Thailand is not classified as rabies-free, but MAPA’s requirement for a valid vaccination applies regardless of origin status.

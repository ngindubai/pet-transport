Checklist

What your pet needs

Every item below must be completed and verified before your pet can travel. Expand each category for the detail.

Microchip
Not explicitly mandated by BAHA's published import conditions, but the UAE side already requires one for municipality registration before the MOCCAE export permit is even issued, so this is a non-issue in practice.
Rabies vaccination
Required for pets over 3 months old, and it must sit at least 30 days before the pet's actual arrival date in Belize, not before departure from the UAE. Puppies and kittens under 4 months travel without it but go into home quarantine in Belize until 30 days after a local vet administers it there. Source: BAHA Import Conditions for Dogs and Cats, June 2023.
Rabies titre test
Not required. BAHA's published import conditions don't ask for a rabies antibody test from any country of origin, including the UAE. A compliant, correctly timed vaccination is what counts on this route, not a blood titre.
Quarantine
None for a compliant pet over 4 months old. Only applies to underage puppies and kittens travelling without the rabies vaccination, held at home until 30 days post-vaccination.
Import permit
BAHA import permit required before travel, BZ$50 (roughly AED 92 at the Belize dollar's fixed 2:1 US dollar peg), valid 90 days from issue and covering up to 10 cats and dogs on a single permit. The importer must also notify BAHA of the arrival date, time and flight number at least 2 days ahead so a veterinary officer is on hand, and pay a BZ$20 (roughly AED 37) inspection fee on landing. Source: BAHA, Application Procedure to Import a Pet into Belize.
Health certificate
International veterinary certificate issued by the UAE-based vet within 14 days of the pet's arrival date in Belize, a tighter window than the MOCCAE export permit's own 30-day validity, so time the appointment close to departure, not early. Records the microchip, vaccination and general clinical health, plus endoparasite and ectoparasite treatment given within 30 days of departure, which BAHA expects even though it isn't a standalone permit step. Source: BAHA Import Conditions for Dogs and Cats, June 2023.
Export permit
No separate export permit distinct from the MOCCAE export health certificate below, valid 30 days from issue. The pet must already be registered with its emirate's municipality (Dubai Municipality or ADAFSA in Abu Dhabi) and current on municipality-mandated vaccines before MOCCAE grants it. Source: moccae.gov.ae, checked 17 July 2026.
Veterinary inspection
A final in-person inspection by a government veterinarian, typically at the airport shortly before departure, confirms the animal matches its paperwork and is fit to fly.
Government endorsement
Health certificate endorsed by MOCCAE (Ministry of Climate Change and Environment) or the relevant emirate's municipality, working alongside MOCCAE's federal system rather than instead of it.
Health certificate
MOCCAE export health certificate confirming the animal meets UAE health and export requirements, drafted around BAHA's import checklist rather than a generic template. Source: moccae.gov.ae, checked 17 July 2026.

Carriers on this route

Not all airlines accept live animals on this route. Confirm the operating carrier's current policy for every flight segment.

Emirates SkyCargo
Runs a nonstop Dubai to Miami service roughly seven times a week, a flight of around 14 hours 45 minutes and one of the longest scheduled routes Emirates operates. Every pet leaves the UAE as manifest cargo, booked through an IATA-certified pet shipping agent rather than a standard cargo reservation. Snub-nosed breeds face a seasonal embargo above 29C (85F) forecast temperature, broadly May through September.
Cargo Only
American Airlines
Covers the second leg, Miami direct to Belize City, in under two hours, part of American's expanded 2025 and 2026 Belize network. Confirm current cargo pet acceptance directly before booking, since availability on this programme has fluctuated in the past.

MOCCAE export health certificate and airport inspection: AED 150-350

BAHA arrival inspection fee, BZ$20: AED 35-40
IATA-compliant travel crate: AED 500-1,800
Cargo, Dubai to Belize City via Miami: AED 7,300-15,400 depending on crate size and both carriers' rates

Total typical range: AED 9,400-20,600. Treat this as a planning estimate; confirm current figures before booking.

Critical points

The MOCCAE export permit runs for only 30 days, considerably shorter than BAHA's own 90-day import permit. Book flights before applying for either, but treat the MOCCAE side as the one that actually sets the deadline.

BAHA needs 2 days' advance notice of your exact flight before it lands, or there may be no veterinary officer at Philip S.W. Goldson International Airport to clear your pet.

Every pet leaving the UAE travels as manifest cargo, never as accompanied baggage, whatever the airline. Book through an IATA-certified pet shipping agent rather than a standard travel agent.

Belize's 14-day health certificate window is tighter than it first looks against a 90-day import permit. A certificate signed even a day too early is void on landing, regardless of how compliant everything else is.

Belize entry requirements at a glance

Getting a pet into Belize is a middle of the road process. The rules are well defined, so most of the work is simply staying on schedule.

Getting the details right

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

Get your pet used to the travel crate weeks in advance. A calm, crate-familiar animal travels far better than one meeting the crate for the first time at the airport.

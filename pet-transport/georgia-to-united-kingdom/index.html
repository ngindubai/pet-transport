Pet Transport from Georgia to the United Kingdom

Planning a pet relocation from Georgia to United Kingdom is easier when you work backwards from your travel date. Allow about 22-28 weeks; the requirements are detailed but predictable once mapped out. Pets with correct documentation skip quarantine entirely. …

In short: Preparing to move a pet from Georgia to United Kingdom typically takes around 22-28 weeks. There is no routine quarantine on arrival for compliant pets. A rabies titre test is required, which adds a fixed waiting period, so start early.

Critical points

The 3-month wait runs from the blood draw date. Miscalculate this by one day and UK entry will be refused.

Confirm the titre test laboratory holds EU/UK approval before drawing blood. Georgia is close to approved labs in Turkey and Germany.

Georgia is a non-listed country for UK pet import. Full APHA protocol applies.

British Airways direct from Tbilisi is a strong option but confirm cargo pet acceptance before booking.

How United Kingdom handles incoming pets

United Kingdom has a detailed set of import rules, and each one needs to be satisfied before travel. None of it is unusual, but the order and timing are what catch people out.

What experienced owners do

Book the titre test laboratory well ahead. Approved labs have limited slots and the sample takes time to process, so this is not something to leave to the final weeks.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

Yes. British Airways operates direct Tbilisi (TBS) to London Heathrow service and BA World Cargo handles pets. Turkish Airlines via Istanbul is the most frequent alternative with good cargo operations. Qatar Airways via Doha and Lufthansa via Frankfurt are also viable.
Georgia may not have an EU/UK approved rabies titre test laboratory. Blood samples are typically shipped to an approved lab in Turkey, Germany, or elsewhere. Confirm laboratory arrangements with your vet before drawing the blood sample, and factor in 2-3 weeks for sample transit and results.
Your official vet in Georgia prepares the health certificate and Georgia’s National Food Agency (NFA) endorses it. The certificate is valid for 10 days from the examination date. Allow a few working days for NFA processing. An IPATA agent familiar with Georgia can manage this process.
Brachycephalic (flat-faced) breeds have restrictions on several carriers. Turkish Airlines, Emirates, and some other carriers restrict flat-faced breeds on certain routes or seasons. If your dog is a Pug, Bulldog, French Bulldog, or similar breed, check each carrier’s breed list before booking. British Airways also has breed restrictions.

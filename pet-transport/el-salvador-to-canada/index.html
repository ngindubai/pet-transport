Pet Transport from El Salvador to Canada

A move from El Salvador to Canada with a dog or cat is mostly about preparing the documents in the right order. Expect about 6-9 weeks. Nothing here is unusually complex, but the deadlines matter. No routine quarantine applies as long as every requirement is …

In short: Preparing to move a pet from El Salvador to Canada typically takes around 6-9 weeks. There is no routine quarantine on arrival for compliant pets. A rabies titre test is not required.

Critical points

El Salvador is on CFIA's list of countries at high-risk for dog rabies. This affects dogs only. The September 2022 ban applies to commercial dogs, defined broadly to include resale, adoption, fostering, breeding, show and research animals; a genuine personal pet dog that will live with its owner is not covered by that ban.

A dog from El Salvador must be at least 7 months old on the date of arrival in Canada. There is no exception for younger puppies on this route.

Entry is restricted to a short list of CFIA-designated airports with an animal inspection facility. Flying into a Canadian airport outside that list with a dog from a high-risk country is not an option, regardless of how convenient the routing looks otherwise.

What Canada requires

Getting a pet into Canada is a middle of the road process. The rules are well defined, so most of the work is simply staying on schedule.

Avoiding the common mistakes

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Microchip before you vaccinate. The microchip must be in place before the rabies shot for the vaccination to count, and getting this order wrong is the most common and costly mistake.

Use an official, government-authorised vet for the health certificate, not just your usual clinic. Many destinations only accept certificates signed by an approved vet.

If this is your first international move, consider an IPATA-registered pet transport agent. They handle the documents, airline booking and crate sizing, and can troubleshoot problems.

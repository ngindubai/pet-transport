Pet Transport from Bolivia to the United Arab Emirates

Relocating a pet from Bolivia to United Arab Emirates follows a clear path once you understand what United Arab Emirates asks for. Allow about 16-20 weeks; the requirements are detailed but predictable once mapped out. Compliant pets are not held in quarantine …

In short: Preparing to move a pet from Bolivia to United Arab Emirates typically takes around 16-20 weeks. There is no routine quarantine on arrival for compliant pets. A rabies titre test is required, which adds a fixed waiting period, so start early.
Regulations checked July 2026
Prepared and reviewed by Pet Transport Global Editorial Team
Step by step

The Bolivia to United Arab Emirates import process

Every step must be completed in sequence. A single missed deadline can add months to your timeline. We own the entire process.

1
Start of planning, at least 4-5 months before travel.
Fit the ISO microchip first, then administer or confirm the rabies vaccination. Getting this order wrong invalidates the vaccination for MOCCAE purposes.

Responsible: Your vet in Bolivia.

2
3-4 months before travel.
Draw blood for the titre test at least 21 days after vaccination (same day is possible with a valid existing booster), send to an accredited laboratory, confirm the 0.5 IU/ml result.

Responsible: Your vet and the accredited laboratory.

3
Runs in the background from the blood draw date.
The fixed 90-day wait begins automatically from the blood draw date. Nothing shortens it.

Responsible: No action needed, just planning.

4
4-6 weeks before travel.
Write to SENASAG requesting the UAE's specific import requirements, then apply for the Certificado Zoosanitario de Exportación.

Responsible: You or your agent.

5
No more than 30 days before the confirmed travel date.
Apply for the MOCCAE import permit online. Time this carefully, since it is valid for only 30 days from issue.

Responsible: You or your agent, via MOCCAE's portal.

6
6-8 weeks before travel.
Book pet-hold space on Santa Cruz to Sao Paulo, then the Emirates nonstop into Dubai or Abu Dhabi.

Responsible: You or a pet transport agent.

7
Day of arrival.
Arrive with the SENASAG-endorsed certificate, titre report and MOCCAE import permit presented together for final inspection.

Responsible: MOCCAE inspector at the port of entry.

Critical points

Bolivia is not on MOCCAE's low-risk list. The titre test and the fixed 90-day wait from the blood draw date are both mandatory, with no waiver available for a genuinely unlisted country of origin.

The MOCCAE import permit is valid for only 30 days from issue. Applying for it before a flight is confirmed risks the permit lapsing before travel; applying too late risks missing the window entirely.

There is no direct flight between Bolivia and the UAE. The Emirates nonstop from Sao Paulo to Dubai is the shortest workable connection, reached via BoA or Gol out of Santa Cruz.

How United Arab Emirates handles incoming pets

United Arab Emirates asks for several pieces of paperwork, and they all need to line up. A checklist and a clear timeline are your best tools here.

What experienced owners do

Keep every original document together and take copies. Border officials want originals, and a missing signature or date can hold up clearance.

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Critical points

The identity verification visit must happen before, and separately from, the RNATT blood draw. Combine the two and the pet defaults to the 30-day Mickleham stay instead of the 10-day one.

The 180-day wait runs from the date the laboratory received the sample, not the vaccination date, the blood draw date, or the day the result comes back.

There is no direct flight between Belgrade and any Australian city. Every plan on this route starts with a Doha or Dubai transfer, and Mickleham quarantine costs are billed separately from anything an agent quotes for the flight itself.

Australia entry requirements at a glance

Entry to Australia is tightly controlled, and officials verify each requirement in turn. Treat it as a sequence where every step gates the next.

Getting the details right

Work backwards from your travel date and add a buffer. Fixed waiting periods mean a single slipped appointment can push the whole move back by weeks.

Check the airline’s crate size and labelling rules early. An undersized or wrongly labelled crate is a frequent reason pets are refused at check-in.

Ask about temperature embargoes for your travel window. Summer and winter cargo restrictions can rule out certain routes and dates entirely.

Book the titre test laboratory well ahead. Approved labs have limited slots and the sample takes time to process, so this is not something to leave to the final weeks.

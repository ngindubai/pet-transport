Import Requirements

Microchip

ISO 11784/11785 required

Rabies Vaccination

Required

Titre Test

Required for: Pets from countries deemed high risk by MAFWR. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml.

Quarantine

No routine quarantine for compliant pets.

Import Permit

Required -- apply in advance from MAFWR

Health Certificate

Official government veterinary certificate required

Breed Restrictions

American Pit Bull Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Rottweiler, Dogo Argentino and others

Frequently Asked Questions

Rabies vaccination is required. Your pet must be microchipped (ISO standard) and have a current, valid rabies vaccination. Additional vaccinations may be recommended but are not required for import. The health certificate must list all vaccinations.

No routine quarantine for compliant pets. Ensure all documentation is complete before travel to avoid penalty quarantine.

Required for: Pets from countries deemed high risk by MAFWR. Minimum: 0.5 IU/ml.

Banned breeds include: American Pit Bull Terrier, Staffordshire Bull Terrier, American Staffordshire Terrier, Rottweiler, Dogo Argentino and others. List subject to change. Verify with MAFWR before booking travel.

Start at least 3 months before travel for most origins. If a titre test is required, start 6-8 months ahead to allow for the post-vaccination wait (30 days), test processing, and the waiting period (90-180 days depending on the route).

How to use this plan

  1. Filter to "Not started" in the Status dropdown above. The "Write next →" bar at the bottom auto-points to the next chronological row.
  2. Click "Open writing brief" on any row to see the full brief: cluster, primary keyword, LLM phrasings, the pre-written 40-60 word direct-answer block, H2 outline, schema requirements, and internal links.
  3. Click "Copy AI prompt" in the modal to get a copy-paste prompt for ChatGPT or Claude. The prompt has all 12 GEO/AEO + humanisation rules baked in.
  4. Mark In progress → Drafted → Published as you go. Status saves to localStorage as ptg_content_plan_status_v1.
  5. Export JSON to back up status snapshots into Git.

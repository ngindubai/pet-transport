Strategic Overview

The goal is to build a diverse, natural backlink profile from relevant, authoritative domains in the pet industry, expat communities, and relocation space. We are not chasing volume — we are chasing topical relevance and domain authority.

Linkable asset: The primary outreach hook is the Global Pet Transport Guide 2026 — a data-rich, free reference page on the site (/blog/global-pet-transport-guide-2026/). Every outreach email leads with this asset, not a homepage link. Journalists and bloggers link to useful data; they do not link to service pages unprompted.

Secondary asset: Individual country guides and route pages (where a target site covers a specific country or destination).

Cadence: 3-5 outreach contacts per week. Monthly review of new links, lost links, and prospect pipeline.

Use for: PetMD, The Spruce Pets, Expatica, AirlineReporter, general media.

We've just published the Global Pet Transport Guide 2026 at PetTransportGlobal — a free reference covering quarantine durations, titre test requirements, import permit lead times, and airline pet policies across 25 countries and 22 airlines. All data is sourced from official government portals (DAFF, APHA, USDA APHIS, MAFF, etc.) and verified in April 2026. Direct link: https://pettransportglobal.surge.sh/blog/global-pet-transport-guide-2026/ The airline comparison table in particular seems hard to find anywhere else in one place — it covers which of the 22 major international carriers allow cabin travel for small pets vs. cargo only. Happy to provide additional data or answer specific questions about any route. Best, Gareth Founder, PetTransportGlobal https://pettransportglobal.surge.sh

Happy to answer questions if anyone's planning a specific route. [Username / Gareth]
